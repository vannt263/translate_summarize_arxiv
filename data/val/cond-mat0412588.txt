    "the quantum two - level system is a model to describe a number of physical objects such as atoms , quantum dots , molecular magnets , etc .",
    "the system can be excited from the ground state to the upper state by changing in time its parameters ( due to external fields ) . if the parameters vary adiabatically slow , the excitation mechanism is called landau - zener ( lz ) transition lzs@xmath0 @xcite ; if the amplitude of the field is small and its frequency is comparable to the level distance , rabi oscillations occur rabi@xmath0 @xcite .",
    "thus the occupation of the levels in a two - state system can be controlled by several parameters , e.g. , by amplitude and frequency of an external field @xcite .",
    "recently , the two - level model was used to describe josephson - junction systems @xcite , i.e. , both charge qubits @xcite and flux qubits @xcite .",

    "<S> the approach is applied to the description of the dynamics of superconducting qubits . in particular , </S>",
    "<S> the case of the interferometer - type charge qubit with periodically varying parameters ( gate voltage or magnetic flux ) is investigated . </S>",
    "<S> the time - averaged energy level populations are calculated as functions of the qubit s control parameters . </S>"
