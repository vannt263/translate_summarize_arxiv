    "topological states of matter exhibit a wealth of novel properties due to their extreme resilience to local perturbations .",
    "a striking manifestation is the quantum hall effect  @xcite , where the exact quantization of the hall response is immune to sample defects  @xcite .",
    "this robustness is intimately linked to the topological chern invariant , which has direct signatures in quantum transport  @xcite .",
    "recent theory and experiments have exposed many new examples of topological phenomena , including the relativistic quantum hall effect in graphene  @xcite , topological insulators  @xcite , and majorana edge modes  @xcite .",
    "work has also focused on the generation of topological floquet states through time - dependent driving  @xcite .    out of equilibrium , much less",
    "is known about the behavior of topological states .",
    "early works on @xmath0 superfluids have shown that topological invariants can be preserved following quenches of the interaction strength  @xcite . in the context of chern insulators , recent studies have shown that the chern number is robust under unitary evolution , even following quenches between topological and non - topological phases  @xcite . however , as we stressed in ref .",
    "@xcite , this does not imply the persistence of all physical observables .",
    "the presence or absence of edge states for example , depends on the final hamiltonian and not just the initial state . in a similar way",
    ", the hall response does not necessarily remain quantized .",
    "this is consistent with recent calculations by wang _",
    "@xcite , who have investigated the hall response following a quantum quench , including an external reservoir to induce decoherence and reduce to the diagonal ensemble .    in this manuscript",
    "we examine the hall response following a quantum quench in a completely isolated system undergoing unitary evolution .",
    "we focus on the haldane model  @xcite , as recently realized using cold atomic gases  @xcite .",
    "we show that the hall response is no longer quantized following a quantum quench between the topological and non - topological phases , in spite of the preservation of the chern index in infinite - size samples . in the zero frequency limit",
    "our results agree with those of refs  @xcite , as oscillatory off - diagonal contributions vanish .",
    "the results are in good agreement with analytical approximations based on the low - energy dirac hamiltonian .",
    "we further examine the detailed properties of the edge currents following a quantum quench in finite - size systems .",
    "we show that the late - time behavior following many traversals of the sample is quantitatively described by a generalized gibbs ensemble ( gge )  @xcite . with a view towards cold atom experiments",

    "<S> we investigate the transport properties of chern insulators following a quantum quench between topological and non - topological phases . </S>",
    "<S> recent works have shown that this yields an excited state for which the chern number is preserved under unitary evolution . however </S>",
    "<S> , this does not imply the preservation of other physical observables , as we stressed in our previous work . here </S>",
    "<S> we provide an analysis of the hall response following a quantum quench in an isolated system , with explicit results for the haldane model . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the hall conductance is no longer related to the chern number in the post - quench state , in agreement with previous work . </S>",
    "<S> we also examine the dynamics of the edge currents in finite - size systems with open boundary conditions along one direction . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the late - time behavior is captured by a generalized gibbs ensemble , after multiple traversals of the sample . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the effects of generic open boundary conditions and confinement potentials . </S>"
