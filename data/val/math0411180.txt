    "<S> we consider generalized closest return times of a complex polynomial of degree at least two . </S>",
    "<S> most previous studies on this subject have focused on the properties of polynomials with particular return times , especially the fibonacci numbers . </S>",
    "<S> we study the general form of these closest return times . </S>",
    "<S> the main result of this paper is that these closest return times are meta - fibonacci numbers . in particular </S>",
    "<S> , this result applies to the return times of a principal nest of a polynomial . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we show that an analogous result holds in a tree with dynamics that is associated with a polynomial . </S>"
