    "<S> we investigate an ideal gas in a time  </S>",
    "<S> dependent external trapping potential . </S>",
    "<S> we use the boltzmann equation with the relaxation time ansatz to explore the time  </S>",
    "<S> dependent energy of an adiabatically isolated system . </S>",
    "<S> in particular we are interested on the dissipation during a potential change along a given protocol with finite velocity . </S>",
    "<S> the role of the relaxation less evolution as a limiting case is studied : starting from an equilibrium distribution and for small times the energy of the gas with relaxation is always smaller than that of the relaxation  less . </S>",
    "<S> this means that relaxation processes show an ambivalent behavior : on the one hand entropy production , but on the other hand reduction of dissipation by driving back the system into equilibrium . </S>"
