    "in this paper we outline our recent investigation about the presence of the disk emission in single peaked bels .",
    "we carried out the following analyzes : i ) fitting of single peaked bels with gaussians and with two component model , and ii ) comparing line parameters of a sample with simulated profiles .    from our investigations",
    "we can conclude that there could be a high probability that the disk emission flux might be present in the single peaked emission line profiles . in principle , the contribution of the disk emission to the total flux of the single peaked bels is in most cases smaller then 50% .",
    "also , we found that the disk inclinations were mainly smaller then @xmath34 .",
    "such small inclinations and large discrepancies from simulated profiles for higher inclinations should be discussed in the contest of unified model , i.e. possible disk orientation to the torus or partial obscuration by the torus .",
