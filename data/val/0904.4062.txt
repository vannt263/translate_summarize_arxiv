    "<S> the semi - classical data attached to stacks of algebroids in the sense of kashiwara and kontsevich are maurer - cartan elements on complex manifolds , which we call extended poisson structures as they generalize holomorphic poisson structures . </S>",
    "<S> a canonical lie algebroid is associated to each maurer - cartan element . </S>",
    "<S> we study the geometry underlying these maurer - cartan elements in the light of lie algebroid theory . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we extend lichnerowicz - poisson cohomology and koszul - brylinski homology to the realm of extended poisson manifolds ; we establish a sufficient criterion for these to be finite dimensional ; we describe how homology and cohomology are related through the evens - lu - weinstein duality module ; and we describe a duality on koszul - brylinski homology , which generalizes the serre duality of dolbeault cohomology . </S>"
