    "asteroseismology is a powerful tool to obtain different kinds of detailed information about the internal structure and evolutionary state of stars which can not be obtained from classical ( non - timeseries ) data .",
    "it is possible to determine fundamental stellar parameters ( mass , radius , surface gravity and mean density ) from the observed oscillation parameters as these are defined by the stellar internal structure .",
