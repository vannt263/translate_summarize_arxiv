    "<S> we investigate the unpolarized pion and kaon fragmentation functions using the nonlocal chiral - quark model . in this model </S>",
    "<S> the interactions between the quarks and pseudoscalar mesons is manifested nonlocally . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , the explicit flavor su(3 ) symmetry breaking effect is taken into account in terms of the current quark masses . </S>",
    "<S> the results of our model are evaluated to higher @xmath0 value @xmath1 by the dglap evolution . </S>",
    "<S> then we compare them with the empirical parametrizations . </S>",
    "<S> we find that our results are in relatively good agreement with the empirical parametrizations and the other theoretical estimations . </S>"
