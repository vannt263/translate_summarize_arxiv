    "experimental tests of gravity on the scale of the solar system show good agreement with predictions of general relativity ( e.g. , @xcite ) .",
    "the nature of the newtonian gravity is the attractive force , which naturally predicts a decelerated expansion of the universe .",
    "contrary to this expectation , it has been discovered that our universe is undergoing an accelerated expansion epoch @xcite . though the accelerated expansion is explained by introducing a cosmological constant , its small but nonzero value",
    "can not be explained naturally @xcite .",
    "the problem might be deeply rooted in the nature of fundamental physics .",
    "this problem has attracted many researchers , and many works have been done , both theoretically and observationally . as a generalisation of the cosmological constant , a dynamical field , called the dark energy model and its variants , are proposed to explain the accelerated expansion of the universe ( see @xcite and references therein ) . as an alternative to the dark energy model , modification of gravity",
    "may explain the accelerated expansion .",
    "general relativity is not considered to be the complete theory , because its quantum theory can not be formulated in a well defined manner .",
    "the theory of gravity might need to be reformulated within a more general framework .    from the observational point of view",
    ", the constraint on the gravity theory on cosmological scales has not been well investigated , compared with the constraint on the scales of the solar system .",
    "many future projects to produce large galaxy surveys are in progress or planned @xcite , which aim to explore the nature of the dark energy .",
    "these surveys are useful for testing the theory of gravity at cosmological scales ( e.g. , @xcite ) .",
    "the dynamical dark energy models may have similar expansion rates as models of modified gravity , but predict different histories for the growth of structures .",
    "the key to testing the gravity theory is the measurement of the evolution of cosmological perturbations , as many authors have concluded recently @xcite .",
    "the cosmic microwave background anisotropies are useful for investigating the cosmological perturbations through the measurements of the integrated sachs - wolfe effect or the lensing effect on the angular power spectrum @xcite .",
    "imaging surveys of galaxies are also useful through the weak lensing statistics or cluster number counts @xcite .",
    "similarly , redshift surveys of galaxies are helpful for testing gravity @xcite . in the present paper",
    ", we revisit the problem of testing the gravity theory through a measurement of the multipole power spectra in the sloan digital sky survey ( sdss ) luminous red galaxy ( lrg ) sample @xcite .",
    "measuring the multipole power spectra is a way to estimate the redshift - space distortions , which reflects the linear growth rate of the matter density perturbations @xcite .",
    "many authors have investigated the clustering nature of the sdss lrg sample @xcite . in the references @xcite ,",
    "recent results on lrgs from the sdss data release ( dr ) 7 are reported . in the reference @xcite ,",
    "a test of gravity is considered using the observed anisotropic correlation function .",
    "three of the authors of the present paper have shown that the sdss lrg sample is useful to test the gravity theory by measuring the quadrupole power spectrum of galaxy distribution , which represents the redshift - space distortions @xcite . in the present paper",

    "in this paper , we determined a cosmological constraint on the viable @xmath0 model based on the redshift - space distortion by measuring the monopole and quadrupole spectra of the sdss lrg sample of dr7 .",
    "the monopole and the quadrupole spectra are used to fit the bias parameters and to constrain the growth factor and the growth rate of the density perturbations , which depend on the compton scale of the @xmath0 model .",
    "our results show that short compton wavelength model fits the data better , while the long compton wavelength model is excluded , though the constraint depends on the evolution parameter @xmath8 . for the case",
    "@xmath64 , our constraint is similar to that from the cluster number counts reported in @xcite .",
    "when we adopt more accurate model for the redshift - space power spectrum @xcite , the constraint becomes slightly weaker .",
    "however , the long compton wavelength case of the @xmath0 model with the smaller value of @xmath8 is excluded .",
    "our results exemplify that the redshift - space distortion is quite useful in testing gravity theory .",
    "we also demonstrated that a future redshift survey like the wfmos / sumire is potentially useful in obtaining a constraint on the compton wavelength scale .",
    "we acknowledge that the widely used theoretical model of the anisotropic power spectrum adopted in the present paper might need careful improvements .",
    "we adopted the peacock and dodds formula and the smith formula for the nonlinear modelling of the mass power spectrum .",
    "our results do not significantly depend on the choice .",
    "however , there might be a need to adopt a more sophisticated formula for the precise nonlinear modelling within the framework of the modified gravity , as demonstrated by koyama , taruya , hiramatsu @xcite .",
    "the treatment of the finger of god effect in our paper was simple , which assumed the exponential distribution function for the pairwise velocity and introduced one free parameter  the pairwise velocity dispersion .",
    "in reality it might not be an adequate model to describe the nonlinear region of the redshift - space power spectrum  @xcite .",
    "we checked the reliability of our results by adopting the other possible model proposed in ref.@xcite , extensively applying the fitting formula to the @xmath0 model , whose accuracy in this case , however , has not been demonstrated .",
    "we found that there is a non - negligible effect on the constraint on the @xmath0 model .",
    "therefore , a more precise modelling of the redshift - space power spectrum should arguably be needed in the future . concerning the modelling of the clustering bias",
    ", we adopted a simple scale - dependent bias . here too there is potentially a lot of room for improvement .",
    "these issues are out of scope for the present paper , but need to be elaborated for a precise test of gravity with the future redshift surveys .",
