    "<S> the high value of brane tension has a crucial role in recovering einstein s general relativity at low energies . in the framework of a recently developed formalism with variable brane tension </S>",
    "<S> one can pose the question , whether it was always that high ? </S>",
    "<S> in analogy with fluid membranes , in this paper we allow for temperature dependent brane tension , according to the corresponding law established by etvs . for cosmological branes </S>",
    "<S> this assumption leads to several immediate consequences : ( a ) the brane universe was created at a finite temperature @xmath0 and scale factor @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> ( b ) both the brane tension and the 4-dimensional gravitational coupling constant increase with the scale factor from zero to asymptotic values . </S>",
    "<S> ( c ) the 4-dimensional cosmological constant evolves with @xmath2 , starting with a huge negative value , passing through zero , finally reaching a small positive value . </S>",
    "<S> such a scale  factor dependent cosmological constant is able to generate a surplus of attraction at small @xmath2 ( as dark matter does ) and a late - time repulsion at large @xmath2 ( dark energy ) . in the particular toy model discussed here the evolution of the brane tension </S>",
    "<S> is compensated by energy interchange between the brane and the fifth dimension , such that the continuity equation holds for the cosmological fluid . </S>",
    "<S> the resulting cosmology closely mimics the standard model at late times , a decelerated phase being followed by an accelerated expansion . </S>",
    "<S> the energy absorption of the brane drives the 5d space - time towards maximal symmetry , becoming anti de sitter . </S>"
