    "it is now feasible to study the structure of nuclei on the edge of neutron drip line .",
    "such nuclei are expected to have unique properties influenced by the large spatial distribution of weakly bound valence neutrons , for examples , halo , skin , new magic numbers and strong soft dipole excitations .",
    "two - neutron halo nuclei ( sometimes referred to as borromean nuclei when there is no bound state between a valence neutron and a core nucleus ) like @xmath0he and @xmath1li have been often described as three - body systems consisting of two valence neutrons interacting with each other , and with the core @xcite .",
    "the three - body hamiltonian with realistic two - body interactions has been solved by the faddeev method @xcite . on the other hand , bertsch and esbensen",
    "have developed a three - body model with a density - dependent delta interaction among the valence neutrons @xcite .",
    "they have subsequently extended their model by taking into account the effect of the recoil of the core nucleus@xcite .",
    "they showed that the density - dependent contact force well reproduces the results of faddeev calculations even though the radial dependence of the adopted interactions are quite different @xcite . to the date ,",
    "the most sophisticated many - body calculations of light nuclei include also three - body forces which play an important role in obtaining the correct binding energies of light nuclei @xcite . to a large extent , such three - body forces can also be simulated effectively by a density dependent force .",
    "recently , a hartree - fock - bogoliubov ( hfb ) model has been applied to study a di - neutron structure of the drip line nuclei .",
    "the dipole response has also been studied with the quasi - particle random phase approximation ( qrpa ) taking into account the continuum effect @xcite .",
    "cluster models have also been often used to study the borromean nuclei including the dipole excitations @xcite .    in this paper",
    ", we undertake a detailed discussion on the ground state as well as the dipole response of neutron - rich nuclei using a three - body model with the density - dependent delta force , paying special attention to the di - neutron structure of valence neutrons .",
    "we particularly study the borromean nuclei , @xmath0he and @xmath1li , and also another drip line nucleus @xmath2o as a comparison .",
    "since @xmath4o is bound , @xmath2o is not a borromean nucleus by definition , although the root - mean - square ( rms ) radius indicates a feature of very extended neutron wave functions .",
    "the model we use is essentially the same as that of bertsch and esbensen@xcite , while the interaction is adjusted to fit the separation energy of each drip line nucleus .",

    "we have studied the role of di - neutron correlations in weakly bound nuclei on the neutron drip line using a three - body model .",
    "the model hamiltonian consists of a woods - saxon potential between a valence neutron and the core , and of a density - dependent pairing interaction among the valence neutrons .",
    "we applied this model to the borromean nuclei , @xmath26he and @xmath1li , as well as a skin nucleus @xmath2o .",
    "for the borromean nuclei , @xmath26he and @xmath1li , we found that the two - particle density has a two peaked structure , one peak at a small opening angle between the valence neutrons and the other at a large angle ( the di - neutron and cigar - like configurations , respectively ) .",
    "we found that the former is dominated by the @xmath3=0 configurations in the @xmath59 coupling scheme and has a long tail . on the other hand",
    ", the latter has a compact shape , and is dominated by the @xmath3=1 configuration for @xmath1li while by the @xmath3=0 configuration for @xmath0he . for @xmath2o",
    ", there is no clear separation between the di - neutron and the cigar - like configurations , and the ground state is dominated by the @xmath3=0 configuration .",
    "we have also studied the dipole response of these nuclei within the same model .",
    "we found strong threshold peaks in the response of @xmath0he and @xmath1li nuclei , where the transition density shows the importance of the coupling to the continuum .",
    "the @xmath3=0 configuration , thus the di - neutron correlation , was found to have a large contribution to the transition density for these peaks . on the other hand ,",
    "no clear sign of the continuum coupling was seen in the response of @xmath2o .",
    "the transition density for the low energy dipole strength of @xmath2o consists of a coherent sum of the @xmath3=0 and the @xmath3=1 components , and therefore the di - neutron correlation plays a much less important role in @xmath2o than in the borromean nuclei .",
    "recently , a new coulomb breakup measurement for @xmath1li has been undertaken at riken @xcite .",
    "it would be interesting to perform a similar experiment also for the @xmath2o nucleus and see a difference between them as we discussed in this paper .",
