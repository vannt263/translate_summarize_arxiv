    "<S> we consider communication problems in the setting of mobile agents deployed in an edge - weighted network . </S>",
    "<S> the assumption of the paper is that each agent has some energy that it can transfer to any other agent when they meet ( together with the information it holds ) . </S>",
    "<S> the paper deals with three communication problems : data delivery , convergecast and broadcast . </S>",
    "<S> these problems are posed for a centralized scheduler which has full knowledge of the instance . </S>",
    "<S> it is already known that , without energy exchange , all three problems are np - complete even if the network is a line . </S>",
    "<S> surprisingly , if we allow the agents to exchange energy , we show that all three problems are polynomially solvable on trees and have linear time algorithms on the line . on the other hand for general undirected and </S>",
    "<S> directed graphs we show that these problems , even if energy exchange is allowed , are still np - complete . </S>"
