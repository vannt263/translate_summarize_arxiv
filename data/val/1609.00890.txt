    "<S> quaternionic linear canonical transforms ( qlcts ) are a family of integral transforms , which generalized the quaternionic fourier transform and quaternionic fractional fourier transform . in this paper , we extend the energy concentration problem for 2d hypercomplex signals ( especially quaternionic signals ) . </S>",
    "<S> the most energy concentrated signals both in 2d spatial and quaternionic linear canonical frequency domains simultaneously are recently recognized to be the quaternionic prolate spheroidal wave functions ( qpswfs ) . </S>",
    "<S> the improved definitions of qpswfs are studied which gave reasonable properties . </S>",
    "<S> the purpose of this paper is to understand the measurements of energy concentration in the 2d spatial and quaternionic linear canonical frequency domains . </S>",
    "<S> examples of energy concentrated ratios between the truncated gaussian function and qpswfs intuitively illustrate that qpswfs are more energy concentrated signals .    </S>",
    "<S> quaternionic linear canonical transforms , energy concentration , quaternionic fourier transform , quaternionic prolate spheroidal wave functions . </S>"
