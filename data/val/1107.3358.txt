    "<S> . of particular interest is a concentration of thin and long elephant trunk - like structures in the southeastern part of ic 1848e . </S>",
    "<S> some of them have an apparently associated star ( or two stars ) at their very tip . </S>",
    "<S> we conducted @xmath0 photometry of several of these stars . </S>",
    "<S> their positions on the @xmath1 color - magnitude diagram as well as the physical parameters obtained by sed fittings indicate that they are low - mass pre - main - sequence stars having ages of mostly one _ myr _ or less . </S>",
    "<S> this strongly suggests that they formed from elongated , elephant trunk - like clouds . </S>",
    "<S> we presume that such elephant trunk - like structures are genetically different from brcs , on the basis of the differences in morphology , size distributions , and the ages of the associated young stars . </S>",
    "<S> we suspect that those clouds have been caused by hydrodynamical instability of the ionization / shock front of the expanding hii region . </S>",
