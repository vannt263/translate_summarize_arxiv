    "the precise measurements on the atmospheric , solar , accelerator and reactor neutrinos have established the phenomenon of neutrino oscillations @xcite .",
    "this means a fact that three flavors of neutrinos should be massive and mixing @xcite .",
    "we hence need new physics beyond the @xmath1 standard model ( sm ) . on the other hand",
    ", the cosmological observations have indicated that the neutrino masses should be in a sub - ev range @xcite . in various seesaw extensions",
    "@xcite of the sm , the small neutrino masses can be induced in a natural way .",
    "specifically , these seesaw models contain some heavy particles .",
    "the neutrino masses then can be highly suppressed by a small ratio of the electroweak scale over these heavy particle masses .",
    "the seesaw models can also help us to understand the cosmic matter - antimatter asymmetry which is the same as a baryon asymmetry @xcite .",
    "this is the famous leptogenesis mechanism . in the conventional leptogenesis @xcite scenario ,",
    "the interactions for giving the neutrino masses can generate a lepton asymmetry in the sm leptons before the @xmath2 sphaleron @xcite processes stop working .",
    "roughly speaking , the sphalerons will keep in equilibrium above the electroweak scale @xcite .",
    "the produced lepton asymmetry thus can be partially converted to a baryon asymmetry .",
    "therefore , we can simultaneously explain the small neutrino masses and the observed baryon asymmetry . in particular , the cp violation required by the leptogenesis only comes from the imaginary part of the neutrino mass matrix @xcite .    usually the leptogenesis is realized after the inflation @xcite .",
    "alternatively , the lepton asymmetry can be produced by the inflaton decays @xcite .",
    "for example @xcite , the inflaton can directly couple to the quasi - degenerate fermion singlets ( the right - handed neutrinos ) for the type - i seesaw .",
    "if the inflaton is lighter than the fermion singlets , it can decay into the sm lepton and higgs doublets through the mediation of the off - shell fermion singlets .",
    "this non - thermal leptogenesis scenario can allow a low reheating temperature to avoid the gravitino problem in the supersymmetric models @xcite .",
    "the existence of non - baryonic dark matter ( dm ) poses another challenge to particle physics and cosmology .",
    "there have been a number of interesting ideas explaining the dm puzzle .",
