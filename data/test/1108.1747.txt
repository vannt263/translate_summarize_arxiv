    "dense molecular gas plays an essential role in the formation and evolution of galaxies .",
    "it could supply the material for starbursts ( hereafter sb ) and for accretion in super massive black holes ( active galactic nuclei , agn ) in galaxies .",
    "it is also critical in understanding galaxy evolutionary processes such as galaxy interaction , agn feedback , etc . in return , its properties",
