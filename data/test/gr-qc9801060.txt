    "linearized _ perturbation _ analysis have revealed important dynamical features of the gravitational collapse of massless neutral fields . in particular , according to the linearized perturbation theory there are two major features which characterize the _ late _ stages of the evolution : quasinormal ringing and inverse power - law tails ( which follows the quasinormal ringing ) .",
