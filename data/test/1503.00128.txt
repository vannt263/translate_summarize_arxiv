    "<S> we present a variational study of pseudo - spin @xmath0 bose gases in a harmonic trap with weak 3d spin - orbit coupling of @xmath1 type . </S>",
    "<S> this spin - orbit coupling mixes states with different parities , which inspires us to approximate the single particle state with the eigenstates of the total angular momentum , i.e. superposition of harmonic @xmath2-wave and @xmath3-wave states . </S>",
    "<S> as the time reversal symmetry is protected by two - body interaction , we set the variational order parameter as the combination of two mutually time reversal symmetric eigenstates of the total angular momentum . </S>",
    "<S> the variational results essentially reproduce the 3d skyrmion - like ground state recently identified by kawakami _ </S>",
    "<S> et al._. we show that these skyrmion - like ground states emerging in this model are primarily caused by @xmath3 wave spatial mode involving in the variational order parameter that drives two spin components spatially separated . </S>",
    "<S> we find the ground state of this system falls into two phases with different density distribution symmetries depending on the relative magnitude of intraspecies and interspecies interaction : phase i has parity symmetric and axisymmetric density distributions , while phase ii is featured with special joint symmetries of discrete rotational and time reversal symmetry . with the increasing interaction strength </S>",
    "<S> the transition occurs between two phases with distinct density distributions , while the topological 3d skyrmion - like spin texture is symmetry protected . </S>"
