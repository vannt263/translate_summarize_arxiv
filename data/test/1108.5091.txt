    "since the foundation of quantum physics there has been huge interest in the nontrivial transition from the quantum to the classical regime . an important observation in this context is the ehrenfest theorem stating that the dynamics of quantum mechanical expectation values is determined by the classical equations of motion @xcite .",
    "going beyond expectation values , an _",
    "ehrenfest time _",
    "scale has been identified as the time below which the quantum time evolution is well approximated by the corresponding classical dynamics @xcite .",
    "the ehrenfest time is the time it takes for the chaotic classical dynamics to stretch an initially narrow wave packet to some relevant classical length scale such as the system size @xmath5 .",
    "since the stretching is exponential in classically chaotic systems , one has @xmath6 with the lyapunov exponent @xmath7 of the classical dynamics , the initial spread @xmath8 of the wave packet and the fermi momentum @xmath9 .    in recent years",
    ", there has been much interest in determining the influence of the ehrenfest time on stationary transport quantities such as the conductance @xcite , its variance @xcite and its behavior under decoherence @xcite , shot noise @xcite and higher moments of the current @xcite , and on time dependent quantities such as the spectral form factor @xcite , the survival probability @xcite and the fidelity @xcite .",
    "most of these papers used the trajectory - based semiclassical approach to transport @xcite which currently is the method of choice for investigating ehrenfest - time dependences of quantum observables @xcite .",

    "in this article we determined the dependence of the conductance variance of a chaotic cavity with tunnel barriers on the tunneling probability @xmath3 .",
    "in particular we find an ehrenfest - time dependence for the general case @xmath273 of non - perfect coupling .",
    "we focused on the contribution which is linear in the ehrenfest time and at leading order in the total number of open channels @xmath83 .",
    "we predict a nonmonotonous sinusoidal behavior with one maximum at @xmath274 and a ( roughly 50% ) deeper minimum at @xmath275 .",
    "this analytical result was derived semiclassically by systematically considering a rather large number of possible configurations , but the general behavior derives from a much smaller set .",
    "we discussed the main contributions which lead to each of the two peaks to provide a better intuitive understanding of the overall structure .",
    "finally , we compared these analytical predictions with numerical simulations performed for the kicked rotor ( in the chaotic regime ) .",
    "there was good agreement between the two , with the analytical curve within the error bars of all the numerically determined datapoints .",
    "although , even at leading order , we treated a large number of possible semiclassical diagrams , they could be constructed , as we showed in this article , in a controlled way from the diagrams that exist when the tunnel barriers are absent .",
    "this calculation could therefore possibly be extended to higher order contributions in @xmath276 and @xmath0 , but a more natural next step would be to extend the ehrenfest - time dependence of the ( leading order in @xmath83 ) results for the full counting statistics of transport moments @xcite to include tunnel barriers in the leads and to account for the extra diagrammatic possibilities that then arise .    on the numerical side ,",
    "we emphasize that the agreement observed in this article is the first observed for the conductance properties of systems with tunnel barriers . neither the results for the conductance nor for shot noise obtained in @xcite could yet be confirmed by numerical simulations .",
    "further numerical investigation in this area would therefore be highly desirable .",
    "finally it would be interesting to check the observed phenomena experimentally .",
    "although ehrenfest - time effects in antidot superlattices were observed more than ten years ago @xcite , none of the other ehrenfest - time dependencies predicted for chaotic systems could be checked so far experimentally .",
    "this has been mainly due to the small range through which the system size can be varied in quantum dots .",
    "however , with the additional parameter of the tunneling probability and in view of the expected double peak structure , the prediction in this article could be amenable to experimental verification .",
