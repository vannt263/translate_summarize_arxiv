    "in conclusion , we have investigated a variety of observable consequences of the fact that the scattering matrix of majorana fermions is real orthogonal rather than complex unitary .",
    "of particular interest is the identification of observables that can detect the sign of the determinant , since @xmath10 signifies the presence of a majorana bound state .",

    "we find that the peak remains hidden if the coupling is ballistic ( unit transmission ) in even a single scattering channel .",
