    "<S> we investigate the effective properties ( conductivity , diffusivity and elastic moduli ) of model random composite media derived from gaussian random fields and overlapping hollow spheres . </S>",
    "<S> the morphologies generated in the models exhibit low percolation thresholds and give a realistic representation of the complex microstructure observed in many classes of composites . </S>",
    "<S> the statistical correlation functions of the models are derived and used to evaluate rigorous bounds on each property . </S>",
    "<S> simulation of the effective conductivity is used to demonstrate the applicability of the bounds . </S>",
    "<S> the key morphological features which effect composite properties are discussed .    2 </S>"
