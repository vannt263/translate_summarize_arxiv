    "the standard model of particle physics is known to have many problems with observations , such as the matter abundance of our universe , and many candidates of new physics have been invented so far . among them ,",
    "the supersymmetry @xcite is widely noticed , due to the possibility to resolve many phenomenological problems encountered in the standard model .",
    "one of the most important experimental observables to probe the @xmath2 violation in the supersymmetric models is the electric dipole moment ( edm ) .",
    "the edm is sensitive to the violation of parity and time reversal symmetry ( or equivalently the @xmath2 symmetry ) , and is known to have very small standard model prediction @xcite .",
    "this fact gives a direct evidence of the @xmath2 violation beyond the standard model if any finite value is observed experimentally .",
    "due to the stringent experimental limits on the edm measured in many systems @xcite , many @xmath2 phases of the supersymmetric models with @xcite and without @xmath0-parity @xcite were constrained so far .    in this work",
    "we focus on the @xmath2 violation of the @xmath0-parity violating ( rpv ) sector @xcite .",
    "the conservation of the @xmath0-parity is often assumed to keep the lightest supersymmetric particle stable , but this is only an _ ad hoc _ assumption , and phenomenologically , the existence of the rpv interactions is still allowed .",
    "the ( trilinear ) rpv interactions contribute to the fermion edm from the two - loop level @xcite , and the barr - zee type process ( see fig . [ fig : barr - zee ] ) is currently known to give the dominant effect @xcite .",
    "barr - zee type diagrams within @xmath0-parity violation .",
