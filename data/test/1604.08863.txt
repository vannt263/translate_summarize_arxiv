    "<S> in this paper we introduce a numerical method for solving nonlinear volterra integro - differential equations . in the first step , </S>",
    "<S> we apply implicit trapezium rule to discretize the integral in given equation . </S>",
    "<S> further , the daftardar - gejji and jafari technique ( djm ) is used to find the unknown term on the right side . </S>",
    "<S> we derive existence - uniqueness theorem for such equations by using lipschitz condition . </S>",
    "<S> we further present the error , convergence , stability and bifurcation analysis of the proposed method . </S>",
    "<S> we solve various types of equations using this method and compare the error with other numerical methods . </S>",
    "<S> it is observed that our method is more efficient than other numerical methods </S>",
