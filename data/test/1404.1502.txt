    "<S> 3d visualizations via the streamwise vorticity of an instantaneous and a time - averaged pattern are presented , as well as 2d visualizations of the average velocity field and the turbulent kinetic energy . </S>",
    "<S> simulations for @xmath0 show the gradual development from uniform turbulence to a pattern with wavelength 20 half - gaps at @xmath1 , to a pattern with wavelength 40 at @xmath2 and finally to laminar flow at @xmath3 . </S>",
