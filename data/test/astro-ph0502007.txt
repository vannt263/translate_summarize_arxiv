    "2003 ; kochanek 2002 , 2003 ) . in most studies",
    ", there are model degeneracy problems in that a number of models can reproduce the limited number of constraints and predict different hubble constant values based on each time delay measurement .",
    "therefore , it is important to map the mass distribution close to the lens galaxy with other independent methods to better constrain the lens models . in many lenses ,",
    "galaxy groups or clusters close to the lenses provide non - negligible contribution to the lens potential @xcite .",
    "x - ray observations are particularly important in this respect because they can accurately constrain the positions and masses of the galaxy groups or clusters . in particular , the centroid of the x - ray emission from the intracluster gas provides a more accurate measurement of the cluster s position than optical studies .",
    "x - ray clusters or groups have been observed in the lenses rx  j0911.4 + 0551 , q0957 + 561 , b1422 + 231 , and pg  1115 + 080 @xcite .",
