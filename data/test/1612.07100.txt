    "the earth is continuously hit by high energy nuclei of galactic origin , known as cosmic rays .",
    "they are the main source of ionization in the troposphere and stratosphere @xcite .",
    "the contribution of galactic cosmic rays ( gcrs ) to the atmospheric ionization is continuous with slight variation in time due to modulation effects in the heliosphere .",
    "occasionally solar energetic particles ( seps ) enter the earth atmosphere , penetrate deep into in the atmosphere or even reach the surface , in a such way leading to ground level enhancements ( gles ) . as a result",
    "they cause an excess of ionization , specifically over the polar caps @xcite .    at the same time",
