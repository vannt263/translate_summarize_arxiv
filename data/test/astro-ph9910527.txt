    "a scalar field is mathematically equivalent to a fluid with a time - dependent speed of sound ( ratra 1991 ) .",
    "this equivalence may be used to show that a scalar field with potential @xmath15 , @xmath2 , acts like a fluid with negative pressure and that the @xmath0 energy density behaves like a cosmological constant that decreases with time .",
