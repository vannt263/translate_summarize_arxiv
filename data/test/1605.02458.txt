    "quantum coherence ( qch ) plays an important role in today s physics , more specifically in physics of low temperature starting from the laws of thermodynamics @xcite and most recently in the emerging area of quantum information theory .",
    "it has been useful in understanding the underlying process of efficient energy transfer in complex biological systems @xcite . in",
    "the recent past researchers have attempted to devolop a framework to formalize the theory of qch within the realms of quantum information theory and quantum resource theories @xcite . in this framework qch",
    "is quantified in terms of the functions of coherence monotones .",
    "some of these better known measures are based on @xmath0 norm , relative entropy @xcite , skew information @xcite and there are many others @xcite .",
    "several works were done in this context to extend the measures of coherence for multipartite systems@xcite . in another work",
    ", researchers studied the creation of qch using unitary operations with limited thermodynamic resources and eventually they found explicitly the unitary transformation that creates the maximal coherence @xcite .",
    "other works were done where people found the average relative entropy of coherence of pure quantum states sampled randomly from the uniform haar measure and showed that it to be typical @xcite .",
    "it was shown that in the asymptotic limit , the minimum amount of noise that is required to fully decohere a quantum system , is equal to the relative entropy of coherence .",
    "this is quantified by the cost of erasing qch @xcite . in another work",
    "it was demonstrated that measurements in a coherent basis are needed to make use of coherence , and that such measurements must be generated by coherent unitary interactions with an environment @xcite .",
    "recently some authors derive complementarity relations between coherence measures on mutually unbiased bases using various coherence measures @xcite . in a similar kind of work the robustness of coherence was suitably defined and was proven a full monotone in the context of the recently introduced resource theories of qch @xcite .",
    "another group of authors showed that all measures of coherence are frozen for an initial state in a strictly incoherent channel if and only if the relative entropy of coherence is frozen for the state @xcite .",
    "further work show the study the relation between qch and quantum discord , a kind of quantum correlation which appears even in non entangled states @xcite . a new framework for measuring qch in finite dimensional systems",
    "was introduced and a theoretical measure satisfying the reliability criteria was established in the context of quantum resource theories @xcite . + the impossibility to clone quantum states is regarded as one of the most fundamental restriction that comes from nature @xcite .",
    "the  no cloning theorem \" states that there exists no quantum mechanical process , which can take two distinct non - orthogonal quantum states @xmath1 , @xmath2 into states @xmath3 , @xmath4 respectively . even though we can not copy an unknown quantum state perfectly but quantum mechanics never rules out the possibility of cloning it approximately @xcite .",
    "it also allows probabilistic cloning as one can always clone an arbitrary quantum state perfectly with some non - zero probability of succsess @xcite . in the year 1996 , buzek et al .",
    "went beyond the idea of perfect cloning and introduced the concept of approximate cloning .",
    "in this process , they have also introduced the state independent quantum copying machine by keeping the fidelity of cloning independent of the input state parameters .",
    "this machine is also popularly known as universal quantum cloning machine ( uqcm ) @xcite . later in another work by gisin and massar",
    "this cloner was shown to be optimal @xcite .",
    "other than this state independent quantum cloning machine ( qcm ) , there are also state dependent qcms @xcite .",
    "state dependent quantum cloning machine can be both dynamic and static depending on the choice of machine parameters @xcite . in this context",
    "it was shown that how the fidelity of quantum cloning machine is related with the amount of correlation generated in the process of cloning @xcite .",
    "+ quantum entanglement @xcite in general quantum correlation is considered as a useful resource as it plays a significant role in computational and communication processes like quantum key generation @xcite , secret sharing @xcite , teleportation @xcite , superdense coding @xcite , entanglement swapping @xcite , remote entanglement distribution @xcite and in many more tasks @xcite .",
    "the more  pure \" is entanglement , more  valuable \" is the given two - particle state at least from the point of view of information processing tasks .",
    "therefore it becomes important to extract pure entanglement from mixed entangled state .",
    "researchers had done a lot of work in past years on these extraction processes @xcite .",
    "this comes at the cost of reduction of number of entangled states .",
    "it is interesting to see that in quantum information processing scenario , there exists tasks which are completely opposite to this from the operational point of view .",
    "this comes under the umbrella of  broadcasting of inseparability \" @xcite .",
    "when there is an exigency in increasing the number of available entangled pairs rather than the purity of it , it becomes important to broadcast the amount of entanglement from a given pair to different pairs .",
    "one possible way to achieve this is by applying local and non local cloing . in general",
    "cloning in this context includes both state dependent and state independent cloning @xcite . in a recent work we have shown that it is impossible to optimally broadcast quantum correlation across different laboratories @xcite .",
    "but if we consider non optimal broadcasting it is indeed possible to broadcast quantum correlation between separated parties @xcite .",
    "other than application of cloning there are different ways to broadcast quantum correlation .",
    "quite recently , many authors showed by using sophisticated methods that correlations in a single bipartite state can be locally or uni locally broadcast if and only if the states are classical ( i.e. having classical correlation ) or classical - quantum respectively @xcite .",
    "+ in this work our basic objective is to broadcast qch present in a pair of qubits to other pairs . keeping in mind the usefulness of qch in resource theories it is also important to broadcast it to more pair of qubits .",

    "<S> quantum coherence ( qch ) is considered to be a key ingredient in quantum resource theories and also plays a pivotal role in the design and implementation of various information processing tasks . </S>",
    "<S> consequently , it becomes important for us to create more number coherent quantum states between two labs from a given coherent state shared initially . </S>",
    "<S> we call this process as * broadcasting of quantum coherence ( qch)*. in this work we extensively study the problem of broadcasting of qch . in order to have a holistic view of broadcasting , we start with a most general representation two qubit mixed state in terms of bloch vectors . as a cloning transformation </S>",
    "<S> we have used universal symmetric optimal buzek - hillery ( bh ) cloner locally as well as non locally . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , we find that in either of the case it is impossible to do optimal broadcasting of qch . </S>",
    "<S> further we show in case of non optimal broadcasting it is impossible to increase qch of the state by both local and non local cloning operations . in non optimal case , </S>",
    "<S> apart from general result we consider examples like , a ) werner like states ( wls ) and b ) bell diagonal states ( bds ) and obtain ranges for broadcasting in terms of input state parameters . </S>"
