    "the surplus of matter over anti - matter within the universe can only be explained with a thorough understanding of cp violation .",
    "the cp phase in the quark mixing matrix of the standard model , which has been confirmed by b - meson experiments  @xcite , is not sufficient to understand the baryon asymmetry of the universe  @xcite .",
    "however , the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm )  @xcite provides new physical phases that are manifestly cp - sensitive . after absorbing non - physical phases , we chose the complex parameters to be the higgsino mass parameter @xmath2 , the u(1 ) , and su(3 ) gaugino mass parameters @xmath1 , and @xmath4 , and the trilinear scalar coupling parameters @xmath5 of the third generation sfermions @xmath6 .",
    "the corresponding phases violate cp and are generally constrained by experimental bounds on electric dipole moments ( edms )  @xcite . however , these restrictions are strongly model dependent  @xcite , such that additional measurements outside the low energy edm sector are required .",
    "+ many cp observables have been proposed and studied in order to measure cp violation .",
    "total cross sections  @xcite , masses  @xcite , and branching ratios  @xcite , are cp - even quantities .",
    "for a direct evidence of cp violation , however , cp - odd ( t - odd ) observables are required .",
    "examples are rate asymmetries of either branching ratios  @xcite , cross sections  @xcite , or angular distributions  @xcite .",
    "since these rate asymmetries require the presence of absorptive phases , they are typically small , of the order of @xmath7 , if they are not resonantly enhanced  @xcite .",
    "larger cp - odd observables which already appear at tree - level are desirable .",
    "these are t - odd triple products of momenta and/or spins , from which cp - odd asymmetries can be constructed .",
    "such triple product asymmetries are highly cp - sensitive , and have been intensively studied both at lepton and hadron colliders  @xcite .",
    "+ third generation sfermions have a rich phenomenology at high energy colliders like the lhc  @xcite or ilc  @xcite due to a sizable mixing of left and right states .",
