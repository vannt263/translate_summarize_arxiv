    "<S> in particular , we show that cyclically symmetric tilings of a generalization of the aztec diamond are always enumerated by a simple product formula . </S>",
    "<S> we also prove an explicit product formula for the number of cyclically symmetric tilings of a quasi - hexagon .    </S>",
