    "the tomographic - probability representation of quantum states , where the quantum state is associated with the so - called symplectic tomogram , was introduced .",
    "the tomogram is a fair probability distribution containing the same information on quantum state that the density operator does ( or such its characteristics as the wigner or husimi  kano functions ) .",

    "we discuss also the formulation of the separability and entanglement properties of composed system in the tomographic probability and diagonal representations .",
