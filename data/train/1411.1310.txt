    "<S> we experimentally realize `` hybrid '' entanglement swapping between discrete - variable ( dv ) and continuous - variable ( cv ) optical systems . </S>",
    "<S> dv two - mode entanglement as obtainable from a single photon split at a beam splitter is robustly transferred by means of efficient cv entanglement and operations , using sources of squeezed light and homodyne detections . </S>",
    "<S> the dv entanglement after the swapping is verified without post - selection by the logarithmic negativity of up to 0.28@xmath00.01 . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , our analysis shows that the optimally transferred state can be post - selected into a highly entangled state that violates a clauser - horne - shimony - holt inequality by more than 4 standard deviations , and thus it may serve as a resource for quantum teleportation and quantum cryptography .    </S>",
    "<S> quantum entanglement can be created between two distant quantum systems that have never directly interacted . </S>",
    "<S> this effect , called entanglement swapping  @xcite , is a building block for quantum communication and computation  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> it was originally proposed and demonstrated for discrete - variable ( dv ) optical systems  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the protocol starts with two entangled pairs , @xmath1-@xmath2 and @xmath3-@xmath4 , each represented either by twin photons or by a single photon split into two distinct modes [ fig .  </S>",
    "<S> [ newfig1](a ) ] . </S>",
    "<S> a joint projective measurement of @xmath2 and @xmath3 onto one of the four two - qubit bell states then leads to an entangled state for @xmath1 and @xmath4 , even though @xmath1 and @xmath4 never directly interact with each other . </S>",
    "<S> entanglement swapping can also be interpreted as the transfer of one half of an entangled state , either from @xmath2 to @xmath4 or from @xmath3 to @xmath1 , by quantum teleportation  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> it is a key element for quantum networking  @xcite , quantum computing  @xcite , quantum cryptography  @xcite , and especially long - distance quantum communication by means of quantum repeaters  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , in this dv setting solely based upon single photons , due to the heralded conditional entangled - state generation and the probabilistic linear - optics bell - state measurement ( bsm ) , successful entanglement swapping events occur very rarely . as a result , </S>",
    "<S> in a quantum repeater for example , long - distance entangled - pair creation rates would be correspondingly low and requirements on the coherence times of the local quantum memories at each repeater station impractically high . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , the observation and verification of the final entanglement between @xmath1 and @xmath4 in the dv scheme typically requires post - selection .    </S>",
    "<S> entanglement swapping was later extended to continuous - variable ( cv ) systems @xcite , where the pairs @xmath1-@xmath2 and @xmath3-@xmath4 each correspond to the two modes of a two - mode squeezed , quadrature - entangled state  @xcite [ fig .  [ newfig1](b ) ] . since </S>",
    "<S> such entangled states are available on demand and a linear - optics bsm in the quadrature basis can be performed without failure , entanglement can be swapped deterministically and verified without post - selection  @xcite . however , due to the finite squeezing of both initial entanglement sources , the final entanglement after swapping in the cv scheme is inevitably degraded by excess noise . </S>",
    "<S> in fact , the entanglement drops exponentially  @xcite and in practice , cv entanglement swapping can always be replaced by a direct transmission through a lossy channel  @xcite </S>",
    "<S> . moreover , purification techniques for this type of degraded entanglement are not so advanced at present  @xcite .        </S>",
    "<S> our scheme combines the best features of the above two approaches , making use of both dv and cv entanglement at the same time [ fig .  </S>",

    "<S> @xcite , using squeezed - state entanglement , homodyne - based bsm , and feedforward by phase - space displacement , dv entanglement is transferred from @xmath1-@xmath2 to @xmath1-@xmath4 . </S>",
    "<S> once the initial single - photon entanglement is conditionally prepared in modes @xmath1 and @xmath2 , all the remaining steps of our scheme are unconditional , achieving a highly efficient transfer of the dv entanglement . because optical cv quantum teleportation runs in a deterministic fashion ( as opposed to optical dv quantum teleportation ) and </S>",
    "<S> dv entanglement is robust against loss ( as opposed to cv entanglement ) , entanglement is efficiently and reliably transferred only in this `` hybrid '' setting . </S>",
