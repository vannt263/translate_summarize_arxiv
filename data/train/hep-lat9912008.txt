    "<S> in order to consider non - perturbative effects of superstrings , we try to apply dynamical triangulations to the type iib superstrings . </S>",
    "<S> the discretized action is constructed from the type iib matrix model proposed as a constructive definition of superstring theory . </S>",
    "<S> the action has the local n=2 supersymmetry explicitly , and has no extra fermionic degrees of freedom . </S>",
    "<S> we evaluate the partition function for some simple configurations and discuss constraints required from the finiteness of partition functions . </S>"
