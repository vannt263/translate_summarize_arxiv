    "<S> we discuss applications of time - delayed feedback control to delay - coupled neural systems and lasers , in the framework of the fitzhugh - nagumo neuron model and the lang - kobayashi laser model , respectively . in the context of neural systems </S>",
    "<S> , we will point out some complex scenarios of synchronized in - phase or antiphase oscillations , bursting patterns , or amplitude death , induced by delayed coupling in combination with delayed self - feedback in simple network motifs . for optical systems , we will show that multiple time - delayed feedback , realized by a fabry - perot resonator coupled to the laser , provides a valuable tool for the suppression of unwanted intensity pulsations , and leads to stable continuous - wave operation .    </S>",
    "<S> time delay , feedback control , neural dynamics , optical control    over the past decade control of unstable and chaotic states has evolved into a central issue in applied nonlinear science @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> various methods of control have been developed since the ground - breaking work of @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> one scheme where the control force is constructed from time - delayed signals @xcite has turned out to be very robust and universal to apply , and easy to implement experimentally . in this time - delayed feedback control </S>",
    "<S> the control signal is built from the difference @xmath0 between the present and an earlier value of an appropriate system variable @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> it is noninvasive since the control forces vanish if the target state ( a periodic state of period @xmath2 or a steady state ) is reached . </S>",
    "<S> thus the unstable states themselves of the uncontrolled system are not changed , but only their neighbourhood is adjusted such that neighbouring trajectories converge to it , i.e. , the control forces act only if the system deviates from the state to be stabilized .    </S>",
    "<S> this paper is organized as follows : in sec .  </S>",

    "<S> the stabilization of steady states in optical systems is studied in sec .  [ </S>",
    "<S> sec : laser ] in the framework of a modified lang - kobayashi model of a semiconductor laser where the time - delayed feedback is realized by a fabry - perot ( fp ) resonator . </S>"
