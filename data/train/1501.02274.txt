    "the survival probability , which is the probability not to have reached a target up to a given time , is a key observable in the study of brownian motion .",
    "it is the cumulative of the first - passage time distribution , _",
    "i.e. _ the distribution of the time to reach a target .",
    "this quantity @xcite , and as a first step the mean first - passage time @xcite , is a standard way to quantify the efficiency of a search process .",
    "it is for example also involved in the calculation of the covered territory @xcite and the mean perimeter of the convex hull of a brownian motion @xcite .",
    "determining the survival probability and the first - passage time distribution is of particular importance in the wedge geometry .",
    "recent related studies include last - passage times determination @xcite , extensions to anomalous diffusion @xcite ( and in particular to fractional brownian motion @xcite ) and applications to virus trafficking in cells @xcite .",
    "interest in this geometry resides in part in the possibility to map one - dimensional diffusion - controlled reaction processes on a wedge domain @xcite .",
    "one important example of this mapping is the fisher - gelfand three - particle problem @xcite .",

    "the survival probability for regular diffusion in a wedge domain is written as an infinite sum of special functions . in the past",
