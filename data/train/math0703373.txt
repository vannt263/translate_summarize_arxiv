    "<S> we study the location and the size of the roots of steiner polynomials of convex bodies in the minkowski relative geometry . </S>",
    "<S> based on a problem of teissier on the intersection numbers of cartier divisors of compact algebraic varieties it was conjectured that these roots have certain geometric properties related to the in- and circumradius of the convex body . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the roots of 1-tangential bodies fulfill the conjecture , but we also present convex bodies violating each of the conjectured properties . </S>"
