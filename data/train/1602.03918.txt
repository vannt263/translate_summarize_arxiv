    "abhyankar proved in @xcite that quasi - ordinary hypersurface singularities are parameterizable by puiseux series .",
    "+ for algebraic hypersurfaces j. mcdonald showed in @xcite the existence of puiseux series solutions with support in strongly convex cones .",

    "extends this result to arbitrary codimension .    in this paper",

    "the series appearing in those parametric equations have support contained in cones which can be described in terms of the connected components of the complement of the amoeba .",
    "these cones are not necessarily strongly convex .",
