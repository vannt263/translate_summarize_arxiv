    "<S> a class of nonlocal nonlinear wave equation arises from the modeling of a one dimensional motion in a nonlinearly , nonlocally elastic medium . </S>",
    "<S> the equation involves a kernel function with nonnegative fourier transform . </S>",
    "<S> we discretize the equation by using fourier spectral method in space and we prove the convergence of the semidiscrete scheme . </S>",
    "<S> we then use a fully - discrete scheme , that couples fourier pseudo - spectral method in space and 4th order runge - kutta in time , to observe the effect of the kernel function on solutions . to generate solitary wave solutions numerically </S>",
    "<S> , we use the petviashvili s iteration method .    </S>",
