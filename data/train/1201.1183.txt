    "thereby , the absorption of infrared divergent wilson coefficients into the condensates at non - zero densities or temperatures requires additional renormalization relations . in @xcite , in - medium relations which are consistent to the vacuum case have been given .",

    "a common approximation is the vacuum saturation hypothesis , which leads to a factorization of four - quark condensates into squares of the chiral condensate .",

    "however , as only certain combinations of four - quark condensates may serve as order parameters of chiral symmetry the factorization of all four - quark condensates in terms of the chiral condensate is not necessarily consistent .",

    "chiral partner sum rules provide assistance as only chirally odd condensates do not cancel out and the number of terms which have to be calculated is significantly reduced .",
