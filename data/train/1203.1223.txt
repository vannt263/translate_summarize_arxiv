    "<S> we show that two superconducting qubits interacting _ via _ a fixed transversal coupling can be decoupled by appropriately - designed microwave field excitations applied to each qubit . </S>",
    "<S> this technique is useful for removing the effects of spurious interactions in a quantum processor . </S>",
    "<S> we also simulate the case of a qubit coupled to a two - level system ( tls ) present in the insulating layer of the josephson junction of the qubit . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we discuss the qubit - tls problem in the context of dispersive measurements , where the qubit is coupled to a resonator . </S>"
