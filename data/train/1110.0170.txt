    "<S> a theory of entangled photons emission from quantum dot in microcavity under continuous and pulsed incoherent pumping is presented . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the time - resolved two - photon correlations drastically depend on the pumping mechanism : the continuous pumping quenches the polarization entanglement and strongly suppresses photon correlation times . </S>",
    "<S> analytical theory of the effect is presented .    </S>",
    "<S> semiconductor quantum dots are a promising source of single photons and entangled photon pairs . </S>",
    "<S> polarization - entangled photons generated during the radiative recombination of the quantum dot biexciton are now in a focus of intensive experimental research  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> the state - of - the art approach to increase the rate of photon pair generation is to place the dot in the specially designed cavity , where the frequencies of the two different photon modes are independently tuned to the biexciton and exciton resonances  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> both exciton and biexciton radiative recombinations are then increased , allowing to observe bright two - photon emission  @xcite . here </S>",
    "<S> we study theoretically the quantum emission properties of such microcavity under incoherent pumping . </S>",
    "<S> we analyze the polarization density matrix of the photon pair , determined by the second - order correlation function of the photons @xmath0  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the incoherent pumping itself is an intrinsic feature of the light source . </S>",
    "<S> however , to the best of our knowledge , the pumping effect on the biexciton emission has not been theoretically analyzed yet , despite the extensive amount of the studies done  @xcite . </S>",
