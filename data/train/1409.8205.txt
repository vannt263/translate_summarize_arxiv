    "<S> the wigner @xmath0 symbols of the quantum angular momentum theory are related to the vector coupling or clebsch - gordan coefficients and to the hahn and dual hahn polynomials of the discrete orthogonal hyperspherical family , of use in discretization approximations . </S>",
    "<S> we point out the important role of the regge symmetries for defining the screen where images of the coefficients are projected , and for discussing their asymptotic properties and semiclassical behavior . </S>",
    "<S> recursion relationships are formulated as eigenvalue equations , and exploited both for computational purposes and for physical interpretations . </S>"
