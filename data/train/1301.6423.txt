    "<S> gaussian , and that assumptions on averages of higher - order fluctuations in tdva are not justified . </S>",
    "<S> these results cast some doubt on an application of tdva to dw systems . </S>",
    "<S> gaussian wavepacket dynamics in anharmonic potential systems is studied also .    </S>",
