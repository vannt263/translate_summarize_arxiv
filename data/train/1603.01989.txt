    "we showed several properties of the polynomials , including important links with the chebyshev polynomials , proving their orthogonality with respect to a suitable weight .",
    "a further progress in our work will consist in studying the distribution of the zeros of @xmath5 .",
    "this topic is the subject of a paper in preparation . by section [ sec:3 ] , the zeros , expressed in terms of nested radicals ,",

    "this formula allows us to obtain several other notable mathematical constants as limits of suitably weighted sequences of zeros of the lucas - lehmer polynomials @xmath5 and @xmath218 .",
