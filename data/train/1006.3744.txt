    "the small size together with the tapered shape from the output gate to the output node could complicate charge transfer between the two and also means that the edge effects and 3d fringing fields can become important .",
    "it turned out that the fringing effect is important because the voltage on the output gate ( og ) is pulled up by the constant 5 v voltage on the neighbouring output gate .",
    "it was found that the og bias should set to below 0 v otherwise the charge will leak from the summing gate directly into the output node .",
    "the og bias value for a typical ccd could be above 1 v. figure [ fig : fringe - effect ] shows an example of such behaviour @xcite .",
    "the sensor was illuminated with led light and then the photogate was lowered while the summing gate was kept high .",
    "the charge collected should be temporarily held within summing gate if the og functions properly .",

    "it is clear that for a low og voltage the charge can be transferred as expected while at high og the transfer is severely hampered by fringe field and happens prematurely .",
