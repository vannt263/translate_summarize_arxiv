    "<S> we present a systematic study of the green functions of a one - dimensional gas of impenetrable anyons . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the one - particle density matrix is the determinant of a toeplitz matrix whose large @xmath0 asymptotic is given by the fisher - hartwig conjecture . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a careful numerical analysis of this determinant for general values of the anyonic parameter , showing in full details the crossover between bosons and fermions and the reorganization of the singularities of the momentum distribution function .    </S>",
    "<S> we show that the one - particle density matrix satisfies a painlev @xmath1 differential equation , that is then used to derive the small distance and large momentum expansions . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the first non - vanishing term in this expansion is always @xmath2 , that is proved to be true for all couplings in the lieb - liniger anyonic gas and that can be traced back to the presence of a delta function interaction in the hamiltonian . </S>"
