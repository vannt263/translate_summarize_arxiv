    "in the quest for size - reduced and possible low - power consuming electronic devices , the proposal @xcite of using molecular junctions for electronics has sparked a large interest in understanding the influence of molecular vibrational modes onto the electron charge transfer through a molecule .",
    "the non - linear current through a molecule device can be controlled by a capacitively coupled external gate @xcite .",
    "interestingly , hysteretic behavior of the @xmath0 curves @xcite has been reported in several experiments when sweeping the voltage with a finite rate .",
    "however , the observed hysteresis are non - universal and depend on the sweeping rate .",
    "for infinitesimally slow sweeping the effect vanishes . in some cases",
    "a sudden drop of the current has been observed with increasing bias voltage @xcite which translates into a negative differential conductance .",
    "this all has been accounted to configural changes of the molecule emphasizing the importance of vibrational couplings in such devices .",
    "many experimental facts have been gathered in the last two decades but there is still a lack of an accurate theoretical description of all the reported phenomena .",
    "an excellent review @xcite by galperin et al .  comprehensively summarizes the different theoretical approaches and experimental findings .",
    "single molecular transistors ( smt ) promise to offer some advantages over their semi - conductor based counterparts @xcite .",
    "both types of single - electron transistors can be controlled by a capacitively coupled external gate @xcite .",
    "the molecular energy scales , however , are larger in smts and reproducibly defined by the chemistry of the molecule . in addition , the coupling to vibrational modes enlarges the parameter space and different physics such a phonon - assisted tunneling , frank - condon blockade or the appearance of inelastic steps in the @xmath0 curve can be observed .",
    "the theoretical description of such molecular junctions only include those molecular levels and vibrationals modes relevant for the quantum transport .",
    "the simplest model proposed @xcite comprise a single level coupled to a local holstein phonon .",
