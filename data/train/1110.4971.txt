    "<S> qcd lattice simulations determine hadron masses as functions of the quark masses . from the gradients of these masses and using the feynman  </S>",
    "<S> hellmann theorem the hadron sigma terms can then be determined . </S>",
    "<S> we use here a novel approach of keeping the singlet quark mass constant in our simulations which upon using an @xmath0 flavour symmetry breaking expansion gives highly constrained ( i.e.  few parameter ) fits for hadron masses in a multiplet . </S>",
    "<S> this is a highly advantageous procedure for determining the hadron mass gradient as it avoids the use of delicate chiral perturbation theory . </S>",
    "<S> we illustrate the procedure here by estimating the light and strange sigma terms for the baryon octet . </S>"
