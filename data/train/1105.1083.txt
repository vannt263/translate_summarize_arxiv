    "much attention has been directed toward multiferroic and magnetoelectric materials in recent years.@xcite the coupling between magnetic and electric parameters increases the degrees of freedom of the ordered ground state , making these materials good candidates for the study of new phenomena in highly correlated electronic systems .",
    "strong magnetoelectric coupling is rather rare in the solid state , since usual microscopic mechanisms for magnetic and electric polarization are mutually exclusive .",
    "magnetism requires strong exchange interactions related to a strong hybridization of the transition ion electrons leading to conductivity .",
