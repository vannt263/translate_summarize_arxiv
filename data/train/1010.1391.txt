    "<S> recently , this journal has published a paper which dealt with basis expansion model ( bem ) based least - squares ( ls ) channel estimation in doubly - selective orthogonal frequency - division multiplexing ( ds - ofdm ) systems . </S>",
    "<S> the least - squares channel estimator computes the pseudo - inverse of a channel estimation matrix . </S>",
    "<S> for the existence of the pseudo - inverse , it is necessary that the channel estimation matrix be of full column rank . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we investigate the conditions that need to be satisfied that ensures the full column - rank condition of the channel estimation matrix . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we derive conditions that the bem and pilot pattern designs should satisfy to ensure that the channel estimation matrix is of full column rank . </S>",
    "<S> we explore the polynomial bem ( p - bem ) , complex exponential bem ( ce - bem ) , slepian bem ( s - bem ) and generalized complex exponential bem ( gce - bem ) . </S>",
    "<S> we present one possible way to design the pilot patterns which satisfy the full column - rank conditions . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , the proposed method is extended to the case of multiple - input multiple - output ( mimo ) ds - ofdm systems as well . </S>",
    "<S> examples of pilot pattern designs are presented , that ensure the channel estimation matrix is of full column rank for a large ds - mimo - ofdm system with as many as six transmitters , six receivers and 1024 subcarriers . * _ index terms _ * - bem , full column rank , ici , mimo , ofdm and rank - nullity theorem . </S>"
