    "<S> integer programs defined by two equations with two free integer variables and nonnegative continuous variables have three types of nontrivial facets : split , triangle or quadrilateral inequalities . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we compare the strength of these three families of inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> in particular we study how well each family approximates the integer hull . </S>",
    "<S> we show that , in a well defined sense , triangle inequalities provide a good approximation of the integer hull . </S>",
    "<S> the same statement holds for quadrilateral inequalities . on the other hand </S>",
    "<S> , the approximation produced by split inequalities may be arbitrarily bad . </S>"
