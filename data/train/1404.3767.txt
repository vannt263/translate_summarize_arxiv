    "normalized b - bases provide shape preserving properties like closure for the affine transformations of the control polygon , convex hull , variation diminishing ( which also implies convexity preserving of plane control polygons ) , endpoint interpolation , monotonicity preserving , hodograph and length diminishing , and a recursive corner cutting algorithm ( also called b - algorithm ) that is the analogue of the de casteljau algorithm of bzier curves . among all normalized totally positive bases of a given vector space of functions",
