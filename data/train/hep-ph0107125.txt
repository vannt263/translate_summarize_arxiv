    "<S> the chiral phase transition of strong - coupling abelian gauge theories is investigated in the brane world . </S>",
    "<S> it is assumed that gauge boson propagates in an extra dimension , i.e. bulk gauge theories . </S>",
    "<S> the phase structure is analytically evaluated by using the low - energy effective theories . </S>",
    "<S> we also numerically solve the ladder schwinger - dyson equation for the full fermion propagator including kaluza - klein ( kk ) excitation modes of the gauge field . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that the chiral phase transition is of the second order , and the critical value of the coupling constant is obtained . </S>",
    "<S> the extra dimension has a large influence on the chiral phase transition for the randall - sundrum ( rs ) brane world . </S>",
    "<S> it is studied how the number of kk modes affect the chiral phase transition . </S>"
