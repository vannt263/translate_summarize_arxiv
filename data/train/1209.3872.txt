    "<S> the @xmath0 and @xmath1 mesons as well as the @xmath2 resonance are included as explicit dynamical degrees of freedom . to obtain a self - consistent theory with respect to constraints we consider the proper relations among the couplings of the effective lagrangian . for the purpose of generating a systematic power counting , the extended on - mass - shell renormalization scheme </S>",
    "<S> is applied in combination with the small - scale expansion . </S>",
    "<S> the results for the electric and magnetic sachs form factors are analyzed in terms of experimental data and compared to previous findings in the framework of chiral perturbation theory . </S>",
    "<S> the pion - mass dependence of the form factors is briefly discussed . </S>"
