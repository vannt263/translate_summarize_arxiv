    "<S> recently , the concept of cumulative residual entropy ( cre ) has been studied by many researchers in higher dimensions . in this article , we extend the definition of ( dynamic ) cumulative past entropy ( dcpe ) , a dual measure of ( dynamic ) cre , to bivariate setup and obtain some of its properties including bounds . </S>",
    "<S> we also look into the problem of extending dcpe for conditionally specified models . </S>",
    "<S> several properties , including monotonicity , and bounds of dcpe are obtained for conditional distributions . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the proposed measure uniquely determines the distribution function . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we also propose a stochastic order based on this measure .    * </S>",
