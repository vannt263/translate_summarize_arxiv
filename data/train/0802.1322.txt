    "<S> the influence of longitudinal structuring on the fast kink modes of coronal loops is investigated . </S>",
    "<S> analytical dispersion relations and mode profiles are derived for the second - order ordinary differential equation governing the z- component of the perturbation in the magnetic field , @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> all other components are given in terms of @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> deviations from the frequencies and mode profiles of homogenous loops are given as functions of the density scale height . </S>",
    "<S> the effects of the fixed and variable column masses , negative scale heights , and density contrasts inside and outside of the loops are studied . </S>",
    "<S> the frequency ratios , mode profiles , and the antinode shifts from those of the sine profiles of the homogenous loops , are tools to estimate solar photospheric parameters . to this end </S>",
    "<S> , we have expanded the relevant factors up to the second order in the stratification parameter . in particular </S>",
    "<S> , we verify that the first overtone antinode shifts are in the mm range and are within the reach of the resolutions of the present day observations . </S>"
