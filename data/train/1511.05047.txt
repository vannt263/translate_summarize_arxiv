    "<S> the size and the shape of the shadow depend on the mass , the charge , and the angular momentum of the compact object . for a given mass , </S>",
    "<S> the size increases with the rotation parameter and decreases with the electric charge . </S>",
    "<S> the distortion with respect to the non rotating case grows with the charge and the rotation parameter . for fixed values of these parameters , </S>",
    "<S> the shadow is slightly larger and less deformed than in the kerr - newman case </S>",
