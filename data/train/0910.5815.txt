    "<S> systematic studies on the production of strange hyperons and the @xmath0  meson as a function of beam energy and system size performed by the na49 collaboration are discussed . </S>",
    "<S> hadronic transport models fail to describe the production of multi strange particles ( @xmath1 , @xmath2 ) , while statistical models are generally in good agreement to the measured particle yields at all energies . </S>",
    "<S> the system size dependence is well reproduced by the core - corona approach . </S>",
    "<S> new data on @xmath3  production are presented . </S>",
    "<S> the yields of these short - lived resonances are significantly below the statistical model expectation . </S>",
    "<S> this is in line with the interpretation that the measurable yields are reduced due to rescattering of their decay products inside the fireball . </S>"
