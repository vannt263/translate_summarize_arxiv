    "supersymmetry , in particular the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm)@xcite offers a natural candidate for the dark matter@xcite of the universe in the form of the lightest supersymmetric particle ( lsp ) .",
    "astrophysical constraints require it to be a colorless and neutral particle , while direct detection experiments disfavor a sneutrino dark matter@xcite .",
    "thus the favored dark matter ( dm ) candidate in the mssm is the lightest neutralino @xmath7 which could be any combination of the neutral gauginos , like the bino ( @xmath8 ) , wino ( @xmath9 ) and higgsinos @xmath10 , @xmath11 , i.e. @xmath12 here @xmath13 for @xmath14 refers to elements of the matrix that diagonalizes the neutralino mass matrix@xcite .    in the simplest version of this model ,",
    "called the constrained mssm ( cmssm ) or the minimal supergravity ( msugra ) model@xcite , the lightest neutralino as a dark matter candidate@xcite is dominantly a bino over most of the parameter space .",
    "since a bino does not carry any gauge charge , its main annihilation mechanism is the so - called bulk annihilation process via sfermion exchange .",
    "but the higgs boson mass bound of 114 gev from lep@xcite implied large sfermion masses in this model@xcite , which was reinforced now with discovery of the higgs boson at the lhc with a mass of about 125 gev@xcite .",
    "this implies a very inefficient bulk annihilation process , resulting in an overabundance of the dark matter relic density over most of the parameter space .",
    "we shall see below that there are only a few strips of parameter space available in the cmssm giving a cosmologically compatible dark matter relic density i.e. , the stau coannihilation , the resonant annihilation , the focus point and the higgsino dark matter regions@xcite - each of which requires some amount of fine - tuning between susy parameters . moreover , large parts of the stau coannihilation and the resonant annihilation regions are disfavored by the higgs boson mass of about 125 gev , while most of the hyperbolic branch@xcite / focus point@xcite region is disfavored by the recent direct dark matter detection experiments@xcite .",
    "while the higgsino dark matter region is unaffected by these results , it corresponds to squark and gluino masses @xmath15 8 - 10  tev in this model , which can not be probed at the lhc@xcite .",
    "therefore this region has little practical interest at least for lhc experiments .    in this work",
