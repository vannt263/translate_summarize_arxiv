    "graphene , the thinnest material to be successfully isolated , manifests prominent many - body effects of electron - electron ( e - e ) and electron - hole ( e - h ) interactions which can be manipulated by using substrate materials @xcite .",
    "the role of interacting quasi - particles in graphene , particularly in the form of considerable e - e and e - h interactions has been revealed by recent reports such as renormalization of the fermi velocity with distortion of the dirac cone @xcite , fractional quantum hall effect @xcite and prominent excitonic effects and interactions , which occur even at high energy in optical absorbance spectra @xcite . in the context of many - body effects ,",
    "optical conductivity measurements of graphene allow one to reveal the roles of both e - e and particularly e - h interactions as optical transitions involve the creation of concomitant e - h states",
    ".    for such studies graphene has been typically measured either on wide band gap substrates @xcite or in the free - standing configuration @xcite .",
    "however graphene on a low ( < @xmath31 ev ) or intermediate band gap substrate ( up to @xmath35 ev ) could be an intriguing system to study as the interfacial optical processes both in graphene and the substrate ( which is absent for a wide band gap substrate ) may affect the intrinsic characteristics of each other .",
    "an ideal example of an intermediate band gap substrate is srtio@xmath0 ( with band gap of @xmath33.2 ev ) . in particular",
    ", srtio@xmath0 is regarded as a model 3@xmath4 system and has been widely used as a substrate generating an interface exhibiting new fundamental phenomena @xcite in recent times .",
    "an optical conductivity study of graphene - srtio@xmath0 interface allows us to reveal how orbitals in graphene hybridize and interact with such ti-3@xmath5 orbitals of transition metal oxides .    in this work",
    "we show new phenomenon of anomalous renormalization of graphene optical conductivity using srtio@xmath0 as the substrate .",
    "the optical conductivity of the graphene layer shows novel features unlike the intrinsic case particularly with almost full quenching beyond @xmath33.2 ev .",
    "these observations are supported by _ ab initio _ many body perturbation theory bethe - salpeter equation ( bse ) calculations which incorporates e - h effects .",
    "our results provide evidence of strong interactions between the ti-_3d _ and graphene _",
    "2p@xmath6 _ orbitals .",
