    "<S> we investigate the new , degenerated parabolic problem in sobolev spaces with weights for well - posedness and positivity of the solution . </S>",
    "<S> then we construct a fitted finite volume difference scheme . </S>",
    "<S> some results from computations are presented .    </S>",
