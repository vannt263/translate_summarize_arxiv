    "<S> this model is based on a one step kinetic reaction and our system is obtained by approximating the standard arrehnius law by an ignition nonlinearity , and by simplifying the term that models radiation . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the existence of 2 solutions using degree theory . </S>"
