    "spin glasses ( sg s ) are disordered magnetic alloys , widely regarded as paradigmatic complex systems.@xcite the degree of anisotropy in the magnetic interactions determines whether a particular alloy is classified as a heisenberg or an ising sg ( ising corresponds to a limit of strong anisotropy ) .",
