    "although our numerical illustrations have provided a promising result , they also suggest that the realized skewness estimator considered in this paper is somewhat noisy to measure the daily skewness of assets .",
    "this is due to the fact that we have to work with relatively low frequency data in practice to avoid market microstructure effects , which leads to the use of rather small samples of daily data .",
