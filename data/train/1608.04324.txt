    "<S> we work in a framework of lack of local integrability of the solution , in which the classical diperna - lions theory of uniqueness and lagrangianity of distributional solutions does not apply due to the insufficient integrability of the commutator . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce a general principle to prove that a solution is lagrangian : we rely on a disintegration along the unique flow and on a new directional lipschitz extension lemma , used to construct a large class of test functions in the lagrangian distributional formulation of the continuity equation .    </S>",
