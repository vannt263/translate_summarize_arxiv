    "<S> in this paper we formulate limit zeno dynamics of general open systems as the adiabatic elimination of fast components . </S>",
    "<S> we are able to exploit previous work on adiabatic elimination of quantum stochastic models to give explicitly the conditions under which open zeno dynamics will exist . </S>",
    "<S> the open systems formulation is further developed as a framework for zeno master equations , and zeno filtering ( that is , quantum trajectories based on a limit zeno dynamical model ) . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss several models from the point of view of quantum control . for the case of linear quantum stochastic systems we present a condition for stability of the asymptotic zeno dynamics .    </S>",
