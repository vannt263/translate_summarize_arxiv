    "<S> we present the minimal supersymmetric standard model with general broken r - parity , focusing on minimal supergravity ( msugra ) . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the origins of lepton number violation in supersymmetry . </S>",
    "<S> we have computed the full set of coupled one - loop renormalization group equations for the gauge couplings , the superpotential parameters and for all the soft supersymmetry breaking parameters . </S>",
    "<S> we provide analytic formul for the scalar potential minimization conditions which may be iterated to arbitrary precision . </S>",
    "<S> we compute the low - energy spectrum of the superparticles and the neutrinos as a function of the small set of parameters at the unification scale in the general basis . specializing to msugra </S>",
    "<S> , we use the neutrino masses to set new bounds on the r - parity violating couplings . </S>",
    "<S> these bounds are up - to five orders of magnitude stricter than the previously existing ones . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , new bounds on the r - parity violating couplings are also derived demanding a non - tachyonic sneutrino spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> we investigate the nature of the lightest supersymmetric particle and find extensive regions in parameter space , where it is _ not _ the neutralino . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to a novel set of supersymmetric signatures , which we classify . </S>"
