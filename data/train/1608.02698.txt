    "<S> we investigate the problem of preparing a pure gaussian state via reservoir engineering . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we consider a linear quantum system with a passive hamiltonian and with a single reservoir which acts only on a single site of the system . </S>",
    "<S> we then give a full parametrization of the pure gaussian states that can be prepared by this type of quantum system . </S>"
