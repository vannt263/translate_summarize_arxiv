    "nonlinear optics and nonlinear fiber optics have led to realization of modern photonic functionalities such as optical frequency conversion , generation of ultrashort pulses , all - optical signal processing and ultrafast switching boyd_academic_2008,agrawal_academic_2007 . in the last decades ,",
    "many efforts have been to the study of nonlinear optical processes assisted by plasmonic nanostructures kauranen _",
    "naturep_2012 because of nanoscale field confinement , field enhancement at metal - dielectric interfaces barnes_nature _ 2003,schuller_naturem_2010 , a strong and ultrafast third - order nonlinear response of metal hache_app_1988 and other novel phenomena not achievable in conventional structures , for example extreme - ultraviolet continuum generations husakou_springer_2015 .    for extended metal - dielectric interfaces",
    ", surface electromagnetic waves can propagate at the metal - dielectric interfaces , which are called surface plasmon polartons ( spps ) .",
    "spp - mediated nonlinear processes have been extensively studied , for example harmonic generation grosse_physl_2012 , four - wave mixing renger_physl_2010 , nonlinear absorption rotenberg_physb_2007 , self - phase modulation davoyan_optxp_2008,degiron_physa_2010,skryabin_optsc_2011,marini_physa_2011 and soliton feigenbaum_optl_2007,liu_physl_2007,ye_physl_2010,marini_optxp_2011,marini_prl_2013 and all - optical modulation macdonald_naturep_2008 .",
    "many theoretical studies of the nonlinear response of spps in different structures of plasmonic waveguides have been performed davoyan_optxp_2008,degiron_physa_2010,conforti_physb_2012,marini_njp_2013 , leon_physa_2014,skryabin_optsc_2011,marini_physa_2011 ,",
    "a common theoretical approach to describe the nonlinear response in optical waveguides and fibers is based on the scalar model agrawal_academic_2007 disregarding influence of a longitudinal component of electric field which is justified for large core waveguides .",
    "full vectorial model afshar_optxp_2009,afshar_optxp_2013 is developed for describing the nonlinear response in subwavelength waveguides . in skryabin_optsc_2011,marini_physa_2011",
    "the authors offered approaches to describe a surface - induced enhancement of the nonlinear response in plasmonic planar waveguides skryabin_optsc_2011 and rod waveguides marini_physa_2011 , which are in good agreement with the full vectorial model afshar_optxp_2009 . in most of these studies",
    "davoyan_optxp_2008,degiron_physa_2010,skryabin_optsc_2011,marini_physa_2011 , feigenbaum_optl_2007,liu_physl_2007,ye_physl_2010,marini_optxp_2011,marini_prl_2013 , a linear metal and a nonlinear dielectric were assumed and an imaginary part of permittivity of the metal @xmath0 is assumed to be small and perturbative .",
    "however , in practice the metal shows a strong nonlinear response hache_app_1988 , and spps are intrinsically nonlinear even in the absence of a nonlinear dielectric .    the nonlinear response of spp based on the ponderomotive nonlinearity of metals in the infrared spectral range ginzburg_optl_2010 and the second - order surface plasmon solitons based on the non - local multipole nonlinearities in metal surface ginzburg_njp_2013 were shown .",
    "marini et al .",
    "marini_njp_2013 studied the effects of the thermo - modulational interband nonlinearity of gold on the propagation of spps guided on gold nanowires at the wavelengths longer than 750 nm .",
    "they also assumed low loss of gold which is justified at the long wavelengths .",
    "however , at shorter wavelengths gold has a high loss johnson_physb_1972 and thus an effect of the high loss of gold is needed to be considered for modeling spp nonlinear propagation .",
    "moreover , the quite large third - order nonlinear susceptibility of gold at these shorter wavelengths , as recently reported in conforti_physb_2012,marini_njp_2013 , gives practical importance of modeling the spp nonlinear propagation in the spectral range leon_physa_2014,leon_optl_2014 .",
    "de leon et al .",

    "<S> we offer a model to describe the intrinsic nonlinear response of surface plasmon polaritons ( spps ) . </S>",
    "<S> relation of the complex nonlinear coefficient of spps to the third - order nonlinear susceptibility of the metal is provided . as reported in a recent study , </S>",
    "<S> gold is highly lossy and simultaneously highly nonlinear due to interband absorption and interband thermo - modulation at a wavelength shorter than 700 nm . </S>",
    "<S> the effect of the high loss of the metal on the spp nonlinear propagation is taken into account in our model . with the model </S>",
    "<S> we show difference in sign of real and imaginary parts between the nonlinear propagation coefficient and the nonlinear susceptibility of component material for the first time to our knowledge . </S>",
    "<S> our model could have practical importance in studying plasmonic devices utilizing the nonlinear phase modulation and the nonlinear absorption of spps . </S>",
    "<S> for example , it allows one to extract the complex nonlinear susceptibility of gold through a measurement of spp nonlinear propagation at the visible range . </S>"
