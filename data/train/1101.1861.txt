    "oscillatory integrals play an important role in the theory of pseudodifferential operators .",
    "they are also a useful tool in mathematical physics , in particular in quantum field theory , where they are used to give meaning to formal fourier integrals in the sense of distributions . for phase functions which are homogeneous of order one",
