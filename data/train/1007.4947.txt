    "<S> we investigate the dynamics of two miscible superfluids experiencing fast counterflow in a narrow channel . </S>",
    "<S> the superfluids are formed by two distinguishable components of a trapped dilute - gas bose - einstein condensate ( bec ) . </S>",
    "<S> the onset of counterflow - induced modulational instability throughout the cloud is observed and shown to lead to the proliferation of dark - dark vector solitons . </S>",
    "<S> these solitons , which we observe for the first time in a bec , do not exist in single - component systems , exhibit intriguing beating dynamics and can experience a transverse instability leading to vortex line structures . experimental results and multi - dimensional numerical simulations are presented .    </S>",
    "<S> superfluids are a robust model system for the investigation of nonlinear fluid flow . </S>",
    "<S> governed by an underlying macroscopic wavefunction , superfluids can display a large variety of nonlinear wave phenomena in the context of matterwaves . in bose - einstein condensates </S>",
    "<S> ( becs ) , nonlinear structures including solitons , vortices and vortex rings have been the focus of intense research efforts @xcite . in this work </S>",
    "<S> , we investigate the regime of fast counterflow between two distinguishable superfluids in a narrow channel and observe dynamics leading to novel structures . </S>",
    "<S> modulational instability ( mi ) , in which small perturbations to a carrier wave , reinforced by nonlinearity , experience rapid growth @xcite , plays a key role in the dynamics . in many nonlinear systems , </S>",
    "<S> mi leads to the breakup of periodic wavetrains , as in sufficiently deep water @xcite , as well as the formation of localized structures in optics @xcite and becs @xcite . in our case , mi - induced regular density modulations , formed throughout the bec , lead to the emergence of a large number of _ beating dark - dark _ solitons . </S>",
    "<S> these solitons  which exhibit periodic energy exchange between the two condensate components @xcite  are a generalization of static dark - dark solitons @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> they are distinctly different from all previously observed solitons in becs , including dark - bright solitons which were generated in a two - component mixture by marginally critical counterflow - induced mi near a density edge @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we perform three - dimensional ( 3d ) numerical simulations to corroborate this interpretation and furthermore identify a subsequent transverse instability resulting in multi - dimensional structures such as vortex lines ( see @xcite for the scalar counterpart ) .    </S>",
    "<S> we study superfluid counterflow with an experimental system consisting of becs with typically @xmath0 atoms of @xmath1rb . </S>",
