    "blazar emission extends over the whole electromagnetic spectrum and is generally interpreted as non - thermal radiation arising from particles accelerated in relativistic jets closely aligned to the line of sight @xcite .",
    "they come in two flavors : the bl lac objects , with featureless optical spectra or only with absorption lines of galactic origin and weak and narrower emission lines , and the flat spectrum radio quasars , with a optical spectra showing broad emission lines . in the following ,",
