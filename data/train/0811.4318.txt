    "<S> gamma distributions , which contain the exponential as a special case , have a distinguished place in the representation of near - poisson randomness for statistical processes ; typically , they represent distributions of spacings between events or voids among objects . here </S>",
    "<S> we look at the properties of the shannon entropy function and calculate its corresponding flow curves , relating them to examples of constrained degeneration from ordered processes . </S>",
    "<S> we consider also univariate and bivariate gamma , as well as weibull distributions since these include exponential distributions .    </S>",
