    "<S> we present the results of a multi - wavelength ( fuv to i - band ) survey of the stellar populations of the globular cluster ngc6752 , using stis , acs and wfc3 on board the _ hubble space telescope_. we have confirmed that two previously identified cv candidates are , in fact , dwarf novae which underwent outbursts during our observations . </S>",
    "<S> we have also identified previously unknown optical counterparts to two x - ray sources . </S>",
    "<S> we estimate the position of the centre of the cluster , and show that the stellar density profile is not well described by a single king model , indicating that this cluster is in a core - collapsed or post - core collapse phase . </S>",
    "<S> the colour - magnitude diagram shows a well - populated horizontal branch , numerous blue stragglers and white dwarfs ( wds ) , as well as 87 sources in the gap region where we expect to find wd - main sequence binaries , including cataclysmic variables ( cvs ) . </S>",
    "<S> the x - ray sources and wd binary systems are the most centrally concentrated populations , with dynamically estimated characteristic masses @xmath0 and @xmath1 , respectively .    [ firstpage ]    </S>",
