    "<S> radiative feedback and magnetic field are understood to have a strong impact on the protostellar collapse . </S>",
    "<S> we present high resolution numerical calculations of the collapse of a 1 m@xmath0 dense core in solid body rotation , including both radiative transfer and magnetic field . using typical parameters for low - mass cores , we study thoroughly the effect of radiative transfer and magnetic field on the first core formation and fragmentation . </S>",
    "<S> we show that including the two aforementioned physical processes does not correspond to the simple picture of adding them separately . </S>",
    "<S> the interplay between the two is extremely strong , via the magnetic braking and the radiation from the accretion shock . </S>"
