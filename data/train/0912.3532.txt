    "inflation is perhaps the most promising paradigm of the early universe .",
    "inflation resolves various problems with the standard big - bang scenario ( see  @xcite for reviews ) and provides seed scalar perturbations for structure formation . besides the scalar perturbations",
    ", inflation also predicts tensor perturbations or gravitational waves .",
    "detection of these tensor perturbations is considered as a `` smoking gun '' for inflationary scenarios .",
    "primordial scalar perturbations create only e - modes of the cmb , while primordial tensor perturbations generate both parity even e - modes and parity odd b - modes polarization  @xcite .",
    "the detection of primordial tensor b - modes   in the cmb would confirm the existence of tensor perturbations in the early universe .",
    "various observational efforts are underway to detect such b - mode signal of the cmb  @xcite . the magnitude of tensor generated b - mode signal is directly related to the hubble parameter @xmath2 during inflation , and thus a detection would establish the energy scale at which inflation happened",

    "<S> distortions in the primordial cosmic microwave background ( cmb ) along the line - of - sight can be modeled and described using 11 fields . </S>",
    "<S> these distortion fields correspond to various cosmological signals such as weak gravitational lensing of the cmb by large - scale structure , screening from patchy reionization , rotation of the plane of polarization due to magnetic fields or parity violating physics . </S>",
    "<S> various instrumental systematics such as gain fluctuations , pixel rotation , differential gain , pointing , differential ellipticity are also described by the same distortion model . </S>",
    "<S> all these distortions produce b - mode that contaminate the primordial tensor b - modes signal . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we show that apart from generating b - modes , each distortion uniquely couples different modes @xmath0 of the cmb anisotropies , generating @xmath1 correlations which for the primordial cmb are zero . </S>",
    "<S> we describe and implement unbiased minimum variance quadratic estimators which using the off diagonal correlations in the cmb can extract the map of distortions . </S>",
    "<S> we perform monte - carlo simulations to characterize the estimators and illustrate the level of distortions that can be detected with current and future experiments . </S>",
    "<S> the estimators can be used to look for cosmological signals , or to check for any residual systematics in the data . as a specific example of primordial tensor b </S>",
    "<S> - mode diagnostics we compare the level of minimum detectable distortions using our method with maximum allowed distortion level for the b - modes detection . </S>",
    "<S> we show that for any experiment , the distortions will be detected at high significance using correlations before they would show up as spurious b - modes in the power spectrum . in another words </S>",
    "<S> if b - modes were to be detected , the presence of any non - primordial contribution could be checked and removed using the off - diagonal correlations in the cmb . </S>"
