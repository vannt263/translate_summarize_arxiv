    "<S> chiral fermions can be embedded into souriau s massless spinning particle model by `` enslaving '' the spin , viewed as a gauge constraint . </S>",
    "<S> the latter is not invariant under lorentz boosts ; spin enslavement can be restored , however , by a wigner - souriau ( ws ) translation , analogous to a compensating gauge transformation . </S>",
    "<S> the combined transformation is precisely the recently uncovered twisted boost , which we now extend to finite transformations . </S>",
    "<S> ws - translations are identified with the stability group of a motion acting on the right on the poincar group , whereas the natural poincar action corresponds to action on the left . </S>",
    "<S> the relation to non - commutative mechanics is explained . </S>"
