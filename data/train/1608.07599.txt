    "<S> the @xmath0-filled band charge - transfer solids are of strong interest , because of the commensurate nature of the charge - ordering in these systems . </S>",
    "<S> the period - four charge - order pattern @xmath11100@xmath1 here is accompanied by two distinct bond distortion patterns , giving rise to bond - charge - density waves ( bcdw ) of types 1 and 2 . using quantum monte carlo methods </S>",
    "<S> , we determine the phase diagram within the extended hubbard hamiltonian that gives both types 1 and 2 bcdw in the thermodynamic limit . </S>",
    "<S> we further investigate the effect of electron - electron and electron - phonon interactions on the amount of charge disproportionation . </S>",
