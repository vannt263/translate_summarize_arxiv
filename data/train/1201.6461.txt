    "<S> in this paper , we study a flower population in which self - reproduction is not permitted . </S>",
    "<S> individuals are diploid , that is , each cell contains two sets of chromosomes , and distylous , that is , two alleles , a and a , can be found at the considered locus s. pollen and ovules of flowers with the same genotype at locus s can not mate . </S>",
