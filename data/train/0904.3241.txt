    "supersymmetry  ( susy )  @xcite is a very well motivated theory to extend the standard model  ( sm ) of particle physics  @xcite .",
    "susy models are not only favored by gauge coupling unification and naturalness considerations , but are also attractive from the cosmological point of view .",
    "for example , the lightest susy particle  ( lsp ) is a good dark matter candidate , if it is stable , massive and weakly interacting  @xcite . most interestingly ,",
    "susy models provide a number of new parameters , among some having physical phases which cause manifest cp violation  @xcite . remarkably in the sm",
    ", the single cp phase in the quark mixing matrix , which is currently confirmed in @xmath0 meson experiments  @xcite , can not explain the observed baryon asymmetry of the universe  @xcite .",
    "additional sources of cp violation in models beyond the sm are required  @xcite .    in the mssm , a set of remaining complex parameters",
    "is obtained after absorbing unphysical phases of parameters by redefining particle fields . in the literature ,",
    "the complex parameters are usually chosen to be the higgsino mass parameter @xmath1 , the @xmath2 and @xmath3 gaugino mass parameters @xmath4 and @xmath5 , respectively , and the trilinear scalar coupling parameters @xmath6 @xmath7 the susy cp phases of these parameters lead to theoretical predictions for the electric dipole moments  ( edms ) of electron , neutron and that of the atoms @xmath8hg and @xmath9tl , which can be ( sometimes orders of magnitude ) beyond the current experimental upper bounds  @xcite .",
    "these strong bounds suggest that either the susy cp phases are severely suppressed ( in particular @xmath10 ) , the susy particles are very heavy ( e.g. the first two generations of sfermions with @xmath11  tev ) , or different loop - contributions to the edms cancel accidentally",
    ". however , these solutions require a fine - tuning of the susy parameters , and would be unnatural .",
    "the need of tuning the susy parameters and phases to fulfill the edm constraints is referred to as the _ susy cp problem _ in the literature  @xcite . in order to analyze the problem ,",
    "it is necessary to independently measure the susy phases at colliders . in particular cp - odd observables",
    "are needed to find or exclude direct evidence of cp violation .    in",
    "this talk we will concentrate on a particular class of cp - odd ( t - odd ) observables , which can be defined with the help of triple products  @xcite .",
    "triple products can lead to large cp asymmetries , since they already appear at tree level due to spin correlations  @xcite .",
    "we will only give a few selected examples to motivate the use of triple products , and can not give a thorough review of the vast amount of literature in the field .",
    "therefore we will discuss the production of neutralinos  @xcite at the international linear collider  ( ilc )  @xcite .",
    "for the large hadron collider  ( lhc )  @xcite , cp asymmetries in top squark decays  @xcite and in the tri - lepton signal  @xcite are discussed .",
