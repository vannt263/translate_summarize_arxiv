    "<S> we show that every multiparameter gaussian process with integrable variance function admits a wiener integral representation of fredholm type with respect to the brownian sheet . </S>",
    "<S> the fredholm kernel in the representation can be constructed as the unique symmetric square root of the covariance . </S>",
    "<S> we analyze the equivalence of multiparameter gaussian processes by using the fredholm representation and show how to construct series expansions for multiparameter gaussian processes by using the fredholm kernel .    </S>",
