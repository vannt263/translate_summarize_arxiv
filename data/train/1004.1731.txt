    "<S> when a single beam - splitter receives two beams of bosons described by fock states ( bose - einstein condensates at very low temperatures ) , interesting generalizations of the two - photon hong - ou - mandel effect take place for larger number of particles . </S>",
    "<S> the distributions of particles at two detectors behind the beam splitter can be understood as resulting from the combination of two effects , the spontaneous phase appearing during quantum measurement , and the quantum angle . </S>",
    "<S> the latter introduces quantum `` population oscillations '' , which can be seen as a generalized hong - ou - mandel effect , although they do not always correspond to even - odd oscillations .    </S>",
