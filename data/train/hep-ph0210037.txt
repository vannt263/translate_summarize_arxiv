    "<S> this formalism allows the systematic derivation of evolution equations for averaged quantities as survival probabilities and higher order distribution moments . </S>",
    "<S> the formalism equally applies to any finite dimensional schrdinger equation in the presence of a stochastic external field . </S>",
    "<S> new integrodifferential equations valid for finite correlated processes are obtained for the first time . for the particular case of exponentially correlated processes a second order ordinary equation </S>",
    "<S> is obtained . as a consequence </S>",
    "<S> , the redfield equation valid for gaussian delta - correlated noise is rederived in a simple way : it has been obtained directly and as the zero - order term of an asymptotic expansion in the inverse of the correlation length . </S>",
    "<S> the formalism , together with the quantum correlation theorem is applied to the computation of higher moments and correlation functions . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that equal and unequal time correlators follow similar differential equations . </S>"
