    "<S> in this second article of the series we study holomorphic families of generic rational matrix functions parameterized by the pole and zero loci . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , the isoprincipal deformations of generic rational matrix functions are proved to be isosemiresidual . </S>",
    "<S> the corresponding rational solutions of the schlesinger system are constructed and the explicit expression for the related tau function is given . </S>",
    "<S> the main tool is the theory of joint system representations for rational matrix functions with prescribed pole and zero structures . </S>"
