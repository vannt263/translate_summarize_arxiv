    "<S> however , its convergence was not guaranteed without extra requirements . very recently , he _ et al . _ </S>",
    "<S> ( siam j. optim . 24 : 1011 - 1040 , 2014 ) proved the convergence of a strictly contractive peaceman - rachford splitting method by employing a suitable underdetermined relaxation factor . </S>",
    "<S> in this paper , we further extend the so - called strictly contractive peaceman - rachford splitting method by using two different relaxation factors , and to make the method more flexible , we introduce semi - proximal terms to the subproblems . </S>",
    "<S> we characterize the relation of these two factors , and show that one factor is always underdetermined while the other one is allowed to be larger than 1 . </S>",
