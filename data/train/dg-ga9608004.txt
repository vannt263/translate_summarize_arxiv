    "we show that , for maps between hermitian manifolds , the classes of pluriharmonic maps and @xmath0holomorphic maps coincide precisely when the complex structure carried by the target manifold is khler .",
    "we find that a necessary and sufficient condition for a map between hermitian manifolds to be a pluriharmonic morphism is that it be a @xmath0holomorphic pluriharmonic map .",
