    "the third year wmap data release @xcite has confirmed beautifully the cosmological standard model of structure formation : during inflation fluctuations in the scalar fields are stretched to super - horizon scales and later on source the cosmic microwave background ( cmb ) anisotropies and the large scale structure . in the standard inflation models the scalar field responsible for the accelerated expansion of the universe",
    ", the inflaton , also provides these fluctuations @xcite .",
    "recently a related but different scenario has become become popular : the curvaton paradigm @xcite . here",
    "the fluctuations are not generated by the inflaton , but by a different scalar field , the curvaton .",
    "a powerful tool to differentiate between different models of the early universe is second order perturbation theory @xcite .",
    "it allows the accurate calculation of higher order statistics such as the primordial bispectrum and the non - linearity parameter @xmath0 . instead of using cosmological perturbation theory at second order ,",
    "the @xmath3-formalism @xcite has proved useful to study non - gaussianity and calculate @xmath0 .",

    "however , both studies used the sudden decay approximation . here",
    "we go beyond sudden decay , using second order gauge - invariant perturbation theory and solve the ensuing equations numerically .",
