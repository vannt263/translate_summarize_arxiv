    "<S> this paper presents an algorithmic method to study structural properties of nonlinear control systems in dependence of parameters . </S>",
    "<S> the result consists of a description of parameter configurations which cause different control - theoretic behaviour of the system ( in terms of observability , flatness , etc . ) . </S>",
    "<S> the constructive symbolic method is based on the differential thomas decomposition into disjoint simple systems , in particular its elimination properties . </S>"
