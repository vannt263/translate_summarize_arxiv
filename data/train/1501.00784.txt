    "exclusive @xmath11 @xmath12 meson decays , especially @xmath13 modes , where @xmath2 stands for a light vector meson ( @xmath14 ) , have aroused a great deal of interest for both theorists  @xcite and in experiments  @xcite .",
    "in contrast to the scalar and pseudoscalar mesons , vector mesons can be produced in several polarization states .",
    "thus , the fraction of a given polarization state is an interesting observable , apart from the decay widths .",
    "phenomenology of the @xmath13 decays offers rich opportunities for our understanding of the mechanism for hadronic weak decays and @xmath0 asymmetry and searching for the effect of new physics beyond the standard model . in general , the underlying dynamics for such decays is extremely complicated , but in the heavy quark limit ( @xmath15 ) , it is greatly simplified due to the factorization of the hadronic matrix elements in terms of the decay constants and form factors .",
    "based on this , a number of two - body hadronic @xmath7 decays had been calculated in this so - called naive factorization approach  @xcite .",
    "however these calculations encounter three major difficulties : ( i ) for the so - called penguin - dominated , and also for the color - suppressed tree - dominated decays , the predicted branching ratios are systematically below the measurements , ( ii ) this approach can not account for the direct @xmath0 asymmetries measured in experiments , and ( iii ) the predictions of transverse polarization fraction in penguin - dominated charmless @xmath13 decays are too small to explain the data , in which large such fractions are measured .",
    "all these indicate that this factorization approach needs improvements , for example by including some more perturbative qcd contributions  @xcite . in the current market , there are essentially three approaches to implement perturbative improvements : qcd factorization ( qcdf )  @xcite , perturbative qcd approach(pqcd )  @xcite , and the soft - collinear effective theory ( scet )  @xcite .",
    "all these frameworks have been employed in the literature to quantitatively study the dynamics of the @xmath16 decays , having light pseudoscalar ( @xmath17)and / or vector ( @xmath2 ) mesons in the final states .    in the @xmath13",

    "the @xmath11 meson is soft , a hard gluon exchange is needed to kick it into an energetic one to form a fast moving light vector meson .",
    "the theoretical picture here is that a hard gluon from the spectator quark connects with the other quarks of the four - quark operators of the weak interaction  @xcite .",
    "the underlying theory is thus a six - quark effective theory , and can be perturbatively calculated  @xcite .",
    "in contrast to the other two approaches ( qcdf and scet ) , the pqcd approach is based on the @xmath21 factorization formalism  @xcite .",
    "the basic idea here is to take into account the transverse momentum @xmath21 of the valence quarks in the hadrons , as a result of which the end - point singularity in the collinear factorization ( employed in the qcdf approach ) can be avoided . on the other hand ,",
    "the transverse momentum dependence introduces an additional energy scale leading to double logarithms in qcd corrections .",
    "these terms could be resummed through the renormalization group approach , which results in the appearance of the sudakov form factor .",
    "this form factor effectively suppresses the end - point contribution of the distribution amplitude of the mesons in the small transverse momentum region , making the calculation in the pqcd approach reliable .",
    "it is worth mentioning that in this framework , the so - called annihilation diagrams are also perturbatively calculable without introducing additional parameters  @xcite .",
    "the pqcd approach has been successfully used to study a number of pure annihilation type decays , and these predictions were confirmed subsequently in experiments  @xcite .",
    "thus , in our view , this method is reliable in dealing with the pure annihilation - type and annihilation - dominated decays as well .",
    "several years ago , h. y. cheng and c. k. chua updated  @xcite the previous predictions  @xcite for @xmath22 decays in the qcdf factorization approach by taking the transverse polarization contributions into account , and using the updated values of the parameters in the input wave functions and the form factors . in the pqcd framework , although many studies of the two - body @xmath11-decays are available  @xcite , a reappraisal is needed for the following reasons : ( i ) in the previous studies , the terms proportional to  @xmath23 \" have been omitted in the amplitudes , especially in the denominator of the propagators of virtual quarks and gluons .",
    "as we point out later , these terms do bring the earlier pqcd predictions in better accord in terms of the measured observables in some problematic cases , such as the @xmath9 and @xmath10 decays , ( ii ) recent progress in the study of the distribution amplitudes of the vector meson , especially for the @xmath24 meson , undertaken in the context of the qcd sum rules , may significantly impact on some of the calculations done earlier , and ( iii ) experimental data for some of the @xmath13 decays , such as the branching ratio and the polarization fractions of @xmath10 , are now available .",
