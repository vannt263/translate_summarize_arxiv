    "in four spacetime dimensions , a stationary , asymptotically flat , vacuum black hole is completely characterized by its mass and spin @xcite . in particular , the topology of its event horizon must be a sphere @xcite .",
    "by contrast , in five dimensions , in addition to the myers - perry black hole @xcite which is natural generalization of the four - dimensional kerr black hole , various exact solutions of black objects with nonspherical horizon topologies have been found ( see @xcite for a review ) . in this paper , we focus attention to the black ring solution with the @xmath1 horizon topology .",
    "the black ring solution rotating in the direction of @xmath0 was found by emparan and reall @xcite .",
    "since a five - dimensional spacetime can have two angular momenta , pomeransky and senkov @xcite extended it to black rings with two independent rotation parameters ( i.e. , doubly spinning in directions of @xmath0 and @xmath2 ) .",
    "a black hole is known to evaporate due to the effect of quantum field theory in curved spacetime as shown by hawking @xcite .",
    "the rate of mass and angular momentum loss of a kerr black hole by the hawking radiation was first studied by page @xcite taking account of fields with spins 1/2 , 1 , and 2 , and it was shown that a kerr black hole spins down to a nonrotating black hole regardless of its initial state .",
    "however , chambers _",

    "this analysis was extended to five - dimensional myers - perry black holes by nomura _",
    "et al _ @xcite .",
    "they showed that any such black hole with nonzero rotation parameters @xmath4 and @xmath5 evolves toward an asymptotic state with @xmath6 . here , this value is independent of the initial values of @xmath4 and @xmath5 .",
    "it is interesting to extend these studies to the case of a black ring .",
    "although the hawking radiation of black rings has been studied in various context @xcite , so far , the time evolution of a black ring has not been studied .",
    "the difficulty in this study is that the method of mode decomposition of the klein - gordon field in this spacetime is not known since separation of variables has not been done , and therefore , two dimensional numerical calculations of eigenfunctions are required . in order to avoid this difficulty",
