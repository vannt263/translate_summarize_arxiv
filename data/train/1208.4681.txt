    "<S> the growth rate of matter density perturbations has been measured from redshift - space distortion ( rsd ) in the galaxy power spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> we constrain the model parameter space for representative modified gravity models to explain the dark energy problem by using the recent data of @xmath0 at the redshifts @xmath1 0.060.8 measured by wigglez , sdss lrg , boss , and 6dfgrs . </S>",
    "<S> we first test the hu - sawicki s @xmath2 dark energy model , and find that only the parameter region close to the standard @xmath3 cold dark matter ( @xmath3cdm ) model is allowed ( @xmath4 12 and 5 for @xmath5 1.5 and 2 , respectively , at 95% cl ) . </S>",
    "<S> we then investigate the covariant galileon model with a de sitter attractor and show that the parameter space consistent with the background expansion history is excluded by the rsd data at more than @xmath6 because of the too large growth rate predicted by the theory . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we consider the extended galileon scenario , and we find that , in contrast to the covariant galileon , there is a model parameter space for a tracker solution that is consistent with the rsd data within a @xmath7 level . </S>"
