    "sensitive dependence on initial conditions is typical in many physical systems .",
    "this sensitivity problem raises nontrivial reliability and stability issues regarding any computational approach to such systems .",
    "moreover , it strongly motivates the study of reliable computational methods for understanding statistical properties of physical systems .    in this note",
    "we consider the rigorous computation of diffusion coefficients in a class of systems where a central limit theorem holds .",
