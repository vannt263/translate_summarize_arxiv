    "the quark - confinement mechanism is one of the most important subjects in the nonperturbative qcd ( np - qcd ) and in the hadron physics . from a phenomenological point of view",
    ", quark confinement is characterized by the linear inter - quark potential @xmath13 with the hadronic string tension @xmath14gev / fm , which is obtained from the regge trajectories @xcite of hadrons and also from the lattice qcd simulation @xcite .",
    "this confining force arises from the one - dimensional squeezing of the gluonic flux between the quark and the anti - quark , which is actually observed in the lattice qcd simulation @xcite .    on the quark - confinement mechanism , nambu @xcite first proposed the dual - superconductor picture in 1974 using the concept of the electromagnetic duality @xcite in the maxwell equation and the analogy with the one - dimensional squeezing of the magnetic flux as the abrikosov vortex in the type - ii superconductor @xcite . in the dual - superconductor picture ,",
