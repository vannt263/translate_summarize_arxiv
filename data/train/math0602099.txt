    "<S> in the weakened 16th hilbert s problem one asks for a bound of the number of limit cycles which appear after a polynomial perturbation of a planar polynomial hamiltonian vector field . </S>",
    "<S> it is known that this number is finite for an individual vector field . in the multidimensional generalization of this problem </S>",
    "<S> one considers polynomial perturbation of a polynomial vector field with invariant plane supporting a hamiltonian dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> we present an explicit example of such perturbation with infinite number of limit cycles which accumulate at some separatrix loop .    </S>"
