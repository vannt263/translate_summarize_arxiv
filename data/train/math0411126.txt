    "<S> a knot in the 3-sphere is called doubly slice if it is a slice of an unknotted 2-sphere in the 4-sphere . </S>",
    "<S> we give a bi - sequence of new obstructions for a knot being doubly slice . </S>",
    "<S> we construct it following the idea of cochran - orr - teichner s filtration of the classical knot concordance group . </S>",
    "<S> this yields a bi - filtration of the monoid of knots ( under the connected sum operation ) indexed by pairs of half integers . </S>",
    "<S> doubly slice knots lie in the intersection of this bi - filtration . </S>",
    "<S> we construct examples of knots which illustrate non - triviality of this bi - filtration at all levels . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , these are new examples of algebraically doubly slice knots that are not doubly slice , and many of these knots are slice . </S>",
    "<S> cheeger - gromov s von neumann rho invariants play a key role to show non - triviality of this bi - filtration . </S>",
    "<S> we also show that some classical invariants are reflected at the initial levels of this bi - filtration , and obtain a bi - filtration of the double concordance group . </S>"
