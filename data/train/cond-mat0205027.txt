    "advances in nanotechnology have made it possible to fabricate quantum dots or artificial atoms and to study their transport properties . due to the confinement and the coulomb interaction in the quantum dot , charge and spin",
    "can be quantized .",
    "quantization of electron charge leads to the almost periodic variation of conductance as a function of the number of electrons @xmath0 or the gate voltage .",
    "a conductance peak is observed whenever the charge fluctuation is allowed or the @xmath0- and @xmath1-states become energetically degenerate .",
    "otherwise electrons in the electrodes can not hop into a quantum dot due to the strong coulomb repulsion .",
    "this coulomb blockade leads to vanishing conductance between two neighboring conductance peaks .",
    "novel kondo effects can enhance the conductance in the coulomb blockade region when the number of electrons at the quantum dot is odd and at least one electron spin is unpaired .",
    "the unpaired @xmath2 in the quantum dot can be screened by the electrons in the external electrodes connected to the quantum dot .",
    "this theoretical prediction@xcite of the novel nonequilibrium kondo effects in quantum dot systems was confirmed experimentally recently@xcite . in the conventional bulk kondo systems where magnetic ions are doped into normal metals",

    "the nonequilibrium situation is easily controlled and model parameters can be varied by adjusting the gate voltages .    the study of the kondo effect in the quantum dot systems has been diversified to see the different aspects .",
