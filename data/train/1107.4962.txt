    "<S> recent cosmological observations , including measurements of the cmb anisotropy and the primordial helium abundance , indicate the existence of an extra radiation component in the universe beyond the standard three neutrino species . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we explore the possibility that the extra radiation has isocurvatrue fluctuations . a general formalism to evaluate isocurvature perturbations in the extra radiation is provided in the mixed inflaton - curvaton system , where the extra radiation is produced by the decay of both scalar fields . </S>",
    "<S> we also derive constraints on the abundance of the extra radiation and the amount of its isocurvature perturbation . </S>",
    "<S> current observational data favors the existence of an extra radiation component , but does not indicate its having isocurvature perturbation . </S>",
    "<S> these constraints are applied to some particle physics motivated models . </S>",
    "<S> if future observations detect isocurvature perturbations in the extra radiation , it will give us a hint to the origin of the extra radiation .    </S>",
