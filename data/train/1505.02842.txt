    "<S> this paper is concerned with finite element approximations of @xmath0 strong solutions of second - order linear elliptic partial differential equations ( pdes ) in non - divergence form with continuous coefficients . a nonstandard ( primal ) </S>",
    "<S> finite element method , which uses finite - dimensional subspaces consisting globally continuous piecewise polynomial functions , is proposed and analyzed . </S>",
    "<S> the main novelty of the finite element method is to introduce an interior penalty term , which penalizes the jump of the flux across the interior element edges / faces , to augment a nonsymmetric piecewise defined and pde  induced bilinear form . </S>",
    "<S> existence , uniqueness and error estimate in a discrete @xmath0 energy norm are proved for the proposed finite element method . </S>",
    "<S> this is achieved by establishing a discrete calderon  </S>",
    "<S> zygmund  type estimate and mimicking strong solution pde techniques at the discrete level . </S>",
    "<S> numerical experiments are provided to test the performance of proposed finite element method and to validate the convergence theory . </S>"
