    "<S> we present numerical evidence for an extended order parameter and conjugate field for the dynamic phase transition in a ginzburg - landau mean - field model driven by an oscillating field . </S>",
    "<S> the order parameter , previously taken to be the time - averaged magnetization , comprises the deviations of the fourier components of the magnetization from their values at the critical period . </S>",
    "<S> the conjugate field , previously taken to be the time - averaged magnetic field , comprises the even fourier components of the field . </S>",
    "<S> the scaling exponents @xmath0 and @xmath1 associated with the extended order parameter and conjugate field are shown numerically to be consistent with their values in the equilibrium mean - field model . </S>"
