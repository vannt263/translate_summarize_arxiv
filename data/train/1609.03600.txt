    "cyber - physical systems ( cps ) are systems which integrate control systems with advanced technologies of computation and communication .",
    "the integration leads to highly automated and collaborative applications such as autonomous vehicles , remote patient monitoring , and smart grid . due to such potentials , the significance on cps",
    "is emphasized by the president s council of advisors on science and technology at 2010  @xcite .",
    "security is of vital importance for cps .",
    "especially , because of the coupling between the cyber and physical layers , cps bear vulnerabilities to cyberattacks which may cause irreparable damage to the physical layer  @xcite .",
    "for example , a natural gas flow control system in russian was temporarily seized in 2000 and a sewage control system in australia was attacked in the same year  @xcite . according to early studies on cps security problems , the types of possible attacks on cps can be categorized into signal attacks and switching attacks .",
    "signal attacks include sensor attacks which tamper sensor readings and actuator attacks which tamper control commands .",
    "the attacks can be launched via communication jamming and malware ; e.g. , trojan .",
    "while signal attacks modify the magnitude or timing of signals , switching attacks alter system structures  @xcite .",
    "this paper considers both signal attacks and switching attacks",
    ".    * literature review .",

    "paper  @xcite investigates the possibilities that an attack can bypass detection algorithms for power systems .",

    "noticeably , this set of papers is restricted to linear systems . *",
    "contributions . *",
    "we study the attack - resilient estimation of a class of switched nonlinear systems subject to stochastic noises .",
    "we formulate the problem as the simultaneous estimation of states , attack vectors and hidden modes .",
    "the proposed algorithm associates an estimator to each mode and the estimators share the same structure . each estimator generates the estimate of state and attack vector .",
    "these estimates enable the mode estimator to choose the most likely mode .",
    "for the special case where the hidden mode is fixed but remains unknown , it is shown that the state estimation errors are exponentially bounded in mean square and bounded probability one , for the true mode . for the same case , we also analyze that the mean square errors of the attack vector estimates for the true mode are bounded by a function of initial state estimation error . on a power system ,",
    "a numerical simulation is conducted to show the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm on a varying - mode case . towards our best knowledge , this is the first paper to systematically study unknown input and state estimation of switched nonlinear systems .    *",

    "<S> this paper studies attack - resilient estimation of a class of switched nonlinear systems subject to stochastic process and measurement noises . </S>",
    "<S> the systems are threatened by both of signal attacks and switching attacks . </S>",
    "<S> the problem is formulated as the joint estimation of state , attack vector and mode of hidden - mode switched systems . </S>",
    "<S> we propose an estimation algorithm which is composed of a bank of state and attack vector estimators and a mode estimator . </S>",
    "<S> the mode estimator selects the most likely mode using the outputs of the state and attack vector estimators . </S>",
    "<S> the stability and mean square estimation error bounds for the designed estimator are formally analyzed for the true mode when the hidden mode is time - invariant but remains unknown . </S>",
    "<S> lastly , we present a numerical simulation on a power system to show the varying - mode performance of the proposed attack - resilient estimator . </S>"
