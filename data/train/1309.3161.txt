    "<S> the ratio @xmath1 is also several times larger for the gaussian ll shape . </S>",
    "<S> amro and zeeman energy splitting lead to a spin current . for typical organic metals and for a medium magnetic field 10 t </S>",
    "<S> this spin current is only a few percent of the charge current , but its value may almost reach the charge current for special tilt angles of magnetic field . </S>",
