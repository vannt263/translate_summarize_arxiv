    "the probability distribution of the number of nodes with @xmath325 outgoing lines has been investigated in randomly evolving trees defined in @xcite .",
    "special attention was paid on the stochastic properties of end - nodes .",
    "we found that the birth and death of end - nodes in randomly evolving trees are playing a decisive role in the dynamics of the process .",
    "it is remarkable that the _ relative variance _ of the number of end - nodes vs. time has well - defined maximum when the evolution is either subcritical or supercritical . in the case of critical evolution",
    "the relative variance increases monotonously with the time . on the contrary",
    ", the _ relative dispersion _ of the number of dead end - nodes vs. time has a minimum just after the beginning of the evolution .",
    "the minimum can be seen in each of evolution states .",
    "we defined the lifetime of randomly evolving trees and derived a non - linear differential equation for the probability that the lifetime is larger than a given positive real number @xmath2 .",
