    "the extension of the hathorcross section calculator for the production of single top - quarks has been presented .",
    "the new part comprises a fast computation of all three production channels , the @xmath4-channel , the @xmath5-channel and the associated @xmath52 production , in lo and nlo of qcd . despite the small amount of computing time needed",
    ", the results agree within less than 1 with those from other programs .",
    "moreover , the complete scale dependence for @xmath24 and @xmath25 of the cross sections is implemented for all orders up to nnlo and the corresponding equations are collected in this paper .",
    "besides the scales , the hathorprogram allows to vary several model parameters and the pdfs .",
    "this can be used to establish thorough systematic studies of the total cross section in a fast and efficient manner .",
    "several examples were presented here , mostly for proton - proton scattering at the . among these are cross section calculations in different orders and the dependence on renormalisation and factorisation scales .",
    "the dependence on the mass of the top quark was studied in some detail and estimates of the top - quark mass from single top - quark production using the latest cross section measurements were given .",
    "furthermore , the impact of the strong coupling constant , @xmath51 , and the parton distribution functions on the cross section uncertainty has been discussed .",

    "<S> we present updated predictions for single top - quark production in hadronic collisions . </S>",
    "<S> the analysis is based on next - to - leading order qcd calculations . </S>",
    "<S> the input parameters are fixed to recent measurements . </S>",
    "<S> we compare different pdf sets and investigate the related uncertainties . </S>",
    "<S> the impact of uncalculated higher orders is estimated using an independent variation of the renormalisation and factorisation scale . </S>",
    "<S> the theoretical predictions are compared with recent measurements from tevatron and lhc . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , the cross section measurements are used to estimate the top - quark mass . to perform the analysis we extended the publicly available hathorprogram to single top - quark production . </S>",
    "<S> we thus provide a unified framework for the fast numerical evaluation of total cross sections for top - quark production , which may be used for example in standard model fits . for future extensions towards nnlo accuracy , we include already all scale dependent terms at nnlo . </S>",
    "<S> we briefly describe how to use the program and provide all required tools to repeat the aforementioned analysis . </S>"
