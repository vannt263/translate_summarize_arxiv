    "<S> the following electromagnetism ( em ) inverse problem is addressed . </S>",
    "<S> it consists in estimating local radioelectric properties of materials recovering an object from the global em scattering measurement , at various incidences and wave frequencies . </S>",
    "<S> this large scale ill - posed inverse problem is explored by an intensive exploitation of an efficient 2d maxwell solver , distributed on high performance computing ( hpc ) machines . applied to a large training data set , </S>",
    "<S> a statistical analysis reduces the problem to a simpler probabilistic metamodel , on which bayesian inference can be performed . considering the radioelectric properties as a dynamic stochastic process , </S>",
    "<S> evolving in function of the frequency , it is shown how advanced markov chain monte carlo methods , called sequential monte carlo ( smc ) or interacting particles , can provide estimations of the em properties of each material , and their associated uncertainties . </S>"
