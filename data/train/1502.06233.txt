    "the quark model predicts the existence of heavy baryons composed of single , double and triple quarks .",
    "essential improvement has been achieved in heavy baryon spectroscopy in resent years .",
    "all baryons with a single charm quark that are predicted by the quark model have been observed in experiments .",

    "remarkable information about the internal structure of baryons can be gained by studying their magnetic moments .",
    "magnetic moments of the heavy baryons have long been under the focus of theoretical physicists , and they have been studied in framework of various approaches .",
    "so far , magnetic moments of charmed heavy baryons have been calculated in framework of the naive quark model @xcite , relativistic quark model @xcite , chiral perturbation model @xcite , hypercentral model @xcite , soliton model @xcite , skyrmion model @xcite , bag model @xcite , qcd sum rules method @xcite , nonrelativistic quark model @xcite , phenomenological relativistic model @xcite , quark model with confinement law potential @xcite , and chiral constituent quark model , respectively .",
    "magnetic moments of heavy hadrons have also been studied in numerous works within the qcd sum rules method .",
