    "furthermore , the presence of the @xmath0-wave correlations is encoded in helical andreev bound states characterized by the spin - momentum locking .",
    "we showed that the supercurrent carried by helical andreev bound states decreases in the presence of the potential barrier in the junction , but can not be completely suppressed due to the klein tunneling of cooper pairs through the barrier .",
    "such superconducting klein tunneling reveals itself in the non - sinusoidal current - phase relation and in the saturation of the critical current at high barrier strengths .",
    "this suggests that the p - wave superconductivity should be detectable in gated ti josephson junctions .",
