    "we established a connection to singular learning theory , and we showed that these volumes can be computed by evaluating the real log canonical threshold of the partial correlation hypersurfaces . throughout the paper",
    "we have made the simplifying assumption of equal noise , i.e.  @xmath24 .",

    "this issue was discussed briefly in example [ ex : specialcases ] .",
    "this paper can be seen as a first step towards developing a theory which would allow to compute the complete asymptotic expansion of particular volumes .",
    "we have concentrated on computing only the leading coefficients of these expansions , and even this question is still open in many cases ( e.g.  example 6.6 ) .",
