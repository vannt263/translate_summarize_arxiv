    "the nonlinear schrdinger ( nls ) equation , as one of the universal models that describe the evolution of slowly varying packets of quasi - monochromatic waves in weakly nonlinear dispersive media , plays an key role in nonlinear optics @xcite .",
    "recently , there are several experiments reported related to the modulational instability ( mi ) and the breather solution @xcite of the nls equation in nonlinear optics . the akhmediev breather ( periodic in space but localized in time )",
    "@xcite , the peregrine soliton or rogue wave ( rw ) solution ( time and space homoclinic ) @xcite and the kuznetsov - ma soliton ( periodic in time but localized in space ) @xcite have recently been experimentally observed in optical fibers dudley , kibler , kibler1 in succession . beside the experimental observation in optical fibers ,",
    "the rws have also been observed in water - wave tanks @xcite and plasmas @xcite .",
    "however , in the regime of ultra - short pulses where the width of optical pulse is in the order of femtosecond ( @xmath1 s ) , the quasi - monochromatic assumption to derive the nls equation is not valid anymore @xcite .",
    "description of ultra - short processes requires a modification of standard slow varying envelope models based on the nls equation .",
    "there are usually two ways to satisfy this requirement in the literature .",
    "the first one is to add several higher - order dispersive terms to yield higher - order nls equation @xcite .",
    "the second one is to construct a suitable fit to the frequency - dependent dielectric constant @xmath2 in the desired spectral range .",
    "several models have been proposed by the latter approach such as the short - pulse ( sp ) equation sch , sko , kim , amir and the complex short pulse ( csp ) equation @xcite .",
    "recently , schfer and wayne derived a short pulse ( sp ) equation @xcite @xmath3 to describe the propagation of ultra - short optical pulses in nonlinear media . here",
    ", @xmath4 is a real - valued function , representing the magnitude of the electric field .",
    "the sp equation has been shown to be completely integrable @xcite .",
    "the periodic and soliton solutions of the sp equation were found in @xcite .",
    "the connection between the sp equation and the sine - gordon equation through the reciprocal transformation was clarified , and then the @xmath0-soliton solutions including multi - loop and multi - breather ones were given in @xcite by using the hirota s bilinear method @xcite .",
    "the integrable discretization and the geometric interpretation of the sp equation were given in @xcite .",
    "most recently , one of the authors proposed a complex short pulse ( csp ) equation @xcite @xmath5 that governs the propagation of ultra short pulse packet along optical fibers .",
    "there are several advantages in using complex representation description of wave phenomenon , especially of the optical waves @xcite .",
    "firstly , amplitude and phase are two fundamental characteristics for a wave packet , the information of these two factors are nicely combined into a single complex - valued function .",
    "secondly , the use of complex representation can make a lot of manipulations including soliton interactions much easier .",
    "such advantages can be observed in many analytical results related to the nls equation , the complex short pulse equation and their coupled models . as is shown in @xcite , in contrast with the fact that one - soliton solution to the sp equation is always a loop soliton without physical meaning",
    ", the one - soliton solution to the csp equation is an envelope soliton with a few optical cycles .",
    "compared to the sp equation , few results are known to the csp equation .",
    "it is necessary to study the csp equation mathematically , as well as its applications in nonlinear optics .",
    "therefore , it is the aim of the present paper to investigate all kinds of solutions of the csp equation by darboux transformation .",
    "based on the previous study @xcite , it is known that the csp equation is linked to a complex coupled dispersionless ( ccd ) equation cd @xmath6through the following reciprocal ( hodograph ) transformation @xmath7 the ccd equation is the first negative flow of the landau - lifshitz hierarchywhile the sp and the csp equations being the first negative flow of wadati - konno - ichikawa ( wki ) hierarchy @xcite . by constructing a generalized darboux transformation to the ccd equation and integrating the integrals exactly involved in the reciprocal ( hodograph ) transformation , we are able to construct the general analytical solutions to the csp equation including the @xmath0-bright soliton , @xmath0-breather solution and higher order rogue wave solutions .",
    "it should be pointed out that the compact formulas for these solutions are more convenient for us to perform the asymptotic analysis .",
    "recently the modulational instability has been also considered as a wave breaking mechanism @xcite .",
    "indeed , if the initial steepness of the monochromatic wave is large , during the process of modulational instability , one wave will start growing and will soon reach the limiting steepness , and break before becoming a rogue wave .",
    "the nls theory does not predict the breaking or overturning of the waves onorato .",
    "different from previous research regarding the rogue wave solution to the nls equation , we find that there exists the wave breaking phenomenon in the rogue wave theory of the csp equation .",
    "these results could deepen our understanding about the mi mechanism @xcite .",

    "<S> in the present paper , we are concerned with the general localized solutions for the complex short pulse equation including soliton , breather and rogue wave solutions . with the aid of a generalized darboux transformation </S>",
    "<S> , we construct the @xmath0-bright soliton solution in a compact determinant form , then the @xmath0-breather solution including the akhmediev breather and a general higher order rogue wave solution . </S>",
    "<S> the first- and second - order rogue wave solutions are given explicitly and illustrated by graphs . </S>",
    "<S> the asymptotic analysis is performed rigourously for both the @xmath0-soliton and the @xmath0-breather solutions . </S>",
    "<S> all three forms of the localized solutions admit either smoothed- , cusped- or looped - type ones for the csp equation depending on the parameters . </S>",
    "<S> it is noted that , due to the reciprocal ( hodograph ) transformation , the rogue wave solution to the csp equation is different from the one to the nonlinear schrdinger ( nls ) equation , which could be a cusponed- or a looped one . </S>",
