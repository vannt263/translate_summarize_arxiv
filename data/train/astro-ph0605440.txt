    "conventionally we can distinguish two different kinds of helicity , kinetic helicity related to primordial plasma motions and magnetic helicity related to a primordial magnetic field ( brandenburg 2001 , christensson , hindmarsh , and brandenburg 2005 , verma & ayyer 2003 , boldyrev and cattaneo 2004 , subramanian 2004 ) .",
