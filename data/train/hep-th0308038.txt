    "<S> we give a mathematically rigorous construction of the moduli space and vacuum geometry of a class of quantum field theories which are @xmath0 supersymmetric wess - zumino models on a cylinder . </S>",
    "<S> these theories have been proven to exist in the sense of constructive quantum field theory , and they also satisfy the assumptions used by vafa and cecotti in their study of the geometry of ground states . since its inception , the vafa - cecotti theory of topological - antitopological fusion , or @xmath1 geometry , has proven to be a powerful tool for calculations of exact quantum string amplitudes </S>",
    "<S> . however , @xmath1 geometry postulates the existence of certain vector bundles and holomorphic sections built from the ground states . </S>",
    "<S> our purpose in the present article is to give a mathematical proof that this postulate is valid within the context of the two - dimensional @xmath0 supersymmetric wess - zumino models . </S>",
    "<S> we also give a simpler proof in the case of holomorphic quantum mechanics . </S>"
