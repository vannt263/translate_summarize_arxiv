    "mechanism design is an important branch of economics .",
    "compared with game theory , it concerns a reverse question : given some desirable outcomes , can we design a game that produces them ?",
    "nash implementation and bayesian implementation are two key topics of the mechanism design theory .",
    "the former assumes complete information among the agents , whereas the latter concerns incomplete information .",
