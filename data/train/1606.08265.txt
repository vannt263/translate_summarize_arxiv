    "<S> this work presents the differential charmonia production cross sections in high energy p+p collisions calculated using nrqcd formalism . </S>",
    "<S> the nrqcd formalism , factorizes the quarkonia production cross sections in terms of short distance qcd cross sections and long distance matrix elements ( ldmes ) . </S>",
    "<S> the short distance cross sections are calculated in terms of perturbative qcd and ldmes are obtained by fitting the experimental data . </S>",
    "<S> measured transverse momentum distributions of @xmath0 , @xmath1(2s ) and j/@xmath1 in p + @xmath2 collisions at @xmath3 1.8 , 1.96 tev and in p+p collisions at @xmath3 7 , 8 and 13 tev are used to constrain ldmes . the feed - down contribution to each state from the higher states </S>",
    "<S> are taken into account . </S>",
    "<S> the formalism provides a very good description of the data in a wide energy range . </S>",
    "<S> the values of ldmes are used to predict the charmonia cross sections in p+p collisions at 13 and 5 tev in kinematic bins relevant for the lhc detectors . </S>"
