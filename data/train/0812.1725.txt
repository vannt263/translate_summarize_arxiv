    "<S> we demonstrate that berry phases may greatly affect the dynamics of spin - orbit coupled bose - einstein condensates . the effective model hamiltonian under consideration is shown to be equivalent to the @xmath0 jahn - teller model first introduced in molecular physics . </S>",
    "<S> the corresponding conical intersection is identified and the berry phase acquired for a wave packet encircling the intersection studied . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that this phase manifests itself in the density profile of the condensate , making it a directly measurable quantity via time - of - flight detection . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , the non - abelian gauge structure of the system is addressed and we verify how it affects the dynamics . </S>"
