    "<S> we report preliminary results for 2d massive qed with two flavours of wilson fermions , using the hermitean variant of lscher s bosonization technique . </S>",
    "<S> the chiral condensate and meson masses are obtained . </S>",
    "<S> the simplicity of the model allows for high statistics simulations close to the chiral and continuum limit , both in the quenched approximation and with dynamical fermions . </S>"
