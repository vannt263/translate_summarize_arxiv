    "the search for scalar top quarks and the determination of their parameters in the framework of supersymmetric models are important aspects of the linear collider physics programme .",
    "a light scalar top could play an important role for baryogenesis and , if the mass difference to the lightest neutralino is small , for the evolution of the dark matter density through co - annihilation  @xcite .",
    "this scenario could be explored with precise measurements of the stop properties , in particular its mass , which would be possible at the ilc  @xcite .",
    "this study applies an iterative discriminant analysis ( ida )  @xcite to a scenario involving a 122.5  gev scalar top and a 107.2  gev neutralino at @xmath2  gev .",
    "the study complements the previous study for the same scenario at @xmath0  gev  @xcite .",
    "a luminosity of 500  fb@xmath1 is assumed .",
