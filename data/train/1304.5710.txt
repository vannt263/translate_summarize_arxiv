    "in this paper , we present a mathematical model of the mutation in the biological environment having 3 alleles .",
    "we have presented two types of mutations .",
    "we have shown that a mutation ( a mixing ) in the system can be considered as a convex combination of mendelian inheritances ( extreme or non - mixing systems ) .",
    "the first mutation is a convex combination of two li - yorke chaotic systems and the second mutation is a convex combination of li - yorke chaotic and regular systems .    in the first case ,",
    "the first mutation can be considered as an evolution process between two different chaotic biological systems . in this case , we have shown that there is one fluctuation point ( transition point ) in order to change one chaotic system into another chaotic system . in the second case ,",
    "the second mutation can be considered as an evolution process between chaotic and regular biological systems . in this case , we need two fluctuation points ( transition points ) in order to change the chaotic system into the regular system . we hope that we can find a similar phenomenon in nature .",
