    "<S> in the viewpoint of quasi normal modes , we describe a novel decoherence mechanism of charge qubit of josephson junctions ( jj ) in a lossy micro - cavity , which can appear in the realistic experiment for quantum computation based on jj qubit . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the nonlinear coupling of a charge qubit to quantum cavity field can result in an additional dissipation of resonant mode due to its effective interaction between those non - resonant modes and a resonant mode , which is induced by the charge qubit itself . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the characterized time of the novel decoherence by making use of the system plus bath method . </S>"
