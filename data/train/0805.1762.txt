    "<S> cut ideals are used in algebraic statistics to study statistical models defined by graphs . </S>",
    "<S> intuitively , topological restrictions on the graphs should imply structural statements about the corresponding cut ideals . </S>",
    "<S> several theorems and many computer calculations support that .    </S>",
    "<S> sturmfels and sullivant conjectured that the cut ideal is generated by quadrics if and only if the graph is free of @xmath0-minors . </S>",
    "<S> parts of the conjecture has been resolved by brennan and chen , and later by nagel and petrovi .    </S>",
    "<S> we prove the full conjecture by introducing a new type of toric fiber product theorem . </S>"
