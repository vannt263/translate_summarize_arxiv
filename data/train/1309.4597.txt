    "<S> we use a method based on hierarchical variational equations to derive asymptotic expansion of the solution with respect to the thickness of the thin layer . </S>",
    "<S> we determine the first two terms of the expansion and prove the error estimate made by truncating the expansion after a finite number of terms . </S>",
    "<S> next , using the first two terms of the asymptotic expansion , we show that we can model the effect of the thin layer by a problem with transmission conditions of order two .    </S>",
