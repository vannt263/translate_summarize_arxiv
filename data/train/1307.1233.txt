    "<S> we consider a non - relativistic quantum particle interacting with a singular potential supported by two parallel straight lines in the plane . </S>",
    "<S> we locate the essential spectrum under the hypothesis that the interaction asymptotically approaches a constant value and find conditions which guarantee either the existence of discrete eigenvalues or hardy - type inequalities . for a class of our models admitting a mirror symmetry , we also establish the existence of embedded eigenvalues and show that they turn into resonances after introducing a small perturbation . </S>"
