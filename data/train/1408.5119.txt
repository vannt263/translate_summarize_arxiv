    "<S> in this paper , we explore the merits of various algorithms for polynomial optimization problems , focusing on alternatives to sum of squares programming . while we refer to advantages and disadvantages of quantifier elimination , </S>",
    "<S> reformulation linear techniques , blossoming and groebner basis methods , our main focus is on algorithms defined by polya s theorem , bernstein s theorem and handelman s theorem . </S>",
    "<S> we first formulate polynomial optimization problems as verifying the feasibility of semi - algebraic sets . </S>",
    "<S> then , we discuss how polya s algorithm , bernstein s algorithm and handelman s algorithm reduce the intractable problem of feasibility of semi - algebraic sets to linear and/or semi - definite programming . </S>",
    "<S> we apply these algorithms to different problems in robust stability analysis and stability of nonlinear dynamical systems . as one contribution of this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we apply polya s algorithm to the problem of @xmath0 control of systems with parametric uncertainty . </S>",
    "<S> numerical examples are provided to compare the accuracy of these algorithms with other polynomial optimization algorithms in the literature .    </S>",
