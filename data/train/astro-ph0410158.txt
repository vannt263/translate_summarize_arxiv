    "be stars are broadly defined as non - supergiant ( luminosity class iii@xmath5v ) b - type stars that show or have shown balmer emission @xcite , although a few supergiant be stars are also known @xcite .",
    "their emission is due to a circumstellar disk that is often highly variable on both short and long time scales .",
    "the emission lines change intensity , and the relative intensity of their double - peaked profiles changes , over short time scales of days and even hours .",
    "in addition , the disks themselves have been observed to disappear and reappear over years or decades @xcite .",
    "be stars are well known to be a class of rapidly rotating stars @xcite , and there are three possible reasons for their rapid rotation .",
    "they may have been born as rapid rotators @xcite , spun up by binary mass transfer @xcite , or spun up during the main - sequence ( ms ) evolution of b stars @xcite .",
    "many observational surveys have searched for be stars in open clusters using multi - band photometry .",
    "@xcite used plots of @xmath6 vs. @xmath7 , @xmath6 vs. @xmath8 magnitude , and @xmath7 vs. @xmath8 to identify be stars in ngc 3766 .",
    "many recent surveys have relied on color - color diagrams , rather than shobbrook s color - magnitude diagrams , to detect be stars more efficiently .",
    "@xcite used a technique somewhat similar to ours (  3 ) to identify be stars in ngc 330 .",
    "they used strmgren @xmath9 , and a narrow - band h@xmath2 filter for their observations , and they plotted a color - color diagram for the cluster using @xmath10 vs h@xmath11 .",
    "they were able to verify almost all of their detections with low resolution spectra , demonstrating the accuracy of this technique .",
    "several other surveys ( @xcite ) have used variations of the color - color diagram technique to identify be stars in open clusters .",
    "@xcite emphasizes that the number of be stars detected in this type of surveys actually represents a lower limit on the true number of be stars for several reasons .",
    "most importantly , since the be state is not permanent , only those stars with current disk outbursts will be detectable as be stars .",
    "in addition , the number of late type be stars is likely to be incomplete since they are fainter , and they also tend to have weaker h@xmath2 emission than early be stars .    in this paper , we present an improved photometric technique based on @xmath12 color - magnitude and @xmath4 color - color diagrams to detect be stars in open clusters . while our method is very similar to that of @xcite ,",
    "we have enhanced it by using cluster isochrone fitting to identify the b - type stars in each cluster .",
    "we have also used a fit of the color - color curve to identify h@xmath2 emitters among the b star population . to demonstrate the accuracy of our technique , we present spectra of 20 stars in the cluster ngc 3766 and correlate their h@xmath2 equivalent widths with their @xmath13 colors .",

    "as figure [ colormag ] shows , our photometry of ngc 3766 does not cover the entire range of b star magnitudes .",
    "unless the observational program is designed to reach a - type stars , the total number of b , and perhaps be , stars will be underestimated in any search for be stars .",
