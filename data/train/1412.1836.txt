    "a stochastic background of primordial magnetic fields ( pmf ) generated prior to recombination can leave several footprints on the anisotropy pattern of the cosmic microwave background ( see e.g. ref .",
    "@xcite for a review ) .",
    "a stochastic background of pmf generates compensated scalar @xcite , vector and tensor perturbations @xcite whose contribution to the cosmic microwave background ( cmb ) anisotropies in temperature and polarization is not suppressed by the silk damping . the dominant vector contribution to temperature anisotropies from pmf at high multipoles which drives the current cmb constraints",
    "@xcite needs therefore to be disentangled from the foreground residuals and secondary anisotropies @xcite .",
    "the @xmath3 statistics of a stochastic background of pmf @xcite makes the contribution to cmb anisotropies fully non - gaussian .",
    "the cmb bispectrum was therefore targeted as a probe for pmf which is independent from the constraints based on the cmb power spectrum @xcite .",
    "subsequent works have been dedicated to refine the predictions for the cmb bispectrum for compensated and passive initial conditions @xcite and to compute the cmb trispectrum predictions @xcite .",
    "a stochastic background of pmf has also distinctive predictions for the cmb polarization pattern .",
    "vector perturbations sourced by pmf lead to a b - mode power spectrum with a broad maximum at high multipole as @xmath4",
    ". such spectrum is not degenerate with the one produced by tensor perturbations , either these were originated during inflation @xcite or passively sourced when neutrinos free stream after the stochastic background of pmf was generated @xcite .",
    "a stochastic background of pmf can also modify the cmb polarization pattern by the faraday effect with the characteristic frequency dependence @xmath5 @xcite .    in this paper we study in detail another interesting aspect of the interplay between pmf and cmb anisotropies in temperature and polarization .",
    "a stochastic background of pmf is characterized in general both by a symmetric and antisymmetric power spectrum and its _ helicity_. helicity measures the complexity of the topology of the magnetic field .",
    "being helicity a p and cp odd - function , its search in the cmb pattern is of primary importance for the understanding of the generation mechanism of pmf . as examples for generation mechanisms",
    ", helicity can be produced by a coupling to a primordial pseudo - scalar field @xcite and be affected by the presence of chiral anomaly in the early universe @xcite .",
    "the helical contribution in a stochastic background of pmf has also been subject of previous investigations @xcite .",
