    "high - precision measurements of the ground - state hyperfine splitting ( hfs ) were performed for various heavy h - like ions , including @xmath1bi , @xmath2ho , @xmath3re , @xmath4pb , @xmath5tl and @xmath6tl @xcite .",
    "progress in experiments motivated intensive theoretical calculations of the hyperfine splitting in highly charged heavy ions @xcite aiming to test quantum electrodynamics ( qed ) in strong electromagnetic fields .",
    "it was found that in heavy ions the qed effects are obscured by the uncertainty of the nuclear magnetization distribution correction ( bohr - weisskopf effect ) .",
    "however , simultaneous study of h- and li - like ions of the same isotope can help to overcome this problem , since the uncertainty of the bohr - weisskopf effect is significantly reduced in the specific difference of the corresponding hfs values @xcite .",
    "high - precision measurements of the hyperfine splitting of h- and li - like bismuth are feasible at the experimental storage ring ( esr ) and the hitrap facility in gsi @xcite .",
    "recently , after 13 years of attempts , the hfs of the ground state li - like bi has been directly observed in gsi @xcite .",
    "these measurements together with accurate theoretical calculations will provide the possibility for the stringent tests of qed in strong fields .",
    "the recent improvements of the theoretical accuracy for the specific difference of the hfs values are related to the calculations of the screened qed corrections @xcite and the two - photon exchange corrections @xcite to the hfs of the li - like ions . now",
    "the uncertainty of the specific hfs difference is mainly determined by the wichmann - kroll part of the screened qed corrections . in refs .",
    "@xcite the screened qed corrections were evaluated by introducing an effective local screening potential in the zeroth - order ( dirac ) equation .",
    "however , the screening potential approximation does not provide reliable estimation of the uncertainty .",
    "recently , the two - electron self - energy diagrams and a dominant part of the two - electron vacuum - polarization diagrams , which represent the leading contribution to this effect , have been evaluated within the systematic qed approach @xcite .",
    "the present paper is devoted to the rigorous evaluation of the two - electron vacuum - polarization diagrams , which have been treated approximately in refs .",
    "in particular , the electric - loop and magnetic - loop diagrams are evaluated to all orders in @xmath0 , including the wichmann - kroll contributions .",
    "the wichmann - kroll terms of the remaining internal - loop contributions are estimated .",
    "the numerical results are presented for the hyperfine structure of li - like bismuth @xmath7 .",
