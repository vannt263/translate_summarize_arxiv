    "<S> in a recent paper gunnells , scott and walden have determined the complete spectrum of the schreier graph on the symmetric group corresponding to the young subgroup @xmath0 and generated by initial reversals . </S>",
    "<S> in particular they find that the first nonzero eigenvalue , or spectral gap , of the laplacian is always 1 , and report that `` empirical evidence '' suggests that this also holds for the corresponding cayley graph . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a simple proof of this last assertion , based on the decomposition of the laplacian of cayley graphs , into a direct sum of irreducible representation matrices of the symmetric group . </S>"
