    "<S> this paper establishes expectation and variance asymptotics for statistics of the poisson  voronoi approximation of general sets , as the underlying intensity of the poisson point process tends to infinity . </S>",
    "<S> statistics of interest include volume , surface area , hausdorff measure , and the number of faces of lower - dimensional skeletons . </S>",
    "<S> we also consider the complexity of the so - called voronoi zone and the iterated voronoi approximation . </S>",
    "<S> our results are consequences of general limit theorems proved with an abstract steiner - type formula applicable in the setting of sums of stabilizing functionals .    </S>",
