    "<S> complex organic molecules ( coms ) are known to be abundant toward _ some _ low - mass young stellar objects ( ysos ) , but how these detections relate to typical com abundance are not yet understood . </S>",
    "<S> we aim to constrain the frequency distribution of coms during low - mass star formation , beginning with this pilot survey of com lines toward six embedded ysos using the iram 30 m telescope . </S>",
    "<S> the sample was selected from the spitzer @xmath0 ice sample and covers a range of ice abundances . </S>",
    "<S> we detect multiple coms , including ch@xmath1cn , toward two of the ysos , and tentatively toward a third . </S>",
    "<S> abundances with respect to ch@xmath1oh vary between 0.7 and 10% . </S>",
    "<S> this sample is combined with previous com observations and upper limits to obtain a frequency distributions of ch@xmath1cn , hcooch@xmath1 , ch@xmath1och@xmath1 and ch@xmath1cho . </S>",
    "<S> we find that for all molecules more than 50% of the sample have detections or upper limits of 110% with respect to ch@xmath1oh . </S>",
    "<S> moderate abundances of coms thus appear common during the early stages of low - mass star formation . </S>",
    "<S> a larger sample is required , however , to quantify the com distributions , as well as to constrain the origins of observed variations across the sample . </S>"
