    "light - matter interaction in the strong coupling regime lies at the root of numerous advances in quantum technologies and quantum information tasks  @xcite .",
    "recent experiments in solid state physics have reported an unprecedented coupling between a two - level system ( qubit ) and an optical / microwave cavity  @xcite , also known as the ultrastrong coupling ( usc ) regime  @xcite , where the coupling strength is comparable to the cavity and qubit frequencies .",
    "this regime has since been extensively studied in various theoretical contexts  @xcite . in the dipolar approximation ,",
    "the qubit - cavity system can be described by the quantum rabi model ( qrm )  @xcite .",
    "low - lying eigenstates of the qrm hold virtual excitations that can not escape from the cavity  @xcite , and it has been demonstrated that those eigenstates are useful for parity - protected quantum computing  @xcite and quantum memory application  @xcite .    apart from fundamental interests in light - matter interaction at the usc regime , the ultrastrong coupled qubit - cavity system or quantum rabi system ( qrs ) is also extensively studied for its potential to speed up quantum information processing at subnanosecond time scales  @xcite , particularly within the framework of circuit quantum electrodynamics ( qed )  @xcite . to be precise , refs .",
    "@xcite proposed the use of a tunable qubit - cavity coupling strength  @xcite to attain ultrafast two - qubit gates  @xcite .",
    "however , the main drawback in these proposals is the strong crosstalk among magnetic fluxes threading complicated flux qubit architectures . with our proposed framework",
    "presented here , current experimental crosstalk problems can be alleviated , while it preserves the fast gating performance . also , there exists a clear distinction between our proposal and cavity - mediated qubit - qubit interaction  @xcite .",
    "the latter shows an effective coupling @xmath0 with @xmath1 symmetry , which conserves the excitation number .",
    "in contrast , mediated coupling via the qrs in our proposal is an ising - type interaction @xmath2 that preserves the @xmath3 parity symmetry .",
    "this symmetry serves as a powerful controlling principle underlying state transitions in the system dynamics .    here",
    ", we propose a parity - preserving usc system that mediates effective two - body interactions , with following compelling characteristics that might have important implications in superconducting circuit - based quantum computing ( i - iii ) and solid - state physics ( iv ) communities .",
    "( i ) improved two - qubit gate performance with an increase in the qubit - cavity coupling strength of the qrs ( @xmath4 ) is demonstrated .",
    "( ii ) the entangling gate is performed by tuning only the qubits energy gap for fixed qrs parameters , without requiring complex flux qubit architectures  @xcite .",
    "( iii ) manipulation of the qubits energy gap does not change the underlying @xmath5 symmetry , with which generalization to a system , with @xmath6 qubits and an qrs , can easily be extended ; thereby intuitive physical insight follows .",
    "( iv ) enhanced excitation transfer between the two qubits with increase in @xmath7 is shown , while one of them is incoherently pumped .",
    "this physical insight might shed light on the cavity - enhanced exciton transport in disordered medium  @xcite , especially within the context of polyatomic molecules in the usc regime  @xcite .",
