    "<S> in this paper , we study the fractional poisson process ( fpp ) time - changed by an independent lvy subordinator and the inverse of the lvy subordinator , which we call tcfpp - i and tcfpp - ii , respectively . various distributional properties of these processes are established . </S>",
    "<S> we show that , under certain conditions , the tcfpp - i has the long - range dependence property and also its law of iterated logarithm is proved . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the tcfpp - ii is a renewal process and its waiting time distribution is identified . </S>",
    "<S> its bivariate distributions and also the governing difference - differential equation are derived . some specific examples for both the processes </S>",
    "<S> are discussed . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we present the simulations of the sample paths of these processes . </S>"
