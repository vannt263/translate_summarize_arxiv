    "<S> in 1853 sylvester introduced a _ family _ of double sum expressions for two finite sets of indeterminates and showed that some members of the family are essentially the polynomial subresultants of the monic polynomials associated with these sets . </S>",
    "<S> a question naturally arises : what are the _ other _ members of the family ? </S>",
    "<S> this paper provides a complete answer to this question . </S>",
    "<S> the technique that we developed to answer the question turns out to be general enough to characterise _ all _ members of the family , providing a uniform method .        </S>",
    "<S> subresultants , double sums , vandermonde determinants . </S>"
