    "<S> _ in this paper we described a novel state of rotating interacting condensates with unequal masses in the thomas - fermi regime . </S>",
    "<S> the possibility of locking the two individual vortex lattices a remarkable demonstration of the behavior of superfluids : the two gasses , rather than equilibrat to the same speed , prefer to move at different angular velocities inversely proportional to their masses . </S>",
    "<S> the vortex - locking of the different - mass condensates is also an example of synchronization : a phenomenon that is ubiquitous in physics , biology , and other fields @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> already in single - mass mixtures , a rich variety of vortex dynamics arises from the extra degrees - of - freedom , resulting in such effects as the formation of square vortex lattice , as well as topologically nontrivial defects such as skyrmions or hedgehogs @xcite . to investigate these effects in the different - mass mixtures , as well as to better establish the locked state we proposed in this manuscript </S>",
    "<S> , this system must be numerically investigated by solving the appropriate dynamical gross - pitaevskii equations . </S>",
    "<S> such numerical investigation will also allow one to find the preferred lattice geometry of the locked vortex - lattice . </S>",
