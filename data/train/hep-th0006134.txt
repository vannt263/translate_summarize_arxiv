    "<S> we study brane - world black holes from randall - sundrum(rs ) models in ( @xmath0)-dimensional anti - de sitter spacetimes . </S>",
    "<S> the solutions are directly obtained by using a slightly generalized rs metric ansatz in @xmath0 dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> the metric of the brane world can be described by the schwarzschild solution promoted to the black cigar solution in @xmath0 dimensions , which is compatible with the recently suggested black cigar solution in @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we show that the ricci flat condition for the brane can be easily derived from the effective gravity defined on the brane by using the rs dimensional reduction . </S>",
    "<S> especially , it is shown that in two dimensions the effective gravity on the brane is described by the polyakov action . </S>"
