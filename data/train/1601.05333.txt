    "vortices of two different core structures have been observed in superfluid -b @xcite .",
    "the vortex that is stable in the major part ( low pressure and temperature ) of the phase diagram was theoretically identified as the double - core vortex .",
    "the vortex that is stable in the high pressure and temperature corner of the superfluid phase diagram was identified as the a - phase - core vortex @xcite .",
    "the main experimental tool to study the vortices has been nuclear magnetic resonance ( nmr ) .",
    "two distinct nmr modes have been used . for small",

    "gets the brinkman - smith mode @xcite , where the texture is erased . at angles exceeding the leggett angle @xmath0 this",

    "several mechanisms contribute to the absorption in the brinkman - smith mode . in this paper",
    "we study theoretically one of these , the leggett - takagi relaxation @xcite . in the model developed by leggett and takagi ,",
    "the magnetization of superfluid is comprised of two parts , a superfluid ( cooper pair ) component and a normal ( quasiparticle ) component .",
    "as the dipole force acts only on the superfluid component , a nonequilibrium arises between the components .",
    "dissipation arises as the components relax towards their mutual equilibrium values . in vortices",
    "the major part of the relaxation arises from the region outside the vortex core .",
    "our calculation uses an analytic solution of the order parameter in this region .",
    "we compare our results with the experiment in ref .",
    "while there is a partial agreement , it seems that leggett - takagi relaxation alone is insufficient to explain the measurements .",
