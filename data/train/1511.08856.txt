    "<S> we demonstrate that ramsey spectroscopy can be used to observe rydberg - dressed interactions . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to many prior proposals , our scheme operates comfortably within experimentally measured lifetimes , and accesses a regime where quantum superpositions are crucial . </S>",
    "<S> we build a spin-1/2 from one level that is rydberg - dressed and another that is not . </S>",
    "<S> these levels may be hyperfine or long - lived electronic states . </S>",
    "<S> an ising spin model governs the ramsey dynamics , for which we derive an exact solution . due to the structure of rydberg interactions , </S>",
    "<S> the dynamics differs significantly from that in other spin systems . </S>",
    "<S> as one example , spin echo can _ increase _ the rate at which coherence decays . </S>",
    "<S> the results also apply to bare ( undressed ) rydberg states as a special case , for which we quantitatively reproduce recent ultrafast experiments without fitting .    </S>",
    "<S> pacs numbers : :    32.80ee , 67.85.-d , 37.10.jk    _ introduction _ - ultracold rydberg atoms allow one to process quantum information  @xcite , study interacting many - body systems  @xcite , and engineer nonlinear quantum optics  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these applications stem from the enormous van der waals interactions between rydberg atoms excited to large principal quantum number @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> since these interactions are proportional to @xmath1 , they are enhanced by many orders of magnitude compared to ground state atoms  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these interactions inhibit the simultaneous excitation of neighbouring atoms to rydberg states ; this is known as the  blockade effect \"  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> experiments have observed dramatic consequences of rydberg interactions , such as the formation of rydberg crystals  @xcite and suppressed excitation number fluctuations in the blockade region  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , observing quantum correlations remains an outstanding challenge . </S>",
    "<S> quantum correlations require superpositions , which in turn require non - commuting terms in the governing hamiltonian . </S>",
    "<S> obtaining such a situation is difficult with pure rydbergs , since their interaction energy often dominates the other scales .    </S>",
    "<S> consequently , in order to explore the many interesting applications where superpositions and quantum correlations are essential  @xcite , it is necessary to controllably reduce the interaction strength  for example , one may seek to make it comparable to the rabi drive in a blockade experiment or comparable to motional energy scales in a trap or lattice . </S>",
    "<S> dressed rydberg atoms , in which a small rydberg fraction is superposed with the ground state , enable this control while simultaneously extending the system lifetime by reducing spontaneous emission  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> an exciting recent breakthrough is the measurement of dressed rydberg interactions between two atoms  @xcite , but observing these interactions in a many - body system remains a major outstanding goal .    </S>",

    "<S> _ conclusion - _ although previous experiments on coherent many - body excitation with rydberg atoms have demonstrated strong correlations , they have not established their _ non - classicality_. ramsey spectroscopy directly creates and probes the superpositions necessary for quantum correlations . </S>",
    "<S> we have shown that this dynamics happens on experimentally favorable timescales . </S>",
    "<S> the exact analytic solutions available for the dynamics will allow for systematic comparison between experiment and theory , despite the strongly correlated dynamics . ( to emphasize the strong correlations , note that for @xmath51 , the dynamics is completely beyond mean - field theory , which predicts a time - independent spin - echo contrast ) . </S>",
    "<S> we showed , as a first step in this direction , that our theory quantitatively agrees with recent ultrafast measurements of bare rydberg atoms , a remarkable demonstration of the universality of the dynamics over six orders of magnitude , from @xmath17s to ps . </S>",
    "<S> we revealed that the contrast dynamics is sensitive to the shape of the interaction potential , as well as to density , principal quantum number , and dressing laser properties . </S>",
    "<S> striking dynamics emerges for @xmath113 : the contrast displays a non - perturbative short time non - analyticity , @xmath114 , due to the @xmath86 character of rydberg interactions . </S>",
    "<S> further interesting dynamical phenomena result from the unique nature of rydberg interactions . </S>",
    "<S> for example , the spin echo pulse enhances the rate of contrast dynamics for small excitation fraction @xmath8 or low densities .    in the future , </S>",
