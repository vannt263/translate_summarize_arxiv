    "attractive interactions between particles destabilize many - body systems against collapse or aggregation .",
    "examples include gravitational collapse of a star and nucleation of drops from supercooled water vapor .",
    "owing to this kind of instability , a dilute bose gas with attractive interactions can not form a stable bose ",
    "einstein condensate ( bec ) in an infinite homogeneous system  @xcite . in a finite system , by contrast , quantum pressure arising from zero - point energies can sustain an attractive force and a metastable bec can form  @xcite .",
    "such a metastable bec with attractive interactions was first realized with trapped @xmath0 atoms  @xcite .",
    "a metastable bec becomes unstable against collapse when the number of atoms or the scattering length exceeds a critical value  @xcite . as a result ,",
    "the number of @xmath0 atoms in the bec was limited  @xcite . when atoms are continuously replenished in the bec , repeated collapse and growth occur  @xcite .",
    "the collapse can be investigated in a controlled manner by tuning the interatomic interaction via a feshbach resonance .",
    "the collapsing dynamics of an @xmath1 bec has been studied using this technique .",
    "the interaction energy is converted into kinetic energy during the collapse , and atomic bursts and jets are produced  @xcite .",
    "a feshbach resonance has also been used to study the amplification of local instabilities  @xcite .",
    "the dynamics of collapsing and exploding becs have been theoretically investigated by many researchers  @xcite .",
    "a trapped metastable bec with an attractive interaction can collapse by macroscopic quantum tunneling .",
    "the tunneling rate can be estimated from an overlap integral  @xcite or a path integral over the semiclassical trajectory  @xcite .",
    "however , the dynamics of a bec collapsing by quantum tunneling have not been studied .",
    "the mean - field approximation , widely used to study becs , can not be applied in this situation , because it neglects many - body quantum fluctuations and the metastable state never collapses if the gross  pitaevskii equation is used .    in the present paper , direct quantum many - body simulations",
    "are preformed to investigate the dynamics of an attractive bec in a metastable state , and collective quantum many - body collapse by quantum tunneling is demonstrated .",
    "owing to the limited computational resource , the system is restricted to a few dozen atoms .",
    "the tunneling decay rate is determined for a metastable bec , and its dependence on the scattering length and on the number of atoms is obtained .",
    "it is shown that the quantum state develops into a superposition between metastable and collapsing states .",
    "the paper is organized as follows .",
