    "<S> we use rice formulae in order to compute the moments of some level functionals which are linked to problems in oceanography and optics : the number of specular points in one and two dimensions , the distribution of the normal angle of level curves and the number of dislocations in random wavefronts . </S>",
    "<S> we compute expectations and , in some cases , also second moments of such functionals . </S>",
    "<S> moments of order greater than one are more involved , but one needs them whenever one wants to perform statistical inference on some parameters in the model or to test the model itself . in some cases , we are able to use these computations to obtain a central limit theorem .    , </S>"
