    "for a typical construction problem of quasi - fuchsian subsurfaces in a closed hyperbolic @xmath0-manifold , such as addressed in theorem [ main - qfsurface ] , one may generally follow two steps : first , decide a suitable finite collection of ( oriented ) nearly geodesic pairs of pants whose cuff lengths are nearly equal ; secondly , glue these pairs of pants up along boundary in a well controlled fashion to output a connected quasi - fuchsian subsurface .",
