    "<S> we present a number of new , exact scalar field cosmologies where the potential consists of two or more exponential terms . </S>",
    "<S> such potentials are motivated by supergravity or superstring models formulated in higher dimensional spacetimes that have been compactified to ( 3 + 1)-dimensions .    we have found solutions in both curved and flat robertson walker spacetimes . </S>",
    "<S> these models have a diverse range of properties and often possess several distinct phases , with a smooth transition between ordinary and inflationary expansion . </S>",
    "<S> while exponential potentials typically produce powerlaw inflation , we find models where the inflationary period contains eras of both powerlaw and exponential growth . </S>"
