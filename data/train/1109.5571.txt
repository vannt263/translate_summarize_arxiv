    "<S> the interacting atom - molecule bec ( ambec ) dynamics is investigated in the mean field approach . </S>",
    "<S> the presence of atom - atom , atom - molecule and molecule - molecule interactions , coupled with a characteristically different interaction representing atom - molecule interconversion , endows this system with nonlinearities , which differ significantly from the standard gross - pitaevskii ( gp ) equation . </S>",
    "<S> exact localized solutions are found to belong to two distinct classes . </S>",
    "<S> the first ones are analogous to the soliton solutions of the weakly coupled gp equation , whereas the second non - equivalent class is related to the solitons of the strongly coupled bec . </S>",
    "<S> distinct parameter domains characterize these solitons , some of which are analogous to the complex profile bloch solitons in magnetic systems . </S>",
    "<S> these localized solutions are found to represent a variety of phenomena , which include co - existence of both atom - molecule complex and miscible - immiscible phases . </S>",
    "<S> numerical stability is explicitly checked , as also the stability analysis based on the study of quantum fluctuations around our solutions . </S>",
    "<S> we also find out the domain of modulation instability in this system . </S>"
