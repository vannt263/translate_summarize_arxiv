    "<S> we prove convergence results for variants of smirnov s fermionic observable in the critical planar ising model in presence of free boundary conditions . </S>",
    "<S> one application of our analysis is a simple proof of a theorem by hongler and kytl on convergence of critical ising interfaces with plus - minus - free boundary conditions to dipolar sle(3 ) , and a generalization of this result to an arbitrary number of arcs carrying plus , minus or free boundary conditions . </S>",
    "<S> another application is a computation of scaling limits of crossing probabilities in the critical fk - ising model with arbitrary number of alternating wired / free boundary arcs . </S>",
    "<S> we also deduce a new crossing formula for the spin ising model .    </S>",
