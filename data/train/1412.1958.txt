    "the purpose of this paper is twofold : first of all , it is of general interest to calculate various geometric quantities of the pd solution in physically well - motivated coordinates . and secondly , the setting of the pd solution may endow the more general , formal similarities between gravitation and electrodynamics with an explicit physical understanding .",
