    "<S> this is a sequel of the article by borichev  golinskii  </S>",
    "<S> kupin @xcite where the authors obtain blaschke - type conditions for special classes of analytic functions in the unit disk which satisfy certain growth hypotheses . </S>",
    "<S> these results were applied to get lieb  thirring inequalities for complex compact perturbations of a selfadjoint operator with a simply connected resolvent set .    </S>",
    "<S> the first result of the present paper is an appropriate local version of the blaschke - type condition from @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we apply it to obtain a similar condition for an analytic function in a finitely connected domain of a special type . </S>",
    "<S> such condition is by and large the same as a lieb  thirring type inequality for complex compact perturbations of a selfadjoint operator with a finite - band spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> a particular case of this result is the lieb  thirring inequality for a selfadjoint perturbation of the schatten class of a periodic ( or a finite - band ) jacobi matrix . </S>",
    "<S> the latter result seems to be new in such generality even in this framework . </S>"
