    "the recombination distance is the distance between an electron and a hole at which they recombine .",
    "if the intrinsic recombination rate at the recombination distance is infinite , an electron and a hole always recombine , once they come within the recombination distance . however , the intrinsic recombination rate at the recombination distance is in general finite .",
    "therefore , even if an electron and a hole come within the recombination distance , some fraction of the pair will escape recombination and separate away again .",
