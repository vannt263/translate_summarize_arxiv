    "scattering observables of three - nucleon ( @xmath0 ) systems , proton - deuteron ( @xmath1 ) scattering and neutron - deuteron ( @xmath2 ) scattering , are good sources of information about unknown aspects of the nuclear forces such as off - shell differences in nucleon - nucleon force ( 2nf ) models , possible evidence of @xmath0 forces ( 3nfs ) , etc . because of technical advantage of treating charged particles as beam , target , or detected particles over doing neutral ones ,",
    "available data sets of the @xmath1 reaction are richer both in quantity and quality than those of the @xmath2 reaction . on the other hand , due to a mathematical difficulty in treating three - body systems with long - range coulomb interactions ,",
    "a precise calculation of the @xmath1 scattering , especially for energies above the three - body breakup threshold ( tbt ) , is one of the most challenging subjects in physics of few - body systems .    in the last decade",
    ", some developments have been made in this problem by calculations based on the kohn variational principle @xcite and on the momentum space faddeev equations @xcite using the screening and renormalization method @xcite .    in this paper , we will present another approach to the @xmath1 scattering problem , which is based on integral equations for wave functions in coordinate space .",
    "calculations by this approach for non - coulombnic @xmath0 systems with realistic 2nfs and 3nfs were performed for @xmath3h in refs .",
    "@xcite , and for the @xmath2 scattering at energies above the tbt in refs .",
    "@xcite . a direct application of the faddeev equation to coulombnic @xmath0 systems , namely the @xmath3he bound state and the @xmath1 scattering ,",
    "is known to bring a severe singularity to the integral kernel due to the long - range character of the proton - proton ( @xmath4 ) coulomb force . in ref .",
    "@xcite , sasakawa and sawada proposed a modification of the faddeev equation to treat the singularity by introducing auxiliary potentials that act between charged spectator particles and the center of mass ( c.m . ) of the rest pair particles .",
    "since we use an iterative method to solve the integral equations , in which one needs to operate the integral kernel on known functions repeatedly , it is essential to establish an accurate kernel operation for precise calculations . in the integral kernel of this coulomb - modified faddeev equation , which will be referred to as the ssf equation , the singularity due to the @xmath4 coulomb potential is expected to be canceled by the auxiliary potentials , on conditions that three particles are bound or no three - body breakup channel is open .",
    "solutions of the ssf equation were successfully obtained for the @xmath3he bound state in refs .",
    "@xcite and for the @xmath1 scattering at energies _ below _ the tbt in refs .",
    "however , the cancellation is not trivially expected when a three - body breakup channel opens . in this paper",
    ", we will treat this problem , and show how our approach practically is applicable for @xmath1 scattering _ above _ the tbt .",

    "<S> we present a practical method to solve the proton - deuteron scattering problem at energies above the three - body breakup threshold , in which we treat three - body integral equations in coordinate space accommodating long - range proton - proton coulomb interactions . </S>",
    "<S> the method is examined for phase shift parameters , and then applied to calculations of differential cross sections in elastic and breakup reactions , analyzing powers , etc . with a realistic nucleon - nucleon force and three - nucleon forces . </S>",
    "<S> effects of the coulomb force and the three - nucleon forces on these observables are discussed in comparing with experimental data . </S>"
