    "<S> models in which the real or virtual photon interacts with a parton of an exchanged pion are able to describe the data . </S>",
    "<S> next - to - leading order perturbative qcd calculations based on pion exchange are found to be in good agreement with the measured cross sections . </S>",
    "<S> the fraction of leading neutron dijet events with respect to all dijet events is also determined . </S>",
