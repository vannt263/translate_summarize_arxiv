    "<S> in the spirit of the thin - layer quantization approach , we give the formula of the geometric influences of a particle confined to curved surface . </S>",
    "<S> the geometric contributions are defined by the reduced commutation relations of normal directive and its post functions of normal variable . according to the formula </S>",
    "<S> , we obtain geometric potential , geometric momentum , geometric orbital angular momentum , geometric linear rashba and cubic dresselhaus spin - orbit couplings . as an example </S>",
    "<S> , a truncated cone surface is investigated . </S>",
    "<S> we find interesting results that the geometric orbital angular momentum can provide an azimuthal polarization for spin , the sign of the geometric dresselhaus spin - orbit coupling can be controlled by the inclination angle of generatrix .    </S>",
