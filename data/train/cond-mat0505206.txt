    "<S> we extend the existing quasiclassical theory for the superconducting proximity effect in a chaotic quantum dot , to include a time - reversal - symmetry breaking magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> random - matrix theory ( rmt ) breaks down once the ehrenfest time @xmath0 becomes longer than the mean time @xmath1 between andreev reflections . as a consequence </S>",
    "<S> , the critical field at which the excitation gap closes drops below the rmt prediction as @xmath2 is increased . </S>",
    "<S> our quasiclassical results are supported by comparison with a fully quantum mechanical simulation of a stroboscopic model ( the andreev kicked rotator ) . </S>"
