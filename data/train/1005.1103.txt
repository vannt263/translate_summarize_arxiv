    "<S> we theoretically investigate the robustness of cluster states in charge qubit system based on quantum dot ( qd ) and double quantum point contact ( dqpc ) . </S>",
    "<S> trap state is modeled by an island structure in dqpc and represents a dynamical fluctuation . </S>",
    "<S> we found that the dynamical fluctuations affect the cluster states more than static fluctuation caused by qd size fluctuation . </S>"
