    "<S> we show that a formal power series in @xmath0 non - commuting indeterminates is a positive non - commutative kernel if and only if the kernel on @xmath1-tuples of matrices of any size obtained from this series by matrix substitution is positive . </S>",
    "<S> we present two versions of this result related to different classes of matrix substitutions . in the general case </S>",
    "<S> we consider substitutions of jointly nilpotent @xmath1-tuples of matrices , and thus the question of convergence does not arise . in the `` convergent '' case </S>",
    "<S> we consider substitutions of @xmath1-tuples of matrices from a neighborhood of zero where the series converges . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , in the first case the result can be improved : the positivity of a non - commutative kernel is guaranteed by the positivity of its values on the diagonal , i.e. , on pairs of coinciding jointly nilpotent @xmath1-tuples of matrices . </S>",
    "<S> in particular this yields an analogue of a recent result of helton on non - commutative sums - of - squares representations for the class of hereditary non - commutative polynomials . </S>",
    "<S> we show by an example that the improved formulation does not apply in the `` convergent '' case . </S>"
