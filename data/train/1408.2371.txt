    "direct photons and dileptons are clean probes to study the fundamental properties of a hot and dense medium created in ultra - relativistic heavy - ion collisions . due to minimal interactions with the strongly interacting medium",
    ", they can bring the information of the whole time evolution and dynamics of the medium .",

    ", some key parameters of the qgp matter such as initial temperature can be obtained  @xcite .",
    "there are two methods for direct photon measurement .",
    "one is the real photon method in which one measures all inclusive photons and then subtracts the hadron decayed photons from them .",
    "the other one is the virtual photon method in which one measures the virtual photon by their associate dielectron pairs and then deduces the direct photon from the relationship between virtual photon and direct photon .",
    "the latter is used in this article .",
    "the dielectron measurement in au+au collisions at 200 gev at star from year 2010 data has been published recently  @xcite .",
