    "<S> we study rational solutions of the boussinesq equation , which is a soliton equation solvable by the inverse scattering method . </S>",
    "<S> these rational solutions , which are algebraically decaying and depend on two arbitrary parameters , are expressed in terms of special polynomials that are derived through a bilinear equation , have a similar appearance to rogue - wave solutions of the focusing nonlinear  ( nls ) equation and have an interesting structure . </S>",
    "<S> further rational solutions of the  i ( kpi ) equation are derived in two ways , from rational solutions of the nls equation and from rational solutions of the boussinesq equation . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the two families of rational solutions of the kpi equation are fundamentally different . </S>"
