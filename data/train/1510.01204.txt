    "<S> integro - differential methods , currently exploited in calculus , provide an inexhaustible source of tools to be applied to a wide class of problems , involving the theory of special functions and other subjects . </S>",
    "<S> the use of integral transforms of the borel type and the associated formalism is shown to be a very effective mean , constituting a solid bridge between umbral and operational methods . </S>",
    "<S> we merge these different points of view to obtain new and efficient analytical techniques for the derivation of integrals of special functions and the summation of associated generating functions as well . </S>"
