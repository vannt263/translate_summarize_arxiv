    "<S> when the temperature falls below a critical value , we find a second - order superfluid phase transition with mean - field critical exponents . in the symmetry - broken phase , </S>",
    "<S> we determine the speed of second sound as a function of temperature . </S>",
    "<S> as the velocity of the superfluid component relative to the normal component increases , the superfluid transition goes through a tricritical point and becomes first - order . </S>"
