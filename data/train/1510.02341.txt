    "main - sequence and subgiant stars of spectral type a to f in detached and semi - detached eclipsing binaries are very interesting targets for asteroseismic studies , i.e. , the determination of the boundaries of instability strip by interpreting the observed oscillation characteristics .",
    "indeed , eclipsing binaries containing a - f stars offer the possibility of determining accurate masses and radii for the components .",
    "unique advantage is if the binary resides in a cluster , determination of the fundamental properties and , in particular for systems belonging to the open clusters , can be used to address relevant astrophysical issues , such as chemical compositions , age and the distance of two stars with accurate masses and radii , allowing a much more discriminating test of physical ingredients of theoretical models .",
