    "cosmological observations of distant ia supernovae discovered that our universe is undergoing an accelerated expansion period @xcite , which is supported by other observations of the cosmic microwave background anisotropies and the large scale structure of galaxies @xcite .",
    "these observations are explained by the cosmological model with the cosmological constant @xmath1 .",
    "the cosmological constant can be regarded as the vacuum energy , however , the smallness of the observed value raises a fine - tuning problem @xcite . to explain the cosmic accelerated expansion ,",
    "many dark energy models have been proposed ( see e.g. , @xcite and the references therein ) .",
    "modification of the gravity theory is an alternative approach , for example , @xmath0 model @xcite and the dvali , gabadadze , and porrati ( dgp ) model in the context of the braneworld scenario @xcite .",
    "many authors have studied dynamical dark energy models @xcite .",
    "dynamical dark energy models may have similar expansion rates to models of modified gravity , because modification of the gravity theory may affect the background expansion history .",
    "therefore , the observations of the background expansion history alone are unable to distinguish between modified gravity and dynamical dark energy .",
    "the key to distinguish between modified gravity and dynamical dark energy is the growth of cosmological perturbations @xcite .",
    "the growth history of cosmological perturbations can be tested with the large scale structure in the universe .",
    "many projects of large survey of galaxies are in progress or planned @xcite , and these surveys might give us a hint in exploring the origin of the accelerated expansion of the universe and the nature of gravity @xcite .",
    "cosmological perturbations in modified gravity models have been investigated by many authors @xcite . in the present paper ,",
