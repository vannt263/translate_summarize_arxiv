    "<S> bobkov ( j.  theoret .  probab .  * </S>",
    "<S> 18*(2 ) ( 2005 ) 399412 ) investigated an approximate de  finetti representation for probability measures , on product measurable spaces , which are symmetric under permutations of coordinates . </S>",
    "<S> one of the main results of that paper was an explicit approximation bound for permanents of complex rectangular matrices , which was shown by a somewhat complicated induction argument . in this paper , we indicate how to avoid the induction argument using an ( asymptotic ) expansion . </S>",
    "<S> our approach makes it possible to give new explicit higher order approximation bounds for such permanents and in turn for the probability measures mentioned above . </S>"
