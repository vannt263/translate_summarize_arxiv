    "does a scalar higgs boson necessarily exist as required in the standard model ?",
    "higgsless models@xcite provide a negative answer to this question , achieving the electroweak symmetry breaking ( ewsb ) without invoking an elementary higgs particle@xcite .",
    "the unitarity of longitudinally polarized @xmath7 and @xmath8 boson scattering is effectively maintained beyond the tev energy scale by the exchange of an infinite tower of massive spin-1 particles@xcite , rather than a spinless higgs boson .",
    "these massive spin-1 particles may be provided as kaluza - klein ( kk ) modes ( @xmath4 , @xmath9 , @xmath10 @xmath11 , @xmath12 , @xmath13 , @xmath14 @xmath11 ) of five dimensional gauge fields compactified on a tev scale interval with appropriate boundary conditions@xcite .",
    "deconstruction@xcite of extra dimensions enables us to construct four dimensional gauge invariant phenomenological models which approximate the five dimensional higgsless theories .",
    "the deconstructed higgsless models@xcite ( or linear moose@xcite models ) can be used as tools to compute the low energy properties of higgsless theories below the cutoff scale .",
    "higgsless models are often regarded as `` dual''@xcite to models of dynamical ewsb@xcite such as `` walking technicolor''@xcite .",
    "the deconstructed version of the higgsless model possesses extended electroweak gauge symmetries , which can be thought as analogues of hidden local symmetries ( hls)@xcite and the vector limit symmetry@xcite in the dynamical chiral symmetry breaking of qcd .",
    "the gauge sector of the higgsless model therefore has some similarities to the bess models@xcite .",
    "any phenomenologically viable ewsb model should satisfy constraints from the precision electroweak parameters@xcite .",
    "an analysis in a very general class of linear moose model shows that a higgsless model with brane localized fermion can not satisfy simultaneously both unitarity bounds and precision electroweak constraints at tree level.@xcite delocalizing fermions within the extra dimension may reduce significantly the sizes of electroweak corrections.@xcite in the deconstruction language the delocalizing fermions may be achieved by allowing fermions to derive electroweak properties from more than one site over the deconstruction lattice.@xcite it has been shown that , for an arbitrary higgsless model with `` ideally '' delocalized fermions@xcite , three @xmath15 of the leading zero - momentum precision electroweak parameters defined by barbieri _",
    "et al._@xcite vanish at tree level .",
    "moreover , the ideal delocalization implies fermiophobic @xmath4 .",
    "presently existing direct @xmath4 searches can not be applied for such a fermiophobic @xmath4 boson .",
    "the strongest constraints on the @xmath4 and @xmath12 masses in the higgsless models then come from the limits on deviations in multi - gauge - boson vertices.@xcite    recently , a three site higgsless model@xcite has been proposed .",
