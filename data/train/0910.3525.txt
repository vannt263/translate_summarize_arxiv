    "<S> a measured solenoid is a laminated space endowed with a tranversal measure invariant by holonomy . </S>",
    "<S> a measured solenoid immersed in a smooth manifold produces a closed current ( known as generalized ruelle - sullivan current ) . </S>",
    "<S> uniquely ergodic solenoids are those for which there is a unique ( up to scalars ) transversal measure . </S>",
    "<S> it is known that for any smooth manifold , any real homology class is represented by a uniquely ergodic solenoid . in this paper , we prove that the currents associated to uniquely ergodic solenoids are dense in the space of closed currents , therefore proving the abundance of such objects . </S>"
