    "<S> we consider the nonlinear schrdinger equation with a point - like source term . </S>",
    "<S> the soliton interaction with such a singular potential yields a critical solution behavior . that is , for the given value of the potential strength and the soliton amplitude </S>",
    "<S> , there exists a critical velocity of the initial soliton solution , around which the solution is either trapped by or transmitted through the potential . in this paper , we propose an efficient method for finding such a critical velocity by using the generalized polynomial chaos method . </S>",
    "<S> for the proposed method , we assume that the soliton velocity is a random variable and expand the solution in the random space using the orthogonal polynomials . </S>",
    "<S> the proposed method finds the critical velocity accurately with spectral convergence . </S>",
    "<S> thus the computational complexity is much reduced . </S>",
    "<S> numerical results for the smaller and higher values of the potential strength confirm the spectral convergence of the proposed method .    </S>",
    "<S> nonlinear schrdinger equation , singular potential , generalized polynomial chaos , stochastic collocation method , split step fourier method , spectral convergence </S>"
