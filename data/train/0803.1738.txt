    "<S> we use bayesian model selection tools to forecast the planck satellite s ability to distinguish between different models for the reionization history of the universe , using the large angular scale signal in the cosmic microwave background polarization spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> we find that planck is not expected to be able to distinguish between an instantaneous reionization model and a two - parameter smooth reionization model , except for extreme values of the additional reionization parameter . </S>",
    "<S> if it can not , then it will be unable to distinguish between different two - parameter models either . </S>",
    "<S> however , bayesian model averaging will be needed to obtain unbiased estimates of the optical depth to reionization . </S>",
    "<S> we also generalize our results to a hypothetical future cosmic variance limited microwave anisotropy survey , where the outlook is more optimistic .    </S>",
