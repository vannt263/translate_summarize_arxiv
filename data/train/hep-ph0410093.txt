    "<S> the new formalism for description of ( un)polarized semi - inclusive dis at moderate energies is developed . </S>",
    "<S> hadron production is modeled as a product of distribution functions and hadronization functions ( hfs ) weighted by the hard scattering cross sections , as it is done in the lepto event generator . </S>",
    "<S> the correct treatment of polarization effects shows that description of semi - inclusive dis within this formalism includes a new nonperturbative input  _ polarized _ hfs . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that this approach does not correspond to the commonly adopted one with the independent quark fragmentation . </S>",
    "<S> the purity method used by the hermes collaboration mixes up the two approaches and ignores the contributions from polarized hfs . </S>",
    "<S> this method can not be considered a precise tool for the extraction of polarized quark distributions from measured sidis asymmetries . </S>"
