    "<S> we consider the cauchy problem for nonlinear schrdinger equations in the presence of a smooth , possibly unbounded , potential . </S>",
    "<S> no assumption is made on the sign of the potential . </S>",
    "<S> if the potential grows at most linearly at infinity , we construct solutions in sobolev spaces ( without weight ) , locally in time . under some natural assumptions , we prove that the @xmath0-solutions are global in time . on the other hand , </S>",
    "<S> if the potential has a super - linear growth , then the sobolev regularity of positive order is lost instantly , not matter how large it is , unless the initial datum decays sufficiently fast at infinity . </S>"
