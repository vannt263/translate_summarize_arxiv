    "<S> density dependent parametrization models of the nucleon - meson effective couplings , including the isovector scalar @xmath0-field , are applied to asymmetric nuclear matter . </S>",
    "<S> the nuclear equation of state and the neutron star properties are studied in an effective lagrangian density approach , using the relativistic mean field hadron theory . </S>",
    "<S> it is known that the introduction of a @xmath0-meson in the constant coupling scheme leads to an increase of the symmetry energy at high density and so to larger neutron star masses , in a pure nucleon - lepton scheme . </S>",
    "<S> we use here a more microscopic density dependent model of the nucleon - meson couplings to study the properties of neutron star matter and to re - examine the @xmath0-field effects in asymmetric nuclear matter . </S>",
    "<S> our calculations show that , due to the increase of the effective @xmath0 coupling at high density , with density dependent couplings the neutron star masses in fact can be even reduced . </S>"
