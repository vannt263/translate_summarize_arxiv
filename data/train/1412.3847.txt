    "<S> since the advent of quantum mechanics different approaches to find analytical solutions of the schrdinger equation have been successfully developed . </S>",
    "<S> here we follow and generalize the approach pioneered by natanzon and others by which the schrdinger equations can be transformed into another well - known equation for transcendental function ( e.g. , the hypergeometric equation ) . </S>",
    "<S> this sets a class of potentials for which this transformation is possible . </S>",
    "<S> our generalization consists in finding potentials allowing the transformation of the schrdinger equation into a triconfluent heun equation . </S>",
    "<S> we find the energy eigenvalues of this class of potentials , the eigenfunction and the exact superpartners . </S>"
