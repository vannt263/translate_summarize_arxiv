    "<S> using the continuous limit approximation in the dynamical system we study a nonlinear partial differential equation which corresponds to the generalization of both the fermi - pasta - ulam and the frenkel - kontorova models . </S>",
    "<S> this generalized model can be considered as a model for the description nonlinear dislocation waves in the crystal lattice . </S>",
    "<S> we obtain the nonlinear partial differential equation for the description of dislocations . taking into account the wave moving in one direction </S>",
    "<S> we obtain another nonlinear evolution equation . using the painlev test </S>",
    "<S> we analyze the integrability of this equation . </S>",
    "<S> we find that there exist an integrable case of the partial differential equation for nonlinear dislocations . </S>",
    "<S> the lax pair for the solution of the cauchy problem is found . </S>",
    "<S> the solution of the cauchy problem for nonlinear evolution equation is discussed . </S>",
    "<S> one and the two - soliton solutions for the nonlinear evolution equation are presented . </S>",
    "<S> the influence of parameters on the propagation of one and the two - soliton solutions is analyzed and demonstrated . </S>"
