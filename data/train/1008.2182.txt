    "recent cosmological observations obtained by snia @xcite , wmap @xcite , sdss @xcite and x - ray @xcite indicate that the observable universe experiences an accelerated expansion .",
    "although the simplest way to explain this behavior is the consideration of a cosmological constant @xcite , the known fine - tuning problem @xcite led to the dark energy paradigm .",
    "the dynamical nature of dark energy , at least in an effective level , can originate from a variable cosmological `` constant '' @xcite , or from various fields , such is a canonical scalar field ( quintessence ) @xcite , a phantom field , that is a scalar field with a negative sign of the kinetic term @xcite , or the combination of quintessence and phantom in a unified model named quintom @xcite .",
    "finally , an interesting attempt to probe the nature of dark energy according to some basic quantum gravitational principles is the holographic dark energy paradigm @xcite ( although the recent developments in horava gravity could offer a dark energy candidate with perhaps better quantum gravitational foundations @xcite ) .",
    "the advantage of phantom cosmology , either in its simple or in its quintom extension , is that it can describe the phantom state of the universe , that is when the dark energy equation - of - state parameter lies below the phantom divide @xmath4 , as it might be the case according to observations @xcite .",
    "additionally , a usual consequence of phantom cosmology in its basic form , is the future big rip @xcite or similar singularities @xcite , and thus one needs additional non - conventional mechanism if he desires to avoid such a possibility @xcite",
    ".    on the other hand power - law cosmology , where the scale factor is a power of the cosmological time , proves to be a very good phenomenological description of the universe evolution , since according to the value of the exponent it can describe the radiation epoch , the dark matter epoch , and the accelerating , dark energy epoch @xcite .",
    "although it is tightly constrained by nucleosynthesis @xcite , considering late universe , it is found to be consistent with age of high - redshift objects such as globular clusters @xcite , with the snia data @xcite , and with x - ray gas mass fraction measurements of galaxy clusters @xcite .",
    "furthermore , in the context of the power - law model , one can describe the gravitational lensing statistics @xcite , the angular size - redshift data of compact radio sources @xcite , and the snia magnitude - redshift relation @xcite .    in this work we desire to impose observational constraints on phantom power - law cosmology , that is on the scenario of a phantom scalar field along with the matter fluid",
