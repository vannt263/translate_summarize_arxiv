    "<S> baryonic correlation functions provide an ideal tool to study parity doubling and chiral symmetry using lattice simulations . </S>",
    "<S> we present a study using @xmath0 flavors of anisotropic wilson clover fermions on the fastsum ensembles and find clear evidence that parity doubling emerges in the quark - gluon plasma . </S>",
    "<S> this result is confirmed on the level of spectral functions , which are obtained using a mem reconstruction . </S>",
    "<S> we further highlight the importance of gaussian smearing in this study . </S>"
