    "the astronomical observations show that our universe is probably an asymptotically de sitter ( ds ) one @xcite .",
    "it raises the interests on ds gauge theories of gravity .",
    "there is a model of ds gravity , which was first proposed in the 1970 s @xcite .",
    "the ds gravity can be stimulated from ds invariant special relativity @xcite and the principle of localization  the full symmetry of the special relativity as well as the laws of dynamics are both localized @xcite  and realized in terms of the ds connection on a kind of totally umbilical submanifolds ( under the ds - lorentz gauge ) and yang - mills type action @xcite .",
    "it has been shown that all vacuum solutions of einstein field equations with a cosmological constant are the vacuum solutions of the set of field equations without torsion @xcite .",
    "in particular , schwarzschild - ds and kerr - ds metrics are two solutions . on the other hand",
    ", it can also been shown that the vacuum solutions of the set of field equations without torsion must be the vacuum solutions of einstein field equations with the same cosmological constant @xcite .",
    "therefore , one may expect that the ds gravity may pass all solar - system - scale observations and experimental tests for general relativity ( gr ) .",
    "it has also been shown @xcite that the ds gravity may explain the accelerating expansion and supply a natural transit from decelerating expansion to accelerating expansion without the help of the introduction of matter fields in addition to dust .",
    "the present paper aims at finding the ds spacetimes with torsion in the ds gravity and studying their properties .",
    "the @xmath2 de sitter spacetime with constant torsion @xcite and the static de sitter spacetime with spherical torsion which satisfy the double duality ansatz @xcite have been presented for some gauge theories of gravity .",
    "other de sitter solutions with nonvanishing torsion are also given for other theories @xcite .",
    "but , all of the theories are different from the ds gravity .",
    "we present the de sitter spacetimes with homogenous and isotropic torsion for spatial curvature @xmath3 and static de sitter spacetime with static , @xmath0-symmetric , vector torsion .",
    "the formers are the only vacuum solutions in the ds gravity for the empty , homogenous , isotropic , constant - curvature - scalar universe .",
    "the latter is the only spherically symmetric , vacuum solution in the ds gravity for a large class of spacetimes .",
