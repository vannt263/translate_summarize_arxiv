    "born - infeld electrodynamics was born as a non - linear extension of maxwell s equations able to render finite the self - energy of the point - like charge @xcite-@xcite .",
    "after decades of relative oblivion , born - infeld theory regained a prominent place in theoretical physics because of its role in the low energy dynamics of strings and branes @xcite-@xcite .",
    "born - infeld theory is distinguished as the only extension of maxwell s theory having causal propagation @xcite and absence of birefringence @xcite .",
    "while its free plane waves do not differ from maxwell s ones , the born - infeld non - linearity provides interactions among plane waves @xcite or between plane waves and static fields @xcite that substantially change the physics of propagation .",
    "born - infeld electrodynamics possesses a magnitude @xmath0 with units of field that rules the scale of field at which maxwell s theory is recovered ( in the same way that @xmath1 rules the newtonian limit of relativistic mechanics ) . in this paper",
    "we will continue the program to obtain born - infeld electrostatic solutions in the euclidean plane .",
    "this program began very early with the articles by pryce @xcite , who used the complex analysis to establish the main features of the electrostatic configurations for isolated point - like charges . in sections 2 and 3",
    "we will present the complex method to generate 2-dimensional electrostatic solutions in a renewed and cleaner way .",
    "recently , the multipolar configurations were worked out @xcite ; these solutions displayed some physically undesirable features that will be healed in sections 5 and 6 .",
    "particular features of the dipole field are examined in sections 7 - 9 , together with general complex expressions for born - infeld electrostatic forces and energies .",
    "section 10 describes the solution for two separated equal but opposite charges .",
    "it is shown that the attractive force reaches a maximum value at a non - null distance , and then it decreases up to vanish when the charges meet together",
