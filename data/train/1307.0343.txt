    "<S> branching processes are classical growth models in cell kinetics . in their construction </S>",
    "<S> , it is usually assumed that cell lifetimes are independent random variables , which has been proved false in experiments . </S>",
    "<S> models of dependent lifetimes are considered here , in particular bifurcating markov chains . under hypotheses of stationarity and multiplicative ergodicity , </S>",
    "<S> the corresponding branching process is proved to have the same type of asymptotics as its classic counterpart in the i.i.d . </S>",
    "<S> supercritical case : the cell population grows exponentially , the growth rate being related to the exponent of multiplicative ergodicity , in a similar way as to the laplace transform of lifetimes in the i.i.d . case . an identifiable model for which the multiplicative ergodicity coefficients and the growth rate can be explicitly computed is proposed . </S>"
