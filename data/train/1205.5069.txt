    "despite the lack of a consensus on the pairing mechanism in the fe - based superconductors on the theoretical side ,  @xcite experimentally , there is accumulating evidence pointing to strong coupling between magnetism and superconductivity .",
    "@xcite it is found that superconductivity occurs in close vicinity to the magnetic instability in many systems .",
    "@xcite upon chemical doping or application of pressure , the long - range antiferromagnetic order in the parent compound is suppressed , followed by the appearance of superconductivity .  @xcite this proximity of antiferromagnetism and superconductivity is similar to that observed in the cuprate superconductors,@xcite suggesting that these correlations might be connected in a similar way .",
