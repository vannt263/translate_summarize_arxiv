    "<S> it is found that the amphoteric refraction , i.e. the refraction can be either positive or negative depending on the incident angles , could occur at a planar interface associated with a uniaxially anisotropic positive index media ( pim ) or an anisotropic negative index media ( nim ) . </S>",
    "<S> particularly , the anomalous negative refraction can occur at a planar interface from an isotropic pim to an anisotropic pim , whereas the anomalous positive refraction occurs at the interface from an isotropic pim to an anisotropic nim . </S>",
    "<S> the optimal conditions to yield the two unusual refractions are obtained . </S>",
    "<S> the difference of the two types of amphoteric refraction is discussed . </S>"
