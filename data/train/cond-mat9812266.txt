    "<S> based on a microscopic density functional theory we calculate the internal structure of the three - phase contact line between liquid , vapor , and a confining wall as well as the morphology of liquid wetting films on a substrate exhibiting a chemical step . </S>",
    "<S> we present a refined numerical analysis of the nonlocal density functional which describes the interface morphologies and the corresponding line tensions . </S>",
    "<S> these results are compared with those predicted by a more simple phenomenological interface displacement model . except for the case that the interface exhibits large curvatures , we find that the interface displacement model provides a quantitatively reliable description of the interfacial structures . </S>"
