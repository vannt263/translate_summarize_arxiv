    "<S> design of space - time block codes ( stbcs ) for maximum likelihood ( ml ) reception has been predominantly the main focus of researchers . </S>",
    "<S> however , the ml decoding complexity of stbcs becomes prohibitive large as the number of transmit and receive antennas increase . </S>",
    "<S> hence it is natural to resort to a suboptimal reception technique like linear minimum mean squared error ( mmse ) receiver . </S>",
    "<S> barbarossa _ </S>",
    "<S> et al _ and liu _ et al _ have independently derived necessary and sufficient conditions for a full rate linear stbc to be mmse optimal , i.e achieve least symbol error rate ( ser ) . </S>",
    "<S> motivated by this problem , certain existing high rate stbc constructions from crossed product algebras are identified to be mmse optimal . </S>",
    "<S> also , it is shown that a certain class of codes from cyclic division algebras which are special cases of crossed product algebras are mmse optimal . </S>",
    "<S> hence , these stbcs achieve least ser when mmse reception is employed and are fully diverse when ml reception is employed .    crossed product algebra , division algebra , space - time codes , mmse receiver </S>"
