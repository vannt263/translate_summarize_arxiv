    "<S> recent theoretical works have demonstrated the realization of fractional quantum anomalous hall states ( also called fractional chern insulators ) in topological flat band lattice models without an external magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> such newly proposed lattice systems play a vital role to obtain a large class of fractional topological phases . here </S>",
    "<S> we report the exact numerical studies of edge excitations for such systems in a disk geometry loaded with hard - core bosons , which will serve as a more viable experimental probe for such topologically ordered states . </S>",
    "<S> we find convincing numerical evidence of a series of edge excitations characterized by the chiral luttinger liquid theory for the bosonic fractional chern insulators in both the honeycomb disk haldane model and the kagom - lattice disk model . </S>",
    "<S> we further verify these current - carrying chiral edge states by inserting a central flux to test their compressibility .    _ </S>",
    "<S> introduction. _ one of the most essential and fascinating topics in condensed matter physics is to explore and classify the various states of matter , among which the integer quantum hall effect ( iqhe ) @xcite and the fractional quantum hall effect ( fqhe ) @xcite have long been the major focus . </S>",
    "<S> it is well known that , at fractional fillings , the fqhe will emerge when interacting particles move in landau levels ( lls ) caused by an external uniform magnetic field . on the other hand , great interest has been aroused to realize both the iqhe and fqhe in lattice models in the absence of an external magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> an initial theoretical attempt was made by haldane @xcite , who proposed a prototype lattice model to achieve the iqhe by introducing two non - trivial topological bands with chern numbers @xmath0  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> haldane s model demonstrates that the iqhe can also be attained without lls , i.e. defines the quantum anomalous hall ( qah ) states . </S>",
    "<S> the lattice version of the fqhe without lls , however , comes much latter because of its intriguing strongly correlated nature .    </S>",
    "<S> the recent proposal of topological flat bands ( tfbs ) fulfils the basic ingredients to explore the intriguing fractionalization phenomenon without lls . </S>",
    "<S> tfbs  @xcite belong to a class of band structures with at least one nearly flat band with non - zero chern number . </S>",
    "<S> this may be viewed as the lattice counterpart of the continuum lls . </S>",
    "<S> several systematic numerical works have been done to explore the correlation phenomenon within tfb models , and abelian @xcite and non - abelian @xcite fqhe states without lls have already been well established numerically . this intriguing fractionalization effect in tfbs without an external magnetic field , defines a new class of fractional topological phases , i.e. fractional quantum anomalous hall ( fqah ) states , now also known as fractional chern insulators ( fcis ) . some new approaches , </S>",
    "<S> e.g. the wannier - basis model wave functions and pseudo - potentials  @xcite , the projected density operator algebra  @xcite , the parton wave - function constructions @xcite , and also the adiabatic continuity paths  @xcite , have been proposed to further understand these fci / fqah states in tfbs . </S>",
    "<S> there are various other proposals of tfb models and material realization schemes @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> very recent systematic numerical studies have also found exotic fci / fqah states in tfbs with higher chern numbers ( @xmath1 ) which do not have the direct continuum analogy in lls  @xcite , and also the hierarchy fci / fqah states  @xcite .     rotational symmetry . </S>",

    "<S> although previous studies have firmly established various properties of a large class of fci / fqah phases , we are here concerned with the less studied edge excitations since they in principle should open another window to reveal the bulk topological order  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> edge excitations might also provide a more viable experimental probe especially considering about the possible future realizations of fci / fqah phases in optical lattice systems  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> some very promising and detailed experimental schemes have been proposed for realizing the tfb models and the fci / fqah phases , e.g. implementing dipolar spin systems with ultracold polar molecules trapped in a deep optical lattice driven by spatially modulated electromagnetic fields  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the chiral edge modes of such topological phases are expected to be directly visualized in optical - lattice - based experiments  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> a recent work has studied edge excitations of the bosonic fqhe in lls generated by an artificial uniform flux in optical lattices  @xcite , where finite - size disks and cylinders with trap potentials have been considered , and the edge spectrum is clearly observed . also , related knowledge of fqhe edge states also exists in the study of orbital entanglement spectra  @xcite . in the present work , </S>",

    "<S> to further check the compressibility of these edge states , we insert a central flux into the disk system and indeed verify these current - carrying chiral edge states upon tuning the flux strength .    _ </S>",
