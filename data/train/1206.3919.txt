    "<S> we consider the amplitude modulation of low - frequency , long wavelength electrostatic drift wave packets in a nonuniform magnetoplasma with the effects of equilibrium density , electron temperature and magnetic field inhomogeneities . </S>",
    "<S> the dynamics of the modulated drift wave packet is governed by a nonlinear schrdinger equation . </S>",
    "<S> the latter is used to study the modulational instability of a stoke s wave train to a small longitudinal perturbation . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the drift wave packet is stable ( unstable ) against the modulation when the drift wave number lies in @xmath0 @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> thus , the modulated drift wave packet can propagate in the form of bright and dark envelope solitons or as a drift wave rogon .    </S>",
    "<S> drift wave , modulational instability , rogue wave , magnetoplasma    a nonuniform magnetoplasma supports a great variety of low - frequency electrostatic and electromagnetic drift - type modes . </S>",
    "<S> examples include the electrostatic drift waves @xcite and coupled drift - alfvn waves @xcite , which play a crucial role in cross - field plasma particle transports @xcite , and the formation of coherent structures in space @xcite and laboratory @xcite plasmas that are magnetized . </S>",
    "<S> both the drift - alfvn waves can be excited by free energy sources that are stored in the equilibrium pressure gradient and in magnetic field inhomogeneity . </S>",
    "<S> nonthermal drift waves attain large amplitudes and start interacting among themselves . in the past , tasso @xcite and orevskii _ et al . _ </S>",
    "<S> @xcite considered nonlinear interactions between one - dimensonal drift waves propagating in a direction orthogonal to the density and temperature gradients and a uniform magnetic field in an electron - ion plasma . </S>",
    "<S> they reported the formation of non - envelope drift solitary pulses that were used in the study of drift wave turbulence comprising an ensemble of drift wave solitons @xcite in magnetized plasmas .    </S>",
    "<S> hasegawa and mima @xcite incorporated the vector nonlinearity associated with the nonlinear ion polarization drift in the study of nonlinearly interacting pseudo - three - dimensional drift waves in a nonuniform plasma without the electron temperature and magnetic field inhomogeities . </S>",
    "<S> the hasegawa - mima equation , which similar to the charney equation @xcite governing the dynamics of the rossby waves in the atmosphere , admits a larichev - rezhnik type - dipolar vortex @xcite as one of the possible stationary states of the drift wave turbulence . </S>",
    "<S> the mode couplings between finite amplitude drift waves can also generate convective cells and zonal flows @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the latter provide a better plasma confinement , since they act as a barrier for inhibiting the transport of the plasma particles across the external magnetic field direction . </S>",
