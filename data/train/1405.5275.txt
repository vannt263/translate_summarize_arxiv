    "in noncentrosymmetric magnetic crystals , chiral asymmetry of the exchange coupling that arises due to the crystallographic handedness leads to the formation of long - period modulations of the magnetization with a fixed rotation sense @xcite .",
    "phenomenologically , these _ chiral _ dzyaloshinskii - moriya ( dm ) interactions are described by energy contributions linear in the first spatial derivatives of the magnetization @xcite @xmath0 where @xmath1 is a unit vector along the magnetization @xmath2 , @xmath3 and @xmath4 .",
    "theoretical analysis shows that invariants of type ( [ form ] ) stabilize different types of _ homochiral _ modulations that propagate in one direction ( _ helices _ ) and two directions ( _ skyrmion lattices _ ) over a broad range of thermodynamical parameters @xcite . during the last four decades",
    ", homochiral long - period modulations have been reported in many of noncentrosymmetric ferro- and antiferromagnets , although only in a form of simple helical waves ( see bibliography in ref .  ) . in bulk chiral magnets complex multidimensional chiral modulations including specific skyrmionic textures",
    "are reported to exist only in close vicinity to the neel temperatures of mnsi and other cubic helimagnets as so called _ precursor states _ ( e.g. , ref .   and bibliography in ref .  ) .",
    "the synthesis of nanolayers of cubic helimagnets @xcite and ultra - thin films of common magnetic metals with induced dm interactions @xcite radically changed the situation . during last few years a large variety of skyrmion - lattice states and nontrivial helical modulations have been discovered in free - standing films and cubic helimagnetic epilayers @xcite and fe nanolayers @xcite .",
    "a sufficiently strong magnetocrystalline anisotropy or / and an applied magnetic field will destroy chiral modulated states and establish homogeneously magnetized phases as thermodynamically stable states @xcite .",
    "in such `` saturated '' phases , the dm interactions stabilize two- and three - dimensional localized states ( _ chiral _ skyrmions ) @xcite .",
    "solutions for axisymmetric 2d chiral skyrmions have been investigated in a number of papers @xcite , numerical solutions for 3d isolated chiral skyrmions ( also known as _ hopfions _ ) are derived in ref .  .",
    "isolated chiral 2d skyrmions have been recently observed in cubic helimagnet films @xcite and fepd nanolayers @xcite .",
    "these axisymmetric solitonic states of nanoscale size have attracted a lot of attention both in fundamental nonlinear physics and as promising objects for different spintronic applications @xcite .",
    "it was found recently that the solutions for skyrmions and helicoids in thin layers of cubic helimagnets are inhomogeneous along the film thickness : their structure can be thought as a superposition of common in - plane modulations and specific _ twisted _ modulations propagating in the perpendicular direction @xcite .",
    "these twisted distortions drastically change the energetics of skyrmion and helicoid phases and stabilize them in a broad range of applied magnetic fields .",
    "experimental investigations of mnsi / si(111 ) epilayers in strong in - plane magnetic fields have led to the discovery of twisted chiral modulations in a form of localized surface states @xcite . these observations and supporting theoretical analysis show that the surface twists persist at arbitrarily high fields and prevent a total saturation of the magnetization .",
    "evidence for this type of surface modulation can also be found in calculations of chiral modulations in confined cubic and uniaxial helimagnets @xcite .    in this paper",
    "we present experimental and theoretical investigations of surface modulation instabilities ( twisted states ) that arise in mnsi / si(111 ) epilayers .",
    "we show that the occurrence of such surface chiral twists is a general phenomenon that exists in both bulk and confined noncentrosymmetric magnets .",
    "we develop a consistent theory of surface twists and investigate their influence on modulated phases in chiral magnets .",
