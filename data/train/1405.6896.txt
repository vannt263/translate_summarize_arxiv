    "both types of transitions produce the same y - shaped conductance profile of two peaks that merge and stick together for a range of parameter values  distinct from the x - shaped profile that happens without a change in @xmath120 .",
    "there is a variety of methods to distinguish the pole transition from the topological phase transition @xcite : since @xmath126 for @xmath127 , one way to suppress the pole transitions is to couple the metal to the superconductor via a small number of modes @xmath15 with a small transmission probability @xmath103 .",
    "the pole transitions are a sample - specific effect , while the topological phase transition is expected to be less sensitive to microscopic details of the disorder .",
