    "<S> correlated scattering occurs naturally in frequency - selective fading channels and its impact on the performance needs to be understood . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we answer the question whether the uncorrelated scattering model leads to an optimistic or pessimistic estimation of the actual average capacity . in the paper </S>",
    "<S> , we use majorization for functions to show that the average rate with perfectly informed receiver is largest for uncorrelated scattering if the transmitter is uninformed . </S>",
    "<S> if the transmitter knows the channel statistics , it can exploit this knowledge . </S>",
    "<S> we show that for small snr , the behavior is opposite , uncorrelated scattering leads to a lower bound on the average capacity . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we provide an example of the theoretical results for an attenuated ornstein - uhlenbeck process including illustrations . </S>"
