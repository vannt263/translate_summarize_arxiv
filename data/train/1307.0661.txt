    "he used classical taylor series expansion for this purpose . for stiff problems , methods of orders up to four",
    "were constructed in @xcite .",

    "<S> exponential runge  kutta methods constitute efficient integrators for semilinear stiff problems . </S>",
    "<S> so far , however , explicit exponential runge  kutta methods are available in the literature up to order  4 only . </S>",
    "<S> the aim of this paper is to construct a fifth - order method . for this purpose , we make use of a novel approach to derive the stiff order conditions for high - order exponential methods . </S>",
    "<S> this allows us to obtain the conditions for a method of order  5 in an elegant way . after stating the conditions , </S>",
    "<S> we first show that there does not exist an explicit exponential runge  </S>",
    "<S> kutta method of order  5 with less than or equal to  6 stages . </S>",
    "<S> then , we construct a fifth - order method with 8 stages and prove its convergence for semilinear parabolic problems . </S>",
    "<S> finally , a numerical example is given that illustrates our convergence bound .    </S>",
    "<S> exponential integrators , exponential runge  kutta methods , stiff order conditions , error bounds , semilinear parabolic problems , stiff problems </S>"
