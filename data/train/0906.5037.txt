    "in the kohn - sham time - independent density functional theory ( dft ) the noninteracting kinetic energy is treated as an exact functional of the occupied orbitals  @xcite and only the exchange - correlation ( xc ) energy @xmath0 has to be approximated .",
    "the `` jacob s ladder '' classification @xcite of the ground - state density - functional approximations for @xmath0 has three complete non - empirical rungs : the local - spin - density approximation ( lsda )  @xcite , the generalized gradient approximation ( gga )  @xcite , and the meta - gga  @xcite .",
    "higher rungs of the ladder require new ingredients in order to satisfy more exact constraints @xcite .",
    "thus , the meta - gga has as ingredients the spin densities @xmath1 and @xmath2 , their gradients @xmath3 and @xmath4 , and the kohn - sham ( ks ) noninteracting kinetic energy densities @xmath5 and @xmath6 . the local and semilocal density functionals ( lsda , gga , and meta - gga ) give accurate predictions of ground - state for atoms , molecules , solids  @xcite , and surfaces  @xcite .",
    "they also work for atomic monolayers  @xcite and other quasi - two - dimensional ( quasi-2d ) systems  @xcite , but they fail badly as the true 2d limit is approached  @xcite .",
    "the failure of the semilocal density functionals to describe the dimensional crossover of the exact xc functional can be avoided by using nonlocal models @xcite such as the weighted density approximation @xcite , or higher rungs of the `` jacob s ladder '' @xcite .",
    "thus , the fourth - rung hyper - gga , a nonlocal correlation functional compatible with exact exchange @xcite , improves considerably the behaviour of semilocal functionals over the whole thickness range of the quasi-2d electron gas @xcite .",
    "the numerically expensive fifth - rung approximations such as the inhomogeneous singwi - tosi - land - sjlander method ( istls ) @xcite and the gw approximation @xcite are remarkably accurate for the description of quasi-2d systems @xcite .",
    "the quasi-2d electron gas is experimentally realizable in silicon metal - oxide - semiconductor field - effect transistor ( mosfet ) and in the widely used semiconductor heterojunctions @xcite .",
    "other physical systems with strong 2d character are the copper - oxide planes of high - temperature superconductors and the electrons bound to the surface of liquid helium @xcite .",

    "<S> commonly used semilocal density functional approximations for the exchange - correlation energy fail badly when the true two dimensional limit is approached . </S>",
    "<S> we show , using a quasi - two - dimensional uniform electron gas in the infinite barrier model , that the semilocal level can correctly recover the exchange - correlation energy of the two - dimensional uniform electron gas . </S>",
    "<S> we derive new exact constraints at the semilocal level for the dimensional crossover of the exchange - correlation energy and we propose a method to incorporate them in any exchange - correlation density functional approximation . </S>"
