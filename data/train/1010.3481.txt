    "<S> we consider 2-state quantum walks ( qws ) on the line , which are defined by two matrices . </S>",
    "<S> one of the matrices operates the walk at only half - time . in the usual qws starting from the origin </S>",
    "<S> , localization does not occur at all . </S>",
    "<S> however , our walk can be localized around the origin . in this paper , we present two limit theorems , that is , one is a stationary distribution and the other is a convergence theorem in distribution .     </S>",
