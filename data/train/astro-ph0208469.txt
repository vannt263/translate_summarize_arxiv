    "cataclysmic variables ( cvs ) are close binary systems consisting of a white dwarf and a red - dwarf secondary transferring matter via roche - lobe overflow .",
    "dwarf novae are a class of cvs showing outbursts , which are believed to be a result of instabilities in the accretion disk [ see @xcite for a review ] .",
    "depending on orbital inclination , some dwarf novae show a various degree of eclipses .",
    "eclipses in such dwarf novae provide a powerful tool in studying the time - variation of the structure of accretion disks ( e.g. @xcite ; @xcite ) .",
