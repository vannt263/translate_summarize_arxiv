    "recent experimental studies @xcite of the dynamics of electrons and holes in graphene ( a single atomic layer of graphite @xcite ) have revealed unusual behaviour related to the dirac nature of its quasiparticles .",
    "two dimensional graphene has a honeycomb lattice structure with two atoms per unit cell .",
    "its band structure consists of two inequivalent pairs of cones with apex at the brillouin zone corners . for zero chemical potential",
    "the lower energy cones are completely filled and the upper empty . in a graphene device",
    "an applied gate voltage can be used to introduce electrons in the upper band or , by voltage reversal , holes in the lower band ( cones ) .",
    "the quasiparticles in graphene obey dirac @xcite rather than the schrdinger equation and this has profound implications for their dynamics .",
    "the unconvetional quantum hall effect was expected theoretically @xcite and recently observed @xcite to have half ( divided by spin and valley degeneracy ) rather than integer filling factors .",
