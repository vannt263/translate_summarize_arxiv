    "however most explicit solutions of the einstein - maxwell system that have been found do not satisfy this property .",
    "there have been some attempts made recently to find exact analytic solutions of the einstein- maxwell system with a linear equation of state .",
    "these include the treatments of ivanov @xcite , sharma and maharaj @xcite , and thirukkanesh and maharaj @xcite .",

    "<S> we provide new exact solutions to the einstein - maxwell system of equations which are physically reasonable . </S>",
    "<S> the spacetime is static and spherically symmetric with a charged matter distribution . </S>",
    "<S> we utilise an equation of state which is quadratic relating the radial pressure to the energy density . </S>",
    "<S> earlier models , with linear and quadratic equations of state , are shown to be contained in our general class of solutions . </S>",
    "<S> the new solutions to the einstein - maxwell are found in terms of elementary functions . </S>",
    "<S> a physical analysis of the matter and electromagnetic variables indicates that the model is well behaved and regular . </S>",
    "<S> in particular there is no singularity in the proper charge density at the stellar centre unlike earlier anisotropic models in the presence of the electromagnetic field . </S>"
