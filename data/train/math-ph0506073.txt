    "since then it has been established and developed by jacobi , abel , gauss and others , and it now becomes fundamental and considerable subjects in both mathematics and physics .",

    "in his thesis  @xcite , zwegers investigated the mock theta functions by use of a real analytic modular form with half - integral weight ( see also ref .  ) .",
