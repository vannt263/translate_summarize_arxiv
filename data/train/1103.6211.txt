    "<S> we study a diffuse interface model for the flow of two viscous incompressible newtonian fluids in a bounded domain . </S>",
    "<S> the fluids are assumed to be macroscopically immiscible , but a partial mixing in a small interfacial region is assumed in the model . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , diffusion of both components is taken into account . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to previous works , we study a model for the general case that the fluids have different densities due to lowengrub and truskinovski  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to an inhomogeneous navier - stokes system coupled to a cahn - hilliard system , where the density of the mixture depends on the concentration , the velocity field is no longer divergence free , and the pressure enters the equation for the chemical potential . </S>",
    "<S> we prove existence of unique strong solutions for the non - stationary system for sufficiently small times .    </S>",
