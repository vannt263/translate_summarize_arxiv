    "<S> we have developed a new method for evaluating the specific heat of lattice spin systems . </S>",
    "<S> it is based on the knowledge of high - temperature series expansions , the total entropy of the system and the low - temperature expected behavior of the specific heat as well as the ground - state energy . by the choice of an appropriate variable ( entropy as a function of energy ) </S>",
    "<S> , a stable interpolation scheme between low and high temperature is performed . </S>",
    "<S> contrary to previous methods , the constraint that the total entropy is @xmath0 for a spin @xmath1 on each site is automatically satisfied . </S>",
    "<S> we present some applications to quantum spin models on one- and two- dimensional lattices . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , in most cases , a good accuracy is obtained down to zero temperature . </S>"
