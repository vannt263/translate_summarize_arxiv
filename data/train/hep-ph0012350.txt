    "neutrino telescopes have the potential for detecting distant sources of high energy neutrinos , for example , from active galactic nuclei and gamma ray bursters @xcite .",
    "upward - going muons from muon neutrino conversions to muons via charged current interactions with nuclei are the main signal of muon neutrinos @xcite .",
