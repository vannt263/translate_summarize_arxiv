    "in many extensions of the standard model ( sm ) new particles are predicted which carry both lepton and baryon number - leptoquarks ( lq ) .",
    "lqs might have masses as low as the electroweak scale .",
    "therefore they have been directly searched for in high energy collider experiments . in the brw model",
    "@xcite lqs are assumed to respect @xmath0 symmetry .",
    "several isospin multiplets can be constructed with fermion number @xmath1 or @xmath2 depending on their couplings to matter and antimatter , and with spin 0 ( scalar ) or spin 1 ( vector ) .",
    "the coupling between lqs and fermions is given by the yukawa coupling @xmath3 . in the most general model couplings of lqs to different fermion generations can mix and therefore would lead to fcnc and lfv processes . in low energy experiments",

    "constraints are obtained from the proton lifetime , rare decays ( e.g. neutrinoless double beta decay ) , atomic parity violation and search for fcnc processes .",
