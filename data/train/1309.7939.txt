    "<S> we study the dynamics of self - trapping in bose - einstein condensates ( becs ) loaded in deep optical lattices with gaussian initial conditions , when the dynamics is well described by the discrete nonlinear schrdinger equation ( dnls ) . in the literature an approximate dynamical phase diagram based on a variational approach was introduced to distinguish different dynamical regimes : diffusion , self - trapping and moving breathers . </S>",
    "<S> however , we find that the actual dnls dynamics shows a completely different diagram than the variational prediction . </S>",
    "<S> we numerically calculate a detailed dynamical phase diagram accurately describing the different dynamical regimes . </S>",
    "<S> it exhibits a complex structure which can readily be tested in current experiments in becs in optical lattices and in optical waveguide arrays . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we derive an explicit theoretical estimate for the transition to self - trapping in excellent agreement with our numerical findings , which may be a valuable guide as well for future studies on a quantum dynamical phase diagram based on the bose - hubbard hamiltonian </S>",
    "<S> .    bose - einstein condensates ( becs ) trapped in periodic optical potentials have proven to be an invaluable tool to study fundamental and applied aspects of quantum optics , quantum computing and solid state physics  @xcite . in the limit of large atom numbers per </S>",
    "<S> well , the dynamics can be well described by a mean - field approximation which leads to a lattice version of the gross - pitaevskii equation , the discrete nonlinear schrdinger equation ( dnls )  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> one of the most intriguing features of the dynamics of nonlinear lattices is that excitations can spontaneously stably localize even for repulsive nonlinearities . </S>",
    "<S> this phenomenon of discrete self - trapping , also referred to as the formation of _ discrete breathers ( db ) _ , is a milestone discovery in nonlinear science that has sparked many studies ( for reviews see  @xcite ) . </S>",
    "<S> dbs have been observed experimentally in various physical systems such as arrays of nonlinear waveguides @xcite and josephson junctions @xcite , spins in antiferromagnetic solids @xcite , and becs in optical lattices @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> self - trapping has also been shown to exist in the dynamics of the bose - hubbard hamiltonian @xcite and in calculations beyond the bose - hubbard model , which include higher - lying states in the individual wells @xcite .    under which conditions </S>",
    "<S> will a gaussian distributed initial condition in an optical lattice become diffusive , or self - trapped or a moving breather after sufficient propagation time ? </S>",
    "<S> this question was addressed in a seminal work @xcite that has become a standard reference in both experimental and theoretical studies involving self - trapping in optical lattices including becs and optical waveguide arrays in the past decade @xcite . by means of a variational approach that approximates the dnls dynamics </S>",
