    "<S> we perform a quantitative , comparative study of the spin pumping , spin seebeck and spin hall magnetoresistance effects , all detected via the inverse spin hall effect in a series of over 20 yttrium iron garnet / pt samples . </S>",
    "<S> our experimental results fully support present , exclusively spin current - based , theoretical models using a single set of plausible parameters for spin mixing conductance , spin hall angle and spin diffusion length . </S>",
    "<S> our findings establish the purely spintronic nature of the aforementioned effects and provide a quantitative description in particular of the spin seebeck effect .    </S>",
    "<S> pure spin currents present a new paradigm in spintronics  @xcite and spin caloritronics  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , spin currents are the origin of spin pumping  @xcite , the spin seebeck effect  @xcite and the spin hall magnetoresistance ( smr )  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> taken alone , all these effects have been extensively studied , both experimentally  @xcite and theoretically  @xcite . from a theoretical point of view , all these effects are governed by the generation of a current of angular momentum via a non - equilibrium process . </S>",
    "<S> the flow of this spin current across a ferromagnet / normal metal interface can then be detected . </S>",
    "<S> the relevant interface property that determines the spin current transport thereby is the spin mixing conductance . </S>",
    "<S> nevertheless , there has been an ongoing debate regarding the physical origin of the measurement data acquired in spin seebeck and smr experiments due to possible contamination with anomalous nernst effect  @xcite or anisotropic magnetoresistance  @xcite caused by static proximity polarization of the normal metal  @xcite . to settle this issue , </S>",
    "<S> a rigorous check of the consistency of the spin - current based physical models across all three effects is needed . if possible contamination effects are absent , according to the spin mixing conductance concept  @xcite , there should exist a generalized ohm s law between the interfacial spin current and the energy associated with the corresponding non - equilibrium process . </S>",
    "<S> this relation should invariably hold for the spin pumping , spin seebeck and spin hall magnetoresistance effects , as they are all based on the generation and detection of interfacial , non - equilibrium spin currents . </S>",
    "<S> we here put forward heuristic arguments that are strongly supported by experimental evidence for a scaling law that links all aforementioned spin(calori)tronic effects on a fundamental level and allows to trace back their origin to pure spin currents .     in the ferromagnet ( yig in our experiments ) </S>",
