    "<S> we study the gravitational effect of non - self - annihilating dark matter on compact stellar objects . </S>",
    "<S> the self - interaction of condensate dark matter can give high accretion rate of dark matter onto stars . </S>",
    "<S> phase transition to condensation state takes place when the dark matter density exceeds the critical value . </S>",
    "<S> a compact degenerate dark matter core is developed and alter the structure and stability of the stellar objects . </S>",
    "<S> condensate dark matter admixed neutron stars is studied through the two - fluid tov equation . </S>",
    "<S> the existence of condensate dark matter deforms the mass - radius relation of neutron stars and lower their maximum baryonic masses and radii . </S>",
    "<S> the possible effects on the gamma - ray burst rate in high redshift are discussed . </S>"
