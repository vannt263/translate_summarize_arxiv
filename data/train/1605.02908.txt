    "<S> lvy walks is a model that excelled in describing such superdiffusive behaviors albeit in one dimension . </S>",
    "<S> here we show that , in contrast to standard random walks , the microscopic geometry of planar superdiffusive lvy walks is imprinted in the asymptotic distribution of the walkers . </S>",
    "<S> the geometry of the underlying walk can be inferred from trajectories of the walkers by calculating the analogue of the pearson coefficient .    _ </S>",
