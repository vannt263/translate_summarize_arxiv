    "the unperturbed conformal field is a generalized free field ( or a wick product thereof ) .",
    "this procedure resolves the problematic issues associated with the perturbation theory around generalized free fields , and at the same time drastically reduces the expected infinite arbitrariness involved in its renormalization .",
    "the most important benefit is the fact , that the boundary fields , if renormalized by this method , do not suffer from the conformal anomaly , i.e. , the conformal symmetry is perturbatively preserved .",
    "we find , however , that the existence of the boundary limit is not automatically guaranteed .",
