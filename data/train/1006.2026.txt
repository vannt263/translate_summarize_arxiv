    "<S> boij and sderberg made a pair of conjectures , which were subsequently proven by eisenbud and schreyer and then extended by boij and sderberg , about the structure of betti diagrams of graded modules . </S>",
    "<S> in the theory , a particular family of posets , and their associated order complexes , play an integral role . </S>",
    "<S> we explore the structure of this family . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we show the posets are bounded complete lattices and the order complexes are vertex - decomposable , hence cohen - macaulay and squarefree glicci .        </S>",
    "<S> boij and sderberg recently conjectured in  @xcite a complete characterisation , up to multiplication by a positive rational , of the structure of betti diagrams of finitely generated graded modules . </S>",
    "<S> their conjectures were proven for the cohen - macaulay case in  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these were further extended to arbitrary graded modules in  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> this characterisation centered around constructing the convex hull of a particular class of betti diagrams and showing it is equal to a geometric realisation of a particular simplicial complex  the order complex of a poset .    </S>",
    "<S> the family of posets described in  ( * ? ? ? </S>",

    "<S> following this , in section  [ sec : basic - structure ] we determine some basic structural results for the posets . in particular , we show that the boij - sderberg posets are indeed bounded complete lattices ( proposition  [ pro : lattice ] ) . and </S>",
    "<S> in section  [ sec : recursive - structure ] we discuss the recursive structure of the posets by finding a recursive atom ordering ( see  @xcite ) for each of the posets ( theorem  [ thm : atom - ordering ] ) . </S>",
