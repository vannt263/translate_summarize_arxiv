    "<S> we discuss coin - weighing problems with a new type of coin : a chameleon . a chameleon coin can mimic a fake or a real coin , and it can choose which coin to mimic for each weighing independently .    </S>",
    "<S> we consider a mix of @xmath0 coins that include exactly two non - real coins : one fake and one chameleon . the task is to use a balance to find two coins one of which has to be fake . </S>",
    "<S> we find bounds for the number of coins for which we can find a solution in a given number of weighings . </S>",
    "<S> we also introduce an important idea of solution scaling . </S>"
