    "<S> we consider the effect of rescattering of pairs of quasiparticles in the cooper channel resulting in the strong renormalization of second - order corrections to the spin susceptibility in a two - dimensional electron system . </S>",
    "<S> we use the fourier expansion of the scattering potential in the vicinity of the fermi surface to find that each harmonic becomes renormalized independently . </S>",
    "<S> since some of those harmonics are negative , the first derivative of the spin susceptibility is bound to be negative at small momenta , in contrast to the lowest order perturbation theory result , which predicts a positive slope . </S>",
    "<S> we present in detail an effective method to calculate diagrammatically corrections to the spin susceptibility to infinite order . </S>"
