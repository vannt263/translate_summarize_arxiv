    "<S> we consider minimal supergravity ( msugra ) models with an additional r - parity violating operator at the grand unification scale . </S>",
    "<S> this can change the supersymmetric spectrum leading on the one hand to a sneutrino , smuon or squark as the lightest supersymmetric particle ( lsp ) . on the other hand , a wide parameter region </S>",
    "<S> is reopened , where the scalar tau is the lsp . </S>",
    "<S> it is vital to know the nature of the lsp , because supersymmetric particles normally cascade decay down to the lsp at collider experiments . </S>",
    "<S> we investigate in detail the conditions leading to non - neutralino lsp scenarios . </S>",
    "<S> we also present some typical lhc signatures .     </S>",
