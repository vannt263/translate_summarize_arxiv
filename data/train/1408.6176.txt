    "<S> it is known that any tropical polytope is the image under the valuation map of ordinary polytopes over the puiseux series field . </S>",
    "<S> the latter polytopes are called lifts of the tropical polytope . </S>",
    "<S> we prove that any pure tropical polytope is the intersection of the tropical half - spaces given by the images under the valuation map of the facet - defining half - spaces of a certain lift . </S>",
    "<S> we construct this lift explicitly , taking into account geometric properties of the given polytope . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , when the generators of the tropical polytope are in general position , we prove that the above property is satisfied for any lift . </S>",
    "<S> this solves a conjecture of develin and yu . </S>"
