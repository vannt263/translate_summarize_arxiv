    "recently , a great deal of attention has been paid to the higher - order gaussian modes of the electromagnetic field . in the paraxial approximation ,",
    "two interesting cases are the laguerre - gaussian ( lg ) and hermite - gaussian ( hg ) modes .",
    "these modes are solutions of the paraxial helmholtz equation @xcite and are eigenstates of the free - space propagator .",
    "it has been shown that the lg modes carry orbital angular momentum in the form of an azimuthal phase @xmath0 in the transverse plane @xcite .",

    "the hg modes may also be of use in quantum information schemes .",
    "in particular , the first - order hg and lg modes can be described and manipulated in a way that is analogous to linear and circular polarization of the electromagnetic field @xcite .",
    "devices that act on the first - order transverse mode in a manner equivalent to polarizing beam splitters , half - wave plates and quarter - wave plates can be constructed using asymmetric interferometers @xcite , dove prisms @xcite and mode converters @xcite .",
    "the first - order modes can thus be used to define qubits and the above devices implement single qubit rotations .",
