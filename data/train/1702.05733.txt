    "<S> we study the production and evolution of charm and bottom quarks in hot partonic medium produced in heavy ion collisions . </S>",
    "<S> the heavy quarks loose energy in the medium which is reflected in the transverse momentum spectra of heavy mesons . the collisional energy loss of heavy quarks has been calculated using qcd calculations . </S>",
    "<S> the radiative energy loss is obtained using two models namely reaction operator formalism and generalized dead cone approach . </S>",
    "<S> the nuclear modification factors , @xmath0 as a function of transverse momentum by including shadowing and energy loss are calculated for @xmath1 and @xmath2 mesons in pbpb collisions at @xmath3 = 5.02 tev and for @xmath1 mesons at @xmath3 = 2.76 tev and are compared with the recent measurements . </S>",
    "<S> the radiative energy loss from generalized dead cone approach alone is sufficient to produce measured @xmath1 meson @xmath0 at both the lhc energies . </S>",
    "<S> the radiative energy loss from reaction operator formalism plus collisional energy loss gives good description of @xmath1 meson @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> for the case of @xmath2 meson , the radiative energy loss from generalized dead cone approach plus collisional energy loss gives good description of the cms data . </S>",
    "<S> the radiative process is dominant for charm quarks while for the bottom , both the radiative process and the elastic collisions are important .    </S>",
