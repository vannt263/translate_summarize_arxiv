    "<S> we consider the problem of finding a best uniform approximation to the standard monomial on the unit ball in @xmath0 by polynomials of lower degree with complex coefficients . </S>",
    "<S> we reduce the problem to a one - dimensional weighted minimization problem on an interval . in a sense , </S>",
    "<S> the corresponding extremal polynomials are uniform counterparts of the classical orthogonal jacobi polynomials </S>",
    "<S> . they can be represented by means of special conformal mappings on the so - called comb - like domains . in these terms , the value of the minimal deviation and the representation for a polynomial of best approximation for the original problem are given . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we derive asymptotics for the minimal deviation .    _ dedicated to the memory of franz peherstorfer _ </S>"
