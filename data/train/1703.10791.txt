    "<S> in the kerr geometry , we calculate various surfaces of constant curvature invariants . these extend well beyond the kerr horizon , and we argue that they might be of observational significance in connection with non - minimally coupled matter fields . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we demonstrate that the principal null directions of the kerr geometry can be obtained by projections involving either the bel  robinson or the kummer tensor . </S>",
    "<S> we conjecture that this is also possible in more general settings .    </S>",
