    "traversable lorentzian wormholes , which have received considerable attention since the paper by morris and thorne @xcite , are solutions of the equations of gravitation that connect two regions of the same universe or two separate universes by a throat that allows the passage from one region to the other @xcite . for static geometries ,",
    "the throat is defined as a minimal area surface that fulfills a flare  out condition @xcite . within the framework of the general relativity theory of gravitation",
    ", wormholes must be threaded by exotic matter that violates the null energy condition @xcite . as it was shown by visser _",
    "et al _ @xcite , the amount of exotic matter required around the throat can be made as small as needed with a suitable choice of the geometry of the wormhole .",
    "+ a well known method for constructing wormholes , introduced by visser @xcite , is by cutting and pasting two manifolds to form a geodesically complete new one with a shell placed in the joining surface . in this case",
    "the exotic matter required for their existence is located at the shell .",
    "stability analysis of thin ",
    "shell wormholes under perturbations preserving the original symmetries has been done previously by several authors .",
    "a linearized analysis of a thin  shell wormhole made by joining two schwarzschild geometries was carried out by poisson and visser @xcite .",
    "this method was applied by barcelo and visser to wormholes constructed using branes with negative tensions @xcite , and the case of transparent spherically symmetric thin  shells and wormholes was studied by ishak and lake @xcite .",
    "the linearized stability analysis was extended to reissner ",
    "nordstrm thin  shell geometries by eiroa and romero @xcite , and to wormholes with a cosmological constant by lobo and crawford @xcite .",
    "dynamical thin ",
    "shell wormholes were considered by lobo and crawford @xcite .",
    "+ the presence of matter violating energy conditions is a shared feature of wormholes and modern cosmology , because if general relativity is assumed as the gravity theory describing the large scale behavior of the universe , the strong energy condition should be violated to explain its accelerated expansion .",
    "models of exotic matter of interest in cosmology have been considered in wormhole construction : phantom energy @xcite , and a chaplygin gas @xcite have been used by several authors as the exotic matter supporting wormholes .",
    "wormholes in higher dimensional spacetimes and in alternative theories of gravitation have been also investigated previously .",
    "studies of wormholes in low energy string theory or in einstein gravity with a scalar field were done in many articles @xcite .",
    "shell wormholes in dilaton gravity were studied by eiroa and simeone @xcite , and wormholes in einstein  gauss ",
    "bonnet theory were analyzed by several authors @xcite .",
    "shell wormholes associated with cosmic strings have been investigated recently @xcite .",
    "other interesting works can be found in refs . @xcite .",
