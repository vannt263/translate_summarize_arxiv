    "two - component fermions interacting via a zero - range and infinite scattering length interaction have attracted intense attention across many subfields of physics  @xcite .",
    "experimentally , such a system can be realized in trapped atoms using the feshbach resonance and has been extensively studied  @xcite .",
    "the most important property of the system is the scale invariance of the interaction , and thus , it can be thought of a rare realization of nonrelativistic conformal field theories  @xcite .    as a consequence of the scale invariance of the interaction ,",
    "all physical quantities at finite density and zero temperature are determined by simple dimensional analysis up to dimensionless constants of proportionality .",
    "such dimensionless parameters are universal depending only on the dimensionality of space .",
