    "<S> we analyse the complementarity between lepton flavour violation ( lfv ) and lhc experiments in probing the supersymmetric ( susy ) grand unified theories ( gut ) when neutrinos got a mass via the see  saw mechanism . </S>",
    "<S> our analysis is performed in an @xmath0 framework , where at least one neutrino yukawa coupling is necessarily as large as the top yukawa coupling . </S>",
    "<S> our study thoroughly takes into account the whole rg running , including the gut and the right handed neutrino mass scales , as well as the running of the observable neutrino spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the upcoming ( meg , superkekb ) and future ( prism / prime , super flavour factory ) lfv experiments will be able to test such susy framework for susy masses to be explored at the lhc and , in some cases , even beyond the lhc sensitivity reach . </S>"
