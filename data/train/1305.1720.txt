    "<S> we consider both known and not previously studied trace functions with applications in quantum physics . by using perspectives we obtain convexity statements for different notions of residual entropy , including the entropy gain of a quantum channel as studied by holevo and others .    </S>",
    "<S> we give new and simplified proofs of the carlen - lieb theorems concerning concavity or convexity of certain trace functions by making use of the theory of operator monotone functions . </S>",
    "<S> we then apply these methods in a study of new types of trace functions . </S>",
    "<S> + keywords : trace function , convexity , entropy gain , residual entropy , operator monotone function . </S>"
