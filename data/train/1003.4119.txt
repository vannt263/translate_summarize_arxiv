    "neutrino physics provides one of the prime examples of the interplay between particle physics and cosmology .",
    "because neutrinos are so abundant , the cosmic neutrino background contributes significantly to the cosmic energy density at all times , and therefore have a profound influence on the evolution of our universe . at early times , around the epoch of neutrino decoupling at @xmath0 mev",

    "there are two ways of improving current data , either measuring larger effective volumes , or going to higher redshifts where structures are more linear . in practise many of the proposed experiments will do both .",
    "one example is measurements of weak gravitational lensing by the large synoptic survey telescope ( lsst ) which will have an effective volume many times larger than the sdss and measure out to redshifts of order 1.5 @xcite .",
