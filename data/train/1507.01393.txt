    "according to schrdinger@xcite , the interference between two branches of a free dirac spectrum induces a quivering quantum motion , usually known as _ zitterbewegung _ ( zb ) . in principle",
    ", zb is not a pure relativistic phenomenon . in recent years",
    ", it has been shown that zb could exist in a plethora of non - relativistic physical systems@xcite including narrow - gap semiconductors@xcite , spin - orbit coupled low - dimensional systems@xcite , graphene@xcite , carbon nanotube@xcite , topological insulator@xcite , superconductors@xcite , sonic crystal@xcite , photonic crystal@xcite , optical superlattice@xcite , bose - einstein condensates@xcite , dichalcogenide materials like mos@xmath1@xcite etc .",
    "the existence of spin - orbit interaction ( soi)@xcite in systems like two - dimensional electron / hole gas ( 2deg/2dhg ) is an interesting topic of contemporary research due to the promising spintronic applications@xcite .",
    "in addition , these systems exhibit various fundamental physical phenomena such as zero field spin - splitting@xcite , spin hall effect@xcite , persistent spin helix@xcite etc .",
    "the absence of structural and bulk inversion symmetries in semiconductor heterostructures induce rashba@xcite and dresselhaus@xcite sois , respectively .",
    "the functional dependence of both sois on momentum in 2deg and 2dhg are different . in the case of a 2deg , they are linear in momentum whereas for the 2dhg both sois are cubic in momentum . systems with cubic sois are particularly important as large spin hall conductivity can be achieved in those systems@xcite . apart from p - doped semiconductor heterostructures , the existence of cubic sois have been confirmed experimentally in a 2deg at the surface of srtio@xmath2@xcite and in a 2dhg in a strained - ge / sige quantum well@xcite .",
    "the problem of zb of an electronic wave packet in a 2deg including both rashba and dresselhaus sois is well studied .",
    "to the best of our knowledge , a less effort has been devoted in searching zb in a spin - orbit coupled 2dhg . in this work ,",
    "we study the zb of the center of a gaussian wave packet in a 2dhg in presence of both cubic rashba and dresselhaus sois .",
    "we find that the zb exhibits transient behavior .",
    "the amplitude of zb is related with the berry connection .",
    "unlike the case of a 2deg , the zb survives for equal strength of the rashba and dresselhaus soi .",
    "we relate this non - vanishing of zb with the behavior of the associated berry phase .",
    "the spin associated with the heavy hole precesses about a soi induced effective magnetic field . a transverse component of the spin - orbit force is generated as a result of this spin precession . using this spin - orbit force picture ,",
    "we have also estimated the magnitude of an induced electric voltage associated with zb .",
