    "electron spins in semiconductor quantum dots ( qds ) have relatively long coherence times in solid state devices  @xcite and potential scalability by utilizing the current extensive semiconductor fabrication techniques .",
    "they are considered good candidates for quantum bits  @xcite in quantum information processing  @xcite .",
    "the required elementary operations on the spin-1/2 qubits for quantum information processing have been demonstrated recently .",
    "the spin states are initialized and read out using the pauli spin blockade ( psb )  @xcite or tunneling to the leads from zeeman split energy levels  @xcite .",
    "rotation of single spins has been realized by electron spin resonance ( esr )  @xcite .",
    "addressability and the speed of single spin rotation are improved by micro - magnet ( mm ) induced esr  @xcite .",
    "high - fidelity single - spin rotation decoupled from the fluctuating nuclear spin environment was demonstrated  @xcite .",
    "entanglement operations of two spins are realized by utilizing exchange interaction and fast two qubit operations have been demonstrated  @xcite .",
    "this scheme of the spin-1/2 qubit is applicable to a wide variety of materials including si , which has a long spin coherence time  @xcite .",
    "scale up of the qd system is crucial to realize larger scale quantum gate operations and also explore multi - spin physics . to this end ,",
    "spin qubit experiments on multiple qds have been reported in recent years . in triple",

    "coupled qqd has been demonstrated in the few - electron regime  @xcite .    in this paper",

    "<S> electron spins in semiconductor quantum dots are good candidates of quantum bits for quantum information processing . </S>",
    "<S> basic operations of the qubit have been realized in recent years : initialization , manipulation of single spins , two qubit entanglement operations , and readout . </S>",
    "<S> now it becomes crucial to demonstrate scalability of this architecture by conducting spin operations on a scaled up system . </S>",
    "<S> here , we demonstrate single - electron spin resonance in a quadruple quantum dot . a few - electron quadruple quantum dot is formed within a magnetic field gradient created by a micro - magnet . </S>",
    "<S> we oscillate the wave functions of the electrons in the quantum dots by applying microwave voltages and this induces electron spin resonance . </S>",
    "<S> the resonance energies of the four quantum dots are slightly different because of the stray field created by the micro - magnet and therefore frequency - resolved addressable control of the electron spin resonance is possible . </S>"
