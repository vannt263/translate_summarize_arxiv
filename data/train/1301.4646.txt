    "<S> but , only the scenario in which the end nodes use @xmath0-psk signal sets is addressed in @xcite and @xcite . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we address the case in which the end nodes use @xmath0-qam signal sets . in a fading scenario , for certain channel conditions @xmath1 , termed singular fade states , the ma phase performance is greatly reduced . by formulating a procedure for finding the exact number of singular fade states for qam , we show that square qam signal sets give lesser number of singular fade states compared to psk signal sets . </S>",
    "<S> this results in superior performance of @xmath0-qam over @xmath0-psk . </S>",

    "<S> , we describe a procedure to analytically partition the complex plane representing the channel condition . </S>",
