    "<S> in the present paper , we consider a cauchy problem for a linear second order in time abstract differential equation with pure delay . in the absence of delay </S>",
    "<S> , this problem , known as the harmonic oscillator , has a two - dimensional eigenspace so that the solution of the homogeneous problem can be written as a linear combination of these two eigenfunctions . </S>",
    "<S> as opposed to that , in the presence even of a small delay , the spectrum is infinite and a finite sum representation is not possible . using a special function </S>",
    "<S> referred to as the delay exponential function , we give an explicit solution representation for the cauchy problem associated with the linear oscillator with pure delay . </S>",
