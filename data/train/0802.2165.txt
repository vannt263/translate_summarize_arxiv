    "<S> the main purpose of these researches is to determine the range of controller parameters that allow stability . </S>",
    "<S> explicit and complete expressions of the boundaries of these regions and computation procedures with a finite number of steps are now available only for first - order plants , provided with one time delay . in this note , </S>",
    "<S> the same results , based on pontryagin s studies , are presented for arbitrary - order plants .    </S>",
