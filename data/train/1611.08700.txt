    "entanglement is an essential resource for quantum computation and quantum metrology .",
    "classically , a parameter can be estimated more precisely by using more particles in the measurement , and the reduction of the statistical error is proportional to the square root of the particle number . in quantum metrology",
    ", the reduction factor can be improved to be linearly proportional to the particle number , which is called the heisenberg limit , by using many - particle entangled states .",
    "the ultimate heisenberg limit can be achieved with the noon state for identical bosons @xcite , which can be understood by the superposition of two modes with only one of them occupied by @xmath0 bosons .",

    "phonons , bosonic quasi - particles , which represent the number of quantized excitations of a vibrational mode @xcite , are proposed as the information carrier for quantum simulation @xcite , boson sampling @xcite , and quantum computation with continuous variables @xcite .",
    "recently , the noon state with @xmath8 has been generated through interference of phonons in each localized harmonic potential @xcite .",
