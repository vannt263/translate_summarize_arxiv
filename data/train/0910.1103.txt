    "from the theoretical point of view , the study of the magnetic moments of baryons presents an opportunity to shed light on an accurate test of qcd in the same way the magnetic moments of the electron and muon provided an accurate test of qed in the past .",
    "there are an important number of works focused on the analysis of baryon magnetic moments ; the approaches include , among others , the quark model ( and its variants ) @xcite , qcd sum rules @xcite , the @xmath4 expansion , where @xmath0 is the number of colors @xcite , chiral perturbation theory @xcite , and lattice gauge theory @xcite , to name but a few .",
    "the @xmath4 expansion and chiral perturbation theory , on general grounds , have had a major impact on the extraction of the low - energy consequences of qcd .",
    "on the one hand , the generalization of qcd from @xmath3 to @xmath5 colors , referred to as the large-@xmath0 limit , provides a framework for studying the nonperturbative qcd dynamics of hadrons .",
    "specifically , in the large-@xmath0 limit , the baryon sector of qcd possesses an exact contracted su(@xmath6 ) spin - flavor symmetry , where @xmath7 is the number of light quark flavors @xcite . the spin - flavor structure for baryons for finite @xmath0",
    "is thus given by analyzing @xmath4 corrections to the large-@xmath0 limit @xcite .",
    "apart from the calculation of baryon magnetic moments , the @xmath4 expansion has also been successfully used in the calculation of other static properties of baryons such as masses @xcite and vector and axial - vector couplings @xcite .",
    "most calculations include corrections of relative order @xmath8 , and even of relative order @xmath9 in the case of baryon masses , for two and three light quark flavors",
    ". the impact of the @xmath4 expansion in the calculation of baryon static properties can be assessed by comparing its predictions with experiment , which are in overall good agreement .    on the other hand ,",
    "heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory @xcite is another formalism that has been implemented to systematically compute the properties of baryons . in this formalism , the expansion of the baryon chiral lagrangian is in powers of @xmath10 and @xmath11 , where @xmath12 is the baryon mass ( for a recent review on chiral perturbation theory see for instance ref .",
    "the effective lagrangian thus obtained can be used to compute chiral logarithms in the effective theory , yielding to a remarkable computational simplicity because there are no gamma matrices left .",
    "one of the earliest applications of heavy baryon chiral perturbation theory can be found in the computation of one - loop corrections to the leading axial - vector couplings in baryon semileptonic decays @xcite .",
    "an important result derived from this analysis was the observation of large cancellations in the loop corrections between graphs with intermediate spin-1/2 octet and spin-3/2 decuplet baryon states .",
    "these cancellations occur as a consequence of the spin - flavor symmetry which is present in the large-@xmath0 limit , and have already been proven both phenomenologically and analytically @xcite .",
