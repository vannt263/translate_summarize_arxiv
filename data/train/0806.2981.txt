    "although accelerator and direct dark matter searches may well provide useful hints on the nature of dark matter , a correct identification is likely to require the combination of different techniques , including the indirect searches , based on the detection of the annihilation products of dark matter particles . the annihilation rate being proportional to the square of the dark matter density integrated along the line of sight , regions with enhanced dark matter density are primary targets of indirect searches . among them are the galactic halo  @xcite , external galaxies  @xcite , galaxy clusters  @xcite , substructures  @xcite , and the galactic center ( gc )  @xcite .",
    "the gc , in particular , has attracted significant interest .",
    "the distribution of dark matter at the gc is actually highly uncertain , due to lack of resolution in n - body simulations , and to the many astrophysical effects that further complicate the situation , such as the presence of the supermassive black hole coincident with sgr a * , gravitational scattering of dark matter off the stellar cusp , and dark matter annihilation  @xcite .",
    "dwarf spheroidal galaxies in the local group  @xcite have also been considered as targets for gamma - ray detection since they seem to represent dark matter dominated regions  @xcite .",
    "more recently , the sloan digital sky survey ( sdss ) revealed the existence of new satellites  @xcite offering appealing features for dark matter searches .",
    "radial velocity dispersion of stars in galaxy satellites usually implies large mass - to - luminosity ratio .",
    "nevertheless , the lack of accurate measurements on the velocity dispersion may induce large systematic effects on the parameters used for the modelling of the dark matter halo . even in case of accurate kinematic data for some of the galaxy satellites ,",
    "only faint annihilation signals are expected for smooth dark matter halos due to their distance of @xmath4100 kpc .",
    "mini - spikes around intermediate mass black holes ( imbhs ) have been recently proposed as promising targets for indirect dark matter detection  @xcite .",
    "accurate predictions , in the context of well - defined astrophysical scenarios , have been derived for the distribution and luminosity of these objects  @xcite .",
