    "<S> we calculate the frequency - dispersed nonlinear transmission signal of a phase - shaped visible pulse to fourth order in the field . </S>",
    "<S> two phase profiles , a phase - step and phase - pulse , are considered . </S>",
    "<S> two dimensional signals obtained by varying the detected frequency and phase parameters are presented for a three electronic band model system . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate how two - photon and stimulated raman resonances can be manipulated by the phase profile and sign , and selected quantum pathways can be suppressed . </S>"
