    "in this paper we have studied tikhonov regularization for solving ill  posed operator equations where the solutions are functions from a surface to the tangent bundles .",
    "such problems appear in a variety of applications such as pulsed wave doppler imaging and optical flow imaging on surfaces .",
    "we extended the existing theory of finite dimensional approximations of tikhonov regularized solutions for ill  posed operator equations to the surface setting .",
    "the additional features of this theory are , that it allows to take into account perturbations of the surface and the tangent bundle , and that it provides an optimal convergence rate by balancing all these parameters according to the level of errors .",
