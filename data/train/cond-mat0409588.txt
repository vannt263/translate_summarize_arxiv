    "<S> in this work we present a model for the photoconductivity of a two dimensional electron system ( 2des ) subjected to a magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> the model includes the microwave and landau contributions in a non - perturbative exact way , impurity scattering effects are treated perturbatively . based on this formalism , we provide a kubo - like formula that takes into account the oscillatory floquet structure of the problem . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss results related with the recently discovered zero - resistance states . </S>"
