    "jets are collimated sprays of particles originating from the fragmentation of hard scattered partons ( quarks and gluons ) produced in pp ( or a  a ) collisions  @xcite . as such",
    ", jets serve as a proxy for the high @xmath5 partons produced in elementary hard scatterings . in pp collisions",
    ", they provide a unique tool to test perturbative quantum chromodynamics ( pqcd )  @xcite .",
    "measurements of jet shapes in particular , provide the details of the jet fragmentation process  @xcite .",
    "jet shapes are also sensitive to the type of partons ( quarks or gluons ) that fragment into hadrons . in addition",
    ", measurements in pp collisions provide a baseline for similar measurements in more complex a  a collisions where various nuclear effects are expected to take place .",
    "jet shape observables have previously been measured by the cdf  @xcite and d0  @xcite collaborations in @xmath7 collisions and more recently by the atlas  @xcite and cms  @xcite collaborations in pp collisions at @xmath0 = 7 tev . in this paper",
