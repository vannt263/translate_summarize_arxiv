    "<S> we study a free boundary problem which is motivated by a particular case of the flow of a non - newtonian fluid , with a pressure depending yield stress given by a drucker - prager plasticity criterion . </S>",
    "<S> we focus on the steady case and reformulate the equation as a variational problem . </S>",
    "<S> the resulting energy has a term with linear growth while we study the problem in an unbounded domain . </S>",
    "<S> we derive an euler - lagrange equation and prove a comparison principle . </S>",
    "<S> we are then able to construct a subsolution and a supersolution which quantify the natural and expected properties of the solution ; in particular we show that the solution has in fact compact support , the boundary of which is the free boundary .    </S>",
    "<S> the model describes the flow of a non - newtonian material on an inclined plane with walls , driven by gravity . </S>",
    "<S> we show that there is a critical angle for a non - zero solution to exist . finally , using the sub / supersolutions we give estimates of the free boundary .    * msc 2010 : 76a05 ; 49j40 ; 35r35 * + * keywords : non - newtonian fluid ; drucker - prager plasticity ; variational inequality ; free boundary * </S>"
