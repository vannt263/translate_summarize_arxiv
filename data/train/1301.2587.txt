    "<S> the stacking order in trilayer graphene plays a critical role in determining the existence of an electric field tunable band gap . </S>",
    "<S> we present spatially - resolved tunneling spectroscopy measurements of dual gated bernal ( aba ) and rhombohedral ( abc ) stacked trilayer graphene devices . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate that while aba trilayer graphene remains metallic , abc trilayer graphene exhibits a widely tunable band gap as a function of electric field . however , we find that charged impurities in the underlying substrate cause substantial spatial fluctuations of the gap size . </S>",
    "<S> our work elucidates the microscopic behavior of trilayer graphene and its consequences for macroscopic devices .    </S>",
    "<S> graphene has great potential to be used in novel electronics applications  @xcite due to its extraordinarily rich physical properties  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> many of these applications require inducing a sizable band gap without sacrificing its high intrinsic carrier mobility  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> while this has thus far not been achieved in single layer graphene , multilayer graphene has the possibility of an electric field tunable band gap . </S>",
    "<S> trilayer graphene exhibits two natural stacking orders , bernal and rhombohedral . </S>",
    "<S> the more commonly found bernal - stacked trilayer graphene is not expected to exhibit a significant field tunable band gap due to its mirror symmetry  @xcite , and experimental evidence thus far has supported this  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> its zero - field low energy band structure behaves roughly like a decoupled stack of bernal - stacked bilayer graphene and single layer graphene  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , rhombohedrally - stacked trilayer graphene behaves differently in an electric field owing to its lack of mirror symmetry . in this case , the low energy bands mimic a single sheet of bernal - stacked bilayer graphene , and as such , a large field tunable band gap is expected in abc trilayer graphene  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> prior work has explored the zero - field band structure of this material via electrical transport measurements and found a small band gap attributed to many - body effects  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the magnitude of the band gap in a tunable electric field has also been studied optically  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recent transport measurements have explored the global behavior of both stacking orders in an electric field  @xcite . however , direct spectroscopic confirmation of the electronic properties of abc trilayer with independent control of the fermi energy and electric field has thus far been lacking .    in this paper , we present scanning tunneling microscopy ( stm ) and scanning tunneling spectroscopy ( sts ) measurements of dual gated exfoliated samples of both aba- and abc - stacked trilayer graphene on si / sio@xmath0 substrates . </S>",
    "<S> our results provide a direct understanding of the behavior of the band gap of abc trilayer graphene in an electric field , and illuminate the effects of local disorder on its electronic properties .    </S>",
