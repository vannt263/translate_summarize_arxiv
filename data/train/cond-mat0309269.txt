    "<S> recently it was shown that the landau - khalatnikov two - fluid hydrodynamics describes the collision - dominated region of a trapped bose condensate interacting with a thermal cloud . </S>",
    "<S> we use these equations to discuss the low frequency hydrodynamic collective modes in a trapped bose gas at finite temperatures . </S>",
    "<S> we derive a variational expressions based on these equations for both the frequency and damping of collective modes . </S>",
    "<S> a new feature is our use of frequency - dependent transport coefficients , which produce a natural cutoff by eliminating the collisionless low - density tail of the thermal cloud . above the superfluid transition , </S>",
    "<S> our expression for the damping in trapped inhomogeneous gases is analogous to the result first obtained by landau and lifshitz for uniform classical fluids . </S>",
    "<S> we also use the moment method to discuss the crossover from the collisionless to the hydrodynamic region . </S>",
    "<S> recent data for the monopole - quadrupole mode in the hydrodynamic region of a trapped gas of metastable @xmath0he is discussed . </S>",
    "<S> we also present calculations for the damping of the analogous @xmath1 monopole - quadrupole condensate mode in the superfluid phase . </S>"
