    "<S> we consider the continuous resonant  ( cr ) system of the 2d cubic nonlinear schrdinger ( nls ) equation . </S>",
    "<S> this system arises in numerous instances as an effective equation for the long - time dynamics of nls in confined regimes ( e.g. on a compact domain or with a trapping potential ) . </S>",
    "<S> the system was derived and studied from a deterministic viewpoint in several earlier works @xcite , which uncovered many of its striking properties . </S>",
    "<S> this manuscript is devoted to a probabilistic study of this system . </S>",
    "<S> most notably , we construct global solutions in negative sobolev spaces , which leave gibbs and white noise measures invariant . </S>",
    "<S> invariance of white noise measure seems particularly interesting in view of the absence of similar results for nls . </S>"
