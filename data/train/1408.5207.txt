    "<S> the synchrotron motion simulator is invented to simulate the longitudinal motion for particles under rf ( radio frequency ) voltage field in a ring accelerator . </S>",
    "<S> it is especially used to study the efficiency of adiabatic capture for a booster ring . </S>",
    "<S> the purpose of adiabatic capture is to optimize rf voltage settings during the ramping of beam energy and obtain the greatest efficiency of particle capture . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we study the longitudinal ( synchrotron ) motion for particles in the tls ( taiwan light source ) booster  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we compare the properties of tls booster as a proton or electron accelerator , using the same ramping scenario of beam energy , and optimize the rf voltage settings to have the best capture efficiency . </S>"
