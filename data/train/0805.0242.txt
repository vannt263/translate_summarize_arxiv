    "<S> _ the theory of the calculus of variations was recently extended to the more general time scales setting , both for delta and nabla integrals . </S>",
    "<S> the primary purpose of this paper is to further extend the theory on time scales , by establishing some basic diamond - alpha dynamic integral inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> we prove generalized versions of hlder , cauchy - schwarz , minkowski , and jensen s inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> for the particular case when alpha is equal to one or alpha is equal to zero one gets , respectively , correspondent delta and nabla inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> if we further restrict ourselves by fixing the time scale to the real or integer numbers , then we obtain the classical inequalities , whose role in optimal control is well known . by analogy , we trust that the diamond - alpha integral inequalities we prove here will be important in the study of control systems on times scales . </S>",
