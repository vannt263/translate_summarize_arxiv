    "is the coupling constant reduction method @xcite based on renormalization - group invariant relations among couplings which are the solutions of the so - called reduction equations @xcite .",
    "though with these relations the models is not necessarily finite , one can reduce the number of free parameters in models and increase the predictive power as well as models with finiteness conditions .",
    "moreover the application of this method to the soft susy breaking sector in ordinary minimal su(5 ) model leads non - universal boundary conditions for soft mass parameters at gut scale .",
