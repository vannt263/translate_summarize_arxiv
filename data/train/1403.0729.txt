    "<S> we study existence and stability properties of entire solutions of a polyharmonic equation with an exponential nonlinearity . </S>",
    "<S> we study existence of radial entire solutions and we provide some asymptotic estimates on their behavior at infinity . as a first result on stability we prove that stable solutions ( not necessarily radial ) in dimensions lower than the conformal one never exist . on the other hand , we prove that radial entire solutions which are stable outside a compact always exist both in high and low dimensions . in order to prove stability of solutions outside </S>",
