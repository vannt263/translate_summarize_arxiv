    "the observation of mott insulator  superfluid transition of ultracold bosons loaded in optical lattices@xcite has triggered huge amount of interest in quantum simulation@xcite .",
    "a lot of efforts have been made to investigate bose - hubbard hamiltonian@xcite , both single@xcite and multiple components@xcite , in cold atom systems ( in double - wellszhang , superlattices@xcite ) , using various techniques ( projection wave - function@xcite , decoupling approximationstoof , chen , field theory@xcite , dynamical mean - field@xcite , etc . ) , aiming to achieve a comprehensible understanding of this many - body model@xcite .    among these ,",
    "multi - component bose - hubbard in optical superlattices attracts special attention due to its rich quantum phases@xcite and promising potential in emulating effective spin - spin interactionsbarthel , bloch .",
    "many works on this topic have revealed the existence of mott - insulator with integer and fractional fillings , which correspond to various charge density wave ( cdw ) and complected dynamical behaviorskuklov , ziegler , roth , cirac , rousseau , bhattacherjee .",
    "people also reported topological phases and exotic localizations in disordered superlatticesbuonsante2,pollet .",
    "recently , to obtain a complete and thorough description of this model , several attempts have been made@xcite .    in this paper",
    ", we reinvestigate this problem by using a mean - field approach , the decoupling approximation . working in the mott insulating phase as an unperturbed ground state , and treating the hopping as perturbations , this method ( though at mean - field level",
    ") can still give comparatively accurate results for phase transition , comparing with numerical simulationsstoof .",
    "besides , since this method decouples the original hamiltonian into a set of single - site hamiltonian , it exhibits simple energy  expressions for different filling situations in the mott state .",
    "therefore , we can analyze and write down all the possible filling configurations in a clear and evident manner .",
    "then , we depict the corresponding phase boundaries for each filling condition , and gain a systematic description of possible quantum phases of this model .",
    "we further consider the effective spin dynamics at strong coupling limit and unit filling , and find both a ferromagnetic and an anti - ferromagnetic  spin - wave excitation as the potential barrier between neighboring sites varies .",
    "the paper is organized as follow : we first introduce the model in sec ii , then deals with the single - component bose - hubbard model in sec .",
    "iii , which acts as a precedent of the two - component case that is discussed in sec .",
    "both sections contain the mean - field calculations , the analysis of possible filling patterns and phase diagrams with corresponding illustrations .",
    "the spin dynamics is analyzed in the last subsection in sec .",
    "we give the conclusion in sec . v.",
