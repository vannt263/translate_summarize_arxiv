    "<S> in this paper , we introduce sound and complete deduction systems for various modal dependence logics </S>",
    "<S> . we also study model - theoretic properties of these team - based modal logics by interpreting them as variants of single - world - based intuitionistic modal logics .    </S>",
    "<S> _ dependence logic _ is a logical formalism , introduced by vnnen @xcite , that captures the notion of _ dependence _ in social and natural sciences . the modal version of the logic is called _ modal dependence logic _ and was introduced in @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> modal dependence logic extends the usual modal logic by adding a new type of atomic formulas @xmath0 , called _ dependence atoms _ , to express dependencies between propositions , and by lifting the usual single - world semantics to the so - called _ team semantics _ , introduced by hodges @xcite </S>",
    "<S> . formulas of modal dependence logic are evaluated on sets of possible worlds of kripke models , called _ </S>",
    "<S> teams_. intuitively , a dependence atom @xmath0 is true if within a team the truth value of the proposition @xmath1 is _ functionally determined _ by the truth values of the propositions @xmath2 .    </S>",
    "<S> research on modal dependence logic and its variants has been active in recent years . </S>",
    "<S> basic model - theoretic properties of the logics were studied in e.g. , @xcite , and a van benthem theorem for the logics was proved in @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the expressive power and the relevant computation complexity problems of the logics were investigated extensively in e.g. , @xcite . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we study a few problems that have not yet been well addressed in the literature , particularly the axiomatization problem and the comparison of team semantics and the single - world semantics .    </S>",
    "<S> sano and virtema gave in @xcite sound and complete hilbert systems and label tableau calculi for modal dependence logic and its extended version . however , their axiomatization did not cover the modal dependence logics with intuitionistic connectives , especially with intuitionistic implication . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we will define hilbert systems for these intuitionistic variants of modal dependence logic . </S>",
    "<S> these systems are extensions of the systems of fisher servi s intuitionistic modal logic @xmath3@xcite and inquisitive logic @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we also introduce a natural deduction system for ( extended ) modal dependence logic by extending the system of propositional dependence logic given in @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we will discuss the disjunctive form of modal dependence logics , which is essentially known in the literature , and apply the normal form to prove the interpolation theorem and the finite model property of these logics .    </S>",
    "<S> team semantics is a natural generalization of the usual single - world semantics . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we will also interpret the team semantics of modal dependence logics as the usual single - world semantics over certain kripke models . </S>",
    "<S> it was proved in @xcite that inquisitive logic @xmath4(which is the propositional base of modal dependence logic with intuitionistic implication ) can be viewed as a weak kreisel - putnam s intermediate logic @xmath5@xcite , and @xmath4is complete ( in the usual single - world semantics sense ) with respect to the class of negative intuitionistic kripke models of @xmath5 . </S>",
    "<S> building on this result we will prove in this paper that modal dependence logics can be viewed as certain intermediate modal logics , and they are complete ( in the usual single - world semantics sense ) with respect to a class of bi - relation or tri - relation intuitionistic kripke models . </S>",
    "<S> these bi - relation models are special bi - relation intuitionistic kripke models of fisher servi s intuitionistic modal logic @xmath3 , and the tri - relation intuitionistic kripke models are endowed with an extra ternary relation interpreting a binary diamond . in particular , we will interpret the tensor ( disjunction ) connective of team semantics as a binary diamond modality under the single - world semantics . </S>",
    "<S> this idea is inspired by @xcite , where tensor is read as a multiplicative conjunction .    </S>",
    "<S> this paper is organized as follows . in section 1 </S>",
    "<S> we recall basics of modal dependence logics . in section 2 </S>",
    "<S> we study the axiomatization problem for modal dependence logics , and also prove a few fundamental properties of the logics , including the interpolation theorem and the finite model property . </S>",
    "<S> section 3 provides single - world semantics interpretation of modal dependence logics .    </S>",
    "<S> preliminary results of this paper were included in the author s dissertation @xcite . </S>"
