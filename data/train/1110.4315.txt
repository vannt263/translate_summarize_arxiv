    "<S> a propagation model of galactic cosmic protons through the heliosphere was implemented using a 2-d monte carlo approach to determine the differential intensities of protons during the solar cycle 23 .  </S>",
    "<S> the model includes the effects due to the variation of solar activity during the propagation of cosmic rays from the boundary of the heliopause down to earth s position .  </S>",
    "<S> drift effects are also accounted for .  </S>",
    "<S> the simulated spectra were found in agreement with those obtained with experimental observations carried out by bess , ams and pamela collaborations .  </S>",
    "<S> in addition , the modulated spectrum determined with the present code for the year 1995 exhibits the latitudinal gradient and equatorial southward offset minimum found by ulysses fast scan in 1995 . </S>"
