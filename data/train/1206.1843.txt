  "abstract_text": [
    "<S> the three - gluon vertex is a basic object of interest in nonabelian gauge theory . </S>",
    "<S> it contains important structural information , in particular on infrared divergences , and also figures prominently in the schwinger - dyson equations . at the one - loop level , it has been calculated and analyzed by a number of authors . </S>",
    "<S> here we use the worldline formalism to unify the calculations of the scalar , spinor and gluon loop contributions to the one - loop vertex , leading to an extremely compact representation . the susy - related sum rule found by binger and brodsky follows from an off - shell extension of the bern - kosower replacement rules . </S>",
    "<S> we explain the relation of the structure of our representation to the low - energy effective action .    </S>",
