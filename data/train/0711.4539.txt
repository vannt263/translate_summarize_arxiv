    "discrete - time systems have a prominent role in many branches of nonlinear sciences .",
    "hamiltonian ( or area - preserving ) maps , for instance , are particularly relevant for the modeling of classical dynamical systems@xcite .",
    "the study of chaos in such systems has followed two main lines .",
    "the first one considers some individual trajectories in order to explore and characterize the system main topological properties@xcite .",
    "the second one investigates the distribution functions of statistical ensembles or , more specifically , some transport properties of the associated maps@xcite .",
    "the present work belongs to this latter group .    in the past , many investigations of transport properties for hamiltonian maps have been motivated by the paradigmatic chirikov - taylor standard map@xcite .",
    "although considerable progress in the study of diffusion has been achieved in the last 25 years @xcite , higher order transport coefficients have been particularly overlooked .",
    "the the fourth order coefficient @xmath0 known as the burnett coefficient@xcite , for instance , plays a central role in the large deviations theory : its magnitude gives the first indication of the deviation of a density function from a gaussian packet .",
