    "we considered the gapless pion excitations which can be realized in compact stars .",
    "we calculated the neutrino emissivity and cooling rate through direct urca processes in pion condensed quark matter with two flavors .",
    "we derived general expressions for neutrino and anti - neutrino emissivity . at temperature",
