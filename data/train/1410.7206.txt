    "<S> we provide a full characterisation of the large - maturity forward implied volatility smile in the heston model . </S>",
    "<S> although the leading decay is provided by a fairly classical large deviations behaviour , the algebraic expansion providing the higher - order terms highly depends on the parameters , and different powers of the maturity come into play . as a by - product of the analysis we provide new implied volatility asymptotics , both in the forward case and in the spot case , as well as extended svi - type formulae . </S>",
    "<S> the proofs are based on extensions and refinements of sharp large deviations theory , in particular in cases where standard convexity arguments fail . </S>"
