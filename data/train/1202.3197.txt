    "most bose - einstein condensates ( becs ) of dilute atomic gases realized thus far have internal degrees of freedom arising from spins .",
    "the spinor becs have been realized in the optical traps which confine the atoms regardless of their spin hyperfine states .",

    "moreover , there exists an interplay between superfluidity and magnetism due to the spin - gauge symmetry .",

    "<S> our method is applicable to other coupled nonlinear systems . </S>"
