    "<S> we report the recent progress on the theoretical aspects of monolayer topological insulators including silicene , germanene and stanene , which are monolayer honeycomb structures of silicon , germanium and tin , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> they show quantum spin hall effects in nature due to the spin orbit interaction . </S>",
    "<S> the band gap can be tuned by applying perpendicular electric field , which induces a topological phase transition . </S>",
    "<S> we also analyze the topological properties of generic honeycomb systems together with the classification of topological insulators . </S>",
    "<S> phase diagram of topological insulators and superconductors in honeycomb systems are explicitly determined . </S>",
    "<S> we also investigate topological electronics including a topological field - effect transistor , the topological kirchhoff s law and the topological spin - valleytronics . </S>"
