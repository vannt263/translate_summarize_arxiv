    "<S> the present calculation , together with recent progress in the sector of @xmath2 purely photonic corrections , contributes to achieve a total theoretical error in luminometry at the @xmath3 level , close to the current experimental precision and important in view of the final analysis of the electroweak precision data .    _ </S>",
