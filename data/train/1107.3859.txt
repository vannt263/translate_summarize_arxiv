    "<S> the study of pions in a superfluid state has called the attention of physicist during many years in different frameworks @xcite . in particular , compact stars </S>",
    "<S> may provide a natural scenario for such a state of matter . </S>",

    "<S> in fact , for high densities , an appropriate combination of chemical potentials can avoid the normal pion decay process . indeed , </S>",
    "<S> if in cold matter the leptons states are completely filled up to the fermi level , there will be no possible allowed final states for the decay of charged pions , unless thermal fluctuations are present . </S>",
    "<S> pion decay properties in a hot and/or dense media have been vastly studied @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the present work is focused on the existence of possible pion metastable states in the normal phase at high densities . as a consequence </S>",
