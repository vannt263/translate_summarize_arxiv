    "<S> we modify the definition of aldous multiplicative coalescent process @xcite and introduce the multiplicative coalescent with linear deletion ( mcld ) . </S>",
    "<S> a state of this process is a square - summable decreasing sequence of cluster sizes . </S>",
    "<S> pairs of clusters merge with a rate equal to the product of their sizes and clusters are deleted with a rate linearly proportional to their size . </S>",
    "<S> we prove that the mcld is a feller process . </S>",
    "<S> this result is a key ingredient in the description of scaling limits of the evolution of component sizes of the mean field frozen percolation model @xcite and the so - called rigid representation of such scaling limits @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> keywords : multiplicative coalescent , feller process + ams msc 2010 : 60j99 , 60b12 , 05c80 </S>"
