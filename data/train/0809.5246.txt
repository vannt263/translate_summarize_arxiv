    "in recent years the process of miniaturization of manmade electronic circuits has permitted to reach the molecular scale providing the opportunity of testing quantum mechanics in nano - electronics measurements@xcite . in this framework ,",
    "one of the most exciting challenges is to encode information by means of the electron spin instead of the charge , giving rise to the so - called spin - based electronics or spintronics@xcite . to face the need of spin - polarizing systems acting as a source in spintronics devices , one promising possibility is to exploit the quantum interference effects by external electric or magnetic fields . in ring - shaped structures made of semiconducting materials a spin sensitive phase , the aharonov - casher phase@xcite ,",
    "is originated by the rashba spin - orbit interaction@xcite .",
    "such phase combined with the aharonov - bohm phase@xcite induced by a magnetic field is an useful tool to achieve spin - polarizing devices@xcite .",
    "another interesting phase interference effect is originated by the periodic modulation of two out - of - phase parameters affecting the scattering properties of a nanostructure .",
    "such phase effect , known in the literature as quantum pumping , was first introduced by thouless@xcite .",
    "after the thouless theory , a scattering approach to the adiabatic quantum pumping was formulated by p. w. brouwer @xcite who showed that the d.c .",
    "current pumped by means of an adiabatic modulation of two out - of - phase independent parameters can be expressed in terms of the parametric derivatives of the scattering matrix . in the adiabatic regime described by the brouwer formula , i.e. when the pumping frequency is much slower than the tunneling rates , a d.c .",

    "<S> we study the time - dependent transport of charge and spin through a ring - shaped region sequentially coupled to a weakly interacting quantum dot in the presence of an aharonov - bohm flux and spin - orbit interaction . </S>",
    "<S> the time - dependent modulation of the spin - orbit interaction , or of the corresponding aharonov - casher flux , together with the modulation of the dot - level induces an electrically pumped spin current even in absence of a charge current . </S>",
    "<S> the results beyond the adiabatic regime show that an additional rectification current proportional to @xmath0 , being @xmath1 the relative phase between the time varying parameters , is generated . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the relevance of such term in connection with recent experiments on out - of - equilibrium quantum dots . </S>"
