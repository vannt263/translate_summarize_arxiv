    "in the past decade , bose coherent effects of 2d excitonic polaritons in a quantum well embedded in a semiconductor microcavity have been the subject of theoretical and experimental studies @xcite . to obtain polaritons ,",
    "two mirrors placed opposite each other form a microcavity , and quantum wells are embedded within the cavity at the antinodes of the confined optical mode .",
    "the resonant exciton - photon interaction results in the rabi splitting of the excitation spectrum .",
    "two polariton branches appear in the spectrum due to the resonant exciton - photon coupling .",
    "the lower polariton ( lp ) branch of the spectrum has a minimum at zero momentum .",
    "the effective mass of the lower polariton is extremely small , and lies in the range @xmath5 of the free electron mass .",
    "these lower polaritons form a 2d weakly interacting bose gas .",
    "the extremely light mass of these bosonic quasiparticles , which corresponds to experimentally achievable excitonic densities , result in a relatively high critical temperature for superfluidity , of @xmath6 or even higher .",
    "the reason for such a high critical temperature is that the 2d thermal de broglie wavelength is inversely proportional to the mass of the quasiparticle .    while at finite temperatures there is no true bec in any infinite untrapped 2d system , a true",
    "2d bec quantum phase transition can be obtained in the presence of a confining potential @xcite .",
    "recently , the polaritons in a harmonic potential trap have been studied experimentally in a gaas / alas quantum well embedded in a gaas / algaas microcavity @xcite . in this trap ,",
    "the exciton energy is shifted using a stress - induced band - gap . in this system , evidence for the bec of polaritons in a quantum",
    "well has been observed @xcite .",
    "the theory of the bec and superfluidity of excitonic polaritons in a quantum well without magnetic field in a parabolic trap has been developed in ref .",
    "the bose condensation of polaritons is caused by their bosonic character @xcite .",
    "while the 2d electron system was studied in quantum wells @xcite in the past decade , a novel type of 2d electron system was experimentally obtained in graphene , which is a 2d honeycomb lattice of the carbon atoms that form the basic planar structure in graphite @xcite .",
    "due to unusual properties of the band structure , electronic properties of graphene became the object of many recent experimental and theoretical studies @xcite .",
    "graphene is a gapless semiconductor with massless electrons and holes which have been described as dirac - fermions @xcite .",
    "the unique electronic properties in graphene in a magnetic field have been studied recently @xcite .",
    "the electron - photon interaction in graphene was discussed , for example , in ref .",
    "the energy spectrum and the wavefunctions of magnetoexcitons , or electron - hole pairs in a magnetic field , in graphene have been calculated in interesting works  @xcite .",
    "the spatially - indirect excitons in coupled quantum wells ( cqws ) , with and without a magnetic field @xmath0 have been studied recently experimentally in refs .",

    "since the exciton binding energies increase with magnetic field , 2d magnetoexcitons survive in a substantially wider temperature range in high magnetic fields @xcite .",

    "the purpose of this paper is to point out the existence of the bec of the magnetoexcitonic polaritons in a qw and a graphene layer embedded in a semiconductor microcavity in a strong magnetic field and to discuss the condition of its realization . since it was shown that the magnetoexcitons in a qw and graphene layer in a high magnetic field can be described by the same effective hamiltonian with the different effective mass of a magnetoexciton @xcite",
