    "<S> the reductions of the heun equation to the hypergeometric equation by polynomial transformations of its independent variable are enumerated and classified . </S>",
    "<S> heun - to - hypergeometric reductions are similar to classical hypergeometric identities , but the conditions for the existence of a reduction involve features of the heun equation that the hypergeometric equation does not possess ; namely , its cross - ratio and accessory parameters . </S>",
    "<S> the reductions include quadratic and cubic transformations , which may be performed only  if the singular points of the heun equation form a harmonic or an equianharmonic quadruple , respectively ; and several higher - degree transformations . </S>",
    "<S> this result cor and extends a theorem in a previous paper , which found only the quadratic transformations . </S>",
