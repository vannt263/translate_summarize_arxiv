    "in recent decades , kondo related physics has been intensively investigated in rare - earth element intermetallics . in the kondo lattice",
    ", the on - site kondo interaction screens the magnetic moment of localized @xmath6 electrons , leading to a nonmagnetically ordered heavy fermion state . besides the kondo interaction , another competing interaction , the ruderman - kittel - kasuya - yosida(rkky ) interaction mediated by the surrounding conduction electrons conversely favors long - range magnetic order .",
    "the competition between the kondo and rkky interactions in heavy fermion systems may result in various ground states , such as , magnetic order , superconductivity , heavy fermion and intermediate valence states @xcite .",
    "such phenomena related to the kondo effect have often been observed in ce , yb and u - based compounds , but eu based heavy fermion materials have not been commonly reported . in eu",
    "based compounds , the eu ion typically adopts one of two electronic configurations : divalent , magnetic eu@xmath4 ( 4f@xmath7 , j=7/2 , l=0 ) or trivalent , nonmagnetic eu@xmath8 ( 4f@xmath9 , j=0 , l=3 ) .",
    "therefore usually either a magnetic state with localized eu@xmath4 , a non - magnetic state with valence fluctuations or trivalent eu@xmath8 occur in eu systems .",
    "however , there have been a few proposed examples of heavy fermion systems such as euni@xmath10p@xmath10 and eucu@xmath10(si@xmath11ge@xmath12)@xmath10  @xcite.the eu valence of euni@xmath10p@xmath10 also shows a significant temperature dependence with the valence changing from + 2.25 at 300  k to + 2.50 at 1.4  k  @xcite , exhibiting strong valence fluctuations in the ground state .",
