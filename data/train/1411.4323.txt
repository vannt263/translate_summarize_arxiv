    "<S> in this paper we are considering a semilinear singular perturbation reaction  diffusion boundary value problem , which contains a small perturbation parameter that acts on the highest order derivative . </S>",
    "<S> we construct a difference scheme on an arbitrary nonequidistant mesh using a collocation method and green s function . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the constructed difference scheme has a unique solution and that the scheme is stable . </S>",
    "<S> the central result of the paper is @xmath0-uniform convergence of almost second order for the discrete approximate solution on a modified shishkin mesh . </S>",
    "<S> we finally provide two numerical examples which illustrate the theoretical results on the uniform accuracy of the discrete problem , as well as the robustness of the method .    </S>",
