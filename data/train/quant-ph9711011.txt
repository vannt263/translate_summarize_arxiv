    "consider a quantum state prepared as a superposition of two coherent states separated in the phase space by @xmath50 .",
    "it is known @xcite that such a state interacting with an environment evolves quickly toward a mixture of the two localized states .",
    "the decoherence time decreases with the classical distance @xmath50 , equal just to the monge distance between both coherent states .",
    "we expect therefore that the monge distance between two arbitrary quantum states might be useful to determine the rate with which the superposition of these two states suffers the decoherence .",
