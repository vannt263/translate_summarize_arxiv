    "second , any predefined geometry can be made a solution of a particular model .",
    "these models have first been proposed in @xcite in the context of fermionic excitations of flat geometry .",
    "here , i shall use them for modeling the dynamics of the universe .",

    "first , a class of purely geometric theories of the universe has been defined by associating a geometric sigma model to every spatially flat , homogeneous and isotropic geometry .",
    "this way , each geometry of this kind becomes a vacuum solution of a particular geometric sigma model .",
    "ultimately , one is provided with the action functional from which the observed cosmology can be derived .",
    "the inflation and the late time acceleration have purely geometric origin .",
    "the second result concerns the vacuum stability of geometric sigma models .",
    "it has been proven true for almost all background geometries .",
    "this is an important achievement because the stability is not guaranteed by the very construction of geometric sigma models . instead",

    "these models possess two distinctive features .",
    "the first is that any metric can be made a solution of a particular geometric sigma model .",
    "the second ensures that the complete matter content can be gauged away . in this paper ,",
    "a geometric sigma model is associated with every homogeneous , isotropic and spatially flat geometry .",
    "as it turns out , the model describes one scalar field in interaction with einstein s gravity .",
    "it possesses the vacuum solution @xmath165 , @xmath179 @xmath180 @xmath181 .",
    "technically , one would want the metric to be that of the standard model cosmology .",
    "ultimately , the geometry of the universe is chosen to fit the astronomical observations .",
    "the present work begins with the recapitulation of the concept of geometric sigma models .",
    "the construction of the generic model is presented , and subsequently applied to the homogeneous , isotropic and spatially flat geometry of the universe .",
    "then , the dynamics of small , localized perturbations of the vacuum is examined .",
    "it is demonstrated how all but two degrees of freedom can be gauged away .",
    "the classical stability of the gauge fixed linearized theory is proven in sec .",
    "this is done by direct calculation , as stability of the vacuum solution is not guaranteed by the very construction of geometric sigma models .",
    "the stability analysis concludes the general considerations of the paper . as a result",

    "<S> geometric sigma model is a purely geometric theory in which spacetime coordinates are seen as scalar fields coupled to gravity . </S>",
    "<S> although it looks like ordinary sigma model , it has the peculiarity that its complete matter content can be gauged away . </S>",
    "<S> the remaining geometric theory possesses a vacuum solution that is predefined in the process of constructing the theory . </S>",
    "<S> the fact that vacuum configuration is specified in advance is another peculiarity of geometric sigma models . in this paper , </S>",
    "<S> i construct geometric sigma models based on the arbitrarily chosen geometry of the universe . </S>",
    "<S> whatever geometry is chosen , the dynamics of its small perturbations is shown to be classically stable . this way , any desirable metric is made a stable solution of a particular model . </S>",
    "<S> the inflationary universe and the standard model universe are considered as examples of how this is done in practice . </S>"
