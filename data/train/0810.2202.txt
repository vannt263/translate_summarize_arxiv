    "( 1971 ) which shows flares with a period of 16.55 days , observed first in the x - ray band ( kaluzienski et al .",

    "1977 , haynes et al . 1978 ; cyg x-3 and ss433 are brighter in radio but their nature is unclear ) , showing extended structures both at arcmin and arcsec scale .",

    "<S> furthermore , a recent paper has shown an x - ray excess on arcmin - scale prominent on the side of the receding radio jet . in our images </S>",
    "<S> we clearly detect x - ray structures both on the side of the receding and the approaching radio jet . </S>",
    "<S> the x - ray emission is consistent with being from synchrotron origin . </S>",
    "<S> our detection is consistent with neutron - star binaries being as efficient as black - hole binaries in producing x - ray outflows , despite their shallower gravitational potential .    </S>",
