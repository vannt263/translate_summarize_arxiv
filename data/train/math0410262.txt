    "<S> periodic points are points on veech surfaces , whose orbit under the group of affine diffeomorphisms is finite . we characterise those points as being torsion points if the veech surfaces is suitably mapped to its jacobian or an appropriate factor thereof . for a primitive veech surface in genus two </S>",
    "<S> we show that the only periodic points are the weierstra  points and the singularities . </S>",
    "<S> our main tool is the hodge - theoretic characterisation of teichmller curves . </S>",
    "<S> we deduce from it a finiteness result for the mordell - weil group of the family of jacobians over a teichmller curve . </S>"
