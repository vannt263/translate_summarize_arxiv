    "<S> we study open - closed orbifold gromov - witten invariants of 3-dimensional calabi - yau smooth toric dm stacks with respect to lagrangian branes of aganagic - vafa type . </S>",
    "<S> we prove an open mirror theorem for toric calabi - yau 3-orbifolds , which expresses generating functions of orbifold disk invariants in terms of abel - jacobi maps of the mirror curves . </S>",
    "<S> this generalizes a conjecture by aganagic - vafa and aganagic - klemm - vafa , proved by the first and the second authors , on disk invariants of smooth toric calabi - yau 3-folds . </S>"
