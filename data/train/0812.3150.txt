    "<S> i.e. _  for dispersive and absorptive features in the propagation of polarized light along a transverse magnetic field in vacuum . </S>",
    "<S> in this note we calculate the standard model contributions to these signatures , thereby illuminating the discovery potential of such experiments in the search for new physics . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the three main sources for a standard model contribution to a dichroism signal : photon splitting , neutrino pair production and production of gravitons . </S>"
