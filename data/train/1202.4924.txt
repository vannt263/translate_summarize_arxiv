    "<S> unconventional quantum states of ultra - cold fermi gases have attracted a lot of attention in the recent theoretical and experimental studies . among them , one of the most desirable states is the topological superfluid @xmath0 phase in a two - dimensional ( 2d ) single - component fermi gas . </S>",
    "<S> the zero - energy majorana modes were predicted to appear inside vertex cores in this p - wave state @xcite . due to their insensitivity to local perturbations , </S>",
    "<S> majorana fermions are very appealing for applications in topologically - protected quantum computation @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> the p - wave superfluid phase was predicted to appear near a p - wave feshback resonance in a fermi gas @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however the lifetime of the fermi gas is severely limited by three - body collisions , and so far this superfluid regime has not been achieved in experiments . </S>",

    "<S> the @xmath0 topological superfluid state can be created in this dipolar fermi gas at low temperatures due to the attractive interaction . </S>",
