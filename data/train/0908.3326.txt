    "<S> we study the dynamics of complex polynomials . we obtain results on poincar return maps defined on certain neighborhoods of a point with bounded orbit under a polynomial . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce a generalization of the yoccoz tau - function , the _ yoccoz return function _ , which codes the returns of a critical point with bounded orbit of any complex polynomial with a disconnect julia set . </S>",
    "<S> we give necessary conditions on yoccoz return functions , which allow for the recursive definition of an abstract tau - function . </S>",
    "<S> these conditions are also sufficient for polynomials that have a disconnected julia set and exactly one critical point with bounded orbit .    </S>",
