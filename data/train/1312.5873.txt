    "<S> we consider the problem of minimizing a fixed - degree polynomial over the standard simplex . </S>",
    "<S> this problem is well known to be np - hard , since it contains the maximum stable set problem in combinatorial optimization as a special case . in this paper , we revisit a known upper bound obtained by taking the minimum value on a regular grid , and a known lower bound based on plya s representation theorem . </S>",
    "<S> more precisely , we consider the difference between these two bounds and we provide upper bounds for this difference in terms of the range of function values . </S>",
    "<S> our results refine the known upper bounds in the quadratic and cubic cases , and they asymptotically refine the known upper bound in the general case . </S>"
