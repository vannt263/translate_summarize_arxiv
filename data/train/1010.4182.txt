    "<S> it is shown that for a wide class of time series , with proper centering and scaling , the maximum deviations of kernel density and regression estimates are asymptotically gumbel . </S>",

    "<S> the asymptotic results can be applied to assess patterns of marginal densities or regression functions via the construction of simultaneous confidence bands for which one can perform goodness - of - fit tests . as an application </S>",
