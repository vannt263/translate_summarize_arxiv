    "<S> we find two types of solutions , distinguished by their holographic free energies . in one type </S>",
    "<S> the holographic free energy is constant , whereas in another type it depends in a simple way on the parameters and is generically complex . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to a conjecture for the localized partition function of a class of @xmath2 supersymmetric gauge theories on these backgrounds .    </S>",
