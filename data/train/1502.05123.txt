    "<S> miller and mocanu proved in their 1997 paper a greatly useful result which is now known as the open door lemma . </S>",
    "<S> it provides a sufficient condition for an analytic function on the unit disk to have positive real part . </S>",
    "<S> kuroki and owa modified the lemma when the initial point is non - real . in the present note , by extending their methods , we give a sufficient condition for an analytic function on the unit disk to take its values in a given sector . </S>"
