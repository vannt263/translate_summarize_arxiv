    "<S> the beam energy scan ( bes ) program is being pursued at rhic to study the qcd phase diagram , and search for the possible qcd phase boundary and possible qcd critical point . </S>",
    "<S> the data for phase - i of the bes program have been collected for au+au collisions at center - of - mass energies ( @xmath0 ) of 7.7 , 11.5 , 19.6 , 27 , and 39 gev . </S>",
    "<S> these collision energies allowed the star experiment to cover a wide range of baryon chemical potential @xmath1 ( 100400 mev ) in the qcd phase diagram . </S>",
    "<S> we report on several interesting results from the bes phase - i covering the high net - baryon density region . </S>",
    "<S> these results shed light on particle production mechanism and freeze - out conditions , first - order phase transition and `` turn - off '' of qgp signatures , and existence of a critical point in the phase diagram . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we give an outlook for the future bes phase - ii program and a possible fixed target program at star . </S>"
