    "<S> the expansion of kummer s hypergeometric function as a series of incomplete gamma functions is discussed , for real values of the parameters and of the variable . </S>",
    "<S> the error performed approximating the kummer function with a finite sum of gammas is evaluated analytically . </S>",
    "<S> bounds for it are derived , both pointwisely and uniformly in the variable ; these characterize the convergence rate of the series , both pointwisely and in appropriate sup norms . </S>",
    "<S> the same analysis shows that finite sums of very few gammas are sufficiently close to the kummer function . </S>",
    "<S> the combination of these results with the known approximation methods for the incomplete gammas allows to construct upper and lower approximants for the kummer function using only exponentials , real powers and rational functions . </S>",
    "<S> illustrative examples are provided .    </S>",
