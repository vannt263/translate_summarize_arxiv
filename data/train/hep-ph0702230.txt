    "in recent years a isospin chemical potential has been introduced into the study for qcd phase structure  @xcite .",
    "it allows one to have a new dimension to study the rich phases of qcd theory . when compared to the baryon chemical , this isospin chemical potential in principle has no fermion sign problem on a lattice simulation  @xcite . from recent lattice calculation and the investigation of chiral perturbation theory at finite isospin chemical potential",
    ", it has been indicated that there will be a pion condensation or pion superfluid phase  @xcite . at finite temperature and finite isospin density",
    "it is in essence a bose - einstein condensation ( bec ) of charged pion in momentum space .",
    "this kind phenomenon has been also studied by using njl model  @xcite and ladder qcd  @xcite .    in the low energy",
    "effective models , the linear sigma model is very simple and illustrative .",
    "when only considering the mesonic part of the model , there are only four scalar fields , the sigma field and the usually three pion fields which display a o(4 ) symmetry .",
    "the model is so called o(4 ) linear sigma model .",
    "it has been usually used to study the chiral phase transition and it is also well suited for describing the physics of meson  @xcite .",
    "this model has been well studied at finite temperature within the cornwall - jackiw - tomboulis ( cjt ) formalism  @xcite . in our previous work",
    ", we have introduced the isospin chemical potential into the linear sigma model and discussed the bec of pions and its relation to the chiral phase transition in the chiral limit within the same formalism  @xcite . in this paper",
