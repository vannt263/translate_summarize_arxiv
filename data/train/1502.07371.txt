    "<S> the sasa - satsuma equation is an integrable higher - order nonlinear schrdinger equation . </S>",
    "<S> higher - order and multicomponent generalisations of the nonlinear schrdinger equation are important in various applications , e.g. , in optics . </S>",
    "<S> one of these equations is the sasa - satsuma equation . </S>",
    "<S> we present the binary darboux transformations for the sasa - satsuma equation and then construct its quasigrammians solutions by iterating its binary darboux transformations . </S>",
    "<S> periodic , one - soliton , two - solitons and breather solutions are given as explicit examples . </S>"
