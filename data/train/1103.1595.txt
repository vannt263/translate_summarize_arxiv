    "<S> let the adiabatic invariant of action variable in slow - fast hamiltonian system with two degrees of freedom have two limiting values along the trajectories as time tends to infinity . </S>",
    "<S> the difference of two limits is exponentially small in analytic systems . </S>",
    "<S> an iso - energetic reduction and canonical transformations are applied to transform the slow - fast systems to form of systems depending on slowly varying parameters in a complexified phase space . on the basis of this method </S>",
    "<S> an estimate for the accuracy of conservation of adiabatic invariant is given for such systems . </S>"
