    "<S> we study a class of random transformations built over finitely many intermittent maps sharing a _ common _ indifferent fixed point . using a young - tower technique , </S>",
    "<S> we show that the map with the fastest relaxation rate dominates the asymptotics . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we prove that the rate of correlation decay for the annealed dynamics of the random map is the same as the _ sharp rate _ of correlation decay for the map with the fastest relaxation rate . </S>"
