    "<S> in this article we propose the calculation of the unconditional wiener measure functional integral with a term of the fourth order in the exponent by an alternative method as in the conventional perturbative approach . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to the conventional perturbation theory , we expand into power series the term linear in the integration variable in the exponent . in such a case </S>",
    "<S> we can profit from the representation of the integral in question by the parabolic cylinder functions . </S>",
    "<S> we show that in such a case the series expansions are uniformly convergent and we find recurrence relations for the wiener functional integral in the @xmath0 - dimensional approximation . in continuum limit we find that the generalized gelfand - yaglom differential equation with solution yields the desired functional integral ( similarly as the standard gelfand - yaglom differential equation yields the functional integral for linear harmonic oscillator ) . </S>"
