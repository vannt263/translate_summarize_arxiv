    "<S> we consider changes in properties of a subgraph of an infinite graph resulting from the addition of open edges of bernoulli percolation on the infinite graph to the subgraph . </S>",
    "<S> we give the triplet of an infinite graph , one of its subgraphs , and a property of the subgraphs . </S>",
    "<S> then , in a manner similar to the way hammersley s critical probability is defined , we can define two values associated with the triplet . </S>",
    "<S> we regard the two values as certain critical probabilities , and compare them with hammersley s critical probability . in this paper , we focus on the following cases of a graph property : being a transient subgraph , having finitely many cut points or no cut points , being a recurrent subset , or being connected . </S>",
    "<S> our results depend heavily on the choice of the triplet . </S>"
