    "<S> a multivariate polynomial is _ stable _ if it is nonvanishing whenever all variables have positive imaginary parts . </S>",
    "<S> we classify all linear partial differential operators in the weyl algebra @xmath0 that preserve stability . </S>",
    "<S> an important tool that we develop in the process is the higher dimensional generalization of plya - schur s notion of multiplier sequence . </S>",
    "<S> we characterize all multivariate multiplier sequences as well as those of finite order . </S>",
    "<S> next , we establish a multivariate extension of the cauchy - poincar interlacing theorem and prove a natural analog of the lax conjecture for real stable polynomials in two variables . using the latter </S>",
    "<S> we describe all operators in @xmath1 that preserve univariate hyperbolic polynomials by means of determinants and homogenized symbols . </S>",
    "<S> our methods also yield homotopical properties for symbols of linear stability preservers and a duality theorem showing that an operator in @xmath0 preserves stability if and only if its fischer - fock adjoint does . </S>",
    "<S> these are powerful multivariate extensions of the classical hermite - poulain - jensen theorem , plya s curve theorem and schur - mal - szeg composition theorems . </S>",
    "<S> examples and applications to strict stability preservers are also discussed . </S>"
