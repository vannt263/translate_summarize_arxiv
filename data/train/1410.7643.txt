    "<S> in this paper , we discuss long - time behavior of sample paths for a wide range of regime - switching diffusions . </S>",
    "<S> firstly , almost sure asymptotic stability is concerned ( i ) for regime - switching diffusions with finite state spaces by the perron - frobenius theorem , and , with regard to the case of reversible markov chain , via the principal eigenvalue approach ; ( ii ) for regime - switching diffusions with countable state spaces by means of a finite partition trick and an m - matrix theory . </S>",
    "<S> we then apply our theory to study the stabilization for linear switching models . </S>",
    "<S> several examples are given to demonstrate our theory .    </S>",
