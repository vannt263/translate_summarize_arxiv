    "<S> one of the key parameters that characterize spiral arms in disk galaxies is a pitch angle that measures the inclination of a spiral arm to the direction of galactic rotation . </S>",
    "<S> the pitch angle differs from galaxy to galaxy , which suggests that the rotation law of galactic disks determines it . in order to investigate the relation between the pitch angle of spiral arms and the shear rate of galactic differential rotation , </S>",
    "<S> we perform local @xmath0-body simulations of pure stellar disks . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the pitch angle increases with the epicycle frequency and decreases with the shear rate and obtain the fitting formula . </S>",
    "<S> this dependence is explained by the swing amplification mechanism . </S>"
