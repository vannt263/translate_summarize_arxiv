    "<S> topological states that exhibit topologically protected excitations and gapless edge modes have attracted much attention in recent years  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the spin - orbit ( so ) coupling plays essential roles in these novel quantum states of solids , such as quantum spin - hall effect  @xcite and majorana fermions  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recently , the experimental realizations of the raman - induced one - dimensional ( 1d ) so coupling in ultracold atomic gases @xcite offer a new paradigm for exploring a variety of topological states , including the gapped topological insulators / superfluids or gapless topological semimetal ( tsm ) states with unprecedented controllability  @xcite . ignoring collisional interactions , a rich variety of interesting topological phenomena have been experimentally observed with ultracold atomic gases in optical lattices , including the su - schrieffer - heeger model  @xcite , hofstadter model  @xcite , haldane model  @xcite , synthetic dimensions  @xcite , and topological charge pumping  @xcite , except for the gapless tsm states which remain to be experimentally demonstrated . </S>",

    "<S> the inhomogeneous ff superfluid is characterized by cooper pairs carrying a nonzero single - valued center - of - mass ( com ) momentum . </S>",
    "<S> unfortunately , the complete rashba so type is difficult to synthesize with cold - atoms , albeit some related recent experimental advances  @xcite . </S>",
