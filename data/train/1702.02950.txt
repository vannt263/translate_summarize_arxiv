    "a kinetic theory is a common framework for studying a wide range of physical properties in condensed matter materials , nuclear physics , and cosmology @xcite .",
    "a relativistic version of such a theory is invaluable in studies of numerous relativistic forms of matter , e.g. , realized in the primordial plasma of the early universe @xcite , relativistic heavy - ion collisions @xcite , compact stars @xcite , as well as the dirac @xcite and weyl @xcite materials .",
    "the corresponding forms of matter are often made of ( approximately ) massless fermionic particles that carry a well - defined chirality .",
    "therefore , the chirality could be treated as a natural additional degree of freedom in a relativistic plasma .",
    "it should be noted , however , that the conservation of the chiral charge is violated in the presence of external electromagnetic fields .",
    "this is a consequence of the celebrated chiral ( triangle ) anomaly @xcite .",
    "nevertheless , this anomaly can be incorporated exactly in the chiral kinetic theory @xcite .",
    "the only limitation of the current formulation of the corresponding theory is that it is valid only to the linear order in electromagnetic fields .",
    "the presence of a chiral chemical potential @xmath0 , which quantifies a chiral asymmetry in a relativistic matter , significantly enriches the properties and types of collective excitations .",
    "the investigation of the corresponding features began in refs .",
    "the authors of ref .",
    "@xcite showed that the triangle anomaly implies the existence of a novel type of collective excitation which stems from the coupling between the density waves of the electric and chiral charges and is known as the chiral magnetic wave ( cmw ) . in this connection , let us also mention that , as was shown in ref .",
    "@xcite , a nonzero chiral chemical potential lifts the degeneracy of the transverse plasma modes , but leaves the longitudinal mode intact .",
    "such a conclusion agrees with a refined analysis in the consistent chiral kinetic theory @xcite .",
    "dirac and weyl materials provide additional opportunities to explore the role of chirality . for example , they allow for a simple realization of axial electric ( or pseudoelectric ) @xmath1 and/or axial magnetic ( or pseudomagnetic ) @xmath2 fields .",
    "these pseudoelectromagnetic fields act on fermions as ordinary electromagnetic fields , but their sign depends on the fermion chirality . in weyl and dirac materials , one can induce a background pseudomagnetic field @xmath2 by various kinds of static strains @xcite . note that , unlike the ordinary magnetic field , a pseudomagnetic field does not break the time reversal symmetry .",
    "in essence , this is due to the fact that weyl nodes in condensed matter materials always come in pairs of opposite chirality @xcite .",
    "a pseudoelectric @xmath1 can be induced by time - dependent strains @xcite .",
    "recently , we showed @xcite that the plasmons in a relativistic matter in constant magnetic and pseudomagnetic fields are , in fact , _ chiral _",
    "( pseudo-)magnetic plasmons .",
    "their chiral nature is manifested by oscillations of the chiral charge density , which are absent for ordinary electromagnetic plasmons .",
    "it is also worth noting that , in the presence of magnetic fields , the quasiparticle plasma in uncompensated metals supports a special type of collective excitations known as helicons .",
    "these transverse low - energy gapless excitations propagate along the background magnetic field . according to the studies in ref .",
    "@xcite , the dispersion relation of helicons encodes information on the chiral shift parameter @xmath3 , which defines the momentum space separation of weyl nodes .",
    "further , by using the formalism of the consistent chiral kinetic theory , it was shown in ref .",
    "@xcite that pseudomagnetic fields allow for a new type of helicons , which we called pseudomagnetic helicons .",
    "although the chiral kinetic theory is quite successful in the description of various processes in relativistic plasma , many physical phenomena require its formulation accurate to the second order in electromagnetic fields . recently , using the wave - packet semiclassical approach @xcite ( for a review , see ref .",
    "@xcite ) , all necessary ingredients for such a formulation were provided in refs .",

    "by making use of the second - order corrections in ( pseudo-)electromagnetic fields to the quasiparticle energy as well as the first - order corrections to the berry curvature , we derived a consistent chiral kinetic theory valid to the second order in the electromagnetic and pseudoelectromagnetic fields for the simplest model of relativistic matter with two weyl fermions .",
    "we found that , even with the non - trivial corrections included , the berry curvature @xmath26 still defines a monopole - type vector field in the momentum space which corresponds to a unit of topological charge .",
    "therefore , the chiral anomaly relation retains its canonical form in the second - order formulation of the chiral kinetic theory .    using the second - order consistent chiral kinetic theory , we analyzed the spectrum of longitudinal collective modes propagating along the direction of background magnetic and pseudomagnetic fields .",
    "we showed that , in the presence of a pseudomagnetic field , there is a new type of collective excitations similar to the chiral magnetic wave , which we call _ the chiral pseudomagnetic wave_. the effects of dynamical electromagnetism play an important role and transform the chiral magnetic and chiral pseudomagnetic waves into plasmons with special properties .",
    "the latter manifest themselves in the oscillations of the chiral current density , which are absent in the case of ordinary electromagnetic plasmons .",
    "we found that the plasmon gaps receive corrections quadratic in magnetic and pseudomagnetic fields , which , however , are estimated to be rather weak compared to the effects of dynamical electromagnetism .",
    "in addition , the coefficients in front of odd powers of the wave vector in the dispersion relation of the chiral pseudomagnetic wave are non - zero and proportional to the background pseudomagnetic field .",
    "the coefficients in front of even powers depend on the square of both magnetic and pseudomagnetic fields . nevertheless , the leading contributions to these coefficients are due to the effects of dynamical electromagnetism",
    ". it would be interesting to investigate how these conclusions about the cmw and cpmw change in the case of strong background magnetic and/or pseudomagnetic fields , and whether the effects of dynamical electromagnetism could be negligible .",
