    "<S> spin - weighted spheroidal harmonics are useful in a variety of physical situations , including light scattering , nuclear modeling , signal processing , electromagnetic wave propagation , black hole perturbation theory in four and higher dimensions , quantum field theory in curved space - time and studies of d - branes . </S>",
    "<S> we first review analytic and numerical calculations of their eigenvalues and eigenfunctions in four dimensions , filling gaps in the existing literature when necessary . </S>",
    "<S> then we compute the angular dependence of the spin - weighted spheroidal harmonics corresponding to slowly - damped quasinormal mode frequencies of the kerr black hole , providing numerical tables and approximate formulas for their scalar products . </S>",
    "<S> finally we present an exhaustive analytic and numerical study of scalar spheroidal harmonics in ( @xmath0 ) dimensions . </S>"
