    "<S> two separate statistical tests are applied to the agasa and preliminary auger cosmic ray energy spectra in an attempt to find deviation from a pure power - law . </S>",
    "<S> the first test is constructed from the probability distribution for the maximum event of a sample drawn from a power - law . </S>",
    "<S> the second employs the tp - statistic , a function defined to deviate from zero when the sample deviates from the power - law form , regardless of the value of the power index . </S>",
    "<S> the agasa data show no significant deviation from a power - law when subjected to both tests . applying these tests to the auger spectrum </S>",
    "<S> suggests deviation from a power - law . </S>",
    "<S> however , potentially large systematics on the relative energy scale prevent us from drawing definite conclusions at this time .    high energy cosmic ray flux  </S>",
