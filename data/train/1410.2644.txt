    "<S> in the present paper we give a proof of the fact that the sub - riemannian cut locus of a wide class of nilpotent groups of step two , called @xmath0-type groups , starting from the origin corresponds to the center of the group . </S>",
    "<S> we obtain this result by completely describing the sub - riemannian geodesics in the group , and using these to obtain three disjoint sets of points in the group determined by the number of geodesics joining them to the origin . </S>"
