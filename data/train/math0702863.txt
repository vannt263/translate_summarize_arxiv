    "<S> we continue the study of such flat fronts in this paper . </S>",
    "<S> first , we introduce the notion of derived schwarz maps of the hypergeometric differential equation and , second , we construct a parallel family of flat fronts connecting the classical schwarz map and the derived schwarz map . </S>"
