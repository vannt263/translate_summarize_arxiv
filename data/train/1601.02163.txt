    "layered transition metal dichalcogenides mx@xmath0 , m = mo , w , x = s , se , te , are widely explored because of their unique properties and their potential for applications in electronic devices.@xcite mx@xmath0  monolayers are direct band gap semiconductors with band gaps in the range of 1 - 2 ev , which have appealing electronic and optoelectronic properties.@xcite mx@xmath0  layers can be obtained via micro - mechanical cleaving,@xcite by chemical vapor deposition ( cvd),@xcite or even by spin coating precursor molecules.@xcite important for applications in devices is the ability to have both electron ( n - type ) and hole ( p - type ) transport in these 2d materials .",
    "charge carrier transport in mx@xmath0  field - effect transistors ( fets ) is usually dominated by electrons ; p - type transport has only been demonstrated in wse@xmath0.@xcite    a major challenge for p - type transport is that mx@xmath0  forms a large schottky barrier ( sb ) for holes with metals commonly used for making electrical contacts . a standard way to reduce",
    "a metal / semiconductor contact resistance is to heavily dope the semiconductor in the contact region , which effectively decreases the sb width .",
    "local doping of a 2d semiconductor is however very challenging ; so far most techniques used for doping 2d materials , such as substitutional doping,@xcite adsorbed molecules@xcite , or electrolytes@xcite , have a limited spatial resolution .",
    "alternatively one tries to decrease the sb height , essentially by covering the metal by a thin layer to increase its work function .",
    "oxides have shown their potential for p - type contacts in organic photovoltaics and light - emitting diodes,@xcite and have also been tested in mos@xmath0  fets.@xcite oxides have also been applied succesfully to reduce the sb height for n - type contacts to mos@xmath0.@xcite    common metals generally give n - type contacts with substantial sb heights , leading to high contact resistances .",
