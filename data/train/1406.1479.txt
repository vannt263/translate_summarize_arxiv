    "cavity - optomechanics deals with the interaction of an optical field in a resonator with confining mirrors  @xcite .",
    "recent experimental advances make it possible to couple cold atoms and bose - einstein condensates ( bec ) , mechanical membrane and nano - sphere with the optomechanical systems @xcite .",
    "hence , these hybrid - opto mechanical systems are playground to study phenomena related to mirror - field interaction , and atom - field interaction which provide founding principles to develop numerous sensors , and devices in quantum metrology  @xcite . in opto - mechanics the mechanical effects of light lead to cooling the motion of a movable mirror to its quantum mechanical ground state @xcite , and to observe strong coupling effects  @xcite . in earlier work",
    "optomechanical systems were proposed precision measurement in gravitational wave detectors  @xcite and to measure displacement with large accuracy  @xcite , and recently the development of optomechanical crystals  @xcite .",
    "recent theoretical discussions and simulations on bistable behavior of bec - optomechanical system @xcite , high fidelity state transfer @xcite , steady - state entanglement of bec and moving end mirror  @xcite , macroscopic tunneling of an optomechanical membrane @xcite and role reversal between matter - wave and quantized light field are guiding towards new avenues in cavity optomechanics . in this submission",
    "we report the classical and quantum dynamics in position and momentum space and show that dispersion is suppressed in quantum domain while classical  @xcite counterpart increases with time following a power - law .",
    "this contrasting behavior is an analog of anderson localization and termed as dynamical localization or exponential localization  @xcite .",
    "dynamical localization is studied in periodically driven nonlinear systems @xcite and provides quantum mechanical limits on classical diffusion of wave packet .",
    "the phenomenon of dynamical localization emerges in the explicitly time dependent system .",

    "<S> we discuss the dynamics of moving end mirror of an optomechanical system that consists of a fabry - perot cavity loaded with dilute condensate and driven by a single mode optical field . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that quantum mechanical phenomenon of dynamical localization occurs both in position and momentum space for moving end mirror in the system . </S>",
    "<S> the parametric dependencies of dynamical localization are discussed . </S>",
    "<S> we also provide a set of parameters which makes this phenomenon experimentally feasible . </S>"
