    "<S> based on that tcm a spectrum hard component was isolated that is related quantitatively to pqcd predictions for jet fragmentation down to low jet energies ( @xmath0 gev ) . we here address jet - related structure in 2d trigger - associated ( ta ) correlations as a more - detailed method to explore the kinematic limits of low - energy jet production and low - momentum jet fragment structure in  collisions . </S>",
    "<S> we derive a tcm for  ta correlations that can be used to isolate 2d jet - related structure . </S>",
    "<S> inferred minimum - bias ( mainly low - energy ) jet - related ta correlations may challenge several major assumptions about jet production in  ( and  ) collisions . </S>",
    "<S> these results should be relevant to  underlying - event studies and monte carlo predictions of multiple parton interactions . </S>"
