    "<S> we systematically investigate the farthest distance function , farthest points , klee sets , and chebyshev centers , with respect to bregman distances induced by legendre functions . </S>",
    "<S> these objects are of considerable interest in information geometry and machine learning ; when the legendre function is specialized to the energy , one obtains classical notions from approximation theory and convex analysis .    </S>",
    "<S> the contribution of this paper is twofold . </S>",
    "<S> first , we provide an affirmative answer to a recently - posed question on whether or not every klee set with respect to the right bregman distance is a singleton . </S>",
    "<S> second , we prove uniqueness of the chebyshev center and we present a characterization that relates to previous works by garkavi , by klee , and by nielsen and nock .    * 2000 mathematics subject classification : * primary 41a65 , 46b20 ; secondary 47h05 , 49j52 , 49j53 , 90c25 .    </S>",
