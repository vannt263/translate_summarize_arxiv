    "<S> we consider a massless scalar field in 1 + 1 dimensions satisfying a robin boundary condition ( bc ) at a non - relativistic moving boundary . </S>",
    "<S> we derive a bogoliubov transformation between input and output bosonic field operators , which allows us to calculate the spectral distribution of created particles . </S>",
    "<S> the cases of dirichlet and neumann bc may be obtained from our result as limiting cases . </S>",
    "<S> these two limits yield the same spectrum , which turns out to be an upper bound for the spectra derived for robin bc . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the particle emission effect can be considerably reduced ( with respect to the dirichlet / neumann case ) by selecting a particular value for the oscillation frequency of the boundary position . </S>"
