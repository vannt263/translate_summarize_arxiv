    "<S> the dynamics of many - body systems spanning condensed matter , cosmology , and beyond is hypothesized to be universal when the systems cross continuous phase transitions . </S>",
    "<S> the universal dynamics is expected to satisfy a scaling symmetry of space and time with the crossing rate , inspired by the kibble - zurek mechanism . </S>",
    "<S> we test this symmetry based on bose condensates in a shaken optical lattice . </S>",
    "<S> shaking the lattice drives condensates across an effectively ferromagnetic quantum phase transition . after crossing the critical point , </S>",
    "<S> the condensates manifest delayed growth of spin fluctuations and develop anti - ferromagnetic spatial correlations resulting from sub - poisson generation of topological defects . </S>",
    "<S> the characteristic times and lengths scale as power - laws of the crossing rate , yielding the temporal exponent 0.50(2 ) and the spatial exponent 0.26(2 ) , consistent with theory . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , the fluctuations and correlations are invariant in scaled space - time coordinates , in support of the scaling symmetry of quantum critical dynamics .    </S>",
    "<S> critical phenomena near a continuous phase transition reveal fascinating connections between seemingly disparate systems that can be described via the same universal principles . </S>",
    "<S> examples exist in superfluid helium @xcite , liquid crystals @xcite , biological cell membranes @xcite , the early universe @xcite , and cold atoms @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> an important prediction is the power - law scaling of the topological defect density with the rate of crossing a critical point , as first discussed by t.  kibble in cosmology @xcite and extended by w.  zurek in the context of condensed matter @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> their theory , known as the kibble - zurek mechanism , has been the subject of intense experimental study that has largely supported the scaling laws @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recent theoretical works further propose the universality hypothesis that the collective dynamics across a critical point should be invariant in the space and time coordinates that scale with the kibble - zurek power - law @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> atomic quantum gases provide a clean , well - characterized , and controlled platform for studying critical dynamics @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> they have enabled experiments on the formation of topological defects across the bose - einstein condensation transition @xcite as well as critical dynamics across quantum phase transitions @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recent experiments using cold atoms in shaken optical lattices @xcite have provided a new vehicle for exploring phase transitions in spin models @xcite .    in this report </S>",
    "<S> we study the critical dynamics of bose condensates in a shaken optical lattice crossing an effectively ferromagnetic quantum phase transition . </S>",
    "<S> the transition occurs when we ramp the shaking amplitude across a critical value , causing the atomic population to bifurcate into two pseudo - spinor ground states @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we measure the growth of spin fluctuations and the spatial spin correlations for ramping rates varied over two orders of magnitude . beyond the critical point </S>",
    "<S> we observe delayed development of spin domains with anti - ferromagnetic correlations , a distinctive feature of the non - equilibrium dynamics . </S>",
