    "among the challenges of the glass transition is how solidity emerges with little apparent change in structure  @xcite . however , using computer simulation and with the advent of particle - resolved studies in colloid experiments  @xcite , it has become possible to construct and use higher - order correlation functions  @xcite .",
    "these can directly identify local geometric motifs in supercooled liquids , long - since thought to suppress crystalisation in glassforming systems  @xcite .",
    "other indirect approaches include the use of reverse monte carlo techniques to extract higher - order information from two - point correlation functions  @xcite which is used in metallic glassformers for example  @xcite .",
    "such measurements have correlated the occurrence of geometric motifs and slow dynamics in a number of glassformers in both particle - resolved experiments on colloids  @xcite and simulation  @xcite .",
    "identification of these motifs has led to the tantalising prospect of finding a structural mechanism for dynamic arrest .",
    "it has been demonstrated that at sufficient supercooling , there should be a coincidence in structural and dynamic lengths , associated with regions undergoing relaxation for fragile glassformers  @xcite .",
    "thus recent years have seen a considerable effort devoted to identifying dynamic and structural lengthscales in a range of glassformers .",
    "the jury remains out concerning the coincidence of structural and dynamic lengthscales , with some investigations finding agreement between dynamic and structural lengthscales in experiment  @xcite and simulation  @xcite , while others find that the while the dynamic lengthscale increases quite strongly , structural correlation lengths grow weakly if at all  @xcite .",
    "other interpretations include decomposing the system into geometric motifs and considering the motif system .",
    "one such effective system exhibits no glass transition at finite temperature  @xcite .",
    "here we consider the approach of geometric frustration  @xcite .",

    "<S> we consider three popular model glassformers , the kob - andersen and wahnstrm binary lennard - jones models and weakly polydisperse hard spheres . </S>",
    "<S> although these systems exhibit a range of fragilities , all feature a rather similar behaviour in their local structure approaching dynamic arrest . </S>",
    "<S> in particular we use the dynamic topological cluster classification to extract a locally favoured structure which is particular to each system . these structures form percolating networks , however in all cases there is a strong decoupling between structural and dynamic lengthscales . </S>",
    "<S> we suggest that the lack of growth of the structural lengthscale may be related to strong geometric frustration . </S>"
