    "<S> we in this paper derive the analytical expressions of ground - state energy , average photon - number , and the atomic population by means of the spin - coherent - state variational method for arbitrary number of atoms in an optomechanical cavity . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that the existence of mechanical oscillator does not affect the phase boundary between the normal and superradiant phases . </S>",
    "<S> however , the superradiant phase collapses by the resonant damping of the oscillator when the atom - field coupling increases to a so - called turning point . as a consequence the system undergoes at this point an additional phase transition from the superradiant phase to a new normal phase of the atomic population - inversion state . </S>",
    "<S> the region of superradiant phase decreases with the increase of photon - phonon coupling . </S>",
    "<S> it shrinks to zero at a critical value of the coupling and a direct atomic population transfer appears between two atom - levels . </S>",
    "<S> moreover we find an unstable nonzero - photon state , which is the counterpart of the superradiant state . in the absence of oscillator </S>",
    "<S> our result reduces exactly to that of dicke model . </S>",
    "<S> particularly the ground - state energy for @xmath0 ( i.e. the rabi model ) is in perfect agreement with the numerical diagonalization in a wide region of coupling constant for both red and blue detuning . </S>",
    "<S> the dicke phase transition remains for the rabi model in agreement with the recent observation . </S>"
