    "<S> we provide a systematic study of the two possible douglas  rachford operators . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the reflectors of the underlying operators act as bijections between the fixed points sets of the two douglas  rachford operators . </S>",
    "<S> some elegant formulae arise under additional assumptions . </S>",
