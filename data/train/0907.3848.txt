    "<S> it is found that , as a rule , the correlators are to be time - ordered in an unusual way . </S>",
    "<S> instances where the difference with the conventional ordering matters are illustrated by means of a simple scattering model . </S>",
    "<S> we apply the results to resolve a discrepancy concerning the third cumulant of charge transport across a tunnel junction . </S>"
