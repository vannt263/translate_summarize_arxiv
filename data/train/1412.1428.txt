    "rydberg atoms have been studied extensively , yielding important information about atomic structure and ionization thresholds @xcite .",
    "rydberg atoms are attracting intense current interest in the area of quantum information processing due to their strong dipole - dipole interactions @xcite .",
    "rydberg dipole blockade provides a strong , switchable interaction between neutral atoms , allowing for the creation of quantum gates and entanglement@xcite .",
    "two - qubit gates have so far been demonstrated in the alkali atoms rubidium @xcite and cesium@xcite .",
    "there is further interest in using the rydberg blockade in ensembles of atoms to create a collectively encoded quantum register @xcite .",
    "collective encoding is most beneficial in atoms with a large number of ground hyperfine states @xcite .",
    "the stable atom with the largest number of hyperfine ground states is holmium .",
    "its nuclear spin is @xmath8 and the electronic angular momentum of the ground state is @xmath9 , providing 128 hyperfine ground states with total angular momentum @xmath10 .",
    "the large angular momentum arises due to the open @xmath11-shell in the ground state electronic configuration . as with other rare - earth elements ,",
    "this open shell structure results in an extremely complex energy spectrum that is challenging to reproduce theoretically due to strong relativistic effects and configuration interactions @xcite",
    ". for rydberg atom quantum information processing , knowledge of the rydberg levels is important for accurate prediction of the dipole - dipole interaction strengths and sensitivity to external fields .",
