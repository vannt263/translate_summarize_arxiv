    "<S> in extreme value statistics the peaks - over - threshold method is widely used . </S>",
    "<S> the method is based on the generalized pareto distribution ( @xcite , @xcite in univariate theory and e.g. @xcite , @xcite in multivariate theory ) characterizing probabilities of exceedances over high thresholds . </S>",
    "<S> we present a generalization of this concept in the space of continuous functions . </S>",
    "<S> we call this the generalized pareto process . differently from earlier papers , </S>",
    "<S> our definition is not based on a distribution function but on functional properties , and does not need any specification with the related max - stable process .    as an application we use the theory to simulate wind fields connected to disastrous storms on the basis of observed extreme but not disastrous storms .    </S>",
