    "<S> in this paper we define the shifted schur process as a measure on sequences of strict partitions . </S>",
    "<S> this process is a generalization of the shifted schur measure introduced in @xcite and @xcite and is a shifted version of the schur process introduced in @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we prove that the shifted schur process defines a pfaffian point process . </S>",
    "<S> we further apply this fact to compute the bulk scaling limit of the correlation functions for a measure on strict plane partitions which is an analog of the uniform measure on ordinary plane partitions . as a byproduct </S>",
    "<S> , we obtain a shifted analog of the famous macmahon s formula .     </S>",
