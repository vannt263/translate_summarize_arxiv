    "<S> cluster emergence of mixed polarities and in ephemeral regions ( ers ) are the most conspicuous forms of bipolar flux appearance within the network . </S>",
    "<S> each of the clusters is characterized by a few well - developed ers that are partially or fully co - aligned in magnetic axis orientation . on average , </S>",
    "<S> the sampled in ers have total maximum unsigned flux of several @xmath0 mx , separation of @xmath1 arcsec , and a lifetime of @xmath2 minutes . </S>",
    "<S> the smallest in ers have a maximum unsigned flux of several @xmath3 mx , separations less than 1 arcsec , and lifetimes as short as 5 minutes . </S>",
    "<S> most in ers exhibit a rotation of their magnetic axis of more than 10 degrees during flux emergence . </S>",
