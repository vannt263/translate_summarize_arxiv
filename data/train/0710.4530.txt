    "<S> this condition is motivated by the itep operator product expansion philosophy . allowed by the ambiguity in the analytization of the perturbative coupling , the proposed class of couplings has three parameters . in the intermediate energy region , </S>",
    "<S> the proposed coupling has low loop - level and renormalization scheme dependence . </S>",
    "<S> the present modification of perturbative qcd must be considered as a phenomenological attempt , with the aim of enlarging the applicability range of the theory of the strong interactions at low energies . </S>"
