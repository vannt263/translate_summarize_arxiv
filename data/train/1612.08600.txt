    "<S> we study the bi - hamiltonian structures for the hierarchy of a 3-component generalization of the degasperis - procesi ( 3-dp ) equation . </S>",
    "<S> we show that all hamiltonian functionals in the hierarchy are homogenous , and hamiltonian functionals of the hierarchy in the negative direction are local . </S>",
    "<S> we construct two different liouville transformations by construct a reciprocal transformation . </S>",
    "<S> the associated system for the first one is a reduction of a negative flow in a modified yajima - oikawa hierarchy . the associated system for the second one and the associated system for another 3-component ch type system under a equivalent transformation are shown to be different reductions of a negative generalized mkdv equation , besides the hamiltonian structures of the 3-dp equation under this liouville transformation are considered . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , we consider a limit for the 3-dp equation . </S>"
