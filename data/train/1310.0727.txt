    "<S> we consider in this note a class of two - dimensional determinantal coulomb gases confined by a radial external field . as </S>",
    "<S> the number of particles tends to infinity , their empirical distribution tends to a probability measure supported in a centered ring of the complex plane . </S>",
    "<S> a quadratic confinement corresponds to the complex ginibre ensemble . in this case </S>",
    "<S> , it is also already known that the asymptotic fluctuation of the radial edge follows a gumbel law . </S>",
    "<S> we establish in this note the universality of this edge behavior , beyond the quadratic case . </S>",
    "<S> the approach , inspired by earlier works of kostlan and rider , boils down to identities in law and to an instance of the laplace method . </S>"
