    "<S> we consider two disjoint and homotopic non - contractible embedded loops on a riemann surface and prove the existence of a non - contractible orbit for a hamiltonian function on the surface whenever it is sufficiently large on one of the loops and sufficiently small on the other one . </S>",
    "<S> this gives the first example of an estimate from above for a generalized form of the biran - polterovich - salamon capacity for a closed symplectic manifold . </S>"
