    "<S> we apply a stochastic differential equation , that we can decouple into a deterministic and stochatic part , while each part can be solved analytically . </S>",
    "<S> such decompositions allow accelerating the methods and preserving , under suitable conditions , the symplecticity of the schemes .    a numerical analysis and application to the stochastic schrdunger equation </S>",
