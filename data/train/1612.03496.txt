    "<S> we study the omega meson effect as well as the sigma meson effect at mean field level on the density dependence of the masses of heavy - light mesons with chiral partner structure . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that the omega meson affects the masses of the heavy - light mesons and their antiparticles in the opposite way , while it affects the masses of chiral partners in the same way . as a result , </S>",
    "<S> the mass difference between chiral partners is proportional to the mean field of sigma , reflecting the partial restoration of chiral symmetry in the nuclear matter . </S>",
    "<S> in addition to the general illustration of the density dependence of the heavy - light meson masses , we also consider two concrete models for nuclear matter , the parity doublet model and skyrmion crystal model in the sense of mean field approximation .    </S>",
