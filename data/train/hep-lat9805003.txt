    "<S> lattice qcd with additional chiral - invariant four - fermion interactions is studied at nonzero temperature . </S>",
    "<S> staggered kogut - susskind quarks are used . </S>",
    "<S> the four - fermion interactions are implemented by introducing bosonic auxiliary fields . </S>",
    "<S> a mean field treatment of the auxiliary fields is used to calculate the model s asymptotic scale parameter and perturbative thermodynamics , including the one - loop gluonic contributions to the energy , entropy , and pressure . in this approach </S>",
    "<S> the calculations reduce to those of ordinary lattice qcd with massive quarks . </S>",
    "<S> hence , the previous calculations of these quantities in lattice qcd using massless quarks are generalized to the massive case .    pertxqcd_fmf </S>"
