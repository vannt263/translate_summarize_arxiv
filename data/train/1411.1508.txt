    "to conclude , the conditions where the o - mode instability can operate efficiently are markedly constrained by the electrostatic instabilities in both the low @xmath52 and large @xmath0 plasmas .",
    "thus the existence of the o - mode instability becomes possible only for sufficiently high values of the plasma beta parameter in parallel direction @xmath94 .",
    "this restriction is particularly important for the low - beta plasmas , since the existence of this instability was claimed in the previous studies for any small value ( without limit ) of the parallel plasma beta @xmath95 .",
    "the instability of the o - mode can develop in the low - beta plasmas but only for @xmath96 and a streaming energy density less than the magnetic energy density ( @xmath97 ) .",
    "further comparison is now possible with the solar wind streaming conditions to establish whether this instability can explain the observed limits of the anisotropy or not . for a supra - unitary anisotropy @xmath98 , the stimulation of this instability by the energetic streams seems to be impossible ( or unrealistic ) in the low - beta plasmas .",
    "these situations appear to be resolved by a more realistic dissipation of the streaming free energy by the electrostatic instabilities .",
