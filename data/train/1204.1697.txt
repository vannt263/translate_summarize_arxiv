    "<S> introduction. _ topological states of matter have been the focus of intensive studies since the discovery of the integer quantum hall effect ( qhe )  @xcite and the fractional qhe ( fqhe )  @xcite . </S>",

    "<S> the precise quantization of hall conductance was found to be directly connected to a topological invariant chern number  @xcite soon after its experimental discovery . </S>",
    "<S> the fqhe is further characterized by quasi - particles with fractional charge  @xcite and fractional statistics  @xcite as well as topological ground - state degeneracy  @xcite , which are manifestations of its topological order @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the idea of flux attachment and composite - particle theory has provided a simple but profound picture of the fqhe  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> recently , a series of numerical works have demonstrated convincing evidence of the abelian  @xcite and non - abelian fqhes  @xcite in topological flat band ( tfb ) models  @xcite without an external magnetic field . </S>",
