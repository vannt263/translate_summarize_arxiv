    "<S> materials that realize kitaev spin models with bond - dependent anisotropic interactions have long been searched for , as the resulting frustration effects are predicted to stabilize novel forms of magnetic order or quantum spin liquids . here </S>",
    "<S> we explore the magnetism of @xmath0-li@xmath1iro@xmath2 , which has the topology of a 3d kitaev lattice of inter - connected ir honeycombs . using resonant magnetic x - ray diffraction </S>",
    "<S> we find a complex , yet highly - symmetric incommensurate magnetic structure with non - coplanar and counter - rotating ir moments . </S>",
    "<S> we propose a minimal kitaev - heisenberg hamiltonian that naturally accounts for all key features of the observed magnetic structure . </S>",
    "<S> our results provide strong evidence that @xmath0-li@xmath1iro@xmath2 realizes a spin hamiltonian with dominant kitaev interactions .    </S>",
    "<S> magnetic materials with bond - dependent anisotropic interactions are candidates to display novel forms of magnetic order or quantum spin liquid states , as exemplified by the kitaev model on the honeycomb lattice @xcite . here </S>",
    "<S> all spins interact via nearest - neighbor ising exchanges , but a different ising axis ( @xmath3 ) applies for the three different bonds emerging out of each lattice site . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to strong frustration effects that stabilize a novel gapless quantum spin liquid state with exotic excitations ( majorana fermions ) , which is exactly solvable in two dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> it was theoretically proposed @xcite that such exotic hamiltonians might be realized in magnetic materials containing edge - sharing cubic iro@xmath4 octahedra . </S>",
    "<S> the magnetic ground state of ir@xmath5 including the cubic crystal field and spin - orbit coupling is a complex spin - orbital doublet with @xmath6 @xcite , and super - exchange through the two 90@xmath7 ir - o - ir paths is expected to lead to a dominant ising interaction for the moment components normal to the ir - o@xmath1-ir plane @xcite . for a three - fold coordinated iro@xmath4 octahedron </S>",
    "<S> this leads to perpendicular ising axes for the three nearest - neighbor bonds , as required for a kitaev model . </S>",
    "<S> the 2d honeycomb - lattice @xmath8-na@xmath1iro@xmath2 @xcite and @xmath8-li@xmath1iro@xmath2 @xcite are being intensively explored as candidate kitaev materials , but as yet no clear evidence for novel kitaev physics has been observed .    </S>",
    "<S> generalizations of the kitaev model to 3d lattices are also expected to have quantum spin liquid states @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the recently - synthesized structural polytypes  hyper - honeycomb \" @xmath9li@xmath1iro@xmath2 @xcite and  harmonic \" honeycomb @xmath10li@xmath1iro@xmath2 @xcite , which maintain the local three - fold coordination of edge - sharing iro@xmath4 octahedra , are prime candidates to display 3d kitaev physics . to test for signatures of such physics </S>",
    "<S> we have performed resonant magnetic x - ray diffraction ( rmxd ) measurements @xcite on single crystals of @xmath0-li@xmath1iro@xmath2 , scattering at the strong ir l@xmath2 resonance @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we have determined the complete magnetic structure for all 16 iridium sites in the unit cell , and found an unexpectedly complex , yet highly symmetric magnetic structure comprised of non - coplanar , counter - rotating iridium magnetic moments located in zig - zag chains . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , the magnetic structure exhibits no net ferromagnetic or antiferromagnetic spin correlations , and as such one can rule out a model hamiltonian whose primary ingredient is the nearest - neighbor heisenberg interaction . instead , motivated by the work of jackeli and khaliullin @xcite , and by arguments based on susceptibility anisotropy @xcite , we present a minimal spin hamiltonian with dominant kitaev interactions that naturally reproduces all key features of the observed magnetic order , in particular , we point out that counter rotating spirals on the zig - zag chains are naturally generated by kitaev interactions . </S>",
    "<S> our results therefore provide strong evidence that dominant kitaev couplings govern the magnetic interactions in @xmath0-li@xmath1iro@xmath2 .     </S>",
