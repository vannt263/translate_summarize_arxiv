    "<S> the theory of random matrices contains many central limit theorems . </S>",
    "<S> we have central limit theorems for eigenvalues statistics , for the log - determinant and log - permanent , for limiting distribution of individual eigenvalues in the bulk , and many others .    in this notes , we discuss the following problem : is it possible to prove the law of the iterated logarithm ? </S>",
    "<S> we illustrate this possibility by showing that this is indeed the case for the log of the permanent of random bernoulli matrices and pose open questions concerning several other matrix parameters . </S>"
