    "<S> when calculating higher terms of the @xmath0-expansion of massive feynman diagrams , one needs to evaluate particular cases of multiple inverse binomial sums . </S>",
    "<S> these sums are related to the derivatives of certain hypergeometric functions with respect to their parameters . </S>",
    "<S> we explore this connection and analytically calculate a number of such infinite sums , for an arbitrary value of the argument which corresponds to an arbitrary value of the off - shell external momentum . </S>",
    "<S> in such a way , we find a number of new results for physically important two - loop two- and three - point feynman diagrams . </S>",
    "<S> the results are presented in terms of generalized polylogarithmic functions .    </S>",
