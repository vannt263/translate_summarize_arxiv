    "<S> we construct a laplacian - level meta - generalized gradient approximation ( meta - gga ) for the non - interacting ( kohn - sham orbital ) positive kinetic energy density @xmath0 of an electronic ground state of density @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> this meta - gga is designed to recover the fourth - order gradient expansion @xmath2 in the appropiate slowly - varying limit and the von weizscker expression @xmath3 in the rapidly - varying limit . </S>",
    "<S> it is constrained to satisfy the rigorous lower bound @xmath4 . </S>",
    "<S> our meta - gga is typically a strong improvement over the gradient expansion of @xmath0 for atoms , spherical jellium clusters , jellium surfaces , the airy gas , hooke s atom , one - electron gaussian density , quasi - two dimensional electron gas , and nonuniformly - scaled hydrogen atom . </S>",
    "<S> we also construct a laplacian - level meta - gga for exchange and correlation by employing our approximate @xmath0 in the tao , perdew , staroverov and scuseria ( tpss ) meta - gga density functional . </S>",
    "<S> the laplacian - level tpss gives almost the same exchange - correlation enhancement factors and energies as the full tpss , suggesting that @xmath0 and @xmath5 carry about the same information beyond that carried by @xmath1 and @xmath6 . </S>",
    "<S> our kinetic energy density integrates to an orbital - free kinetic energy functional that is about as accurate as the fourth - order gradient expansion for many real densities ( with noticeable improvement in molecular atomization energies ) , but considerably more accurate for rapidly - varying ones . </S>"
