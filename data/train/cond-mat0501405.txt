    "recent experiments@xcite have shown that magnetic - field dependences of thermodynamic quantities in the vortex state of type - ii superconductors provide unique information on the pairing symmetry and gap anisotropy .",
    "on the theoretical side , however , calculations of those quantities still remain a fairly difficult task to perform .",
    "the quasiclassical equations derived by eilenberger@xcite provide a convenient starting point for this purpose .",
    "pesch@xcite obtained a compact analytic solution to them based on the lowest landau - level approximation for the pair potential .",
    "it has been used recently to discuss the field dependences of the thermal conductivity,@xcite the density of states,@xcite and thermodynamic quantities.@xcite on the other hand , klein@xcite obtained a full numerical solution for the vortex - lattice state using a standard procedure to solve ordinary differential equations .",

    "it will also be convenient for microscopically calculating responses of the vortex - lattice state to external perturbations,@xcite such as vortex - lattice oscillations .",
