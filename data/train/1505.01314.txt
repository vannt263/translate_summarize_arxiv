    "a wormhole is a topological or geometrical structure that constitutes a short cut or tunnel between two universes or two separate regions of the same universe @xcite .",
    "the existence of such structures implies the violation of the convergence condition , as required by the raychaudhuri equation @xcite . in general relativity ( gr ) , a violation of the convergence condition implies a violation of various energy conditions ( weak , strong , null , dominant , etc . )",
    "therefore , in the context of gr , one needs energy - condition - violating matter ( often called  exotic matter \" ) to sustain a wormhole @xcite .",
    "however , in some alternative or modified theories of gravity , the violation of the convergence condition may not imply a violation of the energy conditions @xcite .",
    "in such theories , these two conditions are separate .",
    "therefore , in such theories , one can have a wormhole geometry with the matter satisfying all the energy conditions but violating the convergence condition .",
    "born - infeld inspired gravity is a modified gravity theory first suggested by deser and gibbons @xcite , inspired by the earlier work of eddington @xcite and the nonlinear electrodynamics of born and infeld @xcite .",
    "they considered the metric formulation of the born - infeld gravitational action .",
    "later , vollick @xcite considered the born - infeld gravity action in the palatini formalism and introduced the matter fields in a nonconventional way .",
    "recently , banados and ferreira @xcite considered the matter coupling in a different way , simpler than that introduced in vollick s work .",
    "we shall focus on the banados and ferreira formulation of born - infeld gravity @xcite , commonly cited as eddington - inspired born - infeld gravity ( eibi ) .",
    "various aspects of eibi gravity have been studied by many researchers in the recent past .",
    "spherically symmetric solutions , cosmology , and astrophysical aspects have been worked out @xcite .",
    "the bouncing solutions obtained in eibi cosmology , with matter satisfying all the energy conditions , implies a violation of the convergence condition .",
    "this gives a hint of having a wormhole geometry supported by ordinary matter , in eibi gravity .",
    "attempts have been made to construct a wormhole geometry in this modified gravity @xcite .",
    "the authors in @xcite obtained a wormhole geometry supported by pressureless dust , in three dimensions .",
    "however , the energy density is made positive with the help of the negative cosmological constant . without the cosmological constant ,",
    "the energy density is negative on one side of the throat and positive on the other side . in @xcite ,",
