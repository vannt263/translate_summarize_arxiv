    "in conclusion , we have investigated the nonlocally coupled phase oscillators with the biharmonic interaction .",
    "we found chimera states with peculiar characteristics resulting from the interplay between the first and second harmonic interactions . in c1p2 and c2p2 chimera states , oscillators in the same coherent cluster spilt into two groups with the phase difference between them at around @xmath0 .",
    "in c2np1 chimera states with spatial period @xmath76 , the phase difference between adjacent coherent clusters is not @xmath75 but around @xmath0 .",
    "we also found the prevalence of multi - cluster chimera states .",
    "multi - cluster chimera states have been found in many systems with kernel function taking the form of step function @xcite . in those works , reducing the coupling range favors the appearance of more coherent clusters .",
    "however , for kernel function taking the exponential one , only transient multi - cluster chimera states are observed for phase oscillators @xcite .",
    "therefore , multi - cluster chimera states with more than 2 coherent clusters are not trivial for nonlocally coupled phase oscillators .",
    "furthermore , we found that the coupling range @xmath24 has little influence on the number of coherent clusters .",
    "in contrast , increasing the coupling range @xmath25 seems to produce more coherent clusters when @xmath25 is not very large .",
