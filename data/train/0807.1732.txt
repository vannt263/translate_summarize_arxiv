    "we studied whether in a theory of a scalar field coupled to a gauge field a homogeneous metastable vacuum can decay in a resonant fashion .",
    "the answer turns out to be negative and thus our result generalizes the no go theorem for resonant tunneling in a pure scalar field theory @xcite .",
