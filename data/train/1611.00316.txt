    "<S> we present high - order compact schemes for a linear second - order parabolic partial differential equation ( pde ) with mixed second - order derivative terms in two spatial dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> the schemes are applied to option pricing pde for a family of stochastic volatility models . </S>",
    "<S> we use a non - uniform grid with more grid - points around the strike price . </S>",
    "<S> the schemes are fourth - order accurate in space and second - order accurate in time for vanishing correlation . in our numerical convergence study </S>",
    "<S> we achieve fourth - order accuracy also for non - zero correlation . </S>",
    "<S> a combination of crank - nicolson and bdf-4 discretisation is applied in time . </S>",
    "<S> numerical examples confirm that a standard , second - order finite difference scheme is significantly outperformed . </S>"
