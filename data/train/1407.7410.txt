    "<S> the einstein - podolsky - rosen paradox  @xcite was an attempt to show that the quantum description of reality can not be complete . </S>",

    "<S> epr used perfectly correlated systems to argue that such elements are derivable from quantum predictions and the principle of relativistic locality . </S>",
