    "a distinctive characteristics of active matter is the conversion of internal chemical energy into , or utilization of energy from the environment for , directed motion @xcite .",
    "the spectrum of biological active systems is wide and ranges from the the macroscopic scale of flocks of birds and mammalian herds @xcite , the cytoskeleton in living cells @xcite , down to moving bacteria @xcite on the micrometer scale .",
    "thereby , nature employs various propulsion strategies .",
    "bacteria are typically propelled by helical flagella @xcite .",
    "the actin filaments of the cytoskeleton are driven forward by molecular motors @xcite .",
    "alike , microtubules in motility assays are propelled by surface - bound dyneins @xcite . for synthetic active particles ,",
    "chemical or physical propulsion mechanism are exploited @xcite .",
    "various features are common to all active systems @xcite , and the challenge of a theoretical description is to find a suitable approach capturing these characteristics .",
    "generically , the activity - induced hydrodynamic flow field of a microswimmer is described by a force dipole @xcite .",
    "experiments , theoretical calculations , and computer simulations , e.g. , for _ e. coli _ bacteria @xcite and _ chlamydomonas reinhardtii _",
    "algae @xcite , confirm such a description for the far - field flow .",
    "however , the near - field flow can be distinctively different from the flow field of a force dipole @xcite .",
    "microswimmers are often described as active brownian particles ( abps ) @xcite , neglecting hydrodynamics .",
    "this minimal stochastic model already yields interesting propulsion and excluded - volume induced emerging structures @xcite .",
    "moreover , abps are an extremely useful model to unravel the out - of - equilibrium statistical features of active systems @xcite .",
    "the properties of connected active particles , such as linear chains @xcite or other arrangements @xcite , are particular interesting systems , because of the coupling of their conformational properties and propulsion .",
    "similar to external forces , the intrinsic activity leads to significant conformational changes , as shown in refs .",
    "@xcite . in this context",
    ", we also like to mention the conformational modulations of polymer embedded in a bath of active brownian particles @xcite .",
    "activity also affects other polymer properties .",
    "an example is the linear viscoelastic response of an entangled , isotropic solution of semiflexible polymers as a model systems for myosin - driven actin filaments @xcite .",
    "here , activity leads to novel time - dependent regimes of the shear modulus .",
    "other aspects are emerging beat patterns @xcite , activity - induced ring closure @xcite , aggregation of individual polymers in two dimensions @xcite , and collective phenomena @xcite .",
    "moreover , the internal dynamics of active dumbbells @xcite and polymers @xcite has been addressed .",
    "the influence of hydrodynamic interactions on the dynamical properties of active polymer properties have been analyzed in refs .",
    "@xcite .    the ( theoretical ) analysis of the nonequilibrium behavior of flexible and semiflexible polymers , e.g. , under shear flow @xcite or during stretching @xcite , reveals the paramount importance of the finite polymer extensibility .",
    "we expect this intrinsic polymer property to be essential also for polymers comprising active monomers .",
    "most theoretical studies have neglected finite polymer extensibility @xcite . only in the analytical treatment of the dynamics of an active dumbbell in ref .",
    "@xcite , the finite extensibility has been taken into account and its fundamental importance for the dumbbell dynamics has been demonstrated .    in this article ,",
    "the conformational properties of flexible and semiflexible active brownian polymers ( abpo ) are studied analytically .",
    "thereby , we consider a polymer composed of active brownian particles , which are assembled in a linear chain .",
    "the diffusive motion of the propulsion velocity of the monomers is described by a gaussian but non - markovian process .",
    "the emphasize is on the conformational properties due to the intimate coupling of the entropic polymer degrees of freedom and the activity of the monomers .",
    "we adopt the gaussian semiflexible polymer model @xcite , which allows us to treat the problem analytically . as an important extension to previous studies",
    ", we account for the finite polymer extensibility and demonstrate that it strongly affects the out - of - equilibrium properties of an active polymer .",
    "evaluation of the polymer relaxation times shows a drastic influence of that constraint on the polymer dynamics . in general , the relaxation times decrease with increasing activity ,",
    "whereby the decline is more pronounced for stiffer polymers . here",
    ", activity induces a transition from semiflexible polymer behavior , determined by bending elasticity , to entropy - dominated behavior of flexible polymers with increasing activity .",
    "correspondingly , the conformational properties depend on activity . in the simpler case of flexible polymers",
    ", activity leads to their swelling over a wide range of activities .",
    "thereby , the dependence on activity is very different from the theoretical prediction of a rouse model @xcite .",
    "interestingly , semiflexible polymers exhibit an activity induced shrinkage .",
    "however , for large activities the polymer conformations are ultimately comparable with those of flexible polymers . shrinkage of active polymers in two dimensions has been observed by simulations in ref .",
    "however , that shrinkage is due to excluded - volume effects and is unrelated to our observations for semiflexible polymers , where excluded - volume interactions are negligible .",
    "our theoretical considerations shed light on the nonequilibrium properties of semiflexible polymers and underline the importance of an adequate description already for moderate activities .",

    "we have presented an analytical approach to study the conformational and dynamical properties of active semiflexible polymers .",
    "we have adopted a continuum representation of a polymer with a certain number of active segments .",
    "each of the segments is considered as an active brownian particle whose orientation changes independently in a diffusive manner .",
    "alternatively , the active random process can be considered as an additional external correlated ( colored ) noise acting on the polymer @xcite .",
    "active polymers have been considered before , both by theoretically and simulations @xcite . as an important extension of the previous studies , we have taken into account the finite polymer extensibility due to its finite contour length . as has been shown , this constraint changes the dynamical behavior of active dumbbells drastically @xcite . taking into account the constraint by a lagrangian",
    "multiplier leads to a linear equation , which is analytically tractable .",
    "evaluation of the polymer relaxation times shows a major influence of the finite contour length on the polymer dynamics .",
    "models without such a constraint , e.g. , the standard rouse model @xcite , would not be able to reproduce and capture the correct dynamics , as reflected in the strong dependence of the stretching coefficient ( lagrangian multiplier ) on the pclet number already for moderate @xmath71 values .",
    "in particular , the relaxation times decrease with increasing activity ( pclet number ) . thereby , the influence of activity on stiff polymers is much more sever . here",

    "<S> the conformational properties of flexible and semiflexible polymers exposed to active noise are studied theoretically . </S>",
    "<S> the noise may originate from the interaction of the polymer with surrounding active ( brownian ) particles or from the inherent motion of the polymer itself , which may be composed of active brownian particles . in the latter case , </S>",
    "<S> the respective monomers are independently propelled in directions changing diffusively . for the description of the polymer , </S>",
    "<S> we adopt the continuous gaussian semiflexible polymer model . </S>",
    "<S> specifically , the finite polymer extensibility is taken into account which turns out to be essentially for the polymer conformations . </S>",
    "<S> our analytical calculations predict a strong dependence of the relaxation times on the activity . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , semiflexible polymers exhibit a crossover from a bending - elasticity - dominated to the flexible - polymer dynamics with increasing activity . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to a significant noise - induced polymer shrinkage over a large range of self - propulsion velocities . for large activities , </S>",
    "<S> the polymers swell and their extension becomes comparable to the contour length . </S>",
    "<S> the scaling properties of the mean square end - to - end distance with respect to the polymer length and monomer activity are discussed . </S>"
