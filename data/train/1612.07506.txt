    ", the inclusion of heavy and , especially , resonant degrees of freedom in chiral efts leads to a more complex situation , because one has to solve the question of implementing a systematic power - counting scheme . to be specific , in relativistic baryon chiral perturbation theory ( rbchpt ) , applying",
    "the same renormalization condition as in mesonic chpt did not work out , because loops containing internal nucleon lines violated the power counting @xcite .",
