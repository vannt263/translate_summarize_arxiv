    "<S> a model of a fluctuating lightcone due to a bath of gravitons is further investigated . </S>",
    "<S> the flight times of photons between a source and a detector may be either longer or shorter than the light propagation time in the background classical spacetime , and will form a gaussian distribution centered around the classical flight time . </S>",
    "<S> however , a pair of photons emitted in rapid succession will tend to have correlated flight times . </S>",
    "<S> we derive and discuss a correlation function which describes this effect . </S>",
    "<S> this enables us to understand more fully the operational significance of a fluctuating lightcone . </S>",
    "<S> our results may be combined with observational data on pulsar timing to place some constraints on the quantum state of cosmological gravitons .    </S>",
