    "<S> orthogonal sets of idempotents are used to design sets of unitary matrices , known as constellations , such that the modulus of the determinant of the difference of any two distinct elements is greater than @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that unitary matrices in general are derived from orthogonal sets of idempotents reducing the design problem to a construction problem of unitary matrices from such sets . </S>",
    "<S> the quality of the constellations constructed in this way and the actual differences between the unitary matrices can be determined algebraically from the idempotents used . </S>",
    "<S> this has applications to the design of unitary space time constellations . </S>"
