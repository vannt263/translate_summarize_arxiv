    "phenomenology of the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm ) has widely been studied .",
    "there are several possibilities of mediation schemes of susy breaking , however , only mssm particles can be accessible by current colliders in many scenarios . since the mediation scale is much higher than the electroweak scale , other sectors are decoupled .",
