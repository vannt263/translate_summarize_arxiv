    "<S> we generalize the gleason  kahane  elazko theorem to modules . as an application </S>",
    "<S> , we show that every linear functional on a hardy space that is non - zero on outer functions is a multiple of a point evaluation . </S>",
    "<S> a further consequence is that every linear endomorphism of a hardy space that maps outer functions to nowhere - zero functions is a weighted composition operator . in neither case is continuity assumed . </S>",
    "<S> we also consider some extensions to other function spaces , including the bergman , dirichlet and besov spaces , the little bloch space and @xmath0 . </S>"
