    "<S> knowledge of the equation of state of the baryon matter plays a decisive role in the description of neutron stars . with an increase of the baryon density </S>",
    "<S> the filling of fermi seas of hyperons and @xmath0 isobars becomes possible . </S>",
    "<S> their inclusion into standard relativistic mean - field models results in a strong softening of the equation of state and a lowering of the maximum neutron star mass below the measured values . </S>",
    "<S> we extend a relativistic mean - field model with scaled hadron masses and coupling constants developed in our previous works and take into account now not only hyperons but also the @xmath0 isobars . </S>",
    "<S> we analyze available empirical information to put constraints on coupling constants of @xmath0s to mesonic mean fields . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the resulting equation of state satisfies majority of presently known experimental constraints . </S>"
