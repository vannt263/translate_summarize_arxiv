    "@xcite and _ traceability codes _ @xcite .",
    "the owner ( distributor ) of the content hides a unique mark called a _ fingerprint _ in each licensed copy bought by a user .",
    "the collection of fingerprint assignments is referred to as a _",
    "code_. if a naive user distributes a copy of his fingerprinted content illegally , then the pirated copy can easily be traced back to the guilty user . however ,",

    "<S> . moreover , even when the coalition is of a larger size @xmath1 @xmath2 , the decoder still provides partial information by tracing one of the groups containing a guilty user . </S>",
    "<S> we establish sufficient conditions for a code to possess the two - level traceability property . </S>",
