    "we have performed a detailed analysis of the background to like - sign dilepton production at both run ii of the tevatron and the lhc .",
    "we find a background from standard model processes of @xmath35 events for @xmath36 integrated luminosity at the tevatron and @xmath37 events for @xmath38 integrated luminosity at the lhc after a set of cuts . if we only consider this background there are large regions of sugra parameter space where resonant slepton production followed by a supersymmetric gauge decay of the slepton is visible above the sm background even for the small values of the   couplings we considered .",
    "this is presumably the strategy which would be adopted in any initial experimental search , looking for an excess of a given type of event over the standard model prediction .",
    "if such an excess where observed it would then be necessary to identify which of the many possible models of beyond the standard model physics was correct .    in the   mssm",
    "such an excess of like - sign dileptons can come from two possible sources , from sparticle pair production followed by the decay of the lsp , and from resonant sparticle production .",
    "we have considered the background to resonant slepton production from sparticle pair production and found that after an additional cut on the number of jets the signal from resonant slepton production is visible above the combined standard model and susy pair production background for large ranges of sugra parameter space .",
