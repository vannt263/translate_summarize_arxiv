    "<S> the magnetization process , the susceptibility and the specific heat of the spin-@xmath0 af - af - f and f - f - af trimerized quantum heisenberg chains have been investigated by means of the transfer matrix renormalization group ( tmrg ) technique as well as the modified spin - wave ( msw ) theory . </S>",
    "<S> a magnetization plateau at @xmath1 for both trimerized chains is observed at low temperature . </S>",
    "<S> the susceptibility and the specific heat show various behaviors for different ferromagnetic and antiferromagnetic interactions and in different magnetic fields . </S>",
    "<S> the tmrg results of susceptibility and the specific heat can be nicely fitted by a linear superposition of double two - level systems , where two fitting equations are proposed . </S>",
    "<S> three branch excitations , one gapless excitation and two gapful excitations , for both systems are found within the msw theory . </S>",
    "<S> it is observed that the msw theory captures the main characteristics of the thermodynamic behaviors at low temperatures . </S>",
    "<S> the tmrg results are also compared with the possible experimental data . </S>"
