    "discrete - time quantum walks have been introduced by aharanov , davidovich , and zagury@xcite as the quantum version of classical random walks .",
    "quantum walks on lattices can spread out ballistically , in contrast with the diffusive behavior of classical random walks .",
    "this characteristic has motivated many studies pursuing quantum algorithms that are faster than their classical counterparts@xcite .",
    "discrete - time quantum walks have been investigated previously on many graphs .",
    "the most studied graph is the one - dimensional line@xcite .",
    "quantum walks have been analyzed on two - dimensional square lattices@xcite , and on the hypercube@xcite .",
    "a spatial search using the discrete - time quantum walk model has been undertaken on the sierpinski gasket@xcite , and on the hanoi network of degree 3.@xcite a quantum walk on the dual sierpinski gasket using the continuous - time quantum walk model has been analyzed by agliari _ _ et .",

    "<S> we analyze discrete - time quantum walks on sierpinski gaskets using a flip - flop shift operator with the grover coin . </S>",
    "<S> we obtain the scaling of two important physical quantities : the mean - square displacement and the mixing time as function of the number of points . </S>",
    "<S> the sierpinski gasket is a fractal that lacks translational invariance and the results differ from those described in the literature for ordinary lattices . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the displacement varies with the initial location . </S>",
    "<S> averaged over all initial locations , our simulation obtain an exponent very similar to classical diffusion . </S>"
