    "<S> we present a study of an hanbury brown and twiss ( hbt ) interferometer realized with anyons . </S>",
    "<S> such a device can directly probe entanglement and fractional statistics of initially uncorrelated particles . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate hbt cross - correlations of abelian laughlin anyons . </S>",
    "<S> the correlations we calculate exhibit partial bunching similar to bosons , indicating a substantial statistical transmutation from the underlying electronic degrees of freedom . </S>",
    "<S> we also find qualitative differences between the anyonic signal and the corresponding bosonic or fermionic signals , indicating that anyons can not be simply thought as intermediate between bosons and fermions .    </S>",
    "<S> two - particle interference is a major pillar of quantum mechanics , very much like the phenomenon of single particle interference . </S>",
    "<S> such interference has been observed with photons in the historical hanbury brown and twiss ( hbt ) experiment  @xcite , and much later with electrons  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> quantum hall systems can exhibit emergent particles ( dubbed anyons ) with fractional statistics  @xcite . despite intensive study , </S>",
    "<S> direct signatures of anyonic statistics remain elusive . here </S>",
    "<S> we study an hbt interferometer with anyons , which can directly probe entanglement and fractional statistics of initially uncorrelated particles . </S>",
    "<S> specifically , we calculate hbt cross - correlations of abelian laughlin anyons . </S>",
    "<S> the correlations exhibit partial bunching similar to bosons , indicating a substantial statistical transmutation from the underlying electronic degrees of freedom  @xcite . </S>",
