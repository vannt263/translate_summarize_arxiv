    "<S> rare @xmath0 decays to three charged kaons are analysed . </S>",
    "<S> the weak decay amplitudes are derived in the qcd factorization approach . </S>",
    "<S> the strong final state interactions between pairs of kaons are described using the kaon scalar and vector form factors . </S>",
    "<S> the scalar form factors at low @xmath1 effective mass distributions are constrained by chiral symmetry and are related to the coupled channel meson - meson amplitudes describing all the transitions between three channels consisting of two kaons , two pions and four pions . </S>",
    "<S> the vector form factors are fitted to the data on @xmath2 collisions . </S>",
    "<S> the model results are compared with the belle and babar data . </S>"
