    "<S> temperature stabilization of oil and gas wells is used to ensure stability and prevent deformation of a subgrade estuary zone . in this work , </S>",
    "<S> we consider the numerical simulation of thermal stabilization using vertical seasonal freezing columns .    a mathematical model of such problems </S>",
    "<S> is described by a time - dependent temperature equation with phase transitions from water to ice . </S>",
    "<S> the resulting equation is a standard nonlinear parabolic equation .    </S>",
    "<S> numerical implementation is based on the finite element method using the package fenics . </S>",
    "<S> after standard purely implicit approximation in time and simple linearization , we obtain a system of linear algebraic equations . because the size of freezing columns are substantially less than the size of the modeled area , we obtain mesh refinement near columns . due to this </S>",
    "<S> , we get a large system of equations which are solved using high performance computing systems . </S>"
