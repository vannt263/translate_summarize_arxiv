    "<S> monotone path polytopes arise as a special case of the construction of fiber polytopes , introduced by billera and sturmfels . </S>",
    "<S> a simple example is provided by the permutahedron , which is a monotone path polytope of the standard unit cube . </S>",
    "<S> the permutahedron is the zonotope polar to the braid arrangement . </S>",
    "<S> we show how the zonotopes polar to the cones of certain deformations of the braid arrangement can be realized as monotone path polytopes . </S>",
    "<S> the construction is an extension of that of the permutahedron and yields interesting connections between enumerative combinatorics of hyperplane arrangements and geometry of monotone path polytopes . </S>"
