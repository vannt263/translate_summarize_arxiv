    "rational bzier curves play a significant role in computer aided design systems . however , the forms of derivatives are quite complex and integral expressions may not exist for high - degree rational bzier curves , so the problem of approximating rational functions with polynomials has been raised and studied .",
    "sederberg and kakimoto @xcite presented the hybrid polynomial approximation to rational curves for the first time in 1991 .",
    "wang et al.@xcite@xcite presented hermite polynomial approximations to rational bzier curves and investigated the convergence condition for the polynomial approximation of rational functions and rational curves .",
    "floater @xcite constructed a high - order approximation of rational curves using polynomial curves .",
    "lee @xcite converted a polynomial approximation of a rational bzier curve to control points approximation of two bzier curves .",
    "an approximate solution was obtained by least - squares method .",
    "huang @xcite presented a simple method for approximating a rational bzier curve with a bzier curve sequence based on degree elevation .",
    "recently , sample - based polynomial approximation of rational bzier curves was investigated by lu @xcite , whose errors smaller than huang s .",
    "hu @xcite proposed a reparameterization - based method for polynomial approximating rational bzier curves with constraints .    in this paper",
    ", we propose a method to obtain a constrained approximation of a rational bzier curve by a polynomial bzier curve based on weighted least - squares @xcite .",
    "the main idea is converting a polynomial approximation of a rational bzier curve to an approximation between two bzier curves so that control points of a bzier curve can be obtained using linear equations .",
    "compared with the related works in paper @xcite , @xcite and @xcite , our method is fast , stable and highly accurate to obtain some higher degree bzier curves as well as do nt need fix parameter @xmath2 .",
