    "<S> also , we investigate the effects of nonlinearity on the properties of the solutions and find that for the special range of the nonlinear parameter , the solutions are not asymptotic ads . </S>",
    "<S> we show that when the rotation parameter is non zero , the spinning string has a net electric charge that is proportional to the magnitude of the rotation parameter . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we use the counterterm method inspired by ads / cft correspondence and calculate the conserved quantities of the solutions . </S>"
