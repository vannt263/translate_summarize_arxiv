    "<S> ng and schauenburg generalized higher frobenius - schur indicators to pivotal fusion categories and showed that these indicators may be computed utilizing the modular data of the drinfeld center of the given category . </S>",
    "<S> we consider two classes of fusion categories generated by a single non - invertible simple object : near groups , those fusion categories with one non - invertible object , and haagerup - izumi categories , those with one non - invertible object for every invertible object . </S>",
    "<S> examples of both types arise as representations of finite or quantum groups or as jones standard invariants of finite - depth murray - von neumann subfactors . </S>",
    "<S> we utilize the evans - gannon computation of the tube algebras to obtain formulae for the frobenius - schur indicators of objects in both of these families . </S>"
