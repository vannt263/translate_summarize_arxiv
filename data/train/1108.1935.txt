    "the connections between random matrices and quantum information theory were pushed forward by hayden , leung , and winter in their studies of aspects of generic entanglement @xcite . together with tools from geometric functional analysis and convex geometric analysis , random matrices and random constructions have led to many important ( and even unexpected ) results , such as hastings s disproof of the famous additivity conjecture for the classical capacity of quantum channels @xcite .",
