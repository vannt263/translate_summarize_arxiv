    "<S> we consider the joint distribution of real and imaginary parts of eigenvalues of random matrices with independent entries with mean zero and unit variance . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the convergence of this distribution to the uniform distribution on the unit disc without assumptions on the existence of a density for the distribution of entries . </S>",
    "<S> we assume that the entries have a finite moment of order larger than two and consider the case of sparse matrices .    </S>",
    "<S> the results are based on previous work of bai , rudelson and the authors extending those results to a larger class of sparse matrices .    and    .    . </S>"
