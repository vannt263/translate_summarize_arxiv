    "the study of spin glasses on finite dimensional lattices is a notoriously difficult problem , because of very strong finite size effects .",
    "recently , there has been a renewed interested in long range models , since these allow to interpolate between mean - field critical behavior and finite dimensional one . in particular , the diluted version of these long range models is very efficient and allows us to simulate very large sizes , thus reducing the finite size effects .    in the present work",
