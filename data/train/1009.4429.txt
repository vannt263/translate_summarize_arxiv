    "<S> the sequences of coefficients are chosen to model heterogeneous media and are piecewise constant and highly oscillatory . </S>",
    "<S> we identify local representation formulas that in the fine phase limit provide upper bounds on the limit superior of the @xmath0 norms of gradient fields . </S>",
    "<S> the local representation formulas are expressed in terms of the weak limit of the gradient fields and local corrector problems . </S>",
