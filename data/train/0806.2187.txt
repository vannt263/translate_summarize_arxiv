    "<S> we consider a boundary - value problem for the second order elliptic differential operator with rapidly oscillating coefficients in a domain @xmath0 that is @xmath1periodically perforated by small holes . </S>",
    "<S> the holes are divided into two @xmath1periodical sets depending on the boundary interaction at their surfaces . </S>",
    "<S> therefore , two different nonlinear robin boundary conditions @xmath2 are given on the corresponding boundaries of the small holes . </S>",
    "<S> the asymptotic analysis of this problem is made as @xmath3 namely the convergence theorem both for the solution and for the energy integral is proved without using extension operators , the asymptotic approximations both for the solution and for the energy integral are constructed and the corresponding error estimates are obtained .    * asymptotic analysis of a boundary - value problem + with the nonlinear boundary multiphase interactions + in a perforated domain *    * taras a.  melnyk and olena a.  sivak *    department of mathematical physics , national taras shevchenko university of kyiv , + 01033 kyiv , ukraine + e - mail : melnyk@imath.kiev.ua , o.a.sivak@gmail.com </S>"
