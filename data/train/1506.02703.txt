    "a _ multicast relay channel _ ( mrc ) is an information network with a source node , a relay node , and two or more destination nodes , and where one message originating at the source should be received reliably at the destinations .",
    "we consider additive white gaussian noise ( awgn ) mrcs and show that certain information rate expressions are quasi - concave in the receiver signal - to - noise ratios ( snrs ) , the transmit correlation coefficient , and the relay position .",
    "quasi - concavity means that efficient algorithms can optimize signaling and the relay position .",
    "for example , suppose one wishes to place a relay to maximize the multicast rate  @xcite .",
    "we studied this problem for a decode - forward ( df ) strategy in the low - snr regime in @xcite .",
    "this paper extends the results to other strategies such as quantize - forward ( qf ) and to general snr regimes .",
