    "<S> it is believed that quark matter can exist in neutron star interior if the baryon density is high enough . </S>",
    "<S> when there is a large isospin density , quark matter could be in a pion condensed phase . </S>",
    "<S> we compute neutrino emission from direct urca processes in such a phase , particularly in the inhomogeneous larkin - ovchinnikov - fulde - ferrell ( loff ) states . </S>",
    "<S> the neutrino emissivity and specific heat are obtained , from which the cooling rate is estimated . </S>"
