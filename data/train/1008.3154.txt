    "hadrons containing only heavy valence quarks play a key role in the study of qcd due to their approximately nonrelativistic nature and their clean spectrum of narrow states .",
    "many precise experimental results are available for heavy quarkonium mesons , and their analysis has contributed significantly to the understanding of the quark - antiquark forces @xcite .",
    "however , the nature of the qcd gauge symmetry is exhibited in the most direct way in the _ baryons _ , where the number of valence quarks is equal to the number of colors .",
    "it is therefore of great interest to explore triply - heavy baryons like the @xmath0 .",
    "masses and other properties of triply - heavy baryons have been calculated using various quark models @xcite and sum rules @xcite .",
    "most potential - model calculations include only two - body forces , but qcd also leads to genuine three - body forces .",
    "a three - body linear confinement term was found in the static three - quark potential calculated nonperturbatively from lattice qcd @xcite . in perturbative qcd , three - body forces first arise at next - to - next - to - leading order @xcite .",
    "triply - heavy baryons have not yet been observed experimentally ( see @xcite for experimental aspects and calculations of production rates in colliders ) , and the predictions of their properties can not yet be compared to the real world .",
    "the model - dependent calculations can however be tested by comparing them to nonperturbative first - principles calculations in lattice qcd .",
    "while there are several recent lattice qcd calculations of singly- and doubly - heavy baryon masses @xcite , there appears to be only one lattice result for a triply - heavy baryon mass in the literature so far : the @xmath6 in ref .",
    "the calculation in @xcite was performed in the quenched approximation , i.e.  neglecting the effects of dynamical light quarks , and the result for @xmath7 obtained there may also have significant discretization errors @xcite due to the use of a relativistic action for the charm quark at the rather large value of @xmath8 ( where @xmath9 is the bare charm quark mass and @xmath10 is the lattice spacing ) .    in the following , a precise calculation of the mass of the triply - bottom baryon @xmath0 in lattice qcd with @xmath1 flavors of dynamical light quarks is reported ( a preliminary result was given in @xcite ) .",
