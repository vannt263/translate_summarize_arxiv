    "<S> quantum optical systems , like trapped ions , are routinely described by master equations . the purpose of this paper is to introduce a master equation for two - sided optical cavities with spontaneous photon emission . </S>",
    "<S> to do so , we use the same notion of photons as in linear optics scattering theory and consider a continuum of traveling - wave cavity photon modes . </S>",
    "<S> our model predicts the same stationary state photon emission rates for the different sides of a laser - driven optical cavity as classical theories . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , it predicts the same time evolution of the total cavity photon number as the standard standing - wave description in experiments with resonant and near - resonant laser driving . </S>",
    "<S> the proposed resonator hamiltonian can be used , for example , to analyse coherent cavity - fiber networks [ kyoseva _ et al . </S>",
