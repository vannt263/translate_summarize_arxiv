    "<S> although the low energy fractional excitations of one dimensional integrable models are often well - understood , exploring quantum dynamics in these systems remains challenging in the gapless regime , especially at intermediate and high energies . </S>",
    "<S> based on the algebraic bethe ansatz formalism , we study spin dynamics in the antiferromagnetic spin-@xmath0 xxz chain with the ising anisotropy via the form - factor formulae . </S>",
    "<S> various excitations at different energy scales are identified crucial to the dynamic spin structure factors under the guidance of sum rules . at small magnetic polarizations , </S>",
    "<S> gapless excitations dominate the low energy spin dynamics arising from the magnetic - field - induced incommensurability . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast , spin dynamics at intermediate and high energies is characterized by the two- and three - string states , which are multi - particle excitations based on the commensurate nel ordered background . </S>",
    "<S> our work is helpful for experimental studies on spin dynamics in both condensed matter and cold atom systems beyond the low energy effective luttinger liquid theory .    </S>",
