    "we have studied symmetry reduced ( traveling waves ) equations of the kaup - boussinesq ( kb ) type of coupled degenerate kdv equations for @xmath11 .",
    "the reduced equation turns out to be such that the square of the derivative of the dependent variable is equal to a fourth degree polynomial of the dependent variable .",
    "there are four arbitrary constants in the polynomial function .",
    "we have investigated all possible cases and gave all solitary wave solutions which rapidly decay to some constants of the @xmath0 kb equations .",
    "there are periodic solutions of this set of coupled kdv equations in terms of the jacobi elliptic functions .",
    "we first introduced special solutions of this type where the zeros of @xmath31 satisfy certain constraints . if we remove these constraints among the zeros we obtained the most general solution in terms of the elliptic functions of kb system under the assumed symmetry .",
    "there are four different such solutions which differ by the initial values at the origin . for illustration",
    "we have given the graphs of some interesting solutions .",
    "we have also initiated the work on the cases for @xmath1 and @xmath2 .",
