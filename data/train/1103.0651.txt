    "<S> optimal pointwise estimates are derived for the biharmonic green function under dirichlet boundary conditions in arbitrary @xmath0-smooth domains . </S>",
    "<S> maximum principles do not exist for fourth order elliptic equations and the green function may change sign . </S>",
    "<S> it prevents using a harnack inequality as for second order problems and hence complicates the derivation of optimal estimates . </S>",
    "<S> the present estimate is obtained by an asymptotic analysis . </S>",
    "<S> the estimate shows that this green function is positive near the singularity and that a possible negative part is small in the sense that it is bounded by the product of the squared distances to the boundary . </S>"
