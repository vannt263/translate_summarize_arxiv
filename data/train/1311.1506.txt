    "<S> when the right and the left handed weyl points are separated in energy , they give rise to a non - dissipative charge current along the direction of a uniform applied magnetic field , even in the absence of an external electric field . </S>",
    "<S> this effect is known as the chiral magnetic effect and is a hallmark of the underlying chiral anomaly of the weyl fermions . according to the linearized continuum theory of weyl fermions , </S>",
    "<S> the induced current is proportional to the magnetic field strength and the energy separation with a universal coefficient @xmath0 . by considering a generic tight binding model for the cubic noncentrosymmetric metals , </S>",
    "<S> we show that such a system naturally supports a set of weyl points , which are separated in energies . </S>",
    "<S> we also show the existence of the chiral magnetic effect for generic band parameters , and recover the universal result of the continuum weyl fermions for a restricted parameter regime . </S>",
    "<S> therefore , cubic noncentrosymmetric metals can serve as suitable platforms for realizing weyl fermions and the exotic chiral elctrodynamic phenomena , which have promising technological applications .    _ </S>",
    "<S> introduction : _ recently it has been recognized that gapless fermionic systems can be endowed with non - trivial momentum space topology@xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the weyl semi - metal is an example of such a gapless state , where the linearly dispersing left and right handed weyl fermions respectively appear as the unit charge monopole and anti - monopole of the berry curvature@xcite . for the topological non - triviality , </S>",

    "<S> therefore , the weyl semi - metal is a unique fermionic quantum critical system with non - trivial topology . </S>",
