    "<S> the grothendieck group of the tower of symmetric group algebras has a self - dual graded hopf algebra structure . inspired by this , we introduce by way of axioms , a general notion of a tower of algebras and study two grothendieck groups on this tower linked by a natural paring . using representation theory , </S>",
    "<S> we show that our axioms give a structure of graded hopf algebras on each grothendieck groups and these structures are dual to each other . </S>",
    "<S> we give some examples to indicate why these axioms are necessary . </S>",
    "<S> we also give auxiliary results that are helpful to verify the axioms . </S>",
    "<S> we conclude with some remarks on generalized towers of algebras leading to a structure of generalized bialgebras ( in the sense of loday ) on their grothendieck groups . </S>"
