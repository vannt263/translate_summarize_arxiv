    "quantum information processing based on superconducting qubits has made considerable progress in recent years ( see for example the reviews @xcite ) .",
    "several platforms have been realised and the first quantum protocols have been already implemented experimentally @xcite . in this respect",
    "superconducting nano - circuits are by now considered among the most promising implementations of solid - state quantum processors .    over the years",
    "the judicious choice of new designs and the improvement in materials and fabrication has increased the coherence properties of superconducting nano circuits by several orders of magnitude . however , beyond standard relaxation and decoherence studies , the dissipation and heat transport properties of these systems are only little investigated and poorly understood , albeit important from the practical point of view .",
    "the newly born field of quantum thermodynamics @xcite has highlighted that quantum coherence and quantum correlations can play prominent role in the design of efficient thermal processes .",
    "several theoretical proposals have already appeared discussing heat engines based on the dynamics of nano - circuits and qubits @xcite , some with a direct implementation with superconductors @xcite . on a related topic ,",
    "quantum limited heat transport by phonons , photons , and electrons has been investigated in solid state circuits both theoretically @xcite and experimentally @xcite .",
    "this large body of activity quests for the research on new designs with enhanced functionalities relying on the quantum properties of the constituent circuits .    here",
    ", we present a new device of this family .",

    "<S> we present a quantum heat switch based on coupled superconducting qubits , connected to two @xmath0 resonators that are terminated by resistors providing two heat baths . to describe the system we use a standard second order master equation with respect to coupling to the baths . </S>",
    "<S> we find that this system can act as an efficient heat switch controlled by the applied magnetic flux . </S>",
    "<S> the flux influences the energy level separations of the system , and under some conditions , the finite coupling of the qubits enhances the transmitted power between the two baths , by an order of magnitude under realistic conditions . at the same time , the bandwidth at maximum power of the switch formed of the coupled qubits is narrowed . </S>"
