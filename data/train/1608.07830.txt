    "<S> construction of graphs with equal eigenvalues ( co - spectral graphs ) is an interesting problem in spectral graph theory . </S>",
    "<S> seidel switching is a well - known method for generating co - spectral graphs . from a matrix theoretic point of view , </S>",
    "<S> seidel switching is a combined action of a number of unitary operations on graphs . </S>",
    "<S> recent works @xcite and @xcite have shown significant connections between graph and quantum information theories . corresponding to laplacian matrices of any graph there are quantum states useful in quantum computing . from this point of view , graph theoretical problems are meaningful in the context of quantum information . </S>",
    "<S> this work describes seidel switching from a quantum perspective . </S>",
    "<S> here , we generalize seidel switching to weighted directed graphs . </S>",
    "<S> we use it to construct graphs with equal laplacian and signless laplacian spectra and consider density matrices corresponding to them . </S>",
    "<S> hence seidel switching is a technique to generate cospectral density matrices . </S>",
    "<S> next , we show that all the unitary operators used in seidel switching are global unitary operators . </S>",
    "<S> global unitary operators can be used to generate entanglement , a benchmark phenomena in quantum information processing . </S>"
