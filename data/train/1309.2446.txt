    "<S> quantum discord is the minimal bipartite resource which is needed for a secure quantum key distribution , being a cryptographic primitive equivalent to non - orthogonality . </S>",
    "<S> its role becomes crucial in device - dependent quantum cryptography , where the presence of preparation and detection noise ( inaccessible to all parties ) may be so strong to prevent the distribution and distillation of entanglement . </S>",
    "<S> the necessity of entanglement is re - affirmed in the stronger scenario of device - independent quantum cryptography , where all sources of noise are ascribed to the eavesdropper .    _ </S>",
    "<S> introduction_.  one of the hot topics in the quantum information theory is the quest for the most appropriate measure and quantification of quantum correlations . for pure quantum states , </S>",
    "<S> this quantification is provided by quantum entanglement  @xcite which is the physical resource at the basis of the most powerful protocols of quantum communication and computation  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , we have recently understood that the characterization of quantum correlations is much more subtle in the general case of mixed quantum states  qdiscord , qdiscord2 .    </S>",
    "<S> there are in fact mixed states which , despite being separable , have correlations so strong to be irreproducible by any classical probability distribution . </S>",
    "<S> these residual quantum correlations are today quantified by quantum discord  @xcite , a new quantity which has been studied in several contexts with various operational interpretations and applications , including work extraction  @xcite , quantum state merging  merging1,merging2 , remote state preparation  @xcite , quantum metrology  @xcite , discrimination of unitaries  @xcite and quantum channel discrimination  @xcite .    in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we identify the basic role of quantum discord in one of the most practical tasks of quantum information , i.e. , quantum key distribution ( qkd )  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the claim that quantum discord must be non - zero to implement qkd is intuitive . </S>",
    "<S> in fact , quantum discord and its geometric formulation are connected with the concept of non - orthogonality , which is the essential ingredient for quantum cryptography . </S>",
    "<S> that said , it is still very important to characterize the general framework where discord remains the only available resource for qkd . necessarily </S>",
    "<S> , this must be a scenario where key distribution is possible despite entanglement being absent .    </S>",
    "<S> here we show that this general scenario corresponds to device - dependent ( or trusted - device ) qkd , which encompasses all realistic protocols where the noise affecting the devices and apparata of the honest parties is assumed to be trusted , i.e. , not coming from an eavesdropper but from the action of a genuine environment . </S>",
    "<S> this can be preparation noise ( e.g. , due to imperfections in the optical switches / modulators or coming from the natural thermal background at lower frequencies  filip , usenko , weedbrook2010,weedbrook2012,weedbrook2014 ) as well as measurement noise and inefficiencies affecting the detectors ( which could be genuine or even added by the honest parties  @xcite ) </S>",
    "<S> . such trusted noise may be high enough to prevent the distribution and distillation of entanglement , but still a secure key  can be extracted due to the presence of non - zero discord .    </S>",
    "<S> by contrast , if the extra noise in the apparata is not trusted but considered to be the effect of side - channel attacks  @xcite , then we have to enforce device - independent qkd  @xcite . in this more demanding scenario , </S>",
    "<S> quantum discord is still necessary for security but more simply becomes an upper bound to the coherent information . </S>",
    "<S> this means that secure key distribution becomes just a consequence of entanglement distillation .    </S>",

    "<S> _ conclusion and discussion_.  quantum discord can be regarded as a bipartite formulation of non - orthogonality , therefore capturing the minimal requisite for qkd . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we have identified the general framework , device - dependent qkd , where discord remains the ultimate cryptographic primitive able to guarantee security in the place of quantum entanglement .    </S>",
    "<S> we have considered a general form of device - dependent protocol , where alice and bob share a bipartite state which can be purified by two systems : one system ( @xmath15 ) is accessible to eve , while the other ( @xmath31 ) is inaccessible and accounts from the presence of trusted noise , e.g. , coming from imperfections in the state preparation and/or the quantum detections . </S>",
    "<S> this is a scenario where the optimal key rate may outperform the coherent information and key distribution may occur in the complete absence of entanglement ( in any form , distillable or bound ) as long as discord is non - zero . as a matter of fact , </S>",
    "<S> any prepare and measure qkd protocol whose security is based on non - orthogonal quantum states can be recast into an entanglement - free device - dependent form which is based on a classical - quantum state , with non - zero discord transmitted through the channel .    </S>",
    "<S> this discord - based representation is secure as long as the extra system @xmath31 is truly inaccessible to eve , i.e. , alice s and bob s private spaces can not be accessed . </S>",
