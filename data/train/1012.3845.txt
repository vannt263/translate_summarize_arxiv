    "<S> this paper is devoted to the study of couplings of the lebesgue measure and the poisson point process . </S>",
    "<S> we prove existence and uniqueness of an optimal ( i.e. asymptotically optimal and translation invariant ) coupling whenever the asymptotic mean transportation cost is finite . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we give precise conditions for the latter which demonstrate a sharp threshold at @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> the cost will be defined in terms of an arbitrary increasing function of the distance .    </S>",
    "<S> the coupling will be realized by means of a transport map ( allocation map ) which assigns to each poisson point a set ( cell ) of lebesgue measure 1 . in the case of quadratic costs </S>",
    "<S> , all these cells will be convex polyhedra . </S>"
