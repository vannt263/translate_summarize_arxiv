    "<S> shub & wilkinson and ruelle & wilkinson studied a class of volume preserving diffeomorphisms on the three dimensional torus that are stably ergodic . </S>",
    "<S> the diffeomorphisms are partially hyperbolic and admit an invariant central foliation of circles . </S>",
    "<S> the foliation is not absolutely continuous , in fact , ruelle & wilkinson established that the disintegration of volume along central leaves is atomic . </S>",
    "<S> we show that in such a class of volume preserving diffeomorphisms the disintegration of volume along central leaves is a single delta measure . </S>",
    "<S> we also formulate a general result for conservative three dimensional skew product like diffeomorphisms on circle bundles , providing conditions for delta measures as disintegrations of the smooth invariant measure . </S>",
