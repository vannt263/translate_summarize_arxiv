    "when solving models in field theory and statistical mechanics , one often faces the challenge to integrate over continuous groups or cosets .",
    "examples can be found in a wide range of applications , comprising fields as different as condensed matter physics  @xcite and quantum gravity  @xcite .",
    "there are also prominent applications in random matrix theory  @xcite , including statistical models in quantum chromodynamics  @xcite . from a mathematics viewpoint",
    ", group integration belongs to the field of harmonic analysis  @xcite . half a century ago , hua  @xcite obtained invariant measures for a large class of spaces . shortly thereafter , harish - chandra  @xcite derived his celebrated integration formula for lie groups .",
    "the radial coordinates on which these integrals depend parametrize the space of the cartan subalgebras . in the unitary case ,",
    "harish - chandra s result coincides with the itzykson - zuber integral  @xcite .",
    "unfortunately , harish - chandra s formula can not be applied to the majority of integrals over orthogonal and unitary - symplectic groups that arise in physics , because these latter integrals depend on radial coordinates defining a space which is outside the group or its algebra , respectively .",
    "hence they can be interpreted as certain matrix generalizations of bessel functions , see a discussion in ref .",
    "group integrals related to but different from those mentioned so far are of high relevance for applications in quantum chromodynamics . particularly important are integrals of the berezin - karpelevich  @xcite and leutwyler - smilga  @xcite type .",
    "although in these cases the integration is over unitary groups , harish - chandra s formula can not be used either , because the integrands are of a different form .",
    "all these considerations carry over to integrals over supergroups .",
    "supermathematics  @xcite  in the present context often referred to as supersymmetry  was introduced to the theory of disordered systems by efetov  @xcite and subsequently to random matrix theory by verbaarschot , weidenmller , and zirnbauer  @xcite .",
    "supersymmetry is nowadays an indispensable tool for many applications , once more including those in quantum chromodynamics  @xcite .    at present , there are three different methods for exact calculations of the integrals discussed above : ( 1 ) the diffusion equation method was developed by itzykson and zuber  @xcite and in refs .",
    "@xcite for ordinary space .",
    "the itzykson - zuber integral was generalized to supermathematics  @xcite by a proper extension of the diffusion equation method which was then further extended to work out the supersymmetric berezin - karpelevich integral  @xcite . in ref .",
    "@xcite , the diffusion equation method was generalized beyond the unitary case to prove the supersymmetric harish - chandra formula that had been conjectured in refs .",
    "this also provided a new proof for the ordinary harish - chandra formula .",
    "( 2 ) balantekin introduced the character expansion method for integrals over unitary groups in ordinary space  @xcite .",
    "the method was further extended in ref .",
    "even some integrals over ordinary orthogonal and unitary - symplectic groups could be calculated using a generalization of the character expansion method  @xcite .",
    "( 3 ) shatashvili  @xcite used an explicit parametrization of the unitary group in terms of gelfand - tzetlin coordinates  @xcite to calculate correlation functions in the itzykson - zuber model .",
    "this method was extended to supersymmetry in ref .",
    "@xcite and considerably generalized to obtain recursive solutions for a wide class of radial functions which include group integrals as special cases  @xcite",
    ".    the main focus of the present contribution is an extension of the character expansion method to supergroups .",
    "this extension then allows us to calculate a supersymmetric version of the leutwyler - smilga integral . the ordinary version of this integral yields the finite - volume partition function of quantum chromodynamics in the so - called epsilon - regime  @xcite , and its superversion has applications in related ( `` partially quenched '' ) theories that contain both fermionic and bosonic degrees of freedom .",
