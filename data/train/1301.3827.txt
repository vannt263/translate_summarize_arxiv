    "<S> we experimentally demonstrate group synchrony in a network of four nonlinear optoelectronic oscillators with time - delayed coupling . we divide the nodes into two groups of two each , by giving each group different parameters and by enabling only inter - group coupling . </S>",
    "<S> when coupled in this fashion , the two groups display different dynamics , with no isochronal synchrony between them , but the nodes in a single group are isochronally synchronized , even though there is no intra - group coupling . </S>",
    "<S> we compare experimental behavior with theoretical and numerical results .    </S>",
    "<S> the last years have seen a vast increase in the interest in coupled dynamical systems , ranging from few coupled elements to complex networks @xcite . besides the focus on network structure and topology , the research area of synchronization in networks has grown rapidly @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the groundbreaking work on the _ master stability function _ ( msf ) by pecora and carroll has bridged the gap between topology and dynamics by allowing predictions about synchronization based solely on the nodes dynamics and the eigenvalue spectrum of the coupling matrix @xcite .    while the msf theory was originally developed for identical , isochronous synchronization , more complex patterns of synchronization </S>",
    "<S> are observed in applications in , e.g. , neural systems , genetic regulation , or optical systems @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these patterns include , for example , sublattice synchronization in coupled loops of identical oscillators with heterogeneous delays @xcite , pairwise synchronization of pairwise identical nodes coupled through a common channel @xcite , and more general group synchronization @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> _ in group synchronization the local dynamics in synchronized clusters can be different from the dynamics in the other cluster(s ) _ , which extends the possibility of synchronization behavior to networks formed of heterogeneous dynamical systems , as they appear in a variety of applications . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , these synchronous patterns can be observed even when there is no intra - group coupling . </S>",
    "<S> sorrentino and ott have generalized the msf approach to group synchronization @xcite , and recent work by dahms _ </S>",
    "<S> et al . _ considers time - delayed coupling of an arbitrary number of groups @xcite .    in this letter </S>",
    "<S> , we demonstrate the successful realization of group synchronization of chaotic dynamics in an array of four optoelectronic oscillators . optoelectronic oscillators with time - delayed feedback </S>",
    "<S> have been found to show a multitude of different dynamical behaviors ranging from steady - state to chaotic dynamics depending on parameters @xcite . in this work </S>",
    "<S> we experimentally demonstrate group synchrony , where the two groups display different fluctuation amplitudes . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , the two groups of synchronized oscillators are not directly coupled to each other ; they are only coupled to those of the other groups .        </S>",
