    "in particular , the model and current data even allow an interesting possibility that the higgs boson self - coupling @xmath167 can flip its sign from the sm value .",
    "in addition , the existence of the heavier higgs singlet @xmath94 in the model gives an additional contribution to the di - higgs production cross section through its mixing with the sm - like higgs boson .",
