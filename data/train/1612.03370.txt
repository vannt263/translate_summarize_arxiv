    "since the seminal works by  @xcite , quantum walks have been the subject of research in two decades .",
    "they were originally proposed as a quantum generalization of random walks  @xcite .",
    "asymptotic properties such as mixing time , mixing rate and hitting time of quantum walks on a line and on general graphs have been studied extensively  @xcite .",
    "applications of quantum walks in quantum information processing have also been investigated .",
    "especially , quantum walks can solve the element distinctness problem  @xcite and perform the quantum searching  @xcite . in some applications",
    ", quantum walks based algorithms can even gain exponential speedup over all possible classical algorithms  @xcite .",
    "the discovery of their capability for universal quantum computations  @xcite indicates that understanding quantum walks is helpful for better understanding quantum computing itself . for a more comprehensive review ,",
    "we refer the readers to  @xcite and the references within .",
    "lackadaisical quantum walks ( lqws ) , first considered by wong et al .",
    "@xcite , are quantum analogous of lazy random walks .",
    "this model also generalizes three - state quantum walks on a line  @xcite , which only have one self - loop at each vertex . in  @xcite",
    ", the authors mainly investigate the effect of extra self - loops on grover s algorithm when formulated as search for a marked vertex on complete graphs .",
    "they find that adding self - loops can either slow down or boost the success probability by choosing different coin operators .",
    "on the other hand , three - state quantum walks on a line have been investigated exhaustively .",
    "most notably , if the walker of a three - state quantum walk is initialized at one site , it will be trapped with large probability near the origin after walking enough steps  @xcite .",
    "this phenomenon is previously found in quantum walks on square lattices  @xcite and is called localization .",
    "researches show that the localization effect happens with a broad family of coin operators in three - state quantum walks  @xcite .",
    "moreover , a weak limit theorem is recently derived in  @xcite for arbitrary coin initial state and coin operator .",
    "however , the properties of lqws , such as localization and spread behavior , are still open . in this paper , we give a in - depth study the lqws on a line .",
    "since the lackadaisical model is more complicated than the standard one , we could expect more intrinsic characteristics .",
