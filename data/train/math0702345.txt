    "<S> a lattice is a tr - lattice if all matroids whose lattices of cyclic flats are isomorphic to it are transversal . </S>",
    "<S> we investigate some sufficient conditions for a lattice to be a tr - lattice ; a corollary is that distributive lattices of dimension at most two are tr - lattices . </S>",
    "<S> we give a necessary condition : each element in a tr - lattice has at most two covers . </S>",
    "<S> we also give constructions that produce new tr - lattices from known tr - lattices . </S>"
