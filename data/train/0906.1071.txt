    "<S> a multivariate polynomial is stable if it is non - vanishing whenever all variables have positive imaginary parts . </S>",
    "<S> a matroid has the weak half - plane property ( whpp ) if there exists a stable polynomial with support equal to the set of bases of the matroid . </S>",
    "<S> if the polynomial can be chosen with all of its nonzero coefficients equal to one then the matroid has the half - plane property ( hpp ) . </S>",
    "<S> we describe a systematic method that allows us to reduce the whpp to the hpp for large families of matroids . </S>",
    "<S> this method makes use of the tutte group of a matroid . </S>",
    "<S> we prove that no projective geometry has the whpp and that a binary matroid has the whpp if and only if it is regular . </S>",
    "<S> we also prove that @xmath0 and @xmath1 fail to have the whpp . </S>"
