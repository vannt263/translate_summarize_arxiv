    "deterministic , piecewise - smooth systems are commonly used as mathematical models of vibro - impacting systems @xcite .",
    "specific examples include atomic force microscopy @xcite , gear assemblies @xcite , metal cutters @xcite , and vibrating heat - exchanger tubes @xcite .",
    "piecewise - smooth systems are characterized by the presence of codimension - one regions of phase space , termed switching manifolds , across which the functional form of the system changes . in the context of vibro - impacting systems ,",
    "switching manifolds correspond to an impact or loss of contact .",
    "motion that involves recurring impacts is often born in the collision of an attracting non - impacting periodic orbit with a switching manifold .",
    "this collision is known as a grazing bifurcation , fig .",
    "[ fig : grazbifschem ] .",
    "grazing bifurcations have been intensely analyzed and may be the cause of complex dynamics including chaos , see @xcite and references within .",
    "in this paper we study the effect of noise on a common class of grazing bifurcations by analyzing a stochastic version of the induced return map .",

    "<S> the lowest order terms of the induced poincar map expanded about a regular grazing bifurcation constitute a nordmark map . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we study a normal form of the nordmark map in two dimensions with additive gaussian noise of amplitude , @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> we show that this particular noise formulation arises in a general setting and consider a harmonically forced linear oscillator subject to compliant impacts to illustrate the accuracy of the map . </S>",
    "<S> numerically computed invariant densities of the stochastic nordmark map can take highly irregular forms , or , if there exists an attracting period-@xmath1 solution when @xmath2 , be well approximated by the sum of @xmath1 gaussian densities centred about each point of the deterministic solution , and scaled by @xmath3 , for sufficiently small @xmath4 . </S>",
    "<S> we explain the irregular forms and calculate the covariance matrices associated with the gaussian approximations in terms of the parameters of the map . </S>",
