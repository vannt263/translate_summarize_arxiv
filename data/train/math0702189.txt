    "<S> gromov - witten theory is used to define an enumerative geometry of curves in calabi - yau 4-folds . </S>",
    "<S> the main technique is to find exact solutions to moving multiple cover integrals . </S>",
    "<S> the resulting invariants are analogous to the bps counts of gopakumar and vafa for calabi - yau 3-folds . </S>",
    "<S> we conjecture the 4-fold invariants to be integers and expect a sheaf theoretic explanation .    </S>",
    "<S> several local calabi - yau 4-folds are solved exactly . </S>",
    "<S> compact cases , including the sextic calabi - yau in @xmath0 , are also studied . </S>",
    "<S> a complete solution of the gromov - witten theory of the sextic is conjecturally obtained by the holomorphic anomaly equation . </S>"
