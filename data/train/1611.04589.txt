    "this is due to the dynamical friction drag experienced by dark matter haloes that move in the primordial neutrino sea .",
    "even though the neutrinos and dark matter can not be observed , these effects make imprints on the galaxies and should be detectable in future surveys . with current surveys , a high s",

    "other effects of neutrinos on large - scale structure have been discussed by @xcite .",
    "their study serves as a method for distinguishing the mass splitting of the neutrinos given a measured constraint on the sum of neutrino masses @xmath142 the authors consider the effect of different neutrino masses on the power spectrum on different scales ",
    "power suppression on small scales and the change of the matter - radiation equality scale on large scales .",
    "they also claim that this method may be used to distinguish the mass splitting of neutrinos given a precise measurement of the matter power spectrum which constrains the sum of neutrino mass .",
    "these effects become measurable on large linear scales and do not suffer from nonlinearities and systematic effects . thus , given a measured constraint on the sum of neutrino masses , this method gives another independent confirmation / test of the mass splitting seen in neutrino oscillation experiments .",
    "yet another impact of neutrinos on large - scale structure was studied by @xcite , who proposes a method in which neutrino mass may be constrained by the measurement of the scale - dependent bias and the linear growth parameter in upcoming large surveys .",
    "notably , similar to our proposal , this measurement is not limited by cosmic variance .",
    "we next discuss some of the major systematics which may affect our predictions for the signal .      our estimates for the s /",
    "n are based on the number density of galaxies in a survey .",
    "using galaxies requires a good estimate of the galaxy bias .",
    "details and precision in defining the galaxy bias ( with respect to the total matter in the presence of neutrinos or to the cold dark matter alone ; * ? ? ?",
    "* ) are required in constraining the mass of neutrinos though the suppression of power on small scales .",
    "however , the accuracy of the galaxy bias and its definition should have minimal effects on our predicted s / n .",
    "the extra scale dependence of the halo bias which may be due to nonlinearities and the free streaming scale of the neutrino is an extra observable proposed by @xcite .",
    "however , these affect the magnitude , not the phase , of the fourier amplitudes at percent level , and thus should not bias our projections for neutrino drag .",
    "the impact of non - linearities on the cdm - neutrino relative velocity was investigated by @xcite in simulations .",
    "the authors were interested in the effectiveness of the simple linear theory approximation given the nonlinear complexities of structure formation .",
    "their measurements reveal that the relative velocity power spectrum in simulations are usually lower than the predictions from linear theory irrespective of the neutrino mass . reconstructing the relative velocity power spectra using the halo density field and the dark matter density field ,",
    "the authors show that they are correlated with the simulations for scales @xmath143 and also have the right direction of the relative velocity with a mass - dependent correlation coefficient .",
    "the magnitude of the reconstruction may be corrected for nonlinearities by the ratio of the nonlinear to linear cdm power spectra .",
    "this reconstruction procedure may be implemented in practice to estimate the full neutrino - cdm relative velocity power spectrum .",
    "a more serious issue is whether non - linear effects in standard non - linear structure formation can mimic the effect of dynamical friction by neutrinos .",
    "after all , the modulation of galaxy ( cross-)power spectrum , or relative velocity , by reconstructed @xmath43 can be interpreted as a particular contribution to the galaxy bispectrum , which might be partially degenerate with the ( much larger ) non - linear halo bispectrum . while appendix [ sec : nonlinearities ] provides a first look at the magnitude of this degeneracy , a more complete study of this degeneracy in @xmath144cdm simulations @xcite is currently underway .      in practice ,",
    "measuring the mass of neutrinos from the dynamical friction effect will be quite challenging , and requires a good knowledge and control of standard non - linear structure formation , neutrino effects , and halo bias .",

    "<S> standard big bang cosmology predicts a cosmic neutrino background at @xmath0k . given the current neutrino oscillation measurements , we know most neutrinos move at large , but non - relativistic , velocities . </S>",
    "<S> therefore , dark matter haloes moving in the sea of primordial neutrinos form a neutrino wake behind them , which would slow them down , due to the effect of _ dynamical friction_. in this paper , we quantify this effect for realistic haloes , in the context of the halo model of structure formation , and show that it scales as @xmath1 @xmath2 relative velocity , and monotonically grows with the halo mass . </S>",
    "<S> galaxy redshift surveys can be sensitive to this effect ( at @xmath3 confidence level , depending on survey properties , neutrino mass and hierarchy ) through redshift space distortions ( rsd ) of distinct galaxy populations .    </S>",
