    "<S> geometrically frustrated systems with a large degeneracy of low energy states are of central interest in condensed - matter physics @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the kagom  net  a pattern of corner - sharing triangular plaquettes  presents a particularly high degree of frustration , reflected in the non - dispersive orbital bands . </S>",
    "<S> the ground state of the kagom  quantum antiferromagnet , proposed to be a quantum spin liquid or valence bond solid @xcite , remains uncertain despite decades of work . </S>",
    "<S> solid - state kagom  magnets @xcite suffer from significant magnetic disorder or anisotropy that complicates the interpretation of experimental results . here , we realize the kagom  geometry in a two - dimensional optical superlattice for ultracold @xmath0rb atoms . </S>",
    "<S> we employ atom optics to characterize the lattice as it is tuned between various geometries , including kagom , one - dimensional stripe , and decorated triangular lattices , allowing for a sensitive control of frustration . </S>",
    "<S> the lattices implemented in this work offer a near - ideal realization of a paradigmatic model of many - body quantum physics .    </S>",
