    "in this paper we have presented a short review of a recently developed asymptotic weak noise approach to the kardar - parisi - zhang equation .",
    "the scheme provides a many body description of a growing interface in terms of a dynamical network of growth modes .",
    "the growth modes are the elementary building blocks and their propagation accounts for the kinetic growth .",
    "kinetic transitions are determined by an associated dynamical action , replacing the customary free energy landscape .",
    "superimposed on the network is a gas of diffusive modes . in 1d",
