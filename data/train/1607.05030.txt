    "<S> this paper is devoted to the 8-vertex model and its edge correlation function . in some particular ( integrable ) cases , we find a closed form of the edge correlation function and we deduce also its asymptotic .    </S>",
    "<S> to do this , we introduce probabilistic cellular automata ( pca ) of order @xmath0 and show that some of them are related to 8-vertex model . because we can find invariant distributions of these pca , we obtain an interpretation of the 8-vertex model in term of pca and , also , in term of a system of particles in interaction . </S>",
    "<S> this system is then studied using various tools that come from combinatorics , random walks and conics .    </S>",
