    "<S> we construct a rotating thin - shell wormhole using a myers - perry black hole in five dimensions , using the darmois - israel junction conditions . </S>",
    "<S> the stability of the wormhole is analyzed under perturbations . </S>",
    "<S> we find that exotic matter is required at the throat of the wormhole to keep it stable . </S>",
    "<S> our analysis shows that stability of the rotating thin - shell wormhole is possible if suitable parameter values are chosen . </S>"
