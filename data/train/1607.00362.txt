    "<S> wigner functions generically attain negative values and hence are not probability densities . </S>",
    "<S> we prove an asymptotic expansion of wigner functions in terms of hermite spectrograms , which are probability densities . </S>",
    "<S> the expansion provides exact formulas for the quantum expectations of polynomial observables . in the high frequency regime </S>",
    "<S> it allows to approximate quantum expectation values up to any order of accuracy in the high frequency parameter . </S>",
    "<S> we present a markov chain monte carlo method to sample from the new densities and illustrate our findings by numerical experiments . </S>"
