    "the controlled formation of monodisperse microbubbles and microdroplets at high production rates is important in many industrial and medical applications .",
    "for example , the food industry seeks new methods to generate _ en masse _",
    "monodisperse droplets and bubbles to accurately control the density and structure of a wide variety of materials @xcite . in medical ultrasound imaging",
    ", microbubbles can be used as ultrasound contrast agents ( uca ) , where the resonance size of the bubble determines its acoustic response @xcite .",
    "commercially available uca are produced using ultrasound - induced bubble formation methods , which results in a wide size distribution , _",
    "i.e. _  uca have a mean of 2@xmath10mbubbles , but bubbles with a size between 1@xmath10mand 20@xmath10mexist in the population .",
    "consequently , resonance occurs only for a small selection of bubbles . improving the sensitivity in diagnostic imaging",
    "can thus be achieved by narrowing the size distribution so that more bubbles are at resonance size .",
