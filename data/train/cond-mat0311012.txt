    "numerous many - body phenomena have been recently demonstrated with bose - einstein condensates ( bec ) in optical lattices  @xcite .",
    "number squeezing has been observed with @xmath0rb atoms in a one - dimensional lattice of pancake - shaped wells @xcite , and superfluid to mott - insulator transitions have been witnessed with such atoms in three - dimensional and one - dimensional optical lattices @xcite .",
    "such transitions were predicted by theoretical studies based on the bose - hubbard model @xcite and by microscopic calculations of the model parameters for bec in optical lattices @xcite .",
    "very important question is whether it is possible to observe superfluid to mott - insulator transitions for the mean occupation number @xmath1 larger or even much larger than one ?",
    "phenomenological single band bose - hubbard model indeed predicts such transitions .",
    "previous calculations of the model parameters @xmath2 , hopping amplitude , and @xmath3 , on - site interaction , were based on the lowest band wannier functions for a single atom in an optical lattice .",

    "this is especially significant in lower dimensional lattices with transverse potential bigger than the lattice wells where large occupations can be achieved without substantial three - body collisional loss . in order to provide theoretical guidance for experimental observation of mott transitions in such systems",
