    "<S> the energy dependences of the electronic self - energy and density of states near the neutrality point are discussed . </S>",

    "<S> we investigate the crossover from the results of the self - consistent born approximation , which are valid far from the neutrality point to the strong - coupling ( diffusive ) regime near the neutrality point . for impurities , which are diagonal in both , valley and sublattice indices , </S>",
    "<S> we obtain finite density of states at the fermi level with the values , which are much bigger than the result of self - consistent born approximation .    </S>",
    "<S> graphene is a two - dimensional system with the dirac electronic dispersion , that possesses unique properties . </S>",
