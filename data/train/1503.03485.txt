    "there is a close analogy between the symmetries of the planar two - dimensional model considered in our work and the spherical sn case .",
    "indeed , the translational symmetry in our model corresponds to the spherical symmetry of the bulb - model and the @xmath25-@xmath28 symmetry is equivalent to the axial symmetry in the spherical case .",
    "nevertheless , our toy model is much simpler than any realistic sn neutrino case , since we are neglecting multi - angle effects , continuous energy spectra , ordinary matter effects and declining neutrino densities .",
    "all these effects would add additional complications and numerical challenges .",
    "however , if our results would apply also to the sn case this would radically change the current description of the self - induced flavor conversions , and would have interesting phenomenological consequences , like e.g. , the generation of a self - induced direction - dependent asymmetry in the lepton number flux .",

    "indeed , in both the cases the use of fourier transform allows one to pass from a partial differential equation problem to a tower of ordinary differential equations for the different fourier modes . in both cases",
    "one is interested to study the evolution of disturbances with respect to a steady one - dimensional behavior .",
    "the amplitude of the excited modes is determined by linear and non - linear term .",
    "neglecting the non - linear term one can apply a stability analysis to the linearized equations of motion as done in  @xcite for the flavor problem and in  @xcite for the navier - stokes equations .",
    "what we have done in the current work is to study the flavor dynamics in the non - linear regime , characterized by a convolution term among different harmonics .",

    "<S> we consider a simplified model for self - induced flavor conversions of a dense neutrino gas in two dimensions , showing new solutions that spontaneously break the spatial symmetries of the initial conditions . as a result of the symmetry breaking induced by the neutrino - neutrino interactions , </S>",
    "<S> the coherent behavior of the neutrino gas becomes unstable . </S>",
    "<S> this instability produces large spatial variations in the flavor content of the ensemble . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , it also leads to the creation of domains of different net lepton number flux . </S>",
    "<S> the transition of the neutrino gas from a coherent to incoherent behavior shows an intriguing analogy with a streaming flow changing from laminar to turbulent regime . </S>",
    "<S> these finding would be relevant for the self - induced conversions of neutrinos streaming - off a supernova core . </S>"
