    "areal photometry was performed on all catalogued egos , using ds9 funtools .",
    "arbitrarily shaped polygon apertures were made to closely match the source morphology in 4.5 @xmath0 m images .",
    "the same polygonal aperture was used for flux density measurements in all four irac bands .",
    "a separate aperture was made to measure the local background , the flux density was integrated over the source , and the area - corrected background subtracted .",
    "the flux densities of egos at 24 @xmath0 m were also measured , since this wavelength , combined with irac , is a good indicator of evolutionary stage in protostars @xcite .",
    "we measured 24 @xmath0 m flux densities from mosaics of the basic calibrated data ( bcd ) from the mipsgal survey .",
    "larger , circular or elliptical apertures were used for the 24 @xmath0 m photometry because the beamsize at 24 @xmath0 m is at least three times larger than at irac wavelengths .",
    "most egos are located in areas of high and/or variable background emission , and uncertainty in the determination of the background level is a dominant contributor to the overall uncertainty in the measured flux densities .",
    "for each band , three measurements were made of the source integrated flux density , using three different background regions .",
    "care was taken in placing the background regions to avoid bright stars and areas of exceptionally strong or weak emission .",
    "the average of the three measured source flux densities is reported in tables  [ fluxtable1]-[fluxoutflowonly ] , along with the standard deviation of the three measurements .",
    "the standard deviation provides an estimate of the uncertainty in the reported source flux densities resulting from uncertain background subtraction .",
    "the uncertainty due to background subtraction is generally greatest for the 8 @xmath0 m flux densities , as diffuse background emission is strongest and most spatially variable in this band .",
