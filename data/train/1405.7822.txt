    "<S> we study the algebraic boundary of a convex semi - algebraic set via duality in convex and algebraic geometry . </S>",
    "<S> we generalize the correspondence of facets of a polytope with the vertices of the dual polytope to general semi - algebraic convex sets . in this case , exceptional families of extreme points might exist and we characterize them semi - algebraically . </S>",
    "<S> we also give an algorithm to compute a complete list of exceptional families , given the algebraic boundary of the dual convex set . </S>"
