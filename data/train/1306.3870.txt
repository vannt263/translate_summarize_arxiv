    "<S> we propose a new approach to create majorana fermions at the edge of a periodically driven semiconductor - superconductor heterostructure .  </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the quasi - energy spectrum of the periodically driven heterostructure by using the floquet s theory .  </S>",
    "<S> when the interaction between different brillouin zones of quasi - energy is neglected , one majorana fermion can be created at each edge of the heterostructure when the ratio of driven amplitude and driven frequency is larger than a minimum .  </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , when the interaction between the nearest brillouin zones of quasi - energy is considered , we restrict the condition of creating majorana fermions above with a lower limit of the driven frequency .  </S>",
    "<S> we also discuss the experimental protocol of creating majorana fermions in the periodically driven heterostructure . </S>"
