    "<S> the means to surpass this limit by modulating the measurement strength with the period proportional to half period of the oscillation has been proposed decades ago @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> such modulated or stroboscopic observation leading to a squeezed state of one quadrature of the oscillator motion with the quantum noise below that of the zero - point fluctuations has been a long - standing goal . here , we report on the generation of a quadrature - squeezed state of an oscillator by stroboscopic back - action evading measurement . </S>",
