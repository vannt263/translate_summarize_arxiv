    "the large hadron collider ( lhc ) has a center - of - mass energy of @xmath6  tev , and will be able to measure collisions with a partonic center - of - mass energy of several tev , more than an order of magnitude larger than the masses of the electroweak gauge bosons .",
    "radiative corrections to scattering processes depend on the ratio of mass scales , and radiative corrections at high energy depend on logarithms of the form @xmath7 . in high energy exclusive processes ,",
    "radiative corrections are enhanced by two powers of a large logarithm for each order in perturbation theory , and the logarithms are often referred to as sudakov ( double ) logarithms .",
    "electroweak sudakov corrections are not small at lhc energies , since @xmath8 at @xmath9  tev .",
    "these sudakov corrections lead to a breakdown of fixed order perturbation theory , and need to be summed to all orders .",
    "electroweak corrections at high energy have double logarithms , even for processes which are conventionally called inclusive , such as the total @xmath10 cross - section at large angles , because the colliding particles are not electroweak gauge singlets  @xcite .",
    "there are no electroweak singlet fields in the standard model . a composite particle such as the proton , while a color singlet , is not an electroweak singlet",
    ".    there is an extensive literature on electroweak sudakov effects  @xcite .",
    "the computations use infrared evolution equations  @xcite , based on an analysis of the infrared structure of the perturbation theory amplitude and a factorization theorem for the sudakov form factor  @xcite .",
    "these summations have been checked against one - loop  @xcite and two - loop  @xcite computations .",
    "the sudakov logarithm @xmath11 can be thought of as an infrared logarithm in the electroweak theory , since it diverges as @xmath12 . by using an effective field theory ( eft ) , these infrared logarithms in the original theory can be converted to ultraviolet logarithms in the effective theory , and summed using standard renormalization group techniques . the effective theory needed is soft - collinear effective theory ( scet )  @xcite , which has been used to study high energy processes in qcd , and to perform sudakov resummations arising from radiative gluon corrections .",
    "this paper studies high energy electroweak sudakov corrections using scet , and expands on our previous work  @xcite . in ref .",
    "@xcite , we showed how to compute @xmath7 corrections to the sudakov form factor for massless fermions using eft methods . in this paper , the results are generalized to massive fermions such as the top quark , and include radiative corrections due to higgs exchange .",
    "the corrections are computed without assuming that the higgs and electroweak gauge bosons are degenerate in mass , as in previous calculations . a new feature of eft matching , the existence of single logarithmic matching corrections  @xcite , is discussed in detail , and proven to be true to all orders in perturbation theory .",
