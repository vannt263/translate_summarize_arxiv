    "<S> we study weak convergence of empirical processes of dependent data @xmath0 , indexed by classes of functions . </S>",
    "<S> our results are especially suitable for data arising from dynamical systems and markov chains , where the central limit theorem for partial sums of observables is commonly derived via the spectral gap technique . </S>",
    "<S> we are specifically interested in situations where the index class @xmath1 is different from the class of functions @xmath2 for which we have good properties of the observables @xmath3 . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce a new bracketing number to measure the size of the index class @xmath1 which fits this setting . </S>",
    "<S> our results apply to the empirical process of data @xmath0 satisfying a multiple mixing condition . </S>",
    "<S> this includes dynamical systems and markov chains , if the perron - frobenius operator or the markov operator has a spectral gap , but also extends beyond this class , e.g.  to ergodic torus automorphisms . </S>"
