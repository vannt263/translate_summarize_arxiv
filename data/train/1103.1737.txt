    "to summarize , we have shown that the ( @xmath0-wave ) geometric spectrum of the efimov energy levels in the unitary limit is semiclassically generated by a single periodic orbit whose action depends logarithmically on the energy .",
    "the smooth part of the @xmath0-state level density , obtained by the periodic orbit theory , is consistent with an attractive inverse - squared radial one - body potential .",
    "we have re - derived the quantum spectrum of the inverse - squared potential with a lower cut - off and shown , for @xmath111 , that it reproduces the geometric efimov spectrum not only for shallow states , but yields the same constant ratio @xmath18 all the way down to the ground state with @xmath1 .",

    "the wkb quantization of the classical system ( including the langer correction ) yields the same spectrum , although slightly phase shifted , which preserves the same constant ratio @xmath18 down to @xmath1 .",
    "the action of the classical system has as its leading term precisely the action obtained from the periodic orbit theory .",
