    "<S> adatom - decorated graphene offers a promising new path towards spintronics in the ultrathin limit . </S>",
    "<S> we combine experiment and theory to investigate the electronic properties of dilutely fluorinated bilayer graphene , where the fluorine adatoms covalently bond to the top graphene layer . </S>",
    "<S> we show that fluorine adatoms give rise to resonant impurity states near the charge neutrality point of the bilayer , leading to strong scattering of charge carriers and hopping conduction inside a field - induced band gap . </S>",
