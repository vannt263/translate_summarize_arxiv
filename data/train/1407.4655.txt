    "<S> magnetic ratchets  two - dimensional systems with superimposed non - centrosymmetric ferromagnetic gratings  are considered theoretically . </S>",
    "<S> it is demonstrated that excitation by radiation results in a directed motion of two - dimensional carriers due to pure orbital effect of the periodic magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> magnetic ratchets based on various two - dimensional systems like topological insulators , graphene and semiconductor heterostructures are investigated </S>",
    "<S> . the mechanisms of the electric current generation caused by both radiation - induced heating of carriers and by acceleration in the radiation electric field in the presence of space - oscillating lorentz force are studied in detail . </S>",
    "<S> the electric currents sensitive to the linear polarization plane orientation as well as to the radiation helicity are calculated . </S>",
    "<S> it is demonstrated that the frequency dependence of the magnetic ratchet currents is determined by the dominant elastic scattering mechanism of two - dimensional carriers and differs for the systems with linear and parabolic energy dispersions . </S>"
