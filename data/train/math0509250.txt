    "<S> that we introduced in a previous work , relies on a max - plus variational formulation , and exploits the properties of projectors on max - plus semimodules . </S>",
    "<S> we prove here a convergence result , in arbitrary dimension , showing that for a subclass of problems , the error estimate is of order @xmath0 , where @xmath1 and @xmath2 are the time and space steps respectively . </S>",
    "<S> we also show how the max - plus analogues of the mass and stiffness matrices can be computed by convex optimization , even when the global problem is non convex . </S>",
    "<S> we illustrate the method by numerical examples in dimension 2 . </S>"
