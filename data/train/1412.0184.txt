    "helimagnets have been discovered a long time ago by yoshimori @xcite and villain @xcite . in the simplest model ,",
    "the helimagnetic ordering is non collinear due to a competition between nearest - neighbor ( nn ) and next - nearest - neighbor ( nnn ) interactions : for example , a spin in a chain turns an angle @xmath0 with respect to its previous neighbor .",
    "low - temperature properties in helimagnets such as spin - waves @xcite and heat capacity @xcite have been extensively investigated .",
    "helimagnets belong to a class of frustrated vector - spin systems . in spite of their long history , the nature of the phase transition in bulk helimagnets as well as in other non collinear magnets such as stacked triangular xy and",
    "heisenberg antiferromagnets has been elucidated only recently @xcite . for reviews on many aspects of frustrated spin systems ,",
    "the reader is referred to ref . .",
    "in this paper , we study a helimagnetic thin film with the quantum heisenberg spin model .",
    "surface effects in thin films have been widely studied theoretically , experimentally and numerically , during the last three decades @xcite . nevertheless , surface effects in helimagnets have only been recently studied : surface spin structures @xcite , monte carlo ( mc ) simulations @xcite and a few experiments @xcite .",
    "helical magnets present potential applications in spintronics with predictions of spin - dependent electron transport in these magnetic materials @xcite .",
    "this has motivated the present work .",
    "we shall use the green s function ( gf ) method to study a quantum spin model on a helimagnetic thin film of body - centered cubic ( bcc ) lattice .",
    "the gf method has been initiated by zubarev @xcite for collinear bulk magnets ( ferromagnets and antiferromagnets ) and by diep - the - hung _",
    "et al . _ for collinear surface spin configurations @xcite . for non collinear magnets , the gf method has also been developed for bulk helimagnets @xcite and for frustrated films @xcite . in helimagnets ,",
    "the presence of a surface modifies the competing forces acting on surface spins . as a consequence ,",
    "as will be shown below , the angles between neighboring spins become non - uniform , making calculations harder .",
    "this explains why there is no microscopic calculation so far for helimagnetic films .",

    "we have studied in this paper surface effects in a helimagnet of body - centered cubic lattice with quantum heisenberg spins . the classical bulk ground - state spin configuration is exactly calculated and is found to be strongly modified near the film surface .",
    "the surface spin rearrangement is however limited to the first four layers in our model , regardless of the bulk angle , namely the nnn interaction strength @xmath14 .",
    "the spin - wave excitation is calculated using a general green s function technique for non collinear spin configurations .",
    "the layer magnetization as a function of temperature as well as the transition temperature are shown for various interaction parameters . among the striking features found in the present paper ,",
    "let us mention i ) the cross - over of layer magnetizations at low temperatures due to the competition between quantum fluctuations and thermal effects , ii ) the existence of low - lying surface spin - wave modes which cause a low surface magnetization , iii ) a strong effect of the surface exchange interaction ( @xmath214 ) which drastically modifies the surface spin configuration and gives rise to a very low surface magnetization , iv ) the transition temperature varies strongly with the helical angle but it is insensitive to the film thickness in agreement with experiments performed on mnsi films @xcite and holmium @xcite , v ) the classical spin model counterpart gives features slightly different from those of the quantum model , both at low and high temperatures .    to conclude ,",

    "<S> we study properties of a helimagnetic thin film with quantum heisenberg spin model by using the green s function method . </S>",
    "<S> surface spin configuration is calculated by minimizing the spin interaction energy . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the angles between spins near the surface are strongly modified with respect to the bulk configuration . </S>",
    "<S> taking into account this surface spin reconstruction , we calculate self - consistently the spin - wave spectrum and the layer magnetizations as functions of temperature up to the disordered phase . </S>",
    "<S> the spin - wave spectrum shows the existence of a surface - localized branch which causes a low surface magnetization . </S>",
    "<S> we show that quantum fluctuations give rise to a crossover between the surface magnetization and interior - layer magnetizations at low temperatures . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the transition temperature and show that it depends strongly on the helical angle . </S>",
    "<S> results are in agreement with existing experimental observations on the stability of helical structure in thin films and on the insensitivity of the transition temperature with the film thickness . </S>",
    "<S> we also study effects of various parameters such as surface exchange and anisotropy interactions . </S>",
    "<S> monte carlo simulations for the classical spin model are also carried out for comparison with the quantum theoretical result .    </S>",
