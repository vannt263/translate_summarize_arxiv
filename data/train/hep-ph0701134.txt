    "the higgs boson is the last not discovered particle of the standard model ( sm ) and so the search for the higgs boson is the prime objective of the lhc and other future colliders .",
    "apart from the sm , the higgs boson is also predicted by its minimal supersymmetric extension - the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm ) . as opposed to the sm",
    ", the mssm has not only one neutral higgs boson but it predicts the existence of two neutral cp - even higgs bosons ( @xmath4 , @xmath5 ) , one neutral cp - odd higgs boson ( @xmath6 ) and two charged higgs bosons ( @xmath7 ) .",
    "the existence of a charged higgs boson or a cp - odd neutral one would be clear evidence for physics beyond the sm .",
    "a further difference in the mssm is the possibility for the higgs bosons to decay not only into sm particles . in case",
    "the supersymmetric ( susy ) partners are not too heavy , the higgs bosons can decay into susy particles as well ( neutralinos @xmath8 , charginos @xmath9 and sfermions @xmath10 ) .",
    "the new decay channels might substantially influence the branching ratios of the mssm higgs bosons . at tree - level",
    "the decays into susy particles were studied in @xcite and one - loop effects of the decays into charginos and neutralinos were analyzed in @xcite and were found not to be negligible . for the case of the cp - odd higgs boson also the full one - loop corrections to the decay into sfermions were analyzed in @xcite .",
    "this paper is the continuation of the effort in @xcite and includes the decays of the remaining higgs bosons of the mssm into sfermions ( including crossed channels @xmath11 ) .",
    "it also extends the susy - qcd one - loop analysis of @xcite by including all susy - qcd and electroweak effects .",
    "the emphasis is put on the decay into 3rd generation sfermions as their masses can be light due to large mixings .",
    "nevertheless , analytical and numerical results are presented for all generations of sfermions ( i.e. @xmath12 and @xmath13 where @xmath14 and @xmath15 ) .",
    "the full electroweak corrections are calculated in the on - shell scheme @xcite in the mssm with real parameters . due to the known problems of the on - shell scheme as demonstrated in @xcite , the artificially large on - shell parameters are replaced by the corresponding counterparts . the numerical analysis is made using the input defined by the supersymmetric parameter analysis project ( spa ) @xcite .",
    "in contrast to @xcite , the actual calculation uses an on - shell input set fully consistent with the spa convention . in order to obtain such a input set ,",
    "the renormalization of the whole mssm is required .",

    "section 3 and 4 show the full electroweak corrections including the bremsstrahlung using the analytic formulae from the appendices a and b. the numerical analysis is presented in section 5 and section 6 summarizes our conclusions .",

    "the numerical results presented in this section are based on the sps1a benchmark point as proposed by the supersymmetric parameter analysis project ( spa ) @xcite .",
    "a consistent implementation of the spa convention into the calculation of a decay width and the numerical analysis is a non - trivial endeavor .",
    "as the electroweak one - loop calculations are carried out in the on - shell scheme and the spa project proposes the susy input set in the scheme at the scale of @xmath115 , a conversion of the input values is necessary .",
    "this conversion requires the renormalization of the whole mssm in order to transform the input parameters correctly .",
    "moreover , the numerical analysis of a decay makes varying fundamental susy parameters necessary .",
    "that is why the above mentioned transformation of parameters has to be carried out for every single parameter point . in our case",
    "this is provided by the not - yet - public routine drbar2os which couples to the spectrum calculator spheno @xcite .",
    "the transformation is performed in the following two steps :    1 .",
    "the spa input , i.e. the on - shell electroweak sm parameters , the strong coupling constant and the masses of the light quarks defined in the scheme and the masses of the leptons and the top quark defined as pole masses and the susy parameters defined in the scheme at 1  , is given to spheno which transforms it to a pure input set including also higher loop corrections .",
    "the pure set is taken as input for the drbar2os routine which yields as output the complete set in the on - shell scheme .",
    "an example of different sets of parameters for the sps1a benchmark point can be seen in table  [ spainp ] .",
    "all plots below show the dependence of the decay width on a parameter . by varying a single parameter and transforming subsequently to the on - shell scheme ,",
    "almost all parameters are influenced .",
    "that means , not only the corresponding on - shell parameter changes , but also the other parameters through loop effects .",
