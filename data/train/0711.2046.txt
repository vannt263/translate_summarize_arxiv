    "the talk was focussed on the calculation of the self - energy of the neutrino in a medium seeded with a uniform classical magnetic field .",
    "the calculations were carried out in the unitary gauge where the unphysical higgs contribution does not appear .",
    "the background is supposed to be comprised of the charged leptons and neutrinos equillibriated at the same temperature .",
    "the magnitude of the magnetic field is such that only linear contributions of the field appear in the charged @xmath1 boson propagators but all orders of the field are present in the charged lepton propagators . in the present calculation",
    "we have assumed that the neutrinos are also in a state of thermal equilibrium .",
    "we explicitly write down the possible form of the neutrino self - energy in a magnetized medium by applying the concepts of lorentz symmetry .",
    "the form of the self - energy becomes involved when the lorentz breaking contributions are taken into account .",
    "the calculation is specially aimed at trying to find out the order @xmath3 contributions to the neutrino self - energy .",
    "this specific order of the coupling is important as we see that in a charge symmetric plasma the neutrino self - energy is proportional to @xmath3 only .",

    "<S> in this talk we present the calculation of the neutrino self - energy in presence of a magnetized medium . </S>",
    "<S> the magnetized medium consists of electrons , positrons , neutrinos and a uniform classical magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> the background magnetic field is assumed to be weak compared to the @xmath0-boson mass as a consequence of which only linear order corrections in the field are included in the @xmath0 boson propagator . </S>",
    "<S> the electron propagator consists all order corrections in the background field . </S>",
    "<S> our calculation is specifically suited for situations where the background plasma may be cp symmetric .     </S>",
