    "<S> fourier  dedekind sums are a generalization of dedekind sums  important number - theoretical objects that arise in many areas of mathematics , including lattice point enumeration , signature defects of manifolds and pseudo random number generators . </S>",
    "<S> a remarkable feature of fourier  dedekind sums is that they satisfy a reciprocity law called rademacher reciprocity . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we study several aspects of fourier  dedekind sums : properties of general fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sums , extensions of the reciprocity law , average behavior of fourier  dedekind sums , and finally , extrema of 2-dimensional fourier  dedekind sums .    on properties of general fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sums we show that a general fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sum is simultaneously a convolution of simpler fourier  dedekind sums , and a linear combination of these with integer coefficients . </S>",
    "<S> we show that fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sums can be extended naturally to a group under convolution . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce  reduced fourier  dedekind sums \" , which encapsulate the complexity of a fourier  dedekind sum , describe these in terms of generating functions , and give a geometric interpretation .    </S>",
    "<S> next , by finding interrelations among fourier  dedekind sums , we extend the range on which rademacher reciprocity theorem holds .    </S>",
    "<S> we go on to study the average behavior of fourier  dedekind sums , showing that the average behavior of a fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sum is described concisely by a lower - dimensional , simpler fourier  </S>",
    "<S> dedekind sum .    finally , we focus our study on 2-dimensional fourier  dedekind sums . </S>",
    "<S> we find tight upper and lower bounds on these for a fixed @xmath0 , estimates on the argmax and argmin , and bounds on the sum of their `` reciprocals '' . </S>"
