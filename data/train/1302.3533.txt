    "the overall spatial distribution of these sources is consistent with the two - stage starburst picture discussed previously , but the intricacy and diversity among the individual `` point '' sources revealed here is striking .",
    "there are clear implications for all yso studies in the magellanic clouds , not to mention at greater distances . in summary ,",
