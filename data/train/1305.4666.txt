    "<S> dirac plasmons in a doped graphene sheet have recently been shown to enable confinement of light to ultrasmall volumes . in this work </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the _ intrinsic _ lifetime of a dirac plasmon in a doped graphene sheet by analyzing the role of electron - electron interactions beyond the random phase approximation . </S>",
    "<S> the damping mechanism at work is intrinsic since it operates also in disorder - free samples and in the absence of lattice vibrations . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate that graphene s sublattice - pseudospin degree of freedom suppresses intrinsic plasmon losses with respect to those that occur in ordinary two - dimensional electron liquids . </S>",
    "<S> we relate our findings to a microscopic calculation of the homogeneous dynamical conductivity at energies below the single - particle absorption threshold . </S>"
