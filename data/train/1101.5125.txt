    "the critical point is the end point of the first order phase transition . to map the qcd phase diagram and to locate the critical point ,",

    "the characteristic signature for the cp is the divergence of the susceptibilities of conserved quantities like net - charge , net - baryon , net - strangeness[4 ] and the correlation length(@xmath2)[1,3 ] .",
