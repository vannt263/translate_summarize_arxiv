    "<S> non - classical correlations play a crucial role in the development of quantum information science . </S>",
    "<S> the recent discovery that non - classical correlations can be present even in separable ( unentangled ) states has broadened this scenario . </S>",
    "<S> this generalized quantum correlation has been increasing relevance in several fields , among them quantum communication , quantum computation , quantum phase transitions , and biological systems . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate here the occurrence of the sudden - change phenomenon and immunity against some sources of noise for the quantum discord and its classical counterpart , in a room temperature nuclear magnetic resonance setup . </S>",
    "<S> the experiment is performed in a decohering environment causing loss of phase relations among the energy eigenstates and exchange of energy between system and environment , resulting in relaxation to a gibbs ensemble .    </S>",
    "<S> the quantum mechanical superposition principle , when applied to composite systems , foresees the appearance of correlations that can not be explained in a classical context @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> initially , the quantum character of a correlated system was attributed to the nonlocal aspect of quantum mechanics and further associated with the violation of bell s inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> the discovery of non - separable ( entangled ) states that do not violate the bell s theorem led eventually to the identification of the quantumness of correlations with the separability problem ; non - classicality was attributed to entanglement . through the development of quantum information science ( qis ) , an operational characterization of entanglement </S>",
    "<S> was introduced , as those correlations that can not be generated by local operations and classical communication @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the development of these ideas led to the so - called theory of entanglement , which turned out to be a fruitful branch of research @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> entanglement is recognized as an important resource for several tasks in qis @xcite . besides this strong correlation exhibited by entangled states </S>",
    "<S> , there is another kind of non - classical correlation . </S>",
    "<S> a composite quantum system in a mixed state may exhibit some non - classical nature in its correlations even if it is separable ( unentangled ) @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> such quantum correlation can be measured as a gap  between the quantum and classical versions of mutual information , which is the information - theoretic measure of the total correlation contained in a bipartite system @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> several approaches have been proposed to quantify this generalized quantum correlation present in a bipartite mixed quantum state @xcite , one of which , quantum discord @xcite , has received special attention @xcite . beyond the importance of non - classical correlations ( other than entanglement ) for the foundations of quantum mechanics , </S>",
    "<S> the relevance of such an issue is increasing in several fields as well in applications in qis . concerning biological systems </S>",
    "<S> , it has been suggested that such correlations could play an important role in photosynthesis @xcite . in condensed matter physics , quantum correlations of separable states </S>",
    "<S> characterize , even at finite temperature , a quantum phase transition @xcite . in the context of quantum field theory , </S>",
    "<S> it has been shown that the unruh effect may also lead to an abrupt change in the behavior of correlations @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> for applications in qis , it is interesting to know that states with nonzero quantum discord can not be locally broadcast @xcite , and this kind of non - classicality is also related to a condition for a complete positive map @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> quantum discord was proposed as a figure of merit for the quantum advantage in some computational models without or with little entanglement @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> it also has a relevant role in mixed - state quantum metrology @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> in particular , the quantum discord has been predicted to show peculiar dynamics under decoherence @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> considering two non - interacting qubits ( quantum bits ) under the action of pauli maps , it was shown that , under certain conditions , the decay rate of the correlations suffers an abrupt modification , a phenomenon denominated sudden change @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , either the classical or the quantum part of correlation may be unaffected by decoherence @xcite , giving rise to two distinct decoherence regimes , the classical and the quantum . in the present experiment </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate that the aforementioned phenomena @xcite are still present in a _ </S>",
    "<S> real _ thermal environment at room temperature , indicating that such peculiar behavior is quite general . </S>",
    "<S> we performed such experimental demonstration in a nuclear magnetic resonance ( nmr ) setup .    </S>",
    "<S> nmr systems have been extensively used as test benches for qis ideas @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> most of these experiments were performed in scenarios where the existence of entanglement was ruled out . </S>",
    "<S> the quantum nature of nmr systems at room temperature may be ascribed to quantum correlations of separable states @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the main feature of the technique for qis is the excellent control of unitary transformations over the qubit provided by the use of radio - frequency pulses , which result in unique methods for quantum state generation and manipulation @xcite . in general , for room temperature liquid state nmr , the markovian environment in which the qubit is immersed can be described by two decoherence channels , the amplitude - damping and the phase - damping , acting locally on each qubit . </S>",
