    "in this paper we have extended the conventional modeling of chemical reactions by including non - markovian memory terms within the evolution equation .",
    "the additional terms give rise to a competitive behavior for both the local reaction and the diffusive transport term .",
    "the influence of the feedback - couplings is essential , in particular in the long time limit . due to the memory effects",
    "the reactants may be localized and therefore a further reaction is prevented .",
    "the resulting asymptotic inert state is stable on a large length scale .",
    "however , there exists a critical wave vector , above it , the reaction can be continued as long as all the reacting particles are annihilated completely .",
    "the reason for such a new behavior is by means of an explicit coupling of the rate of the concentration at the observation time @xmath21 to that one at a previous time .",
    "this time accumulation is further accompanied by an additional spatial accumulation , the effect of which is comparable to the effect a long - range interaction forces and consequently the results are basically independent on spatial dimensions in according to scaling arguments .",
