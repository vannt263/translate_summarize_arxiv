    "<S> the drift term in the predators equation is a nonlocal function of the prey density , so that the movement of predators can be directed towards region with high prey density . </S>",

    "<S> a theorem ensuring existence , uniqueness , continuous dependence of weak solutions and various stability estimates is proved , in any space dimension . </S>",
