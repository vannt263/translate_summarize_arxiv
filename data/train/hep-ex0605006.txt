    "this article describes a study of possible neutrino - photon couplings using neutrinos from nuclear power reactor as source .",
    "a germanium detector of target mass 1.06  kg was adopted as target where data taking and analysis thresholds of 5  kev and 12  kev , respectively , were achieved .",
    "sensitive limits were derived on neutrino magnetic moments and neutrino radiative decay lifetimes .",
    "good background level comparable to those of the underground dark matter experiments was achieved at the 10  kev range .",
    "this is the first experiment operating with such low threshold in a high neutrino flux .",
    "the unique data collected with the hpge detector at ks open the possibilities of studying other phenomena like the intrinsic properties of reactor electron neutrinos  @xcite , possible neutrino - induced nuclear transitions , and searches for reactor axions , all of which are being pursued .",
