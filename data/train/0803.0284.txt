    "<S> generalized fourier integral operators ( fios ) acting on colombeau algebras are defined . </S>",
    "<S> this is based on a theory of generalized oscillatory integrals ( ois ) whose phase functions as well as amplitudes may be generalized functions of colombeau type . </S>",
    "<S> the mapping properties of these fios are studied as the composition with a generalized pseudodifferential operator . finally , the microlocal colombeau regularity for ois and the influence of the fio action on generalized wave front sets are investigated . </S>",
    "<S> this theory of generalized fios is motivated by the need of a general framework for partial differential operators with non - smooth coefficients and distributional data . </S>"
