    "we presented gtc / canaricam si-2 ( @xmath26 m ) imaging and @xmath1 m spectroscopy of six local systems ( eight galaxies ) known to host both an agn and nuclear ( regions of less than 500pc ) star formation activity .",
    "the main goal of this work was to explore the behaviour of the @xmath2 m pah feature in the close vicinity of an agn .",
    "the high angular resolution ( @xmath27arcsec ) of the gtc / canaricam observations allowed us to probe projected distances from the agn between 30 and 210pc , depending on the galaxy .",
    "we also included in our analysis the nuclear mid - ir spectra of 29 rsa galaxies presented by @xcite .",
    "we summarize our main conclusions as follows .    1",
    ".   we detected nuclear ( inner @xmath28pc ) and extended ( a few hundred pc from the agn ) @xmath2 m pah feature emission in all the systems observed with gtc / canaricam .",
    "the only exception is the nuclear region ( inner @xmath29pc ) of ngc  3690 .",
    "however , the upper limit of the ew of the feature is compatible with expectations from the comparison of its nuclear star formation rate with that of its companion galaxy ic  694 .",
    "we measured gtc / canaricam nuclear ew of the @xmath2 m pah feature ranging from values indicating agn - dominated nuclear mid - ir emission ( ngc  3690 , ngc  2273 , and mrk  1073 ) to intermediate cases ( ic  694 ) to starburst - dominated nuclear mid - ir emission ( both nuclei of ngc  6240 , mrk  1066 , and iras  17208@xmath30014 ) .",
    "the gtc / canaricam spatially - resolved spectroscopy shows that the @xmath2 m pah feature emission peaks in the galaxy nuclei with the ew of the feature increasing further away from the agn . at projected distances from the agn of a few hundred pc the ew of the feature reaches values consistent with those of star - forming galaxies .",
    "the nuclear ew of the @xmath2 m pah feature are always lower than those measured from _",
    "spitzer_/irs spectra probing circumnuclear regions , which are typically factors of 7 larger in size than the nuclear regions .",
    "we explained the reduced nuclear ew of the @xmath2 m pah feature as due to an increased agn continuum contribution in the nuclear regions rather than destruction of the pah carriers by the agn radiation .",
    "we investigated the survival of the pah molecules responsible for the @xmath2 m pah feature .",
    "we found no evidence of complete destruction of the molecules responsible for this pah feature in the nuclear regions of agn at least to distances as close as 10pc from the nucleus .    for seyfert - like agn luminosities and distances to the nucleus probed by high angular resolution ( @xmath30arcsec ) mid - ir measurements",
    "the implied minimum column densities of the intervening material needed to protect the molecules responsible for the @xmath2 m pah feature are @xmath31 ( see also * ? ? ?",
    "current modelling of the nuclear ir emission of seyfert galaxies with clumpy torus models indicates that the compact torus is able to provide such column densities , even for most type 1 agn @xcite .",
    "likewise , column densities @xmath15 are derived for the nuclear gas disks of nearby agn @xcite .",

    "<S> we conclude that at least those molecules responsible for the @xmath2 m pah feature survive in the nuclear environments as close as 10pc from the agn and for seyfert - like agn luminosities . </S>",
