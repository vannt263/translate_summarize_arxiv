    "<S> in his 1982 paper , ogus defined a class of cycles in the de rham cohomology of smooth proper varieties over number fields . this notion is a crystalline analogue of @xmath0-adic tate cycles . in the case of abelian varieties , </S>",
    "<S> this class includes all the hodge cycles by the work of deligne , ogus , and blasius . </S>",
    "<S> ogus predicted that such cycles coincide with hodge cycles for abelian varieties . in this paper , we confirm ogus prediction for some families of abelian varieties , under the assumption that these cycles lie in betti cohomology with real coefficients . </S>",
    "<S> these families include abelian varieties that have both prime dimension and nontrivial endomorphism ring . the proof is based on a crystalline analogue of faltings isogeny theorem due to bost and the known cases of the mumford  tate conjecture . </S>"
