    "<S> in this paper we develop a convergence analysis in an _ infinite _ dimensional setting of the levenberg - marquardt iteration for the solution of a _ hybrid conductivity imaging _ problem . </S>",
    "<S> the problem consists in determining the spatially varying conductivity @xmath0 from a series of measurements of power densities for various voltage inductions . </S>",
    "<S> although this problem has been very well studied in the literature , convergence and regularizing properties of iterative algorithms in an infinite dimensional setting are still rudimentary . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a partial result under the assumptions that the derivative of the operator , mapping conductivities to power densities , is injective and the data is noise - free . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we implemented the levenberg - marquardt algorithm and tested it on simulated data .    </S>",
