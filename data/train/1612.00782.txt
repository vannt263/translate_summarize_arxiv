    "<S> we describe a direct method for experimental determination of the negativity of an arbitrary two - qubit state with 11 measurements performed on multiple copies of the two - qubit system . </S>",
    "<S> our method is based on the local two - qubit invariants of makhlin and the experimentally accessible sequences of singlet projections on up to four qubit pairs . in particular </S>",
    "<S> , our method permits application of the peres - horodecki separability criterion to an arbitrary two - qubit state . </S>",
    "<S> we explicitly demonstrate that measuring entanglement in terms of negativity requires three measurements more than detecting two - qubit entanglement . </S>",
    "<S> the reported minimal set of measurements provides a complete description of bipartite quantum entanglement in terms of two - photon interference . </S>",
    "<S> this set is smaller than the set of 15 measurements needed to perform a complete quantum state tomography of an arbitrary two - qubit system . </S>"
