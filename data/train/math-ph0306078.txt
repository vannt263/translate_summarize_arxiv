    "<S> it has been shown by strahov and fyodorov that averages of products and ratios of characteristic polynomials corresponding to hermitian matrices of a unitary ensemble , involve kernels related to orthogonal polynomials and their cauchy transforms . </S>",
    "<S> we will show that , for the unitary ensemble @xmath0 of @xmath1 hermitian matrices , these kernels have universal behavior at the origin of the spectrum , as @xmath2 , in terms of bessel functions . </S>",
    "<S> our approach is based on the characterization of orthogonal polynomials together with their cauchy transforms via a matrix riemann - hilbert problem , due to fokas , its and kitaev , and on an application of the deift / zhou steepest descent method for matrix riemann - hilbert problems to obtain the asymptotic behavior of the riemann - hilbert problem .    </S>",
