    "<S> using isometric embedding of metric trees into banach spaces , this paper will investigate barycenters , type and cotype , and various measures of compactness of metric trees . </S>",
    "<S> a metric tree ( @xmath0 , @xmath1 ) is a metric space such that between any two of its points there is an unique arc that is isometric to an interval in @xmath2 . </S>",
    "<S> we begin our investigation by examining isometric embeddings of metric trees into banach spaces . </S>",
    "<S> we then investigate the possible images @xmath3 , where @xmath4 is a contractive retraction from the ambient banach space @xmath5 onto @xmath0 ( such a @xmath4 always exists ) in order to understand the `` metric '' barycenter of a family of points @xmath6 in a tree @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> further , we consider the metric properties of trees such as their type and cotype . </S>",
    "<S> we identify various measures of compactness of metric trees ( their covering numbers , @xmath7-entropy and kolmogorov widths ) and the connections between them . </S>",
    "<S> additionally , we prove that the limit of the sequence of kolmogorov widths of a metric tree is equal to its ball measure of non - compactness . </S>"
