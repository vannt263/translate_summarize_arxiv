    "<S> we propose a new anomaly - free and family nonuniversal @xmath0 extension of the standard model with the addition of three exotic quark singlets , two scalar singlets and a new scalar doublet . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , the lepton sector includes right - handed neutrinos and sterile majorana leptons which reproduce the elements of the pmns matrix and the squared - mass differences data from neutrino oscillation experiments . </S>",
    "<S> analytical relations of the pmns matrix are obtained via the inverse seesaw mechanism , and numerical fittings of the parameters are obtained in both normal and inverse order scheme for the mass of the phenomenological neutrinos . </S>",
    "<S> also , hierarchical mass structures in the charged lepton sector is obtained without introducing additional global symmetries . </S>",
