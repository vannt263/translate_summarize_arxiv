    "<S> so far , most concepts make use of the seebeck effect in a two - terminal geometry @xcite where heat and charge are both carried by the same particles . here , </S>",
    "<S> we experimentally demonstrate the working principle of a new kind of energy harvester , proposed recently @xcite , using two capacitively coupled quantum dots ( qd ) . </S>",
    "<S> we show that due to its novel three - terminal design which spatially separates the heat reservoir from the conductor circuit , the directions of charge and heat flow become decoupled in our device . </S>",
