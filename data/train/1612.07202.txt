    "the quantum spin liquid ( qsl ) holds particular fascination as a state of matter that exhibits strong quantum entanglement yet is devoid of long - range order @xcite .",
    "these exotic states can possess topologically protected fractionalized excitations , with possible implications for quantum information science @xcite .",
    "a prototypcal example is the kitaev model on a honeycomb lattice@xcite , which can be solved exactly and has a qsl ground state .",
    "an effective hamiltonian with kitaev terms consisting of bond - directional ising couplings can arise in spin - orbit assisted mott insulators with @xmath8 moments in an edge - sharing octahedral environment @xcite . a strong push for the experimental realization of quasi-2d honeycomb lattices showing kitaev physics initially focused on iridate materials with chemical formula a@xmath9iro@xmath1 @xcite , and more recently there has been much interest in @xmath0-rucl@xmath1 .",
    "each of these compounds orders magnetically at low temperatures in a zig - zag or incommensurate phase @xcite , and the effective low energy hamiltonian is believed to be described by a generalized heisenberg - kitaev-@xmath10 model @xcite . despite the appearance of long - range order , broad scattering continua observed via inelastic neutron or raman scattering in @xmath0-rucl@xmath1 @xcite and the iridates @xcite",
    "match the predicted signatures of itinerant majorana fermions in pure kitaev calculations @xcite , suggesting that kitaev interactions play an important role in these materials and that they are proximate to the qsl state .",
