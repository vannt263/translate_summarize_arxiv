    "<S> tunneling experiment is a key technique for detecting majorana fermion in solid state systems . </S>",
    "<S> we use keldysh non - equilibrium green function method to study multi - lead tunneling in superconducting nanowire with rashba and dresselhaus spin - orbit couplings . </S>",
    "<S> a zero - bias _ dc _ conductance peak appears in our setup which signifies the existence of majorana fermion and is in accordance with previous experimental results on insb nanowire . </S>",
    "<S> interestingly , due to the exotic property of majorana fermion , there exists a hole transmission channel which makes the currents asymmetric at the left and right leads . </S>",
    "<S> the _ ac _ current response mediated by majorana fermion is also studied here . to discuss the impacts of coulomb interaction and disorder on the transport property of majorana nanowire </S>",
    "<S> , we use the renormalization group method to study the phase diagram of the wire . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that there is a topological phase transition under the interplay of superconductivity and disorder . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the majorana transport is preserved in the superconducting - dominated topological phase and destroyed in the disorder - dominated non - topological insulator phase . </S>"
