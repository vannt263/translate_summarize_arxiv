    "<S> we consider equilibrium level occupation numbers in a fermi gas with a fixed number of particles , @xmath0 , and finite level spacing . using the method of generating functions and the cumulant expansion </S>",
    "<S> we derive a recurrence relation for canonical partition function and an explicit formula for occupation numbers in terms of single - particle partition function at @xmath0 different temperatures . </S>",
    "<S> we apply this result to a model with equidistant non - degenerate spectrum and obtain close - form expressions in terms of @xmath1-polynomials and rogers - ramanujan partial theta function . </S>",
    "<S> deviations from the standard fermi - dirac distribution can be interpreted in terms of a gap in the chemical potential between the particle and the hole excitations with additional correlations at temperatures comparable to the level spacing . </S>"
