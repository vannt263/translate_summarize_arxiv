    "we have introduced in @xcite a class of discrete - time invertible maps ( [ q_nr_n ] ) on an arbitrary group @xmath2 for which the exact solution ( [ sol ] ) of this map has been found .",
    "maps of form ( [ q_nr_n ] ) , parameterized on a lie group , generate points in the dual ( parameter ) space which sample a trajectory in this space arbitrarily densely .",
    "this curve can be generated forward as well as backward from a given initial condition .",
    "this suggests that the group action ( [ q_nr_n ] ) may correspond to a flow of a differential equation .",
    "we have demonstrated in the present paper that for @xmath100 the map ( [ q_nr_n ] ) , @xmath6 , considered as dynamical system in the dual space , is a stroboscopic map of a @xmath0 bloch equation .",
