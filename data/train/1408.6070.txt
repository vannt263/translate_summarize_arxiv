    "we propose in this paper a behavioral risk aversion model to describe the risk attitude of a mean - variance investor , which takes the piecewise linear form of the surplus or the shortage with respect to some preset investment target .",
    "our new risk aversion model is flexible enough to incorporate the features of `` house money '' and `` breaking - even '' , thus enriching the modeling power to capture the essence of the investor s risk attitude .    as the resulting dynamic mean - variance model with adaptive risk aversion",
    "is time inconsistent , we focus on its time consistent policy by solving a nested mean - variance game formulation . fortunately , we obtain the semi - analytical time consistent behavioral portfolio policy and reveal its piecewise linear form of the surplus and the shortage with respect to the discounted wealth target .",
    "our numerical analysis sheds light on some prominent features of the time consistent behavioral portfolio policy established in our theoretical derivations .",
