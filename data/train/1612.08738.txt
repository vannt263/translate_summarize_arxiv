    "recently , a significant progress has been achieved for novel formulations of supergravity in which duality symmetries in string and m - theory compactification are manifest .",
    "they include the double field theory ( dft ) @xcite , the exceptional field theory ( eft ) @xcite ( see also @xcite for closely related attempts ) as well as the generalized geometry @xcite . one important advantage of these formulations is that they can treat wide variety of spacetimes , such as non - geometric backgrounds @xcite , that are not globally describable in the conventional formulation of supergravity .",
    "as pointed out in @xcite , non - geometric backgrounds arise quite naturally in superstring theories .",
    "backgrounds sourced by exotic branes @xcite are concrete examples . as an application of dft and related formulations such as the @xmath7-supergravity @xcite",
    ", a background of a particular exotic brane , so - called a @xmath8-brane , was studied in @xcite and the exotic @xmath8-brane was identified with a magnetic source of the non - geometric @xmath9-flux @xcite .",
    "one reason why the exotic @xmath8-brane received more attention is that the non - geometric @xmath9-flux , which is intrinsic to the @xmath8-brane background , is related to a @xmath10-duality monodromy , and the much developed dfts efficiently describe such background .",
    "it is known that backgrounds of other exotic branes possess other non - geometric fluxes that are related to the @xmath9-flux via @xmath0-duality monodromies @xcite . in order to describe such non - geometric backgrounds , variants of the @xmath7-supergravity , which can describe the background of an exotic @xmath11-brane ( called a @xmath12-brane ) or a @xmath13-brane , was proposed in @xcite . there , each of these exotic branes was identified as the magnetic sources of a non - geometric @xmath14-flux @xcite or a non - geometric @xmath9-flux associated with a @xmath15-vector , @xmath16 @xcite .",
    "however , the reformulation of @xcite is applicable only to a limited situation ; coexistence of different non - geometric fluxes are not allowed and existence of isometries are assumed .",
    "in fact , eft , a manifestly @xmath17 @xmath0-duality covariant formulation of the supergravity , is a more suitable formulation , and indeed , backgrounds of the exotic @xmath18-brane , @xmath8-brane , and the @xmath19-brane were studied in @xmath20 eft @xcite .",

    "<S> these field theories are known to be reduced to the standard descriptions by introducing appropriately parameterized generalized metric and by applying suitably chosen section conditions . </S>",

    "<S> we construct new effective action for non - geometric backgrounds . </S>",
