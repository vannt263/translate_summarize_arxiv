    "<S> the toric fiber product is a general procedure for gluing two ideals , homogeneous with respect to the same multigrading , to produce a new homogeneous ideal . </S>",
    "<S> toric fiber products generalize familiar constructions in commutative algebra like adding monomial ideals and the segre product . </S>",
    "<S> we describe how to obtain generating sets of toric fiber products in non - zero codimension and discuss persistence of normality and primary decompositions under toric fiber products .    </S>",
    "<S> several applications are discussed , including ( a ) the construction of markov bases of hierarchical models in many new cases , ( b ) a new proof of the quartic generation of binary graph models associated to @xmath0-minor free graphs , and ( c ) the recursive computation of primary decompositions of conditional independence ideals . </S>"
