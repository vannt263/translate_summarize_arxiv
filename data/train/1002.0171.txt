    "recently there have been considerable interests in the investigations of topological ordered states , and the quantum phase transitions between them .",
    "since topological ordered phases usually do not exhibit conventional symmetry breaking , these phase transitions naturally can not be described by the conventional landau - ginzburg paradigm . despite of the lack of local order parameters ,",
