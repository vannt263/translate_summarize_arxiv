    "<S> however , observations - which rely on models to correct for projection effects - have suggested a flat distribution instead . the need for this correction is due to the lack of information regarding the line - of - sight distances . with a two decade </S>",
    "<S> long baseline in astrometric measurements , we are now able to measure significant projected radial accelerations , six of which are newly reported here , that directly constrain line - of - sight distances . here </S>",
    "<S> we present a statistical approach to take advantage of this information and more accurately constrain the shape of the radial density profile of the late - type stellar population in the galactic center . </S>"
