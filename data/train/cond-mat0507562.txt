    "<S> we solve the problem of fermionic pairing mediated by a massless boson in the limit of large coupling constant . at weak coupling </S>",
    "<S> , the transition temperature is exponentially small and superconductivity is robust against phase fluctuation . in the strong coupling limit , the pair formation occurs at a temperature of the order of the fermi energy , however , the actual transition temperature is much smaller due to phase and amplitude fluctuations of the pairing gap . </S>",
    "<S> our model calculations describe superconductivity due to color magnetic interactions in quark matter and in systems close to a ferromagnetic quantum critical point with ising symmetry . </S>",
    "<S> our strong coupling results are , however , more general and can be applied to other systems as well , including the antiferromagnetic exchange in 2d used for description of the cuprates . </S>"
