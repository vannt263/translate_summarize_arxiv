    "this paper presents spectra for isospin @xmath109 and @xmath110 states based on lattice qcd simulations using the quenched approximation , anisotropic lattices with @xmath111 and a pion mass of @xmath112 .",
    "sets of three - quark interpolating fields are used to construct matrices of correlation functions and the variational method is used to extract energies .",
    "smeared quark and gluon fields are used to diminish the coupling to short wavelength fluctuations of lattice qcd .",
    "the result is that diagonalizations are able to extract good signals for low - lying states .",
    "we have obtained as many as 17 energies for @xmath0 states and 10 energies for @xmath1 baryon states , including various spin - parity channels .",
    "the variational method determines the best linear combinations of basis operators for energy eigenstates . for each obtained energy eigenstate , we paid close attention to the stability of the eigenvector components with respect to time and with respect to the two different volumes .",
    "because scattering states are expected to be present in the spectrum , we have calculated spectral weights for all states on two volumes .",
    "however , the weights do not provide evidence for two - particle states in our spectra .",
    "the first positive - parity excited state in the @xmath13 channel is found to have energy significantly higher than the experimentally - known mass of the roper resonance .",
    "better ways are being developed to identify scattering states and resonances , such as the use of operators that should have large couplings to the @xmath113 states and the use of several pion masses in order to better detect s - wave @xmath114 states .    because the minimum spin that is contained in the @xmath3 irrep is @xmath4 , we have found strong evidence for spin @xmath4 or higher for both parities in our spectra for @xmath0 .",
    "we also have found evidence for degenerate partner states corresponding to the subduction of spin @xmath4 or higher to the octahedral irreps .",
    "the lattice results for the @xmath0 low - lying excited states provide the correct number and pattern of octahedral states for the subduction of the spins of the low - lying physical states , with the exception of the roper state .",
    "when the @xmath2 states are included , the overall pattern of lattice results for the lowest energy in each channel is similar to the pattern observed in nature .    the results shown here and in ref .",
    "@xcite represent a first glimpse of the pattern of nucleon and @xmath2 excitations as predicted by qcd , but they are based on the quenched approximation , a 490 mev pion mass and an action that may have significant discretization effects .",
