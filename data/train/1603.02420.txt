    "<S> such a function is invariant in changes of basis . in this article </S>",
    "<S> , we attempt to measure the proximity of two stationary fields by a real and positive number between 0 and 1 . </S>",
    "<S> the extremal values will correspond to uncorrelation and linear dependence , similar to a correlation coefficient which measures linear links between two random variables . </S>",
    "<S> we show that these `` indices of coherence '' are generally not symmetric , and not unique . </S>",
