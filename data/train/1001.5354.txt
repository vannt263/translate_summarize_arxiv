    "<S> we give the algorithm for approximating a center manifold at a typical point of hopf bifurcation ( and an unstable manifold in the vicinity of such a point , where such a manifold exists ) . </S>",
    "<S> then we find the normal form of the equation restricted to the center manifold , by computing the first lyapunov coefficient . </S>",
    "<S> the normal form allows us to establish the stability properties of the periodic solutions occurred by hopf bifurcation </S>",
