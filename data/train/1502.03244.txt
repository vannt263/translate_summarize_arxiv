    "the high energy stereoscopic system ( h.e.s.s . ) is an array of imaging atmospheric cherenkov telescopes ( iact ) in the namibian khomas highland .",
    "iacts detect the cherenkov light emitted by electromagnetic showers that are induced when primary @xmath10-rays interact with air nuclei in the earth s atmosphere .",
    "charged cosmic rays also induce showers in the earth s atmosphere and constitute background for the iact detection of @xmath10-rays .",
    "cosmic ray background events that can not be suppressed during the analysis of h.e.s.s . data",
    "( see @xcite ) are typically treated with a background subtraction technique @xcite .",
    "the background subtraction relies on the definition of a signal region for which a background region is constructed .",
    "the construction of the background region must be performed such that the ratio of the acceptance for background events in the signal and the background region is known from instrumental characteristics .",
    "the definition of the background region enables a comparison between the number of events that are detected in the signal region and the number of background events that are expected in the signal region .",
    "the acceptance for background events of h.e.s.s .",
    "is in general strongly influenced by atmospheric conditions , the pointing zenith angle and the night sky background in the observed field of view .",
