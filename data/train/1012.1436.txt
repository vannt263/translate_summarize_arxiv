    "observation of superconductivity in the fese@xmath0te@xmath1 chalcogenides with interplaying superconductivity and magnetism @xcite is of high interest due to apparent simplicity of these materials in comparison to the fe - based pnictides @xcite .",
    "indeed , the structure of fese@xmath0te@xmath1 contains a simple stacking of edge sharing fe(se , te)@xmath7 tetrahedra @xcite without any sandwiching spacer @xcite .",
    "the superconductivity is very sensitive to defects and disorder @xcite , with the t@xmath8 in the ternary fese@xmath0te@xmath1 system increasing up to a maximum of 14 k @xcite .",
    "however , the missing spacer layer in the fese@xmath0te@xmath1 might be a cause of locally broken symmetry in the ternary system @xcite with the se and te occupying distinct sites .",
    "although , the electronic states near the fermi level in the fese@xmath0te@xmath1 chalcogenides are given by the five fe 3d - orbitals , it is also true that the fundamental transport properties are very sensitive to the chalcogen ( se / te ) height from the fe - fe sublattice @xcite , indicating importance of interaction of fe 3d states with the chalcogen orbitals . therefore , it is of key importance to study details on the electronic structure of the fe 3d and the interacting chalcogen orbitals .",
    "x - ray absorption near - edge structure ( xanes ) spectroscopy is a site specific probe of distribution of the valence electrons and local chemistry , with the final states in the continuum being due to multiple scattering resonances of the photoelectron in a finite cluster @xcite . unlike photoemission experiments , there are negligible surface effects ( and multiplet effects ) , making it a very useful finger print probe of unoccupied valence states and site selective local chemistry .",
    "the xanes spectroscopy has been already used to study the electronic structure and the local geometry in the fe - based refeaso ( re = rare earth ) oxypnictides @xcite and afe@xmath9as@xmath9 ( a = ba , sr , ca ) pnictides @xcite . indeed , important information on the electronic correlations @xcite as well on the local geometry @xcite has been obtained .",
    "however , there is hardly any systematic xanes study on the fe - based chalcogenides . here",
    ", we have used xanes spectroscopy to study electronic structure of unoccupied states in fese@xmath0te@xmath1 system .",
    "a combined analysis of fe and se k - edge xanes has permitted to uncover important features of the unoccupied states near the fermi level .",
    "we find a substantial hybridization between the fe @xmath6 and chalcogen @xmath3 states , getting redistributed systematically with the te substitution .",
    "furthermore , using polarized xanes on single crystal sample , we have found that chalcogen @xmath10 orbitals should be predominantly interacting with the fe 3d orbitals in the chalcogenides .",
    "the results underline importance of the @xmath11 hybridization in the fe - based chalcogenide superconductors .",
    "unpolarized and polarized x - ray absorption measurements were made at the beamline bm29 of the european synchrotron radiation facility ( esrf ) , grenoble , on the fese@xmath0te@xmath1 samples characterized for their superconducting and structural properties @xcite .",

    "the se k - edge absorption spectra were recorded by detecting the se k@xmath14 fluorescence photons , while fe k@xmath14 fluorescence photons were collected over a large solid angle using multi - element ge - detector for measuring the fe k - edge absorption . for the unpolarized spectra on the powder samples we used simultaneous detection of the fluorescence signal and the transmission yield .",
