    "<S> reliable isochronal age - dating for field , solar - type main sequence stars is very difficult given the observational uncertainties and multi - gyr timescales for significant structural evolution . </S>",
    "<S> observationally , significant databases of activity / rotation diagnostics exist for field solar - type field dwarfs ( mainly from chromospheric and x - ray activity surveys ) . _ but how well can we empirically age - date solar - type field stars using activity / rotation diagnostics ? </S>",
