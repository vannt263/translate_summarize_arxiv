    "blazars are a particular subclass of active galactic nuclei ( agns ) with a relativistic jet toward the observers ( e.g. , urry & padovani 1995 ) .",
    "they are divided into two groups , bl lacertae objects ( bl lacs ) and flat spectrum radio quasars ( fsrqs ) .",
    "bl lacs usually have no or weak emission line , while fsrqs have strong emission lines in their optical spectra .",
