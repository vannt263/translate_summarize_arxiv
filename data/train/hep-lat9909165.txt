    "<S> the spectrum of heavy - quark hybrids is studied in the leading born - oppenheimer ( lbo ) approximation and using leading - order nrqcd simulations with an improved gluon action on anisotropic lattices . </S>",
    "<S> the masses of four hybrid states are obtained from our simulations for lattice spacings 0.1 fm and 0.2 fm and are compared to the lbo predictions obtained using previously - determined glue - excited static potentials . </S>",
    "<S> the consistency of results from the two approaches reveals a compelling physical picture for heavy - quark hybrid states . </S>"
