    "in this paper we studied a class of bilinear control systems , motivated by optimal control problems arising in the context of dissipative quantum dynamics .",
    "it was shown that the optimal solution and the reachable set of these systems can be found by solving a semidefinite program . as a practical result , solutions to these problems",
    "give upper bounds for the ability to coherently control quantum mechanical phenomena in presence of dissipation . in the area of coherent spectroscopy , these results translate into the maximum signal that can be obtained in an experiment .",
    "the paper also motivates the use of semidefinite programming to study reachable sets of more general bilinear control systems .",
