    "the physics and chemistry of graphene ( gr ) on metals is one of the exciting fields of surface science  @xcite . these extensive studies allowed to understand the main interaction mechanisms at the graphene - metal interfaces and connect them with the changes in the electronic structure of graphene  @xcite . even in the earlier studies of the graphene / metal interfaces",
    "the aim was not only to obtain information about interface itself ( its geometry , bonding mechanism , its electronic structure ) , but to try modifying the properties of interface and of graphene .",
    "the latter can be realized , _",
    "e.g. _ , via adsorption of different species on top of the system or via intercalation of different materials between a graphene layer and a metallic substrate  @xcite . for the intercalation - like systems the most exciting cases include the intercalation of big molecules , like c@xmath2  @xcite , and molecules of gases , like co  @xcite or oxygen  @xcite .",
    "initial experiments on oxygen intercalation were performed for the nearly free - standing graphene on ir(111 ) ( islands or complete layer )  @xcite or for _ strongly - interacting _ graphene / ru(0001 ) where graphene has an incomplete monolayer coverage that allows using a low partial pressure of oxygen gas  @xcite .",
    "recently , it was demonstrated that oxygen can be also intercalated under full layer of graphene on ru(0001 ) at the partial pressure of oxygen less than 1 mbar and 150@xmath3c  @xcite .",
    "oxygen intercalation under graphene on ir(111 ) or ru(0001 ) leads to the electronic decoupling of graphene from substrate with the corresponding @xmath0-doping of graphene as was found by x - ray photoelectron spectroscopy ( xps )  @xcite .",
    "angle - resolved photoelectron spectroscopy ( arpes ) data give for graphene / o / ir(111 ) the position of the dirac point ( @xmath4 ) at @xmath5ev above the fermi level ( @xmath6 )  @xcite . for graphene / o / ru(0001 )",
    "situation is controversial : the first arpes experiments with a rather poor energy resolution give a @xmath0-doping of graphene in this system with the position of @xmath4 at @xmath7ev above @xmath6  @xcite , whereas the local stm / sts results give this position at @xmath8ev below @xmath6  @xcite , that can be considered as an artefact , because the interpretation of sts data for the graphene / metal systems is not a straightforward task .    in the present work",
    "we demonstrate the possibility to form the graphene / o / ru(0001 ) intercalation system at the relatively low partial oxygen gas pressure and the elevated temperature for the complete initial graphene coverage on ru(0001 ) .",
    "the formed system of very high quality demonstrate the weak moir structure as clearly deduced in scanning tunnelling and atomic force microscopy ( stm and afm ) experiments .",
    "these experiments combined with the respective density functional theory ( dft ) results allow to discriminate between geometric and electronic contributions in the microscopy imaging of this system .",
    "our spectroscopy ( sts ) and dft results show that a graphene layer in the graphene / o / ru(0001 ) system is electronically decoupled from the substrate and demonstrates the @xmath0-doping of the graphene - derived @xmath9 states .",
    "analysis of the electronic structure of graphene , which can be accessed in arpes experiments , is performed via unfolding of the graphene - related bands to the primitive @xmath10 brillouine zone of graphene .",

    "<S> realization of a free - standing graphene is always a demanding task . </S>",
    "<S> here we use scanning probe microscopy and spectroscopy to study the crystallographic structure and electronic properties of the uniform free - standing graphene layers obtained by intercalation of oxygen monolayer in the `` strongly '' bonded graphene / ru(0001 ) interface . </S>",
    "<S> spectroscopic data show that such graphene layer is heavily @xmath0-doped with the dirac point located at @xmath1mev above the fermi level . </S>",
    "<S> experimental data are understood within dft and the observed effects are in good agreement with the theoretical data . </S>"
