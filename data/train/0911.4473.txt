    "<S> these notes provide a description of the abelian categories that arise as categories of coherent sheaves on weighted projective lines . </S>",
    "<S> two different approaches are presented : one is based on a list of axioms and the other yields a description in terms of expansions of abelian categories .    </S>",
    "<S> a weighted projective line is obtained from a projective line by inserting finitely many weights . </S>",
    "<S> so we describe the category of coherent sheaves on a projective line in some detail , and the insertion of weights amounts to adding simple objects . </S>",
    "<S> we call this process ` expansion ' and treat it axiomatically . </S>",
    "<S> thus most of these notes are devoted to studying abelian categories , including a brief discussion of tilting theory . </S>",
    "<S> we provide many details and have tried to keep the exposition as self - contained as possible . </S>"
