    "<S> using mcmullen s hausdorff dimension algorithm , we study numerically the dimension of the limit set of groups generated by reflections along three geodesics on the hyperbolic plane . </S>",
    "<S> varying these geodesics , we found four minima in the two - dimensional parameter space , leading to a rigorous result why this must be so . extending the algorithm to compute the limit measure and its moments </S>",
    "<S> , we study orthogonal polynomials on the unit circle associated with this measure . </S>",
    "<S> several numerical observations on certain coefficients related to these moments and on the zeros of the polynomials are discussed . </S>"
