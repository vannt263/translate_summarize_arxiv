    "<S> we report on the observation of the giant spin - polarized photocurrent in hgte / hgcdte quantum well ( qw ) of critical thickness at which a dirac spectrum emerges . </S>",
    "<S> exciting qw of 6.6  nm width by terahertz ( thz ) radiation and sweeping magnetic field we detected a resonant photocurrent . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , the position of the resonance can be tuned from negative ( -0.4  t ) to positive ( up to 1.2  t ) magnetic fields by means of optical gating . </S>",
    "<S> the photocurent data , accompanied by measurements of radiation transmission as well as shubnikov - de haas and quantum hall effects , give an evidence that the enhancement of the photocurrent is caused by cyclotron resonance in a dirac fermion system . </S>",
    "<S> the developed theory shows that the current is spin polarized and originates from the spin dependent scattering of charge carriers heated by the radiation .    </S>",
    "<S> the electron _ dc _ transport in semiconductor systems with massless dirac fermions has recently moved into the focus of modern research yielding challenging fundamental concepts as well as holding a great potential for applications  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the linear energy spectrum allows the observation of quantum kinetic effects and , on the other hand , gives a rise to a new class of phenomena absent in materials with parabolic dispersion . </S>",
    "<S> the massless dirac fermions are realized in graphene  @xcite , at surface states of bulk topological insulators ( ti )  @xcite , in edge channels of two - dimensional ti  @xcite as well as in hgte / hgcdte qws of critical thickness  @xcite . in the latter case and tis , </S>",
    "<S> the linear energy spectrum is formed by strong spin - orbit interaction which locks the orbital motion of carriers with their spins . </S>",
    "<S> the interest in dirac fermions in such materials resulted in theoretical consideration and observation of such fundamental physical phenomena as the quantum spin hall effect  @xcite , quantum hall effect ( qhe ) on topological surface states  @xcite , magneto - electric effect  @xcite , and quantum interference effects  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> a considerable attention has been also given to the _ nonlinear _ high frequency ( hf ) transport phenomena . </S>",
    "<S> a plethora of such effects has been treated theoretically , including photogalvanics in ti systems  @xcite , and second harmonic generation ( shg )  @xcite as well as radiation - induced qhe  @xcite and topological states  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> while a great number of proposals have been published in the last two years , the number of experiments on the topic is limited so far by a few publications reporting the observation of shg and photogalvanic effects in 3d tis induced by near infrared radiation  @xcite .    here </S>",
