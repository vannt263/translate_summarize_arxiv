    "<S> a hysteresis model based on the assumption of fixed order magnetization reversals is proposed . </S>",
    "<S> the model uses one - dimensional diagram for representing states of a system despite of two - dimensional preisach diagram . </S>",
    "<S> the distinctive feature of the model is that it is applicable to any system compliant with the return - point memory and includes preisach model as a special case . </S>"
