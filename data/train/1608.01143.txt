    "<S> in this article we discuss the existence of local time for a class of gaussian processes which appears as the solutions to some stochastic evolution equations . </S>",
    "<S> we show that on small intervals such processes are gaussian integrators generated by a continuously invertible operators . </S>",
    "<S> this allows us to conclude that the considered processes have a local time on any finite interval with respect to spatial variable . </S>"
