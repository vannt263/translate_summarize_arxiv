    "<S> hyperbolic polynomials are real polynomials whose real hypersurfaces are maximally nested ovaloids , the innermost of which is convex . </S>",
    "<S> these polynomials appear in many areas of mathematics , including optimization , combinatorics and differential equations . here </S>",
    "<S> we investigate the special connection between a hyperbolic polynomial and the set of polynomials that interlace it . </S>",
    "<S> this set of interlacers is a convex cone , which we write as a linear slice of the cone of nonnegative polynomials . in particular , this allows us to realize any hyperbolicity cone as a slice of the cone of nonnegative polynomials . using a sums of squares relaxation </S>",
    "<S> , we then approximate a hyperbolicity cone by the projection of a spectrahedron . </S>",
    "<S> a multiaffine example coming from the vmos matroid shows that this relaxation is not always exact . </S>",
    "<S> using this theory , we characterize the real stable multiaffine polynomials that have a definite determinantal representation and construct one when it exists . </S>"
