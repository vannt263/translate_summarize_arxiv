    "quantum simulation offers a means to probe many - body physics that can not be simulated efficiently by classical computers , using controllable quantum systems to physically realize a desired many - body hamiltonian@xcite . in the analog approach to quantum simulation exemplified by cold atoms in optical lattices@xcite , the simulator s hamiltonian maps to the desired hamiltonian .",
    "compared to digital quantum simulation , realized via complex sequences of gate operations@xcite , analog quantum simulation is usually carried out with simpler building blocks .",
    "for example , the heisenberg and hubbard hamiltonians of great interest in many - body physics are directly synthesized by cold atoms in optical lattices@xcite . although of immense interest and proposed long ago@xcite ,",
    "analog simulation of fermionic hubbard systems has proven to be very challenging@xcite .",

    "we performed atomic resolution measurements resolving spin - spin interactions of interacting dopants , realizing quantum simulation of a two - site hubbard system . analyzing these local measurements of the spectral function@xcite , we find increasing coulomb correlations and entanglement entropy as the system is `` stretched''@xcite in the regime of non - perturbative interaction strengths @xmath10 .",
    "our experiment is the first to combine low effective temperatures @xmath154 at @xmath155 k and single - site measurement resolution , considered essential@xcite to simulate emergent hubbard phenomena@xcite .",
    "lower effective temperatures @xmath156 are possible at @xmath157 k. for example , @xmath158 hubbard lattices with @xmath159 and @xmath160 have recently been associated with both the pairing state and pseudogap in systems exhibiting unconventional superconductivity@xcite .",
    "the approach generalizes to donors , which can be placed in silicon with atomic - scale precision@xcite and spatially measured _ in - situ _ after epitaxial encapsulation@xcite . in contrast to disordered systems@xcite , atomically engineered dopant lattices will require weak coupling to a reservoir , displaced either vertically as demonstrated herein , or a laterally@xcite .",
    "strain could be used to further enhance the splitting between light and heavy holes , or suppress valley interference processes of electrons@xcite .",
    "interestingly , open hubbard systems which may exhibit unusual kondo behaviour@xcite could also be studied by this method .",
    "the demonstrated measurement of spectral functions could be used to directly determine excitation spectra , evaluate correlation functions@xcite , or obtain quasi - particle interference spectra@xcite , all of which contain rich information about many - body states , including charge - ordering effects .",
    "we envision _ in - situ _ control of filling factor@xcite , using a back - gate or patterned side - gate@xcite .",
    "these capabilities will allow for quantum simulation of chains , ladders , or lattices@xcite at low effective temperatures , having interactions that are engineered atom - by - atom .",

    "<S> in quantum simulation , many - body phenomena are probed in controllable quantum systems . recently , simulation of bose - hubbard hamiltonians using cold atoms revealed previously hidden local correlations </S>",
    "<S> . however , fermionic many - body hubbard phenomena such as unconventional superconductivity and spin liquids are more difficult to simulate using cold atoms . to date </S>",
    "<S> the required single - site measurements and cooling remain problematic , while only ensemble measurements have been achieved . here </S>",
    "<S> we simulate a two - site hubbard hamiltonian at low effective temperatures with single - site resolution using subsurface dopants in silicon . </S>",
    "<S> we measure quasiparticle tunneling maps of spin - resolved states with atomic resolution , finding interference processes from which the entanglement entropy and hubbard interactions are quantified . </S>",
    "<S> entanglement , determined by spin and orbital degrees of freedom , increases with increasing covalent bond length . </S>",
    "<S> we find separation - tunable hubbard interaction strengths that are suitable for simulating strongly correlated phenomena in larger arrays of dopants , establishing dopants as a platform for quantum simulation of the hubbard model . </S>"
