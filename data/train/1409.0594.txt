    "<S> a conjecture proposed by j. tripp in 2002 states that the crossing number of any knot coincides with the canonical genus of its whitehead double . in the meantime , it has been established that this conjecture is true for a large class of alternating knots including @xmath0 torus knots , @xmath1-bridge knots , algebraic alternating knots , and alternating pretzel knots . in this paper , we prove that the conjecture is not true for any alternating @xmath2-braid knot which is the connected sum of two torus knots of type @xmath3 and @xmath0 . </S>",
