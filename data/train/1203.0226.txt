    "<S> we study the asymptotic behavior of the schrdinger equation in the presence of a nonlinearity of hartree type in the semi - classical regime . </S>",
    "<S> our scaling corresponds to a weakly nonlinear regime where the nonlinearity affects the leading order amplitude of the solution without altering the rapid oscillations . </S>",
    "<S> we show the validity of the wkb - analysis when the potential in the nonlinearity is singular around the origin . </S>",
    "<S> no new resonant wave is created in our model , this phenomenon is inhibited due to the nonlinearity . </S>",
    "<S> the nonlocal nature of this latter leads us to build our result on a high - frequency averaging effects . in the proof </S>",
    "<S> we make use of the wiener algebra and the space of square - integrable functions . </S>"
