    "<S> long - duration gamma - ray bursts ( lgrbs ) are generally considered to originate from the massive collapsars . </S>",
    "<S> it is believed that the central engine of gamma - ray bursts ( grbs ) is a neutrino - dominated accretion flow ( ndaf ) around a rotating stellar - mass black hole ( bh ) . </S>",
    "<S> the neutrino annihilation above the ndaf is a feasible mechanism to power grb . in this work , </S>",
    "<S> we analyse the distributions of the isotropic gamma - ray radiated energy and jet kinetic energy of 48 lgrbs . </S>",
    "<S> according to the ndaf and fireball models , we estimate the mean accreted masses of lgrbs in our sample to investigate whether the ndafs can power lgrbs with the reasonable bh parameters and conversion efficiency of neutrino annihilation . </S>",
    "<S> the results indicate that most of the values of the accreted masses are less than @xmath0 for the extreme kerr bhs and high conversion efficiency . </S>",
    "<S> it suggests that the ndafs may be suitable for most of lgrbs except for some extremely high energy sources .    </S>",
