    "<S> we generalise the grtner - ellis theorem of large deviations theory . </S>",
    "<S> our results allow us to derive large deviation type results in stochastic optimal control from the convergence of generalised logarithmic moment generating functions . </S>",
    "<S> they rely on the characterisation of the uniqueness of the solutions of max - plus linear equations . </S>",
    "<S> we give an illustration for a simple investment model , in which logarithmic moment generating functions represent risk - sensitive values . </S>"
