    "the mikheyev - smirnov - wolfenstein ( msw ) mechanism of neutrino oscillations in matter @xcite represents a fascinating solution to the long - standing solar neutrino problem @xcite .",
    "the possible observation of the @xmath0 regeneration effect in the earth @xcite would be a spectacular , solar model independent confirmation of this theory ( for reviews , see @xcite ) .",
    "the available data from the real - time solar neutrino experiment at kamioka @xcite are consistent with no earth regeneration effect within the quoted uncertainties .",
    "this information can be used to exclude a region of the neutrino mass - mixing parameters in fits to the solar neutrino data @xcite .",
    "a larger region of oscillation parameters relevant for the earth effect will be probed by the new generation of solar neutrino experiments ( as shown , e.g. , in @xcite ) .",
    "in particular , the superkamiokande experiment @xcite ( running ) and the sudbury neutrino observatory ( sno ) experiment @xcite ( in construction ) are expected to probe possible day - night modulations of the solar neutrino flux with unprecedented statistics and accuracy .",
    "a correct interpretation of the forthcoming high - quality data will demand precision calculations of the earth - related observables .",

    "we have presented an analytic method for approximating the @xmath0 regeneration probability in the earth , based on a first - order perturbative expansion of the msw hamiltonian and on a convenient parametrization of the earth electron density .",
    "we have also shown how time averages of the @xmath0 survival probability can be transformed into weighted averages over the nadir angle , with weights that can be calculated analytically in several relevant cases . mathematical proofs and final results",
    "are described in detail , especially for the case of annual averages .",
    "we have then calculated accurately the following solar model independent observables for the superkamiokande and sno experiments : ( 1 ) the angular distribution of events ; ( 2 ) the night - day asymmetry of the neutrino rates ; ( 3 ) the fractional deviations of the first two spectral moments of the electron energy distribution ; ( 4 ) the night - day fractional variations of such moments ; and ( 5 ) the charged - to - neutral current event ratio for sno .",
    "the approach to the earth effect presented in this paper allows simpler , faster , and more versatile calculations than brute - force integration methods .",
