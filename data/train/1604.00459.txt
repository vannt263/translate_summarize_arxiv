    "<S> we study the stability of networks of multi - agent systems with local pinning strategies and two types of time delays , namely the transmission delay in the network and the pinning delay of the controllers . </S>",
    "<S> sufficient conditions for stability are derived under specific scenarios by computing or estimating the dominant eigenvalue of the characteristic equation . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , controlling the network by pinning a single node is studied . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , perturbation methods are employed to derive conditions in the limit of small and large pinning strengths . </S>",
    "<S> numerical algorithms are proposed to verify stability , and simulation examples are presented to confirm the efficiency of analytic results . </S>"
