    "the standard mean - field description of scalar bose - einstein condensates ( becs ) is given by the gross - pitaevskii ( gp ) equation  @xcite which has proved to be quite instrumental in describing various properties of the system such as collective modes  @xcite and dynamical instabilities  @xcite .",
    "the hydrodynamic theory provides an equivalent yet intuitively appealing description of the system , since the equations of motion are expressed in terms of physical quantities such as the density of particles and the mass current  @xcite .",
    "the mean - field theory of spinor becs has been developed based on multi - component gp equations by ho  @xcite and ohmi and machida  @xcite .",
    "it is natural to ask what are the corresponding hydrodynamic equations and what physical quantities other than the density of particles and the mass current are needed to make a complete mean - field description of the spinor becs .",
    "spin domain formation and texture in a quenched spin-1 bec were observed  @xcite by in - situ and high - resolution imaging technique for magnetization profiles for spinor becs  @xcite , which prompted the hydrodynamic description for ferromagnetic spinor becs  @xcite .",
    "however , the berkeley experiment  @xcite shows that the magnetization is not fully polarized over the entire condensate .",
    "meanwhile , the majorana representation has been employed to describe general spin states  @xcite .",
    "barnett _ et .",
    "@xcite have developed the mean - field hydrodynamic equations that involve the landau - lifshitz equations for the spin - node vectors and reproduce collective excitations from the viewpoint of the point - group symmetry , while lamacraft  @xcite has derived the low - energy lagrangian and obtained the spin - wave spectra . in this paper",
    ", we derive the most general mean - field hydrodynamic equations for spin-1 becs that are equivalent to the multi - component gp equations and expressed in terms of observable quantities such as the magnetization vector and the nematic ( or quadrupolar ) tensor .",

    "<S> we establish a complete set of hydrodynamic equations for a spin-1 bose - einstein condensate ( bec ) , which are equivalent to the multi - component gross - pitaevskii equations and expressed in terms of only observable physical quantities : the spin density and the nematic ( or quadrupolar ) tensor in addition to the density and the mass current that appear in the hydrodynamic description of a scalar bec . </S>",
    "<S> the obtained hydrodynamic equations involve a generalized mermin - ho relation that is valid regardless of the spatiotemporal dependence of the spin polarization . </S>",
    "<S> low - lying collective modes for phonons and magnons are reproduced by linearizing the hydrodynamic equations . </S>",
    "<S> we also apply the single - mode approximation to the hydrodynamic equations and find a complete set of analytic solutions . </S>"
