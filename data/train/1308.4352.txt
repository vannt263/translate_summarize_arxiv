    "<S> rotation is thought to drive cyclic magnetic activity in the sun and sun - like stars . </S>",
    "<S> stellar dynamos , however , are poorly understood owing to the scarcity of observations of rotation and magnetic fields in stars . here </S>",
    "<S> , inferences are drawn on the internal rotation of a distant sun - like star by studying its global modes of oscillation . </S>",
    "<S> we report asteroseismic constraints imposed on the rotation rate and the inclination of the spin axis of the sun - like star hd52265 , a corot prime target known to host a planetary companion . </S>",
    "<S> these seismic inferences are remarkably consistent with an independent spectroscopic observation ( rotational line broadening ) and with the observed rotation period of starspots . </S>",
    "<S> further , asteroseismology constrains the mass of exoplanet hd52265b . under the standard assumption that the stellar spin axis and the axis of the planetary orbit coincide , the minimum spectroscopic mass of the planet </S>",

    "<S> precise measurements of the frequencies of the global modes of acoustic oscillations place tight constraints on the internal structure of these stars @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> for example , improved stellar parameters are used to refine the physics of stellar interiors and find many applications in astrophysics . </S>",
    "<S> accurate determinations of the radii , masses , and ages of planet - host stars are essential for the characterization of exoplanets detected in transits @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> in this paper we present the first unambiguous measurement of mean internal rotation ( rotation averaged over the entire stellar interior ) and inclination angle of a sun - like star by means of asteroseismology . </S>",
