    "the hadronic contributions include local matrix elements of the @xmath2 tensor , vector , and axial vector currents from the operators @xmath21 in the effective hamiltonian , as well as nonlocal matrix elements of products of the operators @xmath22 with the quark electromagnetic current .",
    "the local hadronic matrix elements are expressed in terms of form factors , and can be calculated directly using lattice qcd @xcite .",
    "the treatment of the nonlocal hadronic matrix elements is significantly more challenging , and is usually based on an operator product expansion at high @xmath23 @xcite , and qcd factorization @xcite combined with light - cone sum rules @xcite at low @xmath23 .",
