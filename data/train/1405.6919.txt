    "<S> the corresponding expressions of the multivariate partition functions are then related to multivariate generalizations of eulerian polynomials for colored permutations considered recently by n. williams and the third author , and others . </S>",
    "<S> we also discuss stability and negative dependence properties satisfied by the partition functions . </S>"
