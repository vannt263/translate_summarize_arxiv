    "quantum effects in strong fields of electromagnetic , gravitational or other nature are conveniently examined in the framework of an adequate quantum field theory ( qft ) with an external background imitating such a strong field .",
    "there are nonperturbative calculation methods in which interaction with strong external fields can be taken into account exactly .",
    "the case of homogeneous and constant electric fields interacting with electrons was examined by schwinger @xcite , who obtained the vacuum - vacuum probability using his method of effective action .",
    "such fields admit analytical solutions of the dirac equation and were frequently used in various qft calculations ; see ref .",
    "@xcite for a review .",
    "it was demonstrated that electron - positron pairs must be observed in electric fields having the order of the critical field @xmath3v / m .",
    "the feynman causal propagator approach feyn49 was generalized by nikishov and narozhny nikis70a , narnik70,nikis79 in order to calculate pair creation and pair scattering in the zero - order approximation with respect to the radiative interaction .",
    "depending on the structure of external backgrounds , different approaches were proposed to calculate the pair creation effect in the framework of relativistic quantum mechanics ; see refs .",
    "books82 - 94,ruffini , geltan15 .",
    "recent progress in laser physics makes it possible to hope that this effect will soon be observed experimentally in laboratory conditions ; see refs .",
    "@xcite for a review .",
    "particle creation by external fields has now become an observable effect in the physics of graphene and similar nanostructures ; see the reviews dassarma , vafvish14 . in particular , the particle creation effect is crucial for understanding the conductivity of graphene in the nonlinear regime van+etal10,gavgity12 .",
    "the case of a constant uniform electric field has many similarities with the case of the de sitter background , e.g. , see refs .",
    "@xcite and references therein .",
    "nowadays , we can see increasing interest in the study of these effects in realistic strong fields , time - dependent and inhomogeneous , mainly by using semiclassical and numerical methods .",
    "a well - known example of strong - field qft is the so - called furry picture of qed , in which the matter field ( dirac field ) is quantized with the help of exact solutions of the dirac equation in magnetic fields , such that the feynman diagrams are calculated exactly with respect to the interaction with these magnetic fields @xcite .",
    "the studies of @xcite proposed a generalization of the furry picture to a class of electric - like external backgrounds that violate the vacuum stability ( in other words , they create electron - positron pairs from vacuum ) .",
    "this class of external backgrounds was restricted by fields switched on and off at the initial and final time instants , respectively . in the framework of this generalized furry picture ,",
    "many general results were obtained gavgf87,gavgit92,gavg08,gavgt06,gavgs15 and specific quantum effects were calculated ; see the review @xcite and the more recent studies  gavg96a , gg06 - 08,gavgity12,dvogavgit14 .    by analogy with the original furry picture ,",
    "the new formulation was , in fact , based on the quantization of the dirac field with the help of some adequate exact solutions of the dirac equation with specific uniform asymptotics at @xmath4 .",
    "in fact , the above - mentioned external backgrounds represent so - called @xmath0-electric potential steps ( their exact definition is given below ) . in case the dirac equation with a specific @xmath0-electric potential step",
    "can be solved , the general formulation gitman77,fp79 allows one to calculate , nonperturbatively with respect to the external field , all the qed processes , both without photons ( zero - order processes ) and with an arbitrary number of photons .",
    "the cases when such solutionscan be found explicitly ( analytically )  are  called exactly solvable , e.g. , see a complete collection of exact solutions of relativistic wave equations in ref .  @xcite . in qed with @xmath0-electric potential steps ,",
    "there exist a few exactly solvable cases that have real physical importance .",
    "those are the sauter - like ( or adiabatic ) electric field , the @xmath1-constant electric field ( a uniform electric field which effectively acts during a sufficiently large but finite time interval @xmath5 ) , the exponentially decaying electric field , and their certain combinations .",
    "one can also notice that the case of harmonically alternating electric field is exactly solvable @xcite . in this case",
    "the alternating direction of the electric field can also contribute to the effect , that is , it is not an example of @xmath0-electric potential step to be considered here .    there exist a number of publications where quantum processes involving the effect of particle creation were studied in the cases of the sauter - like electric field @xcite , the @xmath1-constant electric field gavg08,gavgt06,gavgs15,gavg96a , gg06 - 08,gavgity12,dvogavgit14,baggis75 , and the exponentially decreasing electric field @xcite .",
    "it can be seen that these exactly solvable cases are important in astrophysics , neutrino physics , cosmology , condense matter physics , and so on .",
    "they are needed to develop and check new approximation methods of calculating the key technical elements needed for the generalized furry picture : the matrix elements and green functions .",
    "one may encounter some of these results in books and reviews @xcite .",
    "however , many results are scattered over different publications .",
    "we can see that a survey of exactly solvable cases with @xmath0-electric potential steps , presented on the same footing , may prove useful .",
    "furthermore , it may be used as introductory material for understanding a recent generalization of the furry picture @xcite to a new type of external backgrounds that also violate vacuum stability but belong to the case of time - independent inhomogeneous electric - like fields of constant direction .",
    "these fields have a form of so - called @xmath2-electric potential steps .",
    "solutions of the dirac equation with @xmath2-electric potential steps are quite similar to solutions obtained for @xmath0-electric steps where time @xmath0 is replaced by the spatial coordinate @xmath2 .",
