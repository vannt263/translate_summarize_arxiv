    "<S> spin - orbit coupling can be realized as a random function of coordinate correlated on the spatial scale of the order of 10 nm . by analyzing different regimes of electron transport and spin dynamics , </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate that the spin relaxation can be very slow and the resulting noise power spectrum increases algebraically as frequency goes to zero . </S>",
    "<S> this effect makes spin effects in nanowires best suitable for studies by rapidly developing spin - noise spectroscopy .    </S>",
    "<S> nanostructures are the promising hardware elements for spintronics @xcite  a rapidly developing branch of physics and technology aiming at studies and application of spin - dependent phenomena in the charge transport and information processing . </S>",

    "<S> the effects of spin - orbit ( so ) coupling on the transport were clearly demonstrated there @xcite and the nanowire based qubits were introduced @xcite . </S>",
