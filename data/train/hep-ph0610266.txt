    "<S> we illustrate the general scheme of the sum rule ( sr ) method using 2d quantum harmonic oscillator ( 2dqho ) as a toy model . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce correlator , related to green function of 2dqho , and describe the property of asymptotic freedom for 2dqho . </S>",
    "<S> we explain how the duality conception allows one to describe excited states </S>",
    "<S> . finally we present numerical results and extract some lessons to learn from our exposition . </S>",
    "<S> then we switch to the qcd and show that qcd srs supply us the method to study hadrons in non - perturbative qcd . here </S>",
    "<S> the main emphasis is put on the pion and its distribution amplitude and form factors . </S>"
