    "various cosmological observations are telling us that the standard model of particle physics needs some extension .",
    "the observation of the cosmic microwave background ( cmb ) and its anisotropy strongly supports the inflationary cosmology @xcite , which requires a process to generate the standard model particles after the inflation era .",
    "the particle - antiparticle asymmetry should also be generated after or during the reheating process .",
    "also , the dark matter of the universe must also be produced in the course of the cosmological history .",
    "the standard model should be extended to accommodate the inflation , baryogenesis and dark matter of the universe .",
    "one of the clues towards the mysteries of the universe may be the finite neutrino masses , which are another evidence to go beyond the standard model .",
    "once three kinds of right - handed neutrinos are introduced in the same way as other fermions , the global @xmath4 symmetry becomes non - anomalous , and thus can be promoted to a higgsed gauge symmetry .",
    "it seems that all the ingredients to accommodate the realistic cosmology are present in this @xmath4 extended standard model .",
    "the inflation can be driven by the higgs field to break @xmath4 gauge symmetry  @xcite by assuming an appropriate form of the potential based on the idea of the chaotic inflation @xcite .",
    "after the inflation , the @xmath0 higgs field oscillates about the minimum of the potential where @xmath4 is broken .",
    "the spontaneous breaking of the @xmath0 symmetry can give majorana masses to the right - handed neutrinos through the yukawa coupling , explaining the smallness of the neutrino masses by the seesaw mechanism @xcite .",
    "the very same coupling allows the decay of the inflaton oscillation into the right - handed neutrinos to reheat the universe .",
    "the subsequent decay of the right - handed neutrinos can provide the baryon asymmetry of the universe by the leptogenesis mechanism  @xcite .",
    "the lightest right - handed neutrino should also be produced by the inflaton decay .",
    "if it is long - lived , this non - thermal component is a good candidate of the dark matter of the universe .",
