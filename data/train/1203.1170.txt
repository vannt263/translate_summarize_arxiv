    "two classes of processes occur , the direct - photon and the resolved - photon processes . in the direct processes the photon emitted from the beam lepton enters directly the hard interaction ,",
    "whereas in the resolved processes the photon acts as the source of incoming partons , one of which takes part in the hard interaction .",
    "the distinction between these two classes depends on the factorisation scheme and the order in which the calculation is performed .",
    "the production of heavy quarks is calculated either in the massive scheme , where heavy quarks are produced only perturbatively via boson gluon fusion , or in the massless scheme , where heavy quarks are treated as massless partons .",
    "these two schemes are expected to be appropriate in different regions of phase space  @xcite : the massive scheme is expected to be reliable when the transverse momentum @xmath70 of the heavy quark is of similar size compared to the charm mass @xmath71 , whereas the massless scheme is expected to be valid for @xmath72 . in the general - mass variable - flavour - number scheme ( gmvfns ) a smooth transition from the massive to the massless scheme is provided .",
