    "<S> , we have considered the problem of the description of effective local moments in @xmath0-iron based on the electronic spectrum in paramagnetic phase within lda+dmft approximation . </S>",
    "<S> we find that local moments are formed by both @xmath2 and @xmath3 orbital states , each of them contributing a half of the total moment @xmath126 for @xmath3 electronic states we find pronounced features of non - fermi - liquid behavior , which accompanies earlier observed non - quasiparticle form of @xmath2 states . </S>",
    "<S> the local moment and itinerant states interact with itinerant states via hund interaction , yielding magnetic exchange between the local - moment states via the effective rkky  type mechanism . </S>",
    "<S> the obtained exchange integrals are well captured by the lda+dmft approach . </S>",
    "<S> the main origin of the intersite interaction of these moments is attributed to the @xmath2-@xmath3 hybridization , which yields magnetic exchange , dominating on the nearest - neighbour sites . </S>",
    "<S> contrary to the previous studieslichtenstein1987,gornostyrev , we do not however assume some magnetic ordering for the electronic system .    </S>",
    "<S> we also emphasize that non  </S>",
    "<S> local self  energy corrections , as well as vertex corrections , missed in our investigation , can make the described physical picture more precise . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , non - local effects allow for the non  zero non - diagonal @xmath2@xmath3 self - energy matrix elements and therefore possibly renormalize the strength of exchange interaction , as well as the self - energy of @xmath3 electronic states . </S>",
    "<S> the role of the vertex corrections , only roughly accounted in the considered approach , also requires additional study . </S>",
