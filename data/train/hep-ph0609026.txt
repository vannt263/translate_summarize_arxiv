    "the analysis of baryon - baryon , baryon - meson scattering and photo - production experiments require the knowledge of the hadronic coupling constants involving mesons . experimentally , only hadronic coupling constant with pion @xmath6 is determined accurately both from nucleon - nucleon and pion - nucleon scattering .",
    "however , situation for the kaon case is not simple and to reproduce experimental result for kaon - nucleon scattering cross section and kaon photo - production , many phenomenologically undetermined coupling constants are needed ( see @xcite ) .",
    "therefore , it seems a formidable task to determine the kaon - baryon and pion - baryon coupling constants . for this reason , reliable theoretical approach for estimating these coupling constants is needed . among all non - perturbative methods ,",
    "qcd sum rules @xcite are especially powerful in studying the properties of hadrons .",
    "this method is successfully applied to investigation of a variety of problems in hadron physics , in particular the calculation of the meson - baryon coupling constants .",
    "calculation of the pion - nucleon coupling constants received a lot of attention ( see e.g. @xcite ) .",
    "the k meson baryon coupling constants also were studied in the framework of the sum rules ( sr ) method in @xcite and in light cone sr ( lcsr ) in @xcite .",
    "the main result of these works is that the predictions of sr for meson baryon couplings depend strongly on the choice of the dirac structure ( see e.g. @xcite ) .",
    "our numerical calculations show that only the @xmath7 structure leads to a reliable prediction on the meson - baryon coupling constants .    in this work",
    ", we will study pseudoscalar @xmath1 and @xmath0 meson - baryon coupling constants in the framework of an alternative approach to the traditional sr , i.e. lcsr using the most general expression for hadronic currents as well as @xmath2 flavor symmetry breaking strange quark effects .",
    "lcsr is based on the operator product expansion near the light cone , which is an expansion of the time ordered product over the twist rather than the dimension of the operators .",
    "main contribution in this approach comes from the operators having the lowest twist .",
    "the main ingredient of lcsr is the wave functions of hadrons , which define the matrix element of non - local operators between the vacuum and the one particle hadron state ( for more see e.g. @xcite ) .",
