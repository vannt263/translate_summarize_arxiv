    "<S> we introduce the notion of crossings and nestings of a permutation . </S>",
    "<S> we compute the generating function of permutations with a fixed number of weak exceedances , crossings and nestings . </S>",
    "<S> we link alignments and permutation patterns to these statistics . </S>",
    "<S> we generalize to the case of decorated permutations . </S>",
    "<S> finally we show how this is related to the stationary distribution of the partially asymmetric exclusion process ( pasep ) model . </S>"
