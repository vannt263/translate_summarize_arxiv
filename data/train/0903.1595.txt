    "<S> the first author proved that the harmonic convolution of a normalized right half - plane mapping with either another normalized right half - plane mapping or a normalized vertical strip mapping is convex in the direction of the real axis . </S>",
    "<S> provided that it is locally univalent . in this paper , we prove that in general the assumption of local univalency can not be omitted . </S>",
    "<S> however , we are able to show that in some cases these harmonic convolutions are locally univalent . using this </S>",
    "<S> we obtain interesting examples of univalent harmonic maps one of which is a map onto the plane with two parallel slits .    </S>",
