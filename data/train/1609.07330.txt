    "<S> traditionally , the dirichlet - multinomial distribution has been recognized as a key model for contingency tables generated by cluster sampling schemes . </S>",
    "<S> there are , however , other possible distributions appropriate for these contingency tables . </S>",
    "<S> this paper introduces new test - statistics capable to test log - linear modeling hypotheses with no distributional specification , when the individuals of the clusters are possibly homogeneously correlated . </S>",
    "<S> the estimator for the intracluster correlation coefficient proposed in alonso - revenga et al . </S>",
    "<S> ( 2016 ) , valid for different cluster sizes , plays a crucial role in the construction of the goodness - of - fit test - statistic .    * </S>",
