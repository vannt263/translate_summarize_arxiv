    "<S> in this paper we introduce new distributions which are solutions of higher - order laplace equations . it is proved that their densities can be obtained by folding and symmetrizing cauchy distributions . </S>",
    "<S> another class of probability laws related to higher - order laplace equations is obtained by composing pseudo - processes with positively - skewed stable distributions which produce asymmetric cauchy densities in the odd - order case . </S>",
    "<S> a special attention is devoted to the third - order laplace equation where the connection between the cauchy distribution and the airy functions is obtained and analyzed . </S>"
