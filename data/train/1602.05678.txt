    "<S> charge transfer states in organic semiconductors play crucial roles in processes such as singlet fission and exciton dissociation at donor / acceptor interfaces . recently , a time - resolved spectroscopy study of dinaphtho[2,3-b:2@xmath03@xmath0-f]thieno[3,2-b]-thiophene ( dntt ) thin films provided evidence for the formation of mixed frenkel and charge - transfer excitons after the photoexcitation . here </S>",
    "<S> we investigate optical properties and excitation dynamics of the dntt thin films by combining ab initio calculations and a stochastic schrdinger equation . </S>",
    "<S> our theory predicts that the low - energy frenkel exciton band consists of 8 to 47% ct character . </S>",
    "<S> the quantum dynamics simulations show coherent dynamics of frenkel and ct states in 50 fs after the optical excitation . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate the role of charge delocalization and localization in the mixing of ct states with frenkel excitons as well as the role of their decoherence .    </S>",
    "<S> organic semiconductors ( oscs ) are widely investigated as candidates for inexpensive and flexible materials for photovoltaics and other optoelectronic applications .  </S>",
    "<S> @xcite . in recent years </S>",
    "<S> , new oscs have been designed and improved through computational modeling  @xcite and virtual high - throughput screening  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> modeling energy and charge transport properties is also essential for understanding structure - property relationships and thus for rationally designing novel oscs  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> the low - energy optical excitations in oscs lead to the formation of a bound electron  hole ( e  h ) pair , a frenkel exciton . </S>",
    "<S> excitonic properties of organic crystals are substantially different from those of isolated molecules , owing to excitonic couplings , near - field interactions between electronic transitions  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> interactions with a charge - transfer ( ct ) state  a state in which the electron and hole are located on spatially separated regions  can also affect the optical properties of an exciton , as has been demonstrated by several theoretical studies  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> experimentally , the degree of ct character has been studied by momentum - dependent electron - loss spectroscopy  @xcite or electroabsorption spectroscopy  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> ct states can act as precursors for interfacial ct states  @xcite ; mixing of ct states with frenkel excitons would facilitate charge separation in photovoltaic materials . </S>",
    "<S> they have also gained recent attention due to their relevance to singlet fission  @xcite , in which singlet to triplet conversion can proceed via sequential ct steps  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> here we focus on dinaphtho[2,3-b:2@xmath03@xmath0-f]thieno[3,2-b]-thiophene ( dntt ) , a @xmath1-type osc originally introduced by takimiya and co - workers  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> dntt and its derivatives  @xcite have gained attention due to their high hole mobility values and air stability . a recent time - resolved spectroscopy study by ishino et al .  </S>",
    "<S> @xcite concludes that mixed frenkel and ct excitons are formed after the optical excitation . </S>",
    "<S> although the degree of ct character in excited states has been reported as described in the earlier paragraph , its role in excitation dynamics has remained unclear . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , there has been growing interest in the effects of excited - state delocalizations on charge photogeneration processes  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> therefore , we studied the excited - state dynamics in dntt as a model system to provide insight into the role of the ct states in oscs .    in this letter </S>",
    "<S> , we present a theoretical study of optical properties and dissipative excitonic dynamics of dntt . </S>",
    "<S> the optical absorption spectra and associated ct character are computed by introducing a tight - binding hamiltonian that treats frenkel and ct states on the same footing . </S>",
    "<S> the excitation dynamics of the dntt thin films was simulated by a stochastic schrdinger equation with spectral densities derived from molecular dynamics simulations and excited state quantum chemistry calculations . </S>",
    "<S> our simulations show coherent dynamics of the frenkel and ct excitons in 50 fs after optical excitation . </S>",
