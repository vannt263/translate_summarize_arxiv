    "<S> we study the collider phenomenology of the eletroweak phase transition and electroweak baryogenesis in the framework of the effective field theory . </S>",
    "<S> our study shows that the effective theory using the dimension-6 operators can enforce strong first order phase transition and provide sizable cp violation to realize a successful electroweak baryogenesis . </S>",
    "<S> such dimension-6 operators can induce interesting higgs phenomenology to verify at colliders such as the lhc and the planning cepc . </S>",
    "<S> we then demonstrate this effective theory can originate from vector - like quarks and triplet higgs . </S>"
