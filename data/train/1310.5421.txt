    "<S> an analytical expression of the coefficient of restitution for viscoelastic materials is derived for the viscous - dominant case , such as collisions of polymeric melt . </S>",
    "<S> the recently proposed normal impact force model between two colliding viscoelastic droplets is employed . to analytically solve the nonlinear differential equation of deformation caused by the other droplet , a perturbation method is applied . by combining the forward collision and the inverse collision </S>",
    "<S> , we can get an analytical expression for the coefficient of restitution , which can also be used to further analyze other properties related with the viscoelasticity . </S>"
