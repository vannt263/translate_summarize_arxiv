    "the adsorption of gases on solid surfaces is a topic of fundamental interest for various applications @xcite . from the theoretical point of view",
    ", the process can be described in terms of the lattice - gas model @xcite .",
    "a lattice gas is a system of @xmath2 molecules bound not more than one per site to a set of @xmath3 equivalent , distinguishable , and independent sites , and without interactions between bound molecules .",
    "many studies have been carried out on the adsorption behavior of small molecules in such systems .",
    "however , the problem in which a 2d lattice contains isolated points ( vacancies ) as well as @xmath0-mers ( particles occupying @xmath0 adjacent sites ) has not been solved in closed form and still represents a major challenge in surface science .",
    "a previous paper @xcite was devoted to the study of long straight rigid rods adsorbed on square lattices . in ref .",
    "[ @xcite ] , the helmholtz free energy of the system and its derivatives were written in terms of the order parameter @xmath1 , which characterizes the nematic phase occurring in the system at intermediate densities @xcite .",
    "then , using the principle of minimum free energy with @xmath1 as a parameter , the main adsorption properties were calculated .",
    "comparisons with monte carlo ( mc ) simulations revealed that the new thermodynamic description was significantly better than the existing theoretical models developed to treat the polymer adsorption problem .",
    "in contrast to the statistic for the simple particles , where the arrangement of the adsorption sites in space is immaterial , the structure of lattice space plays such a fundamental role in determining the statistics of @xmath0-mers .",
    "then , it is of interest and of value to inquire how a specific lattice structure influences the main thermodynamic properties of adsorbed polyatomics . in this sense ,",
    "the aim of the present work is to extent the study in ref .",
    "[ @xcite ] to triangular lattices .",
    "the problem is not only of theoretical interest , but also has practical importance .",
