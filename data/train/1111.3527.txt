    "<S> we study a family of distributions that arise in critical unitary random matrix ensembles . </S>",
    "<S> they are expressed as fredholm determinants and describe the limiting distribution of the largest eigenvalue when the dimension of the random matrices tends to infinity . </S>",
    "<S> the family contains the tracy  </S>",
    "<S> widom distribution and higher order analogues of it . </S>",
    "<S> we compute the distributions numerically by solving a riemann  </S>",
    "<S> hilbert problem numerically , plot the distributions , and discuss several properties that they appear to exhibit . </S>"
