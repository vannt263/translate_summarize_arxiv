    "in recent years , ultralong - range rydberg molecules ( ulrm ) formed when a ground - state atom is bound to a rydberg atom have developed into a field of intense research  @xcite .",
    "these ultralong - range species , which were predicted theoretically back in 2000  @xcite , exhibit an exotic binding mechanism based on the low - energy collisions between a rydberg electron and a ground - state atom .",
    "the elastic scattering is typically described by @xmath0- and @xmath1-wave fermi - type pseudopotentials  @xcite , and leads to the unusual oscillatory behavior of the corresponding adiabatic potential energy curves .",
    "the polar ( trilobite ) molecular states emerging from the near degenerate high angular momentum atomic rydberg manifold possess a huge dipole moment whereas the non - polar molecular states emerging , e.g. , from quantum defect split @xmath3states exhibit only a minor dipole moment  @xcite .",
    "their vibrational energies are in the ghz and mhz regimes , respectively  @xcite .",

    "a selective excitation of rovibrational molecular states with a variable degree of alignment and antialignment has been demonstrated experimentally by using a magnetic field  @xcite .",
