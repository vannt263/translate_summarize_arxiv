    "<S> to compute solutions of sparse polynomial systems efficiently we have to exploit the structure of their newton polytopes . </S>",
    "<S> while the application of polyhedral methods naturally excludes solutions with zero components , an irreducible decomposition of a variety is typically understood in affine space , including also those components with zero coordinates . </S>",
    "<S> we present a polyhedral method to compute all affine solution sets of a polynomial system . </S>",
    "<S> the method enumerates all factors contributing to a generalized permanent . </S>",
    "<S> toric solution sets are recovered as a special case of this enumeration . for sparse systems as adjacent 2-by-2 minors </S>",
    "<S> our methods scale much better than the techniques from numerical algebraic geometry .    </S>",
    "<S> * key words and phrases . * affine set , irreducible decomposition , newton polytope , monomial map , permanent , polyhedral method , puiseux series , sparse polynomial . </S>"
