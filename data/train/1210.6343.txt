    "<S> we develop a new discrete mathematical model which includes the classical sudoku puzzle , latin squares and gerechte designs . </S>",
    "<S> this problem is described by integer equations and a special type of inequality constraint . </S>",
    "<S> we consider solutions of this generalized problem and derive a necessary condition on these solutions . </S>",
    "<S> the results are illustrated with examples . </S>"
