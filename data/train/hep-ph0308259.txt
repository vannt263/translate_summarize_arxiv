    "semileptonic decay of charm meson is important for studying strong and weak interactions .",
    "it can be used to test techniques developed for solving perturbative and nonperturbative problems in quantum chromodynamics ( qcd ) , and to extract elements of cabibbo - kobayashi - maskawa ( ckm ) matrix .",
    "semileptonic decay is simpler than hadronic decay of charm meson because leptons do not involve strong interaction .",
    "the amplitude of semileptonic decay can be decomposed into several transition form factors due to lorentz property of the hadronic matrix element .",
    "the form factors include all the nonperturbative effects .",
    "several methods can be used to treat these problems , such as quark model , qcd sum rule and lattice qcd , among which , qcd sum rule and lattice qcd are based on the first principle of qcd .",
