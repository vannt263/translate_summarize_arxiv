    "the coulomb gauge is one of the most popular gauges in quantum chromodynamics ( qcd ) .",
    "in addition to the convenient choice for calculations , the coulomb - gauge qcd is also interesting from the theoretical point of view , especially in the context of the canonical quantization @xcite .",
    "in addition , color confinement has been investigated in the coulomb gauge in terms of the color - coulomb interaction @xcite . as a new picture of hadrons",
    ", the gluon - chain picture also stems from the coulomb - gauge qcd @xcite .    in the coulomb - gauge qcd",
    ", the faddeev - popov ( fp ) operator is the important key quantity . actually , the color - coulomb energy is enhanced by the near - zero fp eigenmodes , which would lead to the confining force between color charges .",
    "this confinement scenario is known as the gribov - zwanziger scenario @xcite . from lattice - qcd numerical calculations ,",
    "the color - coulomb energy certainly gives a linear interquark potential @xcite .",
    "the confinement is also characterized by the infinite self - energy of an isolated color - charge . in the coulomb gauge",
    ", the color - coulomb self - energy is expressed in terms of the fp eigenmodes , and its divergence is originated from near - zero fp eigenmodes , as was shown in lattice qcd calculations @xcite .",
    "then , it is meaningful to investigate the fp eigenmodes for the understanding of non - perturbative qcd @xcite . in this paper",
    ", we investigate the fp eigenmodes in terms of the gluon - momentum components .",
    "since the qcd interactions are mediated by gluons , the fp eigenmodes should reflect the properties of the gluon . to analyze the correspondence between the gluon field and the fp eigenmodes ,",
    "we decompose the link - variables in terms of momentum components , and remove infrared or ultraviolet momentum modes in lattice qcd @xcite .",
    "the organization of this paper is as follows . in sec.ii , we review the color - coulomb energy and properties of the fp eigenmodes in the coulomb gauge .",
    "we also introduce the gluon - momentum cut method in lattice qcd . in sec.iii",

    "actually , when the center vortex is removed from the qcd vacuum , the string tension obtained from the wilson loop vanishes and the system becomes non - confining @xcite , which is observed in the ir - momentum cut of gluons @xcite .",
    "also , as greensite and olejnk pointed out , by removing the center vortex in the mc gauge , the instantaneous potential in the coulomb gauge becomes non - confining @xcite , which resembles the ir - cut of gluons , as shown in fig.[figcolorcoulomb](a ) .",

    "these changes are quite similar to those of the ir - momentum cut of gluons , as shown in figs.[figfpspectrumlow](a ) , [ figflambda](a ) , and [ figfpspectrumir](a)-(c ) .",
    "in fact , both operations lead to the similar drastic change of fp eigenmodes . in the center - vortex removal , however , the fp spectrum does not coincide with the @xmath112-functional form of the free - field case , but becomes multi - peaks with a finite width .",

    "we have analyzed low - lying fp eigenmodes with the ir / uv - momentum gluon cut , and also performed the full fp eigenmodes analysis .    in the uv - momentum gluon cut , both color - coulomb energy and fp eigenmodes are almost unchanged , which indicates that high - momentum gluons are irrelevant for confining scenario in the coulomb gauge .",

    "these results indicate the importance of the ir - momentum gluon for non - trivial structure of the fp eigenmodes .",
