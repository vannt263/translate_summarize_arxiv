    "<S> the diffuse interstellar bands ( dibs ) are ubiquitous absorption spectral features arising from the tenuous material in the space between stars  the interstellar medium ( ism ) . </S>",
    "<S> since their first detection nearly nine decades ago , over 400 dibs have been observed in the visible and near - infrared wavelength range in both the milky way and external galaxies , both nearby and distant . </S>",
    "<S> however , the identity of the species responsible for these bands remains as one of the most enigmatic mysteries in astrophysics . </S>",

    "<S> its carrier also remains unclear since its first detection 46 years ago . </S>",
