    "we have considered non - gaussian random normal matrix ensembles .",
    "we have derived analytic expressions for spectral density and @xmath0-eigenvalue correlation functions for arbitrary weights and shown them to be stationary and universal . we have used the mc method for generating the eigenvalues and correlation functions for such ensembles .",
