    "there is considerable current interest in quantifying entanglement in various quantum many - body systems .",
    "entanglement in spin chains , correlated electrons , interacting bosons and other models was studied in detail @xcite .",
    "entanglement is a resource for quantum computation , it shows how much correlation we can use to control quantum devices @xcite .",
    "there are several different measures of entanglement .",
    "the most famous is the von neumann ( entanglement ) entropy of a subsystem @xcite .",
    "this measure has recently been used to detect quantum phase transitions and topological / quantum order @xcite in strongly correlated systems .",
    "we can also use the rnyi entropy to quantify the entanglement .",
    "the renyi entropy was first proposed in information theory @xcite .",

    "studying entanglement also helps to understand the physics of quantum spin systems @xcite .",
    "the model introduced by affleck , kennedy , lieb , and tasaki ( aklt model ) @xcite plays a very important role in condensed matter physics .",
    "the exact ground state of this model is known as the valence - bond solid ( vbs ) state . in 1983 ,",
    "haldane @xcite conjectured that the antiferromagnetic heisenberg hamiltonian describing half - odd - integer spins is gap - less , but for integer spins it has a gap .",
    "the aklt model agrees with this conjecture and enables us to understand the ground - state properties of gapped spin chains in a unified fashion .",
    "the construction of the aklt - type model is not restricted to one dimension .",
    "in fact , the aklt model was formulated on an arbitrary graph and an integration over classical spins was used for the evaluation of correlation functions in the vbs ground state @xcite .",
    "the vbs state has attracted revived interest from the viewpoint of quantum information theory .",
    "an implementation of the aklt model in optical lattices was proposed recently @xcite , and the use of the aklt model for _ universal quantum computation _ was discussed in @xcite . the vbs state is also closely related to the laughlin wave function @xcite and to the fractional quantum hall effect @xcite .",
