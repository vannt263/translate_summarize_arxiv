    "<S> we give a definition of ` coherent tangent bundles ' , which is an intrinsic formulation of wave fronts . in our application of coherent tangent bundles for wave fronts , the first fundamental forms and the third fundamental forms </S>",
    "<S> are considered as induced metrics of certain homomorphisms between vector bundles . </S>",
    "<S> they satisfy the completely same conditions , and so can reverse roles with each other . for a given wave front of a @xmath0-manifold </S>",
    "<S> , there are two gauss - bonnet formulas . by exchanging the roles of the fundamental forms , we get two new additional gauss - bonnet formulas for the third fundamental form . </S>",
    "<S> surprisingly , these are different from those for the first fundamental form , and using these four formulas , we get several new results on the topology and geometry of wave fronts . </S>"
