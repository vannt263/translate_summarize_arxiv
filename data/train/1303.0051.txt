    "<S> we consider the eigenvalue problem for the laplace operator in a planar domain which can be decomposed into a bounded domain of arbitrary shape and elongated `` branches '' of variable cross - sectional profiles . when the eigenvalue is smaller than a prescribed threshold , the corresponding eigenfunction decays exponentially along each branch . </S>",
    "<S> we prove this behavior for robin boundary condition and illustrate some related results by numerically computed eigenfunctions .    </S>",
