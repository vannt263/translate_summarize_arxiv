    "overall , the bss radial distribution is primarily modelled by the long - term effect of dynamical friction acting on the cluster binary population ( and its progeny ) since the early stages of cluster evolution .",
    "in fact , what we call mt - bss today is the by - product of the evolution of a @xmath11 binary that has been orbiting the cluster and suffering the effects of dynamical friction for a significant fraction of the cluster lifetime . the efficiency of dynamical friction decreases for increasing radial distance from the centre , as a function of the local velocity dispersion and mass density .",
