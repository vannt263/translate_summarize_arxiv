    "in this paper we analyse mutations between fake weighted projective spaces ; equivalently we analyse mutations between lattice simplices .",
    "mutations arise naturally when considering mirror symmetry for fano manifolds .",
    "a fano manifold @xmath0 is expected to correspond under mirror symmetry to a laurent polynomial  @xcite . in general",
    "there will be many different laurent polynomials which correspond to a given fano manifold , and it is expected that these laurent polynomials are related via birational transformations analogous to cluster transformations  @xcite .",
    "these cluster - style transformations act on newton polytopes via mutations ; see definition  [ defn : mutation ] below .",
    "a mutation between newton polytopes can be thought of as the tropicalisation of the corresponding cluster - type transformation .",
    "ilten  @xcite has shown that if two lattice polytopes @xmath1 and @xmath2 are related by mutation then the corresponding toric varieties @xmath3 and @xmath4 are deformation equivalent : there exists a flat family @xmath5 such that @xmath6 and @xmath7 .",
    "mutations are thus expected to form the one - skeleton of the tropicalisation of the hilbert scheme .",
    "our understanding of this one - skeleton is rudimentary but improving  @xcite ; in this paper we conduct the first systematic analysis in higher dimensions .",
    "the notion of mutation raises many new and interesting combinatorial questions : for example , how can polytopes be classified up to mutation , and what properties of polytopes are mutation - invariant ? here",
    "we begin to address these questions by analysing the behaviour of lattice simplicies under mutation . in two dimensions ,",
    "akhtar and kasprzyk determined how the weights of a fake weighted projective plane , i.e.  the weights of a lattice triangle , change under mutation , and showed that mutations between fake weighted projective planes are multiplicity - preserving  @xcite .",
    "we show below that the situation in higher dimensions is different .",
    "we give an explicit formula ( theorem  [ mutationformula ] ) for how the weights of a fake weighted projective space , i.e.  the weights of a lattice simplex , change under mutation , and derive a strong necessary condition ( theorem  [ onlyedges ] ) for a mutation to preserve multiplicity . in    [ sec : canonical_sings][sec : terminal_sings ] we apply our results to the study of fake weighted projective spaces of high degree with canonical and terminal singularities .      the authors thank mohammad akhtar and alessio corti for useful conversations .",
