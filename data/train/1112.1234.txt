    "our aim here is to investigate the general many  particle case .",

    "<S> the eigenvalue absorption for a many  particle hamiltonian depending on a parameter is analyzed in the framework of non  relativistic quantum mechanics . </S>",
    "<S> the long  range part of pair potentials is assumed to be pure coulomb and no restriction on the particle statistics is imposed . </S>",
    "<S> it is proved that if the lowest dissociation threshold corresponds to the decay into two likewise non  </S>",
    "<S> zero charged clusters then the bound state , which approaches the threshold , does not spread and eventually becomes the bound state at threshold . </S>",
    "<S> the obtained results have applications in atomic and nuclear physics . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we prove that an atomic ion with the critical charge @xmath0 and @xmath1 electrons has a bound state at threshold given that @xmath2 , whereby the electrons are treated as fermions and the mass of the nucleus is finite . </S>"
