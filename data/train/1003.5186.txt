    "supersymmetry  ( susy )  @xcite is a well motivated theory to extend the standard model  ( sm ) of particle physics .",
    "susy models are not only favored by gauge coupling unification and naturalness considerations , but are also attractive from the cosmological point of view .",
    "for instance the lightest susy particle  ( lsp ) is a good dark matter candidate if it is stable , massive and weakly interacting  @xcite .",
    "susy models can also provide new sources of cp violation  @xcite . in the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm )",
    "@xcite , the complex parameters are conventionally chosen to be the higgsino mass parameter @xmath0 , the @xmath1 and @xmath2 gaugino mass parameters @xmath3 and @xmath4 , respectively , and the trilinear scalar coupling parameters @xmath5 of the third generation sfermions ( @xmath6 ) , @xmath7 these phases contribute to the electric dipole moments ( edms ) , in particular to those of thallium  @xcite , mercury  @xcite , the neutron  @xcite , and the deuteron  @xcite , which can be beyond their current experimental upper bounds  @xcite .",
    "the experimental limits generally restrict the cp phases to be smaller than @xmath8 , in particular the phase @xmath9  @xcite .",
    "however , the extent to which the edms can constrain the susy phases strongly depends on the considered model and its parameters  @xcite .    as shown for example in ref  @xcite ,",
    "due to cancellations among different contributions to the edms , only isolated points in the cp phase space can give large cp - violating signals at the lhc .",
    "it is important to search for these signals , since the cancellations could be a consequence of a deeper model that correlates the phases .",
    "in addition , the existing edm bounds can also be fulfilled by including lepton flavor violating couplings in the slepton sector  @xcite .",
    "this is important when considering for example susy seesaw models , where cp violation in the slepton sector is connected to the neutrino sector and leptogenesis  @xcite",
    ".    thus measurements of susy cp observables outside the low energy edm sector are necessary to independently determine or constrain the phases",
    ". in particular , the phases of the trilinear scalar coupling parameters @xmath5 have a significant impact on the mssm higgs sector  @xcite .",
    "loop effects , dominantly mediated by third generation squarks , can generate large cp - violating scalar - pseudoscalar transitions among the neutral higgs bosons  @xcite . as a result ,",
    "the lightest higgs boson with a mass of order @xmath10  gev or @xmath11  gev @xcite can not be excluded by measurements at lep  @xcite .",
    "the fundamental properties and the phenomenology of cp - violating neutral higgs boson mixings have been investigated in detail in the literature  @xcite .",
    "the phases can also drastically change other susy particle masses , their cross sections , branching ratios  @xcite , and longitudinal polarizations of final fermions  @xcite .",
    "although such cp - even observables can be very sensitive to the cp phases ( the observables can change by an order of magnitude and more ) , cp - odd ( t - odd ) observables have to be measured for a direct evidence of cp violation .",
    "cp - odd observables are , for example , rate asymmetries of cross sections , distributions , and partial decay widths  @xcite .",
    "however , these observables require the presence of absorptive phases , e.g. from loops .",
    "thus they usually do not exceed the size of @xmath12 , unless they are resonantly enhanced  @xcite .",
    "larger cp asymmetries in particle decay chains can be obtained with triple products of final particle momenta  @xcite .",
    "they already appear at tree level due to spin correlations .",
    "triple product asymmetries have been intensively studied in the production and decay of neutralinos  @xcite and charginos  @xcite at the ilc  @xcite , also using transversely polarized beams  @xcite . at the lhc",
