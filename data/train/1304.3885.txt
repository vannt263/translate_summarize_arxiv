    "<S> the impact of renormalization of the electron spectrum on the chemical potential , heat capacity , and oscillating magnetic moment is studied . </S>",
    "<S> the cases of low and high temperatures are considered . at low temperatures , </S>",
    "<S> doped graphene behaves as the usual fermi liquids with the power temperature laws for thermodynamic properties . </S>",
    "<S> however , at high temperatures and relatively low carrier concentrations , it exhibits the collective electron - holes features : the chemical potential tends to its value in the undoped case going with the temperature to the charge neutrality point . </S>",
    "<S> simultaneously , the electron contribution into the heat capacity tends to the constant value , as in the case of the boltzmann statistics . </S>"
