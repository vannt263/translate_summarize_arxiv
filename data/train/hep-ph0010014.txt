    "<S> we present a bound on the imaginary part of the single helicity - flip amplitude for spin@xmath0-spin@xmath0 scattering at small momentum transfer . </S>",
    "<S> the variational method of lagrange multipliers is employed to optimize the single - flip amplitude using the values of @xmath1 , @xmath2 and diffraction slope as equality constraints in addition to the inequality constraints resulting from unitarity . </S>",
    "<S> such bounds provide important information related to the determination of polarization of a proton beam . in the case of elastic proton collisions </S>",
    "<S> the analyzing power at small scattering angles offers a method of measuring the polarization of a proton beam , the accuracy of the polarization measurement depending on the single helicity - flip amplitude . </S>",
    "<S> the bound obtained on the imaginary part of the single helicity - flip amplitude indicates that the analyzing power for proton - proton collisions in the coulomb nuclear interference region should take positive nonzero values at high energies . </S>"
