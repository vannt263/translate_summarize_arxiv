    "<S> we exploit the separation of scales in weakly - bound nuclei to compute e2 transitions and electric form factors in a halo eft framework . </S>",
    "<S> the relevant degrees of freedom are the core and the halo neutron . </S>",
    "<S> the eft expansion is carried out in powers of @xmath0 , where @xmath1 and @xmath2 denote the length scales of the core and halo , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> we include the strong @xmath3-wave and @xmath4-wave interactions by introducing dimer fields . </S>",
    "<S> the dimer propagators are regulated by employing the power divergence subtraction scheme and matched to the effective range expansion in the respective channel . </S>",
    "<S> electromagnetic interactions are included via minimal substitution in the lagrangian . </S>",
    "<S> we demonstrate that , depending on the observable and respective partial wave , additional local gauge - invariant operators contribute in lo , nlo and higher orders .    </S>",
