    "<S> in this paper , we study the motion of spirals by mean curvature type motion in the ( two dimensional ) plane . </S>",
    "<S> our motivation comes from dislocation dynamics ; in this context , spirals appear when a screw dislocation line reaches the surface of a crystal . </S>",
    "<S> the first main result of this paper is a comparison principle for the corresponding parabolic quasi - linear equation . as far as motion of spirals </S>",
    "<S> are concerned , the novelty and originality of our setting and results come from the fact that , first , the singularity generated by the attached end point of spirals is taken into account for the first time , and second , spirals are studied in the whole space . </S>",
    "<S> our second main result states that the cauchy problem is well - posed in the class of sub - linear weak ( viscosity ) solutions . </S>",
    "<S> we also explain how to get the existence of smooth solutions when initial data satisfy an additional compatibility condition .    </S>",
