    "<S> , we first classify the possible light neutrino mass matrices with one and two texture zeros and then numerically evaluate the parameter space in terms of atmospheric mixing angle @xmath0 and dirac cp phase @xmath1 which satisfies the texture zero conditions . </S>",
    "<S> we then feed these parameter values into the numerical analysis in order to study the sensitivity of dune experiment to them . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the dune will be able to exclude some of these texture zero mass matrices which restrict the @xmath2 to a very specific range of values . </S>"
