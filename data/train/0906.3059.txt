    "the coexistence of magnetic and ferroelectric phases in multiferroic materials gives rise to important effects related to cross correlation between order parameters and external fields.@xcite a notable consequence is that the elementary excitations are not purely magnetic or ferroelectric in character .",

    "<S> we propose a model for the pair of electromagnon excitations observed in the class of multiferroic materials _ </S>",
    "<S> r_mno@xmath0 ( _ r _ is a rare - earth ion ) . </S>",
    "<S> the model is based on a harmonic cycloid ground state interacting with a zone - edge magnon and its twin excitation separated in momentum space by two times the cycloid wave vector . </S>",
    "<S> the pair of electromagnons is activated by cross coupling between magnetostriction and spin - orbit interactions . </S>",
    "<S> remarkably , the spectral weight of the twin electromagnon is directly related to the presence of a magnetically induced oscillatory polarization in the ground state . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to the surprising prediction that tbmno@xmath0 has an oscillatory polarization with amplitude @xmath1 times larger than its uniform polarization . </S>"
