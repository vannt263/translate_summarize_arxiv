    "<S> the concept of valence bond resonance plays a fundamental role in the theory of the chemical bond and is believed to lie at the heart of many - body quantum physical phenomena . </S>",
    "<S> here we show direct experimental evidence of a time - resolved valence bond quantum resonance with ultracold bosonic atoms in an optical lattice . by means of a superlattice structure </S>",
    "<S> we create a three - dimensional array of independent four - site plaquettes , which we can fully control and manipulate in parallel . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we show how small - scale plaquette resonating valence bond ( rvb ) states with @xmath0- and @xmath1-wave symmetry can be created and characterized . </S>",
    "<S> we anticipate our findings to open the path towards the creation and analysis of many - body rvb states in ultracold atomic gases .    in his theory of the chemical bond , pauling developed the concept of quantum resonance : a quantum superposition of resonant structures with different arrangements of the valence bonds @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> such resonant states are essential to explain the chemical properties of certain organic molecules like benzene @xcite . in the context of high temperature superconductivity , </S>",
    "<S> anderson extended pauling s notion to a macroscopic level , by proposing that electrons in mott insulating solid state materials could form resonating valence bond ( rvb ) states @xcite . in a mott insulating phase </S>",
    "<S> , electrons are localized to individual atoms or molecules , and the fluctuations in the charge ( density ) degree of freedom are strongly suppressed . </S>",
    "<S> the physics is dictated by the remaining spins , which interact via superexchange interactions . under certain conditions , </S>",
    "<S> the localized spins are expected to evade local order and continue to fluctuate down to zero temperature , forming a coherent superposition of many different arrangements in which the spins are paired up into singlets or valence bonds .    </S>",
    "<S> ultracold atomic gases in optical lattices @xcite and other quantum optical systems are promising candidates for the quantum simulation of rvb states @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> their realization would allow one to gain valuable insight into the entanglement properties of these states as well as to answer fundamental questions in condensed matter physics like their stability under specific hamiltonians such as the hubbard model , or to test their exotic superconducting properties upon doping @xcite . in this work </S>",
    "<S> we create an array of small - scale versions of pauling - like rvb states in four - site plaquettes and study their basic physical properties . </S>",
