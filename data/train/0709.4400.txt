    "metal poor galaxies are the key to understanding star formation and gas enrichment in a nearly pristine interstellar medium , and may provide a template for understanding the formation of the first stars .",
    "extremely metal poor galaxies ( xmpgs ) are extremely rare : fewer than 1% of dwarf galaxies are xmpgs , with a gas - phase oxygen abundance @xmath5 @xcite .",
    "known xmpgs are mostly gas - rich , blue compact galaxies with spectra dominated by emission lines . the first surveys to search for xmpgs were objective prism surveys @xcite .",
    "abundance studies of these surveys revealed up to a dozen xmpgs @xcite , but none so extreme as i zw 18 @xcite .",
    "i zw 18 , now along with sbs 0335 - 052w and ddo 68 , are the most metal poor local galaxies known , with  ranging 7.12 - 7.17 @xcite .",

    "nearby @xmath8 long duration grbs are observed in metal poor galaxies @xcite .",
    "@xcite argue that the link between metal poor galaxies and grbs originates in the atmospheres of massive stars .",
    "massive metal poor stars lack the opacity to support significant stellar winds , and thus can produce the anomalous type 1c supernovae associated with nearby grbs .",
    "@xcite argue that grbs are linked to young starburst populations , which at low redshift happen to be found predominantly in low mass , metal - poor galaxies . at large redshift @xmath9 ,",
    "the metallicity of grb hosts is much harder to determine @xcite .",
    "at least one grb host galaxy is an extremely red and probably metal rich object @xcite , and other grb hosts @xmath10 appear consistent with normal metallicity - luminosity relations @xcite .",
    "grbs clearly occur in different types of host galaxies ; they are not tied exclusively to the most metal poor galaxies .",
    "yet finding and studying metal poor galaxies with properties similar to nearby grb host galaxies may yield critical clues about the environment where nearby grbs form .",
