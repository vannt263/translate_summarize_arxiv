    "<S> we first give a self - contain introduction to the mould formalism of jean ecalle . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a dictionary between moulds and the classical lie algebraic formalism using non - commutative formal power series . </S>",
    "<S> we then give full proofs and details for results announced by j. ecalle and b. vallet about the trimmed form of diffeomorphisms . </S>",
    "<S> we then discuss a mould approach to the classical poincar - dulac normal form of diffeomorphisms . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the universal character of moulds taking place in normalization problems . </S>"
