    "the gauge - invariant construction by lscher@xcite based on the ginsparg - wilson relation provides a procedure to determine the phase of the overlap formula in a gauge - invariant manner for anomaly - free chiral gauge theories .",
    "for dirac fermions , it provides a gauge - covariant and local lattice dirac operator satisfying the ginsparg - wilson relation@xcite .",

    "<S> we consider a numerical method to solve the local cohomology problem related to the gauge anomaly cancellation in u(1 ) chiral gauge theories . in the cohomological analysis of the chiral anomaly </S>",
    "<S> , it is required to carry out the differentiation and the integration of the anomaly with respect to the continuous parameter for the interpolation of the admissible gauge fields . in our numerical approach , </S>",
    "<S> the differentiation is evaluated explicitly through the rational approximation of the overlap dirac operator with zolotarev optimization . </S>",
    "<S> the integration is performed with a gaussian quadrature formula , which turns out to show rather good convergence . </S>",
    "<S> the poincar lemma is reformulated for the finite lattice and is implemented numerically . </S>",
    "<S> we compute the current associated with the cohomologically trivial part of the chiral anomaly in two - dimensions and check its locality properties . </S>"
