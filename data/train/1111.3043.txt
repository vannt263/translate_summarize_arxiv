    "<S> the willmore flow is well known problem from the differential geometry . </S>",
    "<S> it minimizes the willmore functional defined as integral of the mean - curvature square over given manifold . for the graph formulation , we derive modification of the willmore flow with anisotropic mean curvature . </S>",
    "<S> we define the weak solution and we prove an energy equality . </S>",
    "<S> we approximate the solution numerically by the complementary finite volume method . to show the stability , we re - formulate the resulting scheme in terms of the finite difference method . </S>",
    "<S> by using simple framework of fdm we show discrete version of the energy equality . </S>",
    "<S> the time discretization is done by the method of lines and the resulting system of odes is solved by the runge - kutta - merson solver with adaptive integration step . </S>",
    "<S> we also show experimental order of convergence as well as results of the numerical experiments , both for several different anisotropies .    </S>",
