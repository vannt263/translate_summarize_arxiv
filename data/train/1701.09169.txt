    "gravitational lensing is one of useful tools to search for dark and compact objects .",
    "gravitational lensing under a quasi - newtonian approximation has been discussed widely  @xcite while gravitational lensing without the approximation has been also investigated  @xcite . in 1959",
    "darwin pointed out that an infinite number of ghost images appear near a light sphere or photon sphere  @xcite in the schwarzschild spacetime  @xcite .",
    "the gravitational lensing of the faint images has been discussed by several authors  @xcite .",
    "gravitational lensing of light rays with a deflection angle which is almost @xmath0 is called retrolensing . holz and wheeler",
    "discussed the retrolensing of sun lights reflected by the light sphere of a black hole near the solar system  @xcite .",
    "one can distinguish between the light curves of retrolensing and other light curves since the retrolensing light curves have a characteristic shape and are symmetric in time . if one observe a light curve with the characteristic shape and with solar spectra on the elliptic , he or she can say that it is a retrolensing light curve of the sun .",
    "a black hole in the galactic center as a retrolens was also investigated  @xcite .",
    "the effects of a electrical charge  @xcite and rotation  @xcite of a black hole on a retrolensing light curve and a double image  @xcite was also considered .",
    "gravitational lensing is also caused by a wormhole  @xcite .",
    "one can survey wormholes with a negative arnowitt - deser - misner ( adm ) mass  @xcite , a vanishing adm mass  , and a positive adm mass  @xcite by gravitational lensing . in 1973",
    "ellis obtained a static and spherically symmetric wormhole solution of einstein equations with a phantom scalar field  @xcite .",
    "the traversable wormhole with vanishing adm masses is called ellis wormhole or ellis - bronnikov wormhole since it was also obtained by bronnikov in a scalar - tensor theory in the same year  @xcite .",
    "the wormhole is often also referred the morris - thorne wormhole  @xcite without mentioning ellis  @xcite and bronnikov s works  @xcite .",
    "the instability of the ellis wormhole was revealed by several authors  @xcite , contrary to a conclusion of an earlier work  @xcite .",
    "some static and spherically symmetric wormholes have the same metric as the one of the ellis wormhole in a vanishing adm mass case  @xcite . in 2013 bronnikov",
    "_ et al . _ showed that a wormhole with the same metric as the metric of the ellis wormhole and with electrically charged dust with negative energy density is linearly stable under spherically symmetric and axial perturbations  @xcite .",
    "the quasinormal mode was also investigated  @xcite .",
