    "<S> we present the calculations of the complete next - to - leading order ( nlo ) inclusive total cross sections for the associated production processes @xmath0 in the minimal supersymmetric standard model at the cern large hadron collider . </S>",
    "<S> our calculations show that the total cross sections for the @xmath1 production for the lighter top squark masses in the region 100 gev @xmath2 160 gev can reach 1 pb in the favorable parameter space allowed by the current precise experiments , and in other cases the total cross sections generally vary from @xmath3 fb to several hundred fb except both @xmath4 500 gev and the @xmath5 production channel . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we find that the nlo qcd corrections in general enhance the leading order total cross sections significantly , and vastly reduce the dependence of the total cross sections on the renormalization / factorization scale , which leads to increased confidence in predictions based on these results . </S>"
