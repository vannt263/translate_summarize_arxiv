    "to summarize , in this work we construct a gauged non - linear - o(3)-@xmath48 model .",
    "the gauging of the u(1 ) subgroup includes nonminimal coupling in the covariant derivative .",
    "the gauge - field dynamics are governed by both maxwell and chern - simons terms .",
    "it is worthwhile to note that when one chooses a specific value for the nonminimal coupling constant , namely , @xmath107 , some fields decouple and the higgs potential changes , leading the system to behave like a pure chern - simons system .",

    "the solitons found have topological and non - topological characteristics .",
