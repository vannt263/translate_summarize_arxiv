    "<S> using low - temperature scanning tunneling spectroscopy applied to the cs - induced two - dimensional electron system ( 2des ) on _ p_-type insb(110 ) , we probe electron - electron interaction effects in the quantum hall regime . </S>",
    "<S> the 2des is decoupled from bulk states and exhibits spreading resistance within the insulating quantum hall phases . in quantitative agreement with calculations we find an exchange enhancement of the spin splitting . </S>",
