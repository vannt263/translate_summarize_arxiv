    "<S> non - equilibrium driving of biochemical reactions is believed to enable their robust functioning despite the presence of thermal fluctuations and other sources of disorder . </S>",
    "<S> such robust functions include sensory adaptation , enhanced enyzmatic specificity and maintenance of coherent oscillations . </S>",
    "<S> non - equilibrium biochemical reactions can be modeled as a master equation whose rate constants break detailed balance . </S>",
    "<S> we find that non equilibrium fluxes can support topologically protected boundary modes that resemble similar modes in electronic and mechanical systems . </S>",
    "<S> we show that when a biochemical network can be decomposed into two ordered bulks that meet at a possibly disordered interferace , the ordered bulks can be each associated with a topologically invariant winding number . if the winding numbers are mismatched , we are guaranteed that the steady state distribution is localized at the interface between the bulks , even in the presence of strong disorder in reaction rates . </S>",
    "<S> we argue that our work provides a framework for how biochemical systems can use non equilibrium driving to achieve robust function .    </S>",
    "<S> mechanisms used by biological systems to process information and achieve ordered states are far - from - equilibrium and require energy dissipation  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> kinetic proofreading mechanisms used by the cell to ensure high fidelity copying of genetic material use futile energy consuming cycles to decrease the error rates in dna replication  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> non - equilibrium forces have also been implicated in the functioning of biochemical networks responsible for adaptation  @xcite , ultra - sensitivity@xcite@xcite , and timing of events in cell cycle  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> while the behavior and characteristics of equilibrium systems - where no energy is dissipated - are well known , general principles governing the steady state or fluctuations in it in far - from - equilibrium conditions are just being discovered  @xcite . here , </S>",
    "<S> we show that non - equilibrium stochastic systems can support localized modes that have a character similar to topologically protected boundary modes in mechanical and electronic systems@xcite . </S>",
    "<S> our results are applicable to idealized non - equilibrium markov state models of kinetic proofreading networks@xcite , sensory adaptation networks  @xcite , and other biophysical processes . </S>",
    "<S> our results provide a framework for understanding how non - equilibrium biochemical networks are able to perform their function robustly in fluctuating conditions  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> non - equilibrium markov state networks that support topologically protected modes have properties that are much more robust to disorder in kinetic rates , unlike networks without protection . </S>"
