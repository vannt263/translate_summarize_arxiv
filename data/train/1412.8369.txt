    "<S> the transport and continuum equations exhibit a number of conservation laws . for example </S>",
    "<S> , scalar multiplication is conserved by the transport equation , while positivity of probabilities is conserved by the continuum equation . </S>",
    "<S> certain discretization techniques , such as particle based methods , conserve these properties , but converge slower than spectral discretization methods on smooth data . </S>",
    "<S> standard spectral discretization methods , on the other hand , do not conserve the invariants of the transport equation and the continuum equation . </S>",
    "<S> this article constructs a novel spectral discretization technique that conserves these important invariants while simultaneously preserving spectral convergence rates . </S>",
    "<S> the performance of this proposed method is illustrated on several numerical experiments . </S>"
