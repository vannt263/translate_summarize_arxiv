    "<S> properties of random mixed states of dimension @xmath0 distributed uniformly with respect to the hilbert - schmidt measure are investigated . </S>",
    "<S> we show that for large @xmath0 , due to the concentration of measure , the trace distance between two random states tends to a fixed number @xmath1 , which yields the helstrom bound on their distinguishability . </S>",
    "<S> to arrive at this result we apply free random calculus and derive the symmetrized marchenko  pastur distribution , which is shown to describe numerical data for the model of coupled quantum kicked tops . </S>",
    "<S> asymptotic value for the root fidelity between two random states , @xmath2 , can serve as a universal reference value for further theoretical and experimental studies . </S>",
    "<S> analogous results for quantum relative entropy and chernoff quantity provide other bounds on the distinguishablity of both states in a multiple measurement setup due to the quantum sanov theorem . </S>",
    "<S> we study also mean entropy of coherence of random pure and mixed states and entanglement of a generic mixed state of a bi  partite system .    </S>",
