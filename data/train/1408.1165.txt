    "in quantum mechanics , heisenberg s uncertainty principle says that the more precisely the position of some particle is determined , the less precisely its momentum can be known .",
    "this uncertainty principle is a fundamental result in quantum mechanics . by using the shannon entropy of the measurement , entropic uncertainty principles",

    "<S> the classical uncertainty principles deal with functions on abelian groups . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we discuss the uncertainty principles for finite index subfactors which include the cases for finite groups and finite dimensional kac algebras . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the hausdorff - young inequality , young s inequality , the hirschman - beckner uncertainty principle , the donoho - stark uncertainty principle . </S>",
    "<S> we characterize the minimizers of the uncertainty principles . </S>",
    "<S> we also prove that the minimizer is uniquely determined by the supports of itself and its fourier transform . </S>",
    "<S> the proofs take the advantage of the analytic and the categorial perspectives of subfactor planar algebras . </S>",
    "<S> our method to prove the uncertainty principles also works for more general cases , such as popa s @xmath0-lattices , modular tensor categories etc .    </S>",
