    "<S> we derive explicit analytic expressions for the lateral force for two different configurations with corrugations , parallel plates and concentric cylinders . by making use of the multiple scattering formalism </S>",
    "<S> , we calculate the force for a scalar field under the influence of a delta - function potential that has sinusoidal dependence in one direction simulating the corrugations . by making a perturbative expansion in the amplitude of the corrugation we find the leading order for the corrugated concentric cylinders and the next - to - leading order for the corrugated parallel plates . </S>"
