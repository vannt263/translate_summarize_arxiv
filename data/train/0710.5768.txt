    "<S> hard exclusive reactions are the tool to learn about generalized parton distributions , which provide a more complete parametrization of the nucleon than the ordinary parton distribution functions . </S>",
    "<S> recent measurements by the hermes collaboration of the exclusive production of photons , i.e. , deeply - virtual compton scattering , are summarized and compared to model calculations , focusing on the measurements and model comparisons relevant to the extraction of quark orbital angular momentum and on the measurements on heavy nuclei . </S>"
