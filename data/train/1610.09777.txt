    "<S> recently developed notion of shortcut to adiabaticity ( sta ) has spawned intriguing prospects . </S>",
    "<S> so far , the most experimental investigations of sta are implemented in the ideal thermal gas or the weakly interacting ultracold bose gases . here </S>",
    "<S> we report the first demonstration of a many - body sta in a 3d anisotropically trapped unitary fermi gas . </S>",
    "<S> a new dynamical scaling law is demonstrated on such a strongly interacting quantum gas . by simply engineering the frequency aspect ratio of a harmonic trap , </S>",
