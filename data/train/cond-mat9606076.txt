    "recently stringari @xcite has noted that the gp equation can be rewritten as equations for the condensate density and phase variables and that within a thomas - fermi approximation ( tfa ) which neglects the kinetic energy associated with density fluctuations , these have the familiar structure of the hydrodynamic equations of a superfluid at @xmath0 @xcite .",
    "this allows one to give a simple theory of the low - frequency excitations of a trapped bose gas , without the necessity of solving the full set of coupled bogoliubov equations @xcite .    in the present paper",
    ", we develop the approach of ref .",
