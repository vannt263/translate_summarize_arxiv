    "<S> the multiplicity , average transverse momentum , and charged particle transverse momentum distributions have recently been measured in lhc experiments . </S>",
    "<S> the multiplicity and average transverse momentum grow with beam energy . </S>",
    "<S> such growth is expected in the theory of the color glass condensate , a theory that incorporates the physics of saturation into the evolution of the gluon distribution . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the energy dependence of the @xmath0 data and the lhc data for @xmath1 scattering at @xmath2 gev may be simply described using a minimal amount of model input . </S>",
    "<S> such a description uses parameters consistent with the color glass condensate descriptions of hera and rhic experimental data . </S>"
