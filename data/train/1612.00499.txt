    "<S> in the present paper , we consider large - scale continuous - time differential matrix riccati equations having low rank right - hand sides . </S>",
    "<S> these equations are generally solved by backward differentiation formula ( bdf ) or rosenbrock methods leading to a large scale algebraic riccati equation which has to be solved for each timestep . </S>",
    "<S> we propose a new approach , based on the reduction of the problem dimension prior to integration . </S>",
    "<S> we project the initial problem onto an extended block krylov subspace and obtain a low - dimentional differential algebraic riccati equation . </S>",
    "<S> the latter matrix differential problem is then solved by backward differentiation formula ( bdf ) method and the obtained solution is used to reconstruct an approximate solution of the original problem . </S>",
    "<S> we give some theoretical results and a simple expression of the residual allowing the implementation of a stop test in order to limit the dimension of the projection space . </S>",
    "<S> some numerical experiments will be given .    </S>",
    "<S> extended block krylov , low rank approximation , differential matrix riccati equations . </S>"
