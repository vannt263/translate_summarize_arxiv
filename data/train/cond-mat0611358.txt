    "the spatio - temporal behaviour and the formation of spatial patterns became recently an important topic in hydrodynamics systems , nonlinear optics , oscillatory chemical reactions and in theoretical ecology  @xcite-@xcite . during the last three decades",

    "<S> a spatially extended lotka - volterra system of two competing species in the presence of two correlated noise sources is analyzed : ( i ) an external multiplicative time correlated noise , which mimics the interaction between the system and the environment ; ( ii ) a dichotomous stochastic process , whose jump rate is a periodic function , which represents the interaction parameter between the species . </S>",
    "<S> the moment equations for the species densities are derived in gaussian approximation , using a mean field approach . within this formalism </S>",
    "<S> we study the effect of the external time correlated noise on the ecosystem dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the time behavior of the @xmath0 order moments are independent on the multiplicative noise source . </S>",
    "<S> however the behavior of the @xmath1 order moments is strongly affected both by the intensity and the correlation time of the multiplicative noise . </S>",
    "<S> finally we compare our results with those obtained studying the system dynamics by a coupled map lattice model . </S>"
