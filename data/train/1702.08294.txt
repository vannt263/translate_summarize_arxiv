    "<S> these works employ a measure - evolve - measure ( mem ) sequence , in which a first quantum non - demolition ( qnd ) measurement produces a state with reduced projection noise , a period of free evolution accumulates signal , and a second qnd measurement detects the change relative to the first measurement . </S>",
