    "<S> we construct rotating magnetic solutions to the three - dimensional einstein - maxwell - chern - simons - dilaton theory with a liouville potential . </S>",
    "<S> these include a class of black hole solutions which generalize the warped ads black holes . </S>",
    "<S> the regular black holes belong to two disjoint sectors . </S>",
    "<S> the first sector includes black holes which have a positive mass and are co - rotating , while the black holes of the second sector have a negative mass and are counter - rotating . </S>",
    "<S> we also show that a particular , non - black hole , subfamily of our three - dimensional solutions may be uplifted to new regular non - asymptotically flat solutions of five - dimensional einstein - maxwell - chern - simons theory . </S>"
