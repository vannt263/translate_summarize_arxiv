    "<S> a dynamic cluster quantum monte carlo approximation is used to study the effective pairing interaction of a 2d hubbard model with a near neighbor hopping @xmath0 and an on - site coulomb interaction @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> the effective pairing interaction is characterized in terms of the momentum and frequency dependence of the eigenfunction of the leading eigenvalue of the irreducible particle - particle vertex . </S>",
    "<S> the momentum dependence of this eigenfunction is found to vary as @xmath2 over most of the brillouin zone and its frequency dependence is determined by the exchange energy @xmath3 . </S>",
    "<S> this implies that the effective pairing interaction is attractive for singlets formed between near - neighbor sites and retarded on a time scale set by @xmath4 . </S>",
    "<S> the strength of the pairing interaction measured by the size of the d - wave eigenvalue peaks for @xmath1 of order the bandwidth @xmath5 . </S>",
    "<S> it is found to increase as the system is underdoped . </S>"
