    "the rgb bump is the effect of a well known phase of the rgb evolution of low mass stars ( see * ? ? ?",
    "* ; * ? ? ?",
    "* and references therein ) . when the h - burning shell of a rgb star encounters the chemical discontinuity left behind by the maximum penetration of the convective envelope , the luminosity of the star has a slight drop",
