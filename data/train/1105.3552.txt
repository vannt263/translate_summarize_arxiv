    "<S> the large and moderate deviation theory can achieve this goal . </S>",
    "<S> motivated by the delta method in weak convergence , a general delta method in large deviations is proposed . </S>",
    "<S> the new method can be widely applied to driving the moderate deviations of estimators and is illustrated by examples including the wilcoxon statistic , the kaplan  </S>",
    "<S> meier estimator , the empirical quantile processes and the empirical copula function . </S>",
    "<S> we also improve the existing moderate deviations results for @xmath0-estimators and @xmath1-statistics by the new method . </S>",
    "<S> some applications of moderate deviations to statistical hypothesis testing are provided .    .    . </S>"
