    "<S> on a si(111 ) vicinal face near the structural transition temperature , the @xmath0 structure and the @xmath1 structure coexist in a terrace : the @xmath0 structure is in the lower side of the step edge and the @xmath1 structure in the upper side . </S>",
    "<S> the diffusion coefficient of adatoms is different in the two structures . taking account of the gap in the diffusion coefficient at the step , we study the possibility of step wandering induced by drift of adatoms . </S>",
    "<S> a linear stability analysis shows that the step wandering always occurs with step - down drift if the diffusion coefficient has a gap at the step . </S>",
    "<S> formation of straight grooves by the step wandering is expected from a nonlinear analysis . </S>",
    "<S> the stability analysis also shows that step bunching occurs irrespective of the drift direction if the diffusion in the lower side of the step is faster . </S>",
    "<S> the step bunching disturbs the formation of grooves . </S>",
    "<S> if step - step repulsion is strong , however , the step bunching is suppressed and the straight grooves appear . </S>",
    "<S> monte carlo simulation confirms these predictions . </S>"
