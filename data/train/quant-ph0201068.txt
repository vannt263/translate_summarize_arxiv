    "building a practical quantum computer with a large number of qubits has recently attracted much attention since the useful quantum algorithms  @xcite and the quantum error - correction codes  @xcite were developed .",
    "basic quantum logic gates were implemented on trapped ions  @xcite , qed cavities  @xcite , and liquid state nmr  @xcite .",
    "solid - state devices have advantages of large scale integration , flexibility in the design , and easy connection to conventional electronic devices .",
    "some of solid - state devices proposed are as follows : electron spins in quantum dots  @xcite , nuclear spins of donor atom in silicon@xcite , and ultrasmall josephson junctions  @xcite .",
    "two types of superconducting qubits based on ultrasmall josephson junctions were proposed .",
    "one is to use a number of excess cooper pairs on a superconducting cooper - pair box , called the superconducting charge qubit  @xcite . and the other is to utilize a single flux quantum of a superconducting loop , called the superconducting flux qubit  @xcite . here , we focus on superconducting charge qubits . in theoretical aspects ,",
