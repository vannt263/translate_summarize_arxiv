    "<S> in the framework of the method of constraint system quantization , a quantum gravitational system ( qgs ) with the maximally symmetric geometry is studied . </S>",
    "<S> the state vector of the qgs satisfies the set of wave equations which describes the time evolution of a quantum system in the space of quantum fields . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that this state vector can be normalized to unity . </S>",
    "<S> the generalization of the wave equations to the domain of negative values of the cosmic scale factor is made . </S>",
    "<S> for the arrow of time from past to future , the state vector describes the qgs contracting for the negative values of the scale factor and expanding for its positive values . </S>",
    "<S> the intensity distributions of matter are calculated for two exactly solvable models of spatially closed and flat qgss formed by dust and radiation . </S>",
    "<S> the analogies with the motion in time of minimum wave packet for spatially closed qgs and with the phenomenon of diffraction in optics for flat qgs are drawn .    </S>",
