    "all other branching fraction results are first reported measurements .    a structure at @xmath3 is found to be an admixture of a spin-1 and a spin-3 resonance with a significance of at least 10 standard deviations .",

    "the properties of those states and of the @xmath4 resonance are measured .",
    "the spin of the @xmath4 resonance is experimentally determined for the first time , and is confirmed to be 2 .",
    "the mass and width of this state are determined with significantly better precision than previous measurements  @xcite .",
    "the result for the width is consistent with the previous world average .",
    "the result for the mass , however , is somewhat below the previous average , which is dominated by a measurement by the babar collaboration  @xcite based on inclusive production in @xmath194 collisions .",
    "the dalitz plot analysis technique used in this paper ensures that the background under the @xmath4 peak is small and does not contain large contributions from decays of higher resonances , resulting in much lower systematic uncertainties on the measured parameters compared to the inclusive approach .",
