    "<S> we determine the concentration of the generated plasma , the maximum electric field , the front width and the voltage over the @xmath1 base as functions of front velocity and doping of the @xmath1 base . </S>",
    "<S> theory takes into account that drift velocities and impact ionization coefficients differ between electrons and holes , and it makes quantitative predictions for any semiconductor material possible . </S>"
