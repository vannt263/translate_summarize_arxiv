    "<S> we study operators acting on a  tensor product hilbert space and investigate their product numerical range , product numerical radius and separable numerical range . </S>",
    "<S> concrete bounds for the product numerical range for hermitian operators are derived . </S>",
    "<S> product numerical range of a  non - hermitian operator forms a  subset of the standard numerical range containing the barycenter of the spectrum . while the latter set is convex , the product range needs not to be convex nor simply connected . </S>",
    "<S> the product numerical range of a  tensor product is equal to the minkowski product of numerical ranges of individual factors . </S>"
