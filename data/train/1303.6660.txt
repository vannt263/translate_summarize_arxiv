    "<S> we study the asymptotic distribution of resonances for scattering by compactly supported potentials in @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> we first establish an upper bound for the resonance counting function that depends only on the dimension and the support of the potential . </S>",
    "<S> we then establish the sharpness of this estimate by proving the a weyl law for the resonance counting function holds in the case of radial potentials vanishing to some finite order at the edge of the support . as an application of the existence of potentials that saturate the upper bound , we derive additional resonance asymptotics that hold in a suitable generic sense . </S>",
    "<S> these generic results include asymptotics for the resonance count in sectors . </S>"
