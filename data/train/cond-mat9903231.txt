    "<S> we have shown that via the renormalized charging energy and the renormalized environment conductance , our nonperturbative expressions of the total action can be mapped onto the corresponding perturbative formulas . </S>",
    "<S> this provides a straightforward way to go beyond the perturbation theory . for the impedance being a pure resistance </S>",
