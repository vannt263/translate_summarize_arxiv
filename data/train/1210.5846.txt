    "<S> tropical algebraic geometry is a degeneration of classical geometry which loose the property of unique factorization for polynomials . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we explore a structure that is known to be a semi - degeneration between the classical algebra and the tropical algebra , and show that unique factorization fails in several variables due to geometric reasons , not just algebraic . </S>",
    "<S> we also show that unique factorization does hold for a certain interesting subset of polynomials . </S>"
