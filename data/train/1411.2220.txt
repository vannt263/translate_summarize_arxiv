    "<S> we construct a nonstandard finite difference numerical scheme to approximate stochastic differential equations ( sdes ) using the idea of weighed step introduced by r.e . mickens . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the strong convergence of our scheme under locally lipschitz conditions of a sde and linear growth condition . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the preservation of domain invariance by our scheme under a minimal condition depending on a discretization parameter and unconditionally for the expectation of the approximate solution . </S>",
    "<S> the results are illustrated through the geometric brownian motion . </S>",
    "<S> the new scheme shows a greater behavior compared to the euler - maruyama scheme and balanced implicit methods which are widely used in the literature and applications .    </S>",
