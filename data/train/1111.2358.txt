    "cavity quantum electrodynamics ( cqed ) is an ideal scenario for research on fundamentals of quantum theory and quantum information .",
    "achievements on both , the construction of high quality cavities and control of atom - field interactions , are associated with the use of the entanglement properties for the successful generation of quantum states of light , such as einstein - podolski - rosen ( epr ) , schrdinger cat and fock states  @xcite .",
    "an important consequence of the high experimental control in cqed was the successful reconstruction of quantum states of light prepared inside a high quality cavity  @xcite .",
    "this procedure allows , for example , the observation of decoherence process of a schrdinger cat - like state , through the analysis of snapshots of the wigner function  @xcite . the same setup was also used to reconstruct the wigner function of fock states with more than one photon  @xcite .",
    "recently , cqed setups has been used in order to study three - photon correlations  @xcite , the apparition of electromagnetically induced transparency using rubidium  @xcite and cesium  @xcite single atoms and quantum jumps  @xcite .",
    "a particular cqed experimental setup could include a bimodal cavity . in this kind of device",
    ", two bosonic modes with different polarizations are prepared inside the cavity  @xcite . in the context of quantum information theory ,",
    "a bimodal cavity is interesting because the additional mode acts as a third photonic qubit ( besides the atom and the first cavity mode qubits ) , opening new possibilities for implementation of quantum information protocols  @xcite .",
    "potential applications of bimodal cavities have been analyzed in some recent works .",
    "those include the implementation of quantum logic gates  @xcite and generation of entangled states  @xcite .",
    "entanglement between the two modes of a superconducting cavity was experimentally demonstrated  @xcite , where a maximally entangled state was created .",
    "schrdinger cat states can be generated by interaction between atoms and the electromagnetic field confined in a high - quality cavity .",
    "cqed schemes are used to prepare a superposition of two packages , as the experiment reported by delglise _ et .",
