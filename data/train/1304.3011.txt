    "<S> we give a sufficient condition under which vanishing property of cochran  orr  teichner knot concordance obstructions splits under connected sum . </S>",
    "<S> the condition is described in terms of self - annihilating submodules with respect to higher - order blanchfield linking forms . </S>",
    "<S> this extends results of levine and the authors on distinguishing knots with coprime alexander polynomials up to concordance . as an application </S>",
    "<S> , we show that the knots constructed by cochran , orr and teichner as the first examples of nonslice knots with vanishing casson  gordon invariants are not concordant to any knot of genus  1 . </S>"
