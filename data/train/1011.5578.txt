    "a droplet of ink falling into water deforms from a sphere to a toroidal shape due to the formation of a vortex ring  @xcite .",
    "an air bubble rising in water exhibits complicated dynamics such as zigzag and spiral motion  @xcite .",
    "such phenomena are caused by the interaction between the droplet ( ink ) or the bubble ( air ) with the medium ( water ) , where the former moves through the latter .",
    "the subject of the present paper is the dynamics of phase - separated two - component superfluids in similar situations , that is , a bubble of one component moving through the other component .    a variety of dynamical properties have been observed in two - component bose ",
    "einstein condensates ( bec )  @xcite .",
    "two immiscible components prepared in a spatially overlapped state separate dynamically  @xcite and develop into a domain structure  @xcite .",
    "a similar structure is observed in a miscible system with counterflow  @xcite . in rotating two - component becs ,",
    "a square vortex lattice has been observed  @xcite .",
    "these dynamical properties of two - component becs have been studied theoretically  @xcite .",
    "theoretical predictions have been made on various structures in two - component becs ; for example , skyrmions  @xcite , solitary wave complexes  @xcite , and vortex bright solitons  @xcite .",
    "various interface instabilities known in classical fluid mechanics are predicted to emerge in two - component becs with an interface , namely the rayleigh  taylor instability  @xcite , the kelvin ",
    "helmholtz instability  @xcite , and the richtmyer  meshkov instability  @xcite .",
    "the behavior of two - component becs strongly depends on the miscibility , which is determined by the intra- and inter - component interactions .",
    "recently , these interactions in two - component systems have been controlled using the feshbach resonance  @xcite , and phase separation dynamics have been studied in a controlled manner  @xcite .    in the present paper , we investigate the dynamics of a bubble in a phase - separated two - component bec , where a small fraction of one component moves through the other component . we show that the system exhibits a rich variety of dynamics depending on the parameters and dimensionality . in two dimensions ( 2d ) , a circular bubble at rest deforms into an ellipse as it accelerates and breaks into pieces with quantized vortices . for strong phase separation",
    ", a vortex street is formed in the wake of the moving bubble , as in the bnard ",
    "von krmn vortex street in a single - component bec  @xcite . in three dimensions ( 3d ) , a spherical bubble deforms into a torus , as in classical fluids  @xcite .",
    "the significant difference from classical fluids is that the toroidal bubble is accompanied by a quantized vortex ring .",
    "when two or more rings are generated , they leapfrog each other .",
