    "<S> we can not perform the projective measurement of a momentum on a half line since it is not an observable . </S>",
    "<S> nevertheless , we would like to obtain some physical information of the momentum on a half line . </S>",
    "<S> we define an optimality for measurement as minimizing the variance between an inferred outcome of the measured system before a measuring process and a measurement outcome of the probe system after the measuring process , restricting our attention to the covariant measurement studied by holevo . extending the domain of the momentum operator on a half line by introducing a two dimensional hilbert space </S>",
    "<S> to be tensored , we make it self - adjoint and explicitly construct a model hamiltonian for the measured and probe systems . by taking the partial trace over the newly introduced hilbert space , the optimal covariant positive operator valued measure ( povm ) of a momentum on a half line is reproduced . </S>",
    "<S> we physically describe the measuring process to optimally evaluate the momentum of a particle on a half line . </S>"
