    "the use of copulas in statistics allows for the dependence of random variables to be modeled separately from the marginal distributions .",
    "this property makes copulas a very convenient tool to be used by statistical modelers ( @xcite ) .",

    "a simulation study ( section [ ch : simstudy ] ) demonstrated that our bayesian model selection approach is superior to @xcite s frequentist one .",
    "the better performance of our bayesian selection scheme can be attributed to its simultaneous and prior - informed selection of the pair copula families @xmath188 of a given level @xmath47 , while dimann s algorithm selects them one - by - one .",
    "in addition to the simulation study , a real data example ( section [ ch : case ] ) illustrated how regular vine copulas can be used to achieve superior portfolio risk forecasts and investment decisions .",

    "furthermore , we present the first bayesian inference method for selecting the regular vine @xmath14 as the building plan of the pair copula construction jointly with the pair copula families @xmath9 .",
    "a major selling point of our approach is that we demonstrated its superiority to existing procedures in a simulation study under controlled conditions ( see section [ ch : simstudy ] ) as well as in an application study using real data ( see section [ ch : case ] ) .",

    "<S> second , our method represents the first bayesian model selection approach to include the regular vine density construction in its scope of inference . </S>",
    "<S> the merits of our approach are established in a simulation study that benchmarks against methods suggested in current literature . </S>",
