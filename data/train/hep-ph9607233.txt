    "<S> we present a complete analysis of the heavy quark effective theory lagrangian at order @xmath0 in the leading logarithmic approximation , including effects induced by spectator quarks . at this order </S>",
    "<S> new correction terms appear in the effective lagrangian , as four - quark operators containing both heavy and light quark fields . </S>",
    "<S> we compute the coefficients of these operators in the leading - logarithmic approximation , and the matching conditions at one - loop order . </S>",
    "<S> two of these operators break the heavy quark spin symmetry , and we estimate their contribution to the hyperfine splitting of the heavy mesons . </S>",
    "<S> we find that they make a positive contribution to the hyperfine splitting of about 10% of the measured splitting in the charm case and of up to 5% in the bottom case . </S>"
