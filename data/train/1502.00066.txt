    "<S> the hamiltonians we consider are examples of frustration - free rokhsar - kivelson models , which are known to be in one - to - one correspondence with classical stochastic systems in the same spatial dimensionality . by exploring this classical - quantum mapping , </S>",
    "<S> we are able to construct a large class of microscopic models which , in a closed manifold , have a non - degenerate gapped symmetric ground state describing the universal properties of spt states . examples of one and two dimensional spt states which illustrate our approach are discussed . </S>"
