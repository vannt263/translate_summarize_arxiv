    "<S> the current availability of integrated biomolecular data sets from twelve cancer types ( `` pancan12 '' ) in the cancer genome atlas ( tcga ) provides an opportunity for thorough data mining , so that such common patterns can be computationally discovered and defined with high accuracy .    </S>",
    "<S> our data mining approach@xcite uses an iterative algorithm to identify patterns that manifest themselves as distinct molecular signatures , called attractor metagenes , several of which were found in nearly identical form following separate analysis of data sets from multiple different cancer types . </S>",
