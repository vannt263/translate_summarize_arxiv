    "<S> a universal representation theorem is derived that shows any graph is the intersection graph of one chordal graph , a number of co - bipartite graphs , and one unit interval graph . </S>",
    "<S> central to the the result is the notion of the clique cover width which is a generalization of the bandwidth parameter . </S>",
    "<S> specifically , we show that any planar graph is the intersection graph of one chordal graph , four co - bipartite graphs , and one unit interval graph . </S>",
    "<S> equivalently , any planar graph is the intersection graph of a chordal graph and a graph that has clique cover width of at most seven . </S>",
    "<S> we further describe the extensions of the results to graphs drawn on surfaces and graphs excluding a minor of crossing number of at most one . </S>"
