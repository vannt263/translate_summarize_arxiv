    "nonclassical states of electromagnetic field are an important theoretical and experimental resource in quantum optics , being they relevant for fundamental aspects of quantum mechanics and also for applications in quantum information and computation @xcite .",
    "`` quantum field state engineering '' has thus become of interest ; cavity quantum electrodynamics ( cqed ) has been successful as a suitable framework to this purpose @xcite .",
    "cqed schemes have been proposed to generate quantum field states using the interaction of consecutive atoms with a high-@xmath0 cavity @xcite .",
    "a two - photon fock state was also generated and probed @xcite .",
    "an important class of nonclassical electromagnetic field states is that of the @xmath1-photon generalized binomial states ( @xmath1gbss ) @xcite .",
    "recently , @xmath1gbss have been object of a new interest because they can be utilized as reference states in schemes to measure the canonical phase of quantum electromagnetic field states @xcite .",
    "it has been recently pointed out @xcite that the @xmath1gbss are the exact electromagnetic correspondent of the well - known coherent atomic states @xcite .",
    "this correspondence suggests that @xmath1gbss could be naturally produced by interactions between two - level atoms and quantum electromagnetic field .",
    "in fact , it has been shown that it is possible to efficiently generate both single @xmath3gbss and their quantum superpositions @xcite .",
    "entangled 2gbss can also be obtained , in two spatially separated cavities , by standard resonant atom - cavity interactions @xcite .    in order to investigate the quantum - classical border",
    "it appears important to consider quantum superpositions of orthogonal states that approach the classical limit . within the electromagnetic field states ,",
    "fock ( number ) states present nonclassical features also at high @xmath1 while coherent states , although suitable for the classical limit , are never exactly orthogonal . on the other hand ,",
    "@xmath1gbss are intermediate between number and coherent states because for large @xmath1 they approach coherent states and have thus a classical limit , while given an arbitrary @xmath1gbs it is always possible to find an orthogonal one @xcite .",
    "moreover , they present nonzero field expectation values .",
    "these characteristics allow to construct quasi - classical superpositions of orthogonal @xmath1gbss .",
    "these superpositions could also find applications in quantum information .",
    "for this , it is crucial to find realistic efficient schemes to generate @xmath1gbss with @xmath2 .",
