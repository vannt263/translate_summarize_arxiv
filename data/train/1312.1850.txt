    "optical tweezers are now well - established since the pioneering work of a. ashkin @xcite .",
    "this non - contact technique is of large interest in many scientific domains such as biochemistry , physics and medicine .",
    "the application of optical near - field tweezers allows to lower the trapping intensities or to trap smaller particles .",
    "cavities of dielectric @xcite or photonic waveguides @xcite have been used to collect and trap micro - particles . exploiting the strong optical field gradient of surface plasmon cavities allowed the realization of stable nanoparticle trapping .",
    "different geometries such as strips @xcite , dipole antennas @xcite , nano - block pairs @xcite or double nano - holes @xcite were successfully applied .",
    "the use of optical fibers attracts increasing attention as highly flexible tools for particle trapping .",
    "fiber - based optical tweezers do not require substrates or bulky high numerical aperture objectives .",
    "they provide easy access to the trapped particle , which is useful for the implementation of further manipulation or characterization elements .",
    "examples of tweezers with two facing optical fibers includes micro - fluidic actuators using two cleaved fibers @xcite , tweezers based on fiber tips grown by photo - polymerization @xcite or using tapered lensed fiber tips @xcite .",
    "single fiber tip tweezers were realized with a micro - lensed cleaved fibers @xcite , a multicore lensed fiber @xcite , single- or multi - mode chemically etched fiber tip @xcite or by gradient index chemically etched fiber tips creating 3d bottle beams @xcite .",
    "the measurement of the optical forces acting on the trapped particles is of paramount interest .",
    "it allows to determine the trapping efficiency .",
    "moreover , due to the very weak optical forces , it can be used for high sensitivity force or displacement sensing @xcite .",
    "different theoretical approaches allow to determine the optical forces of an optical tweezers from the trapped particle position fluctuations . in @xcite , k. berg - srensen and h. flyvberg present an overview of these models .",
    "high frequency quadrant positions sensors are currently used for particle position recording .",
    "it was , however , shown that cmos camera videos with frame rates of some hundred hz are sufficient for the accurate force measurements @xcite .    in the present paper ,",
    "we report on optical trapping of micrometer size dielectric particles using one or two bare optical fiber nano - tips .",
    "these chemically etched fiber tips with nanometer size apex were already used for high resolution optical scanning microscopy @xcite . the trapping efficiency at different light powers and fiber distances",
    "is evaluated by analyzing the experimental data within three different models that find very consistent results .",
    "optical fiber tips are elaborated by chemical etching in aqueous hydrofluoric acid of standard pure silica core single mode optical fibers ( s630-hp , nufern )  @xcite .",
    "the obtained fiber tips are reproducible with smooth surfaces , full angle of about @xmath0 , and apex diameters of 60  nm [ fig .",
