    "<S> various error bounds for the quadrature formula are obtained to quantify more precisely the errors . </S>",
    "<S> applications in numerical integration are given . </S>",
    "<S> with these error bounds , which are generally better than the usual peano bounds , the composite formulas can be applied to integrands with lower order derivatives .    </S>",
