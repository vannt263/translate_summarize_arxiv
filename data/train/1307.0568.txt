    "we presented a robust method to diagnose substructures and dynamical states of galaxy clusters by using the optical photometric data of member galaxy distribution .",
    "the distribution of member galaxies is smoothed by using a gaussian kernel and a weight of their luminosities .",
    "the asymmetry factor , @xmath41 , the ridge flatness , @xmath42 , and the normalized deviation , @xmath43 , are then calculated from the smoothed map , based on which a relaxation parameter , @xmath0 , is defined to quantify dynamical states of clusters .",
    "the smooth - scale and parameter combination are then optimized by using a test sample of 98 clusters with known dynamical states previously classified as relaxed and unrelaxed based on x - ray , optical and radio data .",
    "the newly defined relaxation parameter , @xmath0 , can be used to distinguish the known relaxed and unrelaxed clusters with a success rate of 94% , only a few exception of mergers along the line of sight .",

    "<S> dynamical state of galaxy clusters is closely related to their observational properties in x - ray , optical and radio wavelengths . </S>",
    "<S> we develop a method to diagnose the substructure and dynamical state of galaxy clusters by using photometric data of sloan digital sky survey ( sdss ) . to trace mass distribution , </S>",
    "<S> the brightness distribution of member galaxies is smoothed by using a gaussian kernel with a weight of their optical luminosities . after deriving the asymmetry , the ridge flatness and the normalized deviation of the smoothed optical map </S>",
    "<S> , we define a relaxation parameter , @xmath0 , to quantify dynamical state of clusters . </S>",
    "<S> this method is applied to a test sample of 98 clusters of @xmath1 collected from literature with known dynamical states and can recognize dynamical state for relaxed ( @xmath2 ) and unrelaxed ( @xmath3 ) clusters with a success rate of 94% . </S>",
    "<S> we then calculate relaxation parameters of 2092 rich clusters previously identified from the sdss , of which 28% clusters are dynamically relaxed with @xmath2 . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the dominance and absolute magnitude of the brightest cluster galaxies closely correlate with dynamical state of clusters . </S>",
