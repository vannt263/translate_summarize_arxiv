    "<S> we employ a nuclear magnetic resonance ( nmr ) quantum information processor to simulate the ground state of an xxz spin chain and measure its nmr analog of entanglement , or pseudo - entanglement . </S>",
    "<S> the observed pseudo - entanglement for a small - size system already displays singularity , a signature which is qualitatively similar to that in the thermodynamical limit across quantum phase transitions , including an infinite - order critical point . </S>",
    "<S> the experimental results illustrate a successful approach to investigate quantum correlations in many - body systems using quantum simulators .    </S>",
    "<S> entanglement , delineated as the non - local correlation , is one `` spooky '' characteristic trait of quantum mechanics @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the famous dispute between bohr and einstein on the fundamental question of quantum mechanics , schrdinger - cat paradox , and the transitions from quantum to classical worlds essentially involve entanglement . </S>",
    "<S> recent development of quantum information rekindles the interest in entanglement , more as a possible resource for information processing  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> various methods have been proposed to characterize entanglement qualitatively and quantitatively  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> one immediate application of the entanglement is the investigation of quantum phase transitions ( qpts )  @xcite in many - body systems , which occur at zero temperature ( t=0 k ) , where the transitions are driven by quantum fluctuations and the ground - state wave function is expected to develop drastic change . </S>",
    "<S> the entanglement properties extend and complement the traditional statistical - physical methods for qpts , such as the correlation functions and low - lying excitation spectra . </S>",
    "<S> however , how to describe and measure entanglement in many - body systems is still a challenging task in both theoretical and experimental aspects  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> most schemes for directly measuring entanglement focus on the entanglement between two qubits  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> although the degree of entanglement for a medium - size or lager system can in principle be probed  @xcite , it has not been experimentally measured directly .    </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to classical approaches , quantum simulators  @xcite provide a promising approach for investigating many - body systems and enable one to efficiently simulate other quantum systems by actively controlling and manipulating a certain quantum system , and to test , probe and unveil new physical phenomena . </S>",
    "<S> one interesting aspect is to simulate the ground states of many - body systems , where usually rich phases can exist , such as ferromagnetism , superfluidity , and quantum hall effect , just to name a few . in this letter </S>",
