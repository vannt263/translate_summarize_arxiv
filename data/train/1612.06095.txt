    "<S> how can we interpret the infimum of lipschitz constants in a conjugacy class of interval maps ? </S>",
    "<S> for positive entropy maps , the exponential of the topological entropy gives a well - known lower bound . </S>",
    "<S> we show that for piecewise monotone maps , these two quantities are equal , but for countably piecewise monotone maps , the inequality can be strict . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , in the transitive and markov case , we characterize the infimum of lipschitz constants as the exponential of the salama entropy of a certain reverse markov chain associated with the map . </S>",
    "<S> dynamically , this number represents the exponential growth rate of the number of iterated preimages of nearly any point . </S>"
