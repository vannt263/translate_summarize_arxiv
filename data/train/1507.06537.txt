    "the first - order phase transition is among the most general phenomena in physics .",
    "this phase transition exists in atomic and/or molecular systems , in the system of interacting nucleons ( nuclear matter ) , and , most probably , in the qcd , between hadrons and quark - gluon plasma at large baryonic densities .",

    "the cp demonstrates some universal features typical for the second - order phase transitions , particularly , anomalously large fluctuations .",
