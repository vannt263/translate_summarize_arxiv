    "the complexity may originate from the topologically semimetallic nature of hopdbi .",
    "any dirac semimetal can be viewed as two copies of weyl semimetals with opposite chiralities , and thus two sets of surface arcs .",
    "when magnetic field is weak , these two fermi arcs are bound together through the bulk and oscillations with a single period arise . with magnetic field increasing , link between two surface arcs are broken and separate orbits on each surface appear , generating quantum oscillations with different period .",
    "crossover from one set of orbits to another one may occur as a reentrant behavior of sdh oscillations or interference between two oscillations.@xcite to check whether this concept works for hopdbi , sdh measurements in high magnetic fields are necessary .",
