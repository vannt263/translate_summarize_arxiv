    "<S> algebraic geometry , although little explored in signal processing , provides tools that are very convenient for investigating generic properties in a wide range of applications . </S>",
    "<S> generic properties are properties that hold `` almost everywhere '' . </S>",
    "<S> we present a set of conditions that are sufficient for demonstrating the generic uniqueness of a certain structured matrix factorization . </S>",
    "<S> this set of conditions may be used as a checklist for generic uniqueness in different settings . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss two particular applications in detail . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a relaxed generic uniqueness condition for joint matrix diagonalization that is relevant for independent component analysis in the underdetermined case . </S>",
    "<S> we present generic uniqueness conditions for a recently proposed class of deterministic blind source separation methods that rely on mild source models . for the interested reader we provide some intuition on how the results are connected to their algebraic geometric roots .    </S>",
    "<S> structured matrix factorization , structured rank decomposition , blind source separation , direction of arrival , uniqueness , algebraic geometry </S>"
