    "<S> consider a hermitian matrix model under an external potential with spiked external source . </S>",
    "<S> when the external source is of rank one , we compute the limiting distribution of the largest eigenvalue for general , regular , analytic potential for all values of the external source . </S>",
    "<S> there is a transitional phenomenon , which is universal for convex potentials . </S>",
    "<S> however , for non - convex potentials , new types of transition may occur . </S>",
    "<S> the higher rank external source is analyzed in the subsequent paper . </S>"
