    "<S> the period is a classical complex analytic invariant for a compact riemann surface defined by integration of differential 1-forms . </S>",
    "<S> it has a strong relationship with the complex structure of the surface . in this chapter , </S>",
    "<S> we review another complex analytic invariant called the harmonic volume . </S>",
    "<S> it is a natural extension of the period defined using chen s iterated integrals and captures more detailed information of the complex structure . </S>",
    "<S> it is also one of a few explicitly computable examples of complex analytic invariants . as an application </S>",
    "<S> , we give an algorithm in proving nontriviality for a class of homologically trivial algebraic cycles obtained from special compact riemann surfaces . </S>",
    "<S> the moduli space of compact riemann surfaces is the space of all biholomorphism classes of compact riemann surfaces . </S>",
    "<S> the harmonic volume can be regarded as an analytic section of a local system on the moduli space . </S>",
    "<S> it enables a quantitative study of the local structure of the moduli space . </S>",
    "<S> we explain basic concepts related to the harmonic volume and its applications of the moduli space .    14h40 , 14h50 , 30f30 , 32g15 .    </S>",
