    "<S> self - similar stable mixed moving average processes can be related to nonsingular flows through their minimal representations . </S>",
    "<S> self - similar stable mixed moving averages related to dissipative flows have been studied , as well as processes associated with identity flows which are the simplest type of conservative flows . </S>",
    "<S> the focus here is on self - similar stable mixed moving averages related to periodic and cyclic flows . </S>",
    "<S> periodic flows are conservative flows such that each point in the space comes back to its initial position in finite time , either positive or null . </S>",
    "<S> the flow is cyclic if the return time is positive .    </S>",
    "<S> self - similar mixed moving averages are called periodic , resp .  </S>",
    "<S> cyclic , fractional stable motions if their minimal representations are generated by periodic , resp .  </S>",
    "<S> cyclic , flows . </S>",
    "<S> these processes , however , are often defined by a nonminimal representation . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a way to detect whether they are periodic or cyclic even if their representation is nonminimal . by using these identification results </S>",
    "<S> , we obtain a more refined decomposition of self - similar mixed moving averages . </S>"
