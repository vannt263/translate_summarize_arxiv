    "the set of unifiers of a solvable instance supports a natural order , and the instances are classified depending on the properties of their maximal unifiers . in this paper",
    ", we provide a complete ( first - order , decidable ) classification of solvable instances of the unification problem over de morgan and kleene algebras with respect to their unification type .",
