    "hermitian matrix integrals have been used effectively in the study of topology of moduli spaces of riemann surfaces with marked points .",
    "these works rely on a relation between graphs and riemann surfaces ( cf .",
    "there is a burst of developments in this direction lately @xcite .",
    "real symmetric matrix integrals are used for the study of moduli spaces of real algebraic curves @xcite . in a context of finite groups , a striking relation between random matrices and representation theory of symmetric groups is discovered in @xcite .",
    "we have seen in this article that an extension of these matrix integrals shows yet another interesting relation between surface geometry and finite group theory .",
    "there is a completely different proof of the counting formula theorem  [ thm : orientable ] due to freed and quinn @xcite .",
    "they use chern - simons gauge theory with a finite gauge group . to establish the formula ,",
    "they construct a chern - simons gauge theory on each riemann surface @xmath0 .",
    "interestingly , our approach does not start with a specific manifold , which is usually the space - time in physics . in a sense",
    "our non - commutative integral is a quantum field theory on a finite group _ without _ space - time , and surfaces appear in its feynman diagram expansion . yet the result shows that chern - simons gauge theory on a surface is mathematically equivalent to our non - commutative integrals over group algebras . to be more precise , the group algebra model is a generating function of chern - simons gauge theory with finite gauge group for _ all _ closed surfaces .",
