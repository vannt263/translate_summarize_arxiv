    "<S> we consider the modified craig  sneyd ( mcs ) scheme which forms a prominent time stepping method of the alternating direction implicit type for multidimensional time - dependent convection - diffusion equations with mixed spatial derivative terms . </S>",
    "<S> such equations arise often , notably , in the field of financial mathematics . in this paper a first convergence theorem for the mcs scheme is proved where the obtained bound on the global temporal discretization errors has the essential property that it is independent of the ( arbitrarily small ) spatial mesh width from the semidiscretization . </S>",
    "<S> the obtained theorem is directly pertinent to two - dimensional convection - diffusion equations with mixed derivative term . </S>",
    "<S> numerical experiments are provided that illustrate our result .    </S>",
    "<S> * key words : * initial - boundary value problems , convection - diffusion equations , adi splitting schemes , convergence analysis . </S>"
