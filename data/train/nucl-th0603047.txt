    "+ one of the most fundamental parameters in the standard model ( sm ) , the weak mixing angle has been at the center of research activities , involving both theoretical sm calculations @xcite and experimental efforts to determine its value .",
    "while all @xmath0 measurements near the @xmath7 pole @xcite and for low @xmath8 values @xcite are in good agreement with the sm prediction , an experiment by the nutev collaboration at @xmath9 does not seem to corroborate the calculated running of the weinberg angle @xcite .",

    ", little effort has been put in the intermediate - energy regime , where an adequate description in terms of hadronic rather than partonic degrees of freedom is needed .",

    "<S> , the influence of nuclear effects on the pw relation is investigated . </S>",
