    "the systematic investigation of four - loop tadpole integrals started about a decade ago . in this article",
    "we have briefly touched the techniques which have been developed to perform the reduction to master integrals and to obtain results for the latter .",
    "furthermore , we have described in some detail the most important applications . among them",
    "are four - loop corrections to the electroweak @xmath0 parameter , the precise determination of charm and bottom quark masses and the decoupling constants in qcd .",
    "the latter have a close connection to higgs boson production and decay into gluons and photons which has also been elaborated .",
