    "<S> we discuss the single diffractive production of @xmath0 pairs and charmed mesons at the lhc . </S>",
    "<S> in addition to standard collinear approach , for a first time we propose a @xmath1-factorization approach to the diffractive processes . </S>",
    "<S> the transverse momentum dependent ( the unintegrated diffractive parton distributions ) in proton are obtained with the help of the kimber - martin - ryskin prescription where collinear diffractive pdfs are used as input . in this calculation the transverse momentum of the pomeron is neglected with respect to transverse momentum of partons entering the hard process . </S>",
    "<S> the results of the new approach are compared with those of the standard collinear one . </S>",
    "<S> significantly larger cross sections are obtained in the @xmath1-factorization approach where some part of higher - order effects is effectively included . </S>",
    "<S> some correlation observables , like azimuthal angle correlation between @xmath2 and @xmath3 , and @xmath0 pair transverse momentum distribution were obtained for the first time . </S>"
