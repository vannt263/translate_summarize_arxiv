    "<S> to the coverage problem of sensor networks , vin de silva and robert ghrist developed several approaches @xcite based on ( persistent ) homology theory . </S>",
    "<S> their criteria for the coverage are formulated on the rips complexes constructed by the sensors , in which their locations are supposed to be fixed . </S>",
    "<S> however , the sensors are in general affected by perturbations ( e.g. , natural phenomena ) , and hence the stability of the coverage criteria should be also discussed . in this paper , we present a coverage theorem stable under perturbation . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we also introduce a method of eliminating redundant cover after perturbation . </S>",
    "<S> the coverage theorem is derived by extending the rips interleaving theorem studied in the paper @xcite into an appropriate relative version . </S>"
