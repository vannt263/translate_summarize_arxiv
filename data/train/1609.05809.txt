    "<S> correlation functions in quantum field theory are calculated using feynman amplitudes , which are finite dimensional integrals associated to graphs . </S>",
    "<S> the integrand is the exponential of the ratio of the first and second symanzik polynomials associated to the feynman graph , which are described in terms of the spanning trees and spanning 2-forests of the graph , respectively .    in a previous paper with bloch , burgos and fresn , we related this ratio to the asymptotic of the archimedean height pairing between degree zero divisors on degenerating families of riemann surfaces . motivated by this </S>",
    "<S> , we consider in this paper the variation of the ratio of the two symanzik polynomials under bounded perturbations of the geometry of the graph . </S>",
    "<S> this is a natural problem in connection with the theory of nilpotent and sl2 orbits in hodge theory .    </S>",
    "<S> our main result is the boundedness of variation of the ratio . for this </S>",
    "<S> we define the exchange graph of a given graph which encodes the exchange properties between spanning trees and spanning 2-forests in the graph . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a description of the connected components of this graph , and use this to prove our result on boundedness of the variations </S>",
