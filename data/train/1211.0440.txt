    "<S> we study dynamics of two coupled periodically driven oscillators . </S>",
    "<S> the internal motion is separated off exactly to yield a nonlinear fourth - order equation describing inner dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> periodic steady - state solutions of the fourth - order equation are determined within the krylov - bogoliubov - mitropolsky approach  we compute the amplitude profiles , which from mathematical point of view are algebraic curves .    in the present paper </S>",
    "<S> we investigate metamorphoses of amplitude profiles induced by changes of control parameters near singular points of these curves . </S>",
    "<S> it follows that dynamics changes qualitatively in the neighbourhood of a singular point . </S>"
