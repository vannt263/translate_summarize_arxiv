    "<S> entanglement lies at the heart of quantum mechanics and plays a crucial role in quantum information processing . </S>",
    "<S> recently , special types of multipartite entangled states , the so - called the graph states @xcite , have moved into the center of interest . </S>",
    "<S> a graph quantum state is described by a mathematical graph , i.e. a set of vertices connected by edges . </S>",
    "<S> a vertex represents a physical system , e. g. a qubit ( 2-dimensional hilbert space ) , qudit ( d - dimensional hilbert space ) , or cv ( continuous hilbert space ) . </S>",
    "<S> an edge between two vertices represents the physical interaction between the corresponding systems . </S>",
    "<S> an interesting feature is that many entanglement properties of graph states are closely related to their underlying graphs . </S>",
    "<S> they not only provide an efficient model to study multiparticle entanglement @xcite , but also find applications in quantum error correction @xcite , multi - party quantum communication @xcite and most prominently , serve as the initial resource in one - way quantum computation @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> considerable efforts have been stepped toward generating and characterizing cluster state with linear optics experimentally @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the principle feasibility of one - way quantum computing model has been experimentally demonstrated through photon cluster state successfully @xcite .    </S>",

    "<S> the transformations of qubit graph states under local clifford operations were studied by hein @xcite and van den nest @xcite . </S>",

    "<S> the corresponding local clifford unitary is a single and simple form . </S>",
    "<S> the successive application of this rule suffices to generate the complete orbit of any qubit graph state under local clifford operations . in this paper , </S>",
