    "<S> we study a model for a pair of qubits which interact with a single off - resonant cavity mode and , in addition , exhibit a direct inter - qubit coupling . </S>",
    "<S> possible realizations for such a system include coupled superconducting qubits in a line resonator as well as exciton states or electron spin states of quantum dots in a cavity . </S>",
    "<S> the emergent dynamical phenomena are strongly dependent on the relative energy scales of the inter - qubit coupling strength , the coupling strength between qubits and cavity mode , and the cavity mode detuning . we show that the cavity mode dispersion enables a measurement of the state of the coupled - qubit system in the perturbative regime . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the effect of the direct inter - qubit interaction on a cavity - mediated two - qubit gate . </S>",
    "<S> further , we show that for asymmetric coupling of the two qubits to the cavity , the direct inter - qubit coupling can be controlled optically via the ac stark effect . </S>"
