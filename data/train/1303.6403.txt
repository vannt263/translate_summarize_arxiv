    "<S> a separable state is a statistical mixture of product states  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> a quantum state is entangled , whenever it can not be represented in this form . </S>",
    "<S> various forms of multipartite entanglement are known  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the most prominent and nonequivalent forms of entangled multipartite quantum states are the ghz - state  @xcite and the w - state  @xcite , which have been generalized to so - called cluster and graph states  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> another classification is given in terms of partial and full ( or genuine ) multipartite entanglement , for an introduction see e.g.  @xcite . </S>",

    "<S> multipartite entanglement has been shown to be essential to reach the maximal sensitivity in metrological tasks  @xcite . in this context , the quantum fisher information has been used to characterize the entanglement  @xcite .    the detection of entanglement is typically done via the construction of proper entanglement witnesses  @xcite , being equivalent to the method of positive , but not completely positive maps . </S>",
    "<S> a witness is an observable , which is non - negative for separable states , and it can have a negative expectation value for entangled states . for different kinds of entanglement , different types of witnesses have been considered : bipartite witnesses  @xcite ; schmidt number witnesses  @xcite ; and multipartite witnesses for partial and genuine entanglement  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> a systematic approach for witnessing entanglement in complex quantum systems is missing yet .    </S>",
    "<S> recently , we considered the construction of bipartite entanglement witnesses with the so - called separability eigenvalue equations  @xcite . </S>",
