    "<S> if one has to attain high accuracy over long timescales during the numerical computation of the @xmath0-body problem , the method called lie - integration is one of the most effective algorithms . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we present a set of recurrence relations with which the coefficients needed by the lie - integration of the orbital elements related to the spatial @xmath0-body problem can be derived up to arbitrary order . similarly to the planar case , these formulae yields identically zero series in the case of no perturbations . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , the derivation of the formulae has two stages , analogously to the planar problem . </S>",
    "<S> namely , the formulae are obtained to the first order , and then , higher order relations are expanded by involving directly the multilinear and fractional properties of the lie - operator . </S>"
