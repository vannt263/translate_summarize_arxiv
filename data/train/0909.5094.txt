    "<S> ensembles of random density matrices determined by various probability measures are analysed . </S>",
    "<S> a simple and efficient algorithm to generate at random density matrices distributed according to the bures measure is proposed . </S>",
    "<S> this procedure may serve as an initial step in performing bayesian approach to quantum state estimation based on the bures prior . </S>",
    "<S> we study the distribution of purity of random mixed states . </S>",
    "<S> the moments of the distribution of purity are determined for quantum states generated with respect to the bures measure . </S>",
    "<S> this calculation serves as an exemplary application of the `` deform - and - study '' approach based on ideas of integrability theory . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that painlev equation appeared as a part of the presented theory .    . </S>"
