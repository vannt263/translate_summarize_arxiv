    "<S> the anisotropic interaction has a strong influence both on the maximal value of long - range entanglement and the time of reaching the maximum long - range entanglement . </S>",
    "<S> the local coupling has a notable impact on the long - range entanglement , but it can be neglected in the time of reaching the maximal long - range entanglement </S>",
