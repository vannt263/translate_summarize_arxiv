    "<S> the effect of quantum fluctuations on a nearly flat , nonrelativistic two - dimensional membrane with extrinsic curvature stiffness and tension is investigated . </S>",
    "<S> the renormalization group analysis is carried out in first - order perturbative theory . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to thermal fluctuations , which soften the membrane at large scales and turn it into a crumpled surface , quantum fluctuations are found to _ stiffen _ the membrane , so that it exhibits a hausdorff dimension equal to two . </S>",
    "<S> the large - scale behavior of the membrane is further studied at finite temperature , where a nontrivial fixed point is found , signaling a crumpling transition . </S>"
