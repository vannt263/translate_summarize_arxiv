    "<S> we provide analytical and numerical evidence of a spin - triplet fflo superconductivity in the itinerant kitaev - heisenberg model ( anti - ferromagnetic kitaev coupling and ferromagnetic heisenberg coupling ) on the honeycomb lattice around quarter filling . </S>",
    "<S> the strong spin - orbit coupling in our model leads to the emergence of 6 inversion symmetry centers for the fermi surface at non zero momenta in the first brillouin zone . </S>",
    "<S> we show how the cooper pairs condense into these non - trivial momenta , causing the spatial modulation of the superconducting order parameter . applying a ginzburg - landau expansion analysis </S>",
    "<S> , we find that the superconductivity has three separated degenerate ground states with three different spin - triplet pairings . </S>",
    "<S> this picture is also supported by exact diagonalizations on finite clusters .    </S>",
    "<S> 10000    _ introduction_- mott insulator and high-@xmath0 superconductor are closely related since the latter can be obtained from doping the half - filled mott insulator @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> one key element in superconductivity is the emergence of off - diagonal long - range order which results in the bardeen - cooper - schrieffer ground state where cooper pairs have a zero net momentum . </S>",
    "<S> the @xmath1 pairing , proposed by c. n. yang @xcite , binds electrons with momenta @xmath2 and @xmath3 , and therefore involves a superconductivity with non - zero cooper pair momentum . </S>",
    "<S> this superconductivity is referred to as the fulde - ferrell - larkin - ovchinnikov ( fflo ) superconductivity @xcite . the fflo superconductivity , which supports a spatial modulation for the electron pairing due to the non - trivial cooper pair momentum , was first proposed in the 60s in a system with significant zeeman interaction , which shifts the fermi surfaces for the up and down spins . experimental realizations of fflo superconductivity </S>",
    "<S> have been proposed , for example , in heavy - fermions @xcite , ultra - cold atom systems @xcite , bec analogues @xcite and in magnetic analogue materials @xcite . however </S>",
    "<S> , this exotic phase of matter has not yet been conclusively observed , especially in real materials .    </S>",
    "<S> lately , the studies of `` iridates '' , a family of materials with significant spin - orbit coupling , have aroused great interests @xcite partly because of the emergence of topological mott physics @xcite and its connection to the kitaev anyon model @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> it has been shown both theoretically and experimentally that the existence of zigzag - magnetic order results from a kitaev - heisenberg magnetic coupling in the two - dimensional sodium iridate family @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> an additional symmetric - off diagonal exchange term can also be added in the analysis @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> doping these spin - orbit mott insulators has been addressed theoretically @xcite and has started to attract some experimental attention @xcite . here , we address superconductivity in the presence of a large hubbard interaction and adopt a localized magnetism point of view where the kitaev - heisenberg spin hamiltonian originates from super - exchange processes @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> such kitaev - heisenberg physics can also be realized in cold atom systems @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> using both analytical and numerical methods , we provide convincing evidences of a spin - triplet fflo superconductor thanks to the spin - orbit coupling close to quarter - filling without breaking the time - reversal symmetry . </S>",
    "<S> superconductivity around quarter - filling has also attracted some attention in the context of graphene @xcite .     </S>",
