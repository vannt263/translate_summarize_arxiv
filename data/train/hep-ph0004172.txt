    "non  perturbative quantities such as the structure and fragmentation functions contribute the above question can not be answered by means of perturbation theory for the process as a whole . however , since both the parton densities involved in the space and time  like process factorize if the virtuality @xmath235 of the four  momentum transfer is large a related question can be asked for the crossing behavior of the respective evolution kernels , which are computable within perturbation theory . in the calculation of both inclusive processes only two types of singularities occur , the collinear singularity and the ultraviolet singularity .",
    "these divergences are absorbed into the bare parton densities and the coupling constant , respectively .",
    "two distinct renormalization group equations are implied .",
    "they quantify the impact of the factorization and the renormalization scale on the dis structure functions and fragmentation functions when the perturbation series is truncated up to a given order .",
    "however one can construct factorization ",
    "scale independent evolution kernels which describe the scheme  invariant evolution of these physical quantities in terms of a kinematic variable given by @xmath37 .",
    "this scheme invariant evolution is guaranteed up to any finite order in perturbation theory .",
    "notice that in finite order this method does not remove the dependence of the physical quantities on the renormalization scheme of the strong coupling constant or its scale @xmath47 .",
