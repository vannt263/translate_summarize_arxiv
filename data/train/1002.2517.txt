    "<S> the airy transform is an ideally suited tool to treat problem in classical and quantum optics . </S>",
    "<S> even though the relevant mathematical aspects have been thoroughly investigated , the possibility it offers are wide and some aspects , as the link with special functions and polynomials , still contains unexplored aspects . in this note </S>",
    "<S> we will show that the so called airy polynomials are essentially the third order hermite polynomials . </S>",
    "<S> we will also prove that this identification opens the possibility of developing new conjectures on the properties of this family of polynomials . </S>"
