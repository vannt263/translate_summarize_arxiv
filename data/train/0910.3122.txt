    "<S> gravitational properties of a hedge - hog type topological defect in two extra dimensions are considered in general relativity employing a vector as the order parameter . </S>",
    "<S> all previous considerations were done using the order parameter in the form of a multiplet in a target space of scalar fields . </S>",
    "<S> the difference of these two approaches is analyzed and demonstrated in detail . </S>",
    "<S> regular solutions of the einstein equations are studied analytically and numerically . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the existence of a negative cosmological constant is sufficient for the spontaneous symmetry breaking of the initially plain bulk . </S>",
    "<S> regular configurations have a growing gravitational potential and are able to trap the matter on the brane . </S>",
    "<S> if the energy of spontaneous symmetry breaking is high , the gravitational potential has several points of minimum . </S>",
    "<S> identical in the uniform bulk spin - less particles , being trapped within separate minima , acquire different masses and appear to the observer on brane as different particles with integer spins . </S>"
