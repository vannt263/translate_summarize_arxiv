    "quantum phase transitions happen at zero absolute temperature when there are competing interactions trying to order the sample in different ways @xcite .",

    "then , a small tilt of the field moves the system into one of the phases changing its properties significantly ( e.g. magnetization , correlation functions , etc . ) .",
