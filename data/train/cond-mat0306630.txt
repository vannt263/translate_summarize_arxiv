    "<S> we study theoretically the ultrafast nonlinear optical response of quantum well excitons in a perpendicular magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> we address the role of many - body correlations originating from the electron scattering between landau levels ( ll ) . in the linear optical response , the processes involving inter - ll transitions are suppressed provided that the magnetic field is sufficiently strong . </S>",
    "<S> however , in the _ nonlinear _ response , the auger processes involving inter - ll scattering of _ two _ photoexcited electrons remain unsuppressed . </S>",
    "<S> we show that auger scattering plays the dominant role in the coherent exciton dynamics in strong magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> we perform numerical calculations for the third - order four - wave - mixing ( fwm ) polarization which incorporate the auger processes nonperturbatively . we find that inter - ll scattering leads to a strong enhancement and to oscillations of the fwm signal at negative time delays . </S>",
    "<S> these oscillations represent quantum beats between optically - inactive two - exciton states related to each other via auger processes . </S>"
