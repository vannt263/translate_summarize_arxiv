    "<S> we employ a high - order perturbative expansion to characterize the ground state of the mott phase of the one - dimensional bose - hubbard model . </S>",
    "<S> we compute for different integer filling factors the energy per lattice site , the two - point and density - density correlations , and expectation values of powers of the on - site number operator determining the local atom number fluctuations ( variance , skewness , kurtosis ) . </S>",
    "<S> we compare these expansions to numerical simulations of the infinite - size system to determine their range of applicability . </S>",
    "<S> we also discuss a new sum rule for the density - density correlations that can be used in both equilibrium and non - equilibrium systems . </S>"
