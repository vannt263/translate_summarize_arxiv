    "<S> 46 years ago the quark model replaced the sakata model as the standard explanation of the hadron structure . </S>",
    "<S> the major alleged defect of the sakata model was its prediction of just too many types of particles , which have not been seen in experiments . </S>",
    "<S> however , this allegation was made without detailed consideration of the forces acting between sakatons . in this article </S>",
    "<S> we suggest a set of pairwise sakaton - sakaton and sakaton - antisakaton potentials that describe stability and masses of strongly interacting elementary particles in a good agreement with observations . </S>"
