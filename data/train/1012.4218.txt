    "weinstein manifolds are symplectic counterparts of affine ( stein ) complex manifolds .",
    "they are exact symplectic manifolds which admit symplectic handle decompositions with isotropic core disks . in particular the index of any handle",
    "is at most half of the dimension of the manifold .",
    "we refer the reader to @xcite for precise definitions .",
    "middle dimensional handles are called _ critical _ and handles below middle dimension _",
    "subcritical_. given a weinstein manifold @xmath1 , its symplectic homology @xmath2 is a symplectic invariant which was first defined by a.  floer and h.  hofer in @xcite .",
    "it vanishes if @xmath1 is subcritical , see @xcite .",
    "a general weinstein manifold @xmath1 is obtained by attaching critical handles to a subcritical manifold @xmath3 along a collection @xmath4 of legendrian spheres in the contact manifold @xmath5 which is the ideal boundary of @xmath3 . in @xcite , @xmath2 was expressed in terms of the legendrian homology algebra of @xmath6 .",
    "symplectic homology comes with tqft - like operations which on the homology level are all expressed in terms of a ( pair - of - pants ) product and a bv - operator , see @xcite . in this paper",
    "we express the product and the bv - operator in terms of the legendrian homology algebra of @xmath4 and some additional operations on it .",
    "the bv - operator is determined by the algebra itself , whereas the expression of the product involves a duality operation which can be identified with the first term in the extension of legendrian homology algebra to legendrian rational symplectic field theory ( sft ) .",
