    "<S> this paper deals with the problem of location and existence of limit cycles for real planar polynomial differential systems . </S>",
    "<S> we provide a method to construct poincar  bendixson regions by using transversal conics . </S>",
    "<S> we present several examples of known systems in the literature showing different features about limit cycles : hyperbolicity , hopf bifurcation , sky - blue bifurcation , rotated vector fields ,  for which the obtained poincar  bendixson region allows to locate the limit cycles . </S>",
    "<S> our method gives bounds for the bifurcation values of parametrical families of planar vector fields and intervals of existence of limit cycles . </S>"
