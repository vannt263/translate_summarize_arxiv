    "this article is a companion to the contribution of dirk kreimer @xcite to this volume . with his discovery",
    "that divergent feynman graphs can be regarded as elements of a hopf algebra whose antipode implements renormalization @xcite , kreimer has initiated a dynamic development on the frontier of quantum field theory and noncommutative geometry . in particular",
    "since alain connes and dirk kreimer @xcite established a structural link of this renormalization hopf algebra to a hopf algebra found in the study of an index problem in noncommutative geometry @xcite , this topic belongs to the most promising ones towards a unification of quantum field theory with gravity .",
    "connes and kreimer realized that a subalgebra of the hopf algebra of renormalization ( of a qft given by a single divergent feynman graph ) is isomorphic to the dual of the diffeomorphism group of a manifold .",
    "the central quest is now the search for the counterpart of the entire renormalization hopf algebra ( of a realistic qft ) replacing the diffeomorphism group .",
    "there is no doubt that the latter object will deliver precious information on the short distance structure of spacetime .",
    "renormalization is our most powerful microscope to see the smallest structures of the world !    compared with these dreams ,",
    "this article is extremely modest .",
    "we review how the hopf algebra is derived from feynman graphs .",
