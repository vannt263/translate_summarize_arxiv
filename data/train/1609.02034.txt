    "lambert w function has been widely applied in the research of science and engineering problems . in this paper",
    ", we construct the time response of scalar systems with multiple discrete delays based on the laplace transform .",
    "we first establish the state transition function as the sum of infinite series of exponentials acting on eigenvalues inside countable branches of the lambert w functions . and the time response function",
    "is then obtained in terms of initial condition , preshape function , and the convolution between state transition function and input function .",
    "eigenvalues in each branch of lambert w function are computed by a numerical iteration .",
    "once the eigenvalues are obtained , the approximation of time responses is calculated .",
    "numerical examples are presented to conform the successful application of lambert w function in establishing the time response of multi - delay systems .",
    "our main contribution is to construct an analytical formula in representing the time response which can act as the concise foundation of designing the feedback controller for multi - delay systems ."
