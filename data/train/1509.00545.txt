    "<S> this paper is devoted to a study of the null controllability problems for one - dimensional linear degenerate wave equations through a boundary controller . </S>",
    "<S> first , the well - posedness of linear degenerate wave equations is discussed </S>",
    "<S> . then the null controllability of some degenerate wave equations is established , when a control acts on the non - degenerate boundary . </S>",
