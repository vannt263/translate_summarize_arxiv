    "<S> a particle produced in a hard collision can lose energy through bremsstrahlung . </S>",
    "<S> it has long been of interest to calculate the effect on bremsstrahlung if the particle is produced inside a finite - size qcd medium such as a quark - gluon plasma . for the case of very high - energy particles traveling through the background of a weakly - coupled quark - gluon plasma , </S>",
    "<S> it is known how to reduce this problem to an equivalent problem in non - relativistic two - dimensional quantum mechanics . </S>",
    "<S> analytic solutions , however , have always resorted to further approximations . </S>",
    "<S> one is a harmonic oscillator approximation to the corresponding quantum mechanics problem , which is appropriate for sufficiently thick media . </S>",
