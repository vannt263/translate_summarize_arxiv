    "<S> break spontaneously six - dimensional poincar invariance leading to a finite four - dimensional planck mass and to a regular geometry both in the bulk and on the core of the vortex . while the tensor modes of the metric are decoupled and exhibit a normalizable zero mode , the vector fluctuations , present in the gauge sector of the theory , </S>",

    "<S> the fluctuations of the abelian gauge field itself admit a normalizable zero mode .    </S>"
