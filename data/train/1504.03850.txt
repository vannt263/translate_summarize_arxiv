    "<S> this paper is devoted to settle two still open problems , connected with the existence of ample and nef divisors on a @xmath0factorial complete toric variety . </S>",
    "<S> the first problem is about the existence of ample divisors when the picard number is 2 : we give a positive answer to this question , by studying the secondary fan by means of @xmath1linear gale duality . </S>",
    "<S> the second problem is about the minimum value of the picard number allowing the vanishing of the @xmath2 cone : we present a 3dimensional example showing that this value can not be greater then 3 , which , under the previous result , is also the minimum value guaranteeing the existence of non  projective examples . </S>"
