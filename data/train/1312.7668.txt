    "<S> high precision asteroseismic data provide a unique opportunity to test input microphysics such as stellar opacities , chemical composition or equation of state . </S>",
    "<S> these tests are possible because pulsational frequencies as well as amplitudes and phases of the light variations are very sensitive to the internal structure of a star </S>",
    "<S> . we can therefore compute pulsation models and compare them with observations . </S>",
    "<S> the agreement or differences should tell us whether some models are adequate or not , and which input data need to be improved . </S>"
