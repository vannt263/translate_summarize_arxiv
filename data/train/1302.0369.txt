    "in the present paper we study dynamics of a small ball moving vertically in a gravitational field and impacting with a periodically moving limiter ( a table ) .",
    "this model belongs to the field of nonsmooth and nonlinear dynamical systems @xcite . in such systems",
    "nonstandard bifurcations such as border - collisions and grazing impacts leading often to complex chaotic motions are typically present .",
    "it is important that nonsmooth systems have many applications in technology @xcite .",
    "impacting systems studied in the literature can be divided into three main classes : bouncing ball models @xcite , impacting oscillators @xcite and impacting pendulums lenci2000,awrejcewicz2007 , see also @xcite . in dynamics with impacts",
    "it is usually difficult or even impossible to solve nonlinear equation for an instant of the next impact .",
    "for example , in the bouncing ball models the table s motion has been usually assumed in sinusoidal form , cf .",
    "@xcite and references therein .",
    "this choice of the limiter s motion leads indeed to nontractable nonlinear equation for time of the next impact . to tackle this problem we proposed a sequence of models in which periodic motion of the table is assumed ( in one period of limiter s motion ) as a low - order polynomial of time @xcite .",
    "it is thus possible to approximate the sinusoidal motion of the table more and more exactly and conduct analytical computations . carrying out this plan we have studied several such models with linear , quadratic and cubic polynomials aobr2009,aobr2011,aobr2012a , aobr2012b .    in the present work we conduct analytical and numerical investigations of the model in which sinusoidal displacement of the table is approximated in one period by four cubic polynomials .",
