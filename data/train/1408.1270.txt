    "we have studied the quantum effect on the inflationary parameters , @xmath0 and @xmath1 , in a simple @xmath7 theory with non - minimal scalar - curvature coupling .",
    "the effective lagrangian is obtained up to the leading log order by the generalized coleman - weinberg approach .",

    ", we systematically evaluate the typical behavior for @xmath0 and @xmath1 as a function of the bare couplings , the renormalization scale or the e - folding number .",
    "it is found that the quantum corrections have a non - universal contribution for @xmath0 and @xmath1 .",
    "the inflationary parameters , @xmath0 and @xmath1 , do not reach the attractor ( [ lim : strong ] ) until @xmath53 . however , the @xmath43- and @xmath4-dependences for @xmath0 and @xmath1 suppressed as the scalar - curvature coupling , @xmath3 , increases . thus the inflationary parameters , @xmath0 and @xmath1 ,",

    "our model is a simple toy model but we discuss the consistency with the constraints from the cosmological observations .",
    "it is known that the planck constraint is consistent with the @xmath7 theory with a finite scalar - curvature coupling @xcite .",
