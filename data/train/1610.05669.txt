    "quantum cromodynamics  ( qcd ) , is believed as the correct theory of the strong interactions which is one of the four fundamental interactions of nature .",
    "one of the most important questions in qcd not yet solved , is the non - perturbative regime . however , with the relativistic constituent quark model  ( cqm ) , is possible to obtain answers for hadronic physics in terms of the degrees freedom from the qcd , i.e. , quarks and gluons  @xcite . before the advent of qcd , the pion , the lowest mass hadronic bound state , providing the long - range attraction part of the nucleon - nucleon potential  @xcite with the intermediate energies .",
    "the main proposal of the light - front approach is to describe consistently the hadronic pion bound state to both , higher and lower momentum transfer regime .    with this purpose ,",
    "the light - front quantization is utilized to compute the hadronic bound state wave functions  @xcite , which are simpler compared to the instant form quantum field theory  @xcite . as known , in the light - front models , the bound states wave functions are defined in the hypersurface @xmath0 , and the wave functions are covariant under kinematical front - form boosts , because the fock - state decomposition stability  @xcite .",
    "the bound state wave functions with the light - front constituent quark model ( lfcqm ) have received much attention lately  @xcite .",
    "the quark models show an impressive success in the description of the electromagnetic properties of the hadronic wave functions , for pseudoscalar and spin half particles  @xcite and also vector particles  @xcite .",
    "the extraction of the electromagnetic form factor with the light - front approach depends on which component of the electromagnetic current is utilized to calculate the form - factors , due to the problems related with the rotational symmetry breaking and the zero modes , namely a non - valence contribuition to the matrix elements of the electromagnetic current  @xcite .",
    "it is found in references  @xcite for spin-1 particles , that the plus component of the electromagnetic current ,  ( `` @xmath1 '' ) , is not free from the pair terms contribution  ( or non - valence contributions ) in the breit frame ( @xmath2 ) , and thus the rotational symmetry is broken .",
    "then , the matrix elements of the electromagnetic current in the light - front formalism have other contributions for the electromagnetic current besides the valence contribution ; that contribution corresponds to the non - valence components or pair terms added to the matrix elements of the electromagnetic current  @xcite in order to restore the covariance .",
