    "<S> . however , all sensors must tolerate imperfections if they are to be practical . </S>",

    "<S> we quantify the effect of distinguishability on quantum metrology experiments , and report results of an experiment to verify that two- and four - photon states containing partially - distinguishable photons can achieve quantum - enhanced sensitivity with low - visibility quantum interference . </S>",
