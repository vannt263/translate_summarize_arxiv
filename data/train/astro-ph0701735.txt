    "<S> the aim of this study is to establish the relationship , if any , between the sites of optical and x - ray variability , and thereby to elucidate the origins of x - ray production in pms stars . in a previous paper we showed that optical and x - ray variability in pms stars are very rarely time - correlated . here , </S>",
