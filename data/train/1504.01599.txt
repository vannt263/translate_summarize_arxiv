    "<S> compass is a high - energy physics experiment operating at the sps at cern . </S>",
    "<S> wide physics program of the experiment comprises study of hadron structure and spectroscopy with high energy muon and hadrons beams . as for the muon - program , </S>",
    "<S> one of the important objectives of the compass experiment is the exploration of the transverse spin structure of the nucleon via spin ( in)dependent azimuthal asymmetries in single - hadron production in deep inelastic scattering of polarized leptons off transversely polarized target . for this purpose a series of measurements </S>",
    "<S> were made in compass , using 160 gev / c longitudinally polarized muon beam and transversely polarized @xmath0 ( in 2002 , 2003 and 2004 ) and @xmath1 ( in 2007 and 2010 ) targets . </S>",
    "<S> the experimental results obtained by compass for unpolarized target azimuthal asymmetries , sivers and collins effects and other azimuthal observables play an important role in the general understanding of the three - dimensional nature of the nucleon . giving access to the entire `` twsit-2 '' set of transverse momentum dependent parton distribution functions and fragmentation functions compass data </S>",
    "<S> triggers constant theoretical interest and is being widely used in phenomenological analyses and global data fits . in this review main focus </S>",
    "<S> is given to the very recent results obtained by the compass collaboration from first ever multi - dimensional extraction of transverse spin asymmetries . </S>"
