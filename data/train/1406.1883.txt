    "<S> the pentagram map was introduced by r. schwartz more than 20 years ago . in 2009 , v. ovsienko , r. schwartz and s. tabachnikov established liouville complete integrability of this discrete dynamical system . in 2011 , m. glick interpreted the pentagram map as a sequence of cluster transformations associated with a special quiver . using compatible poisson structures in cluster algebras and poisson geometry of directed networks on surfaces , we generalize glick s construction to include the pentagram map into a family of discrete integrable maps and we give these maps geometric interpretations . </S>"
