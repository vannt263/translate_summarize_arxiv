    "<S> we construct a large class of completely positive and trace preserving non - markovian dynamical maps for an open quantum system . </S>",
    "<S> these maps arise from a piecewise dynamics characterized by a continuous time evolution interrupted by jumps , randomly distributed in time and described by a quantum channel . </S>",
    "<S> the state of the open system is shown to obey a closed evolution equation , given by a master equation with a memory kernel and a inhomogeneous term . </S>",
    "<S> the non - markovianity of the obtained dynamics is explicitly assessed studying the behavior of the distinguishability of two different initial system s states with elapsing time .    </S>",
    "<S> open quantum systems naturally arise in quantum mechanics due to lack of isolation , and one of the basic difficulties in the field is the derivation of closed irreversible evolution equations for the system only , taking into account the interaction with the environment @xcite . in particular </S>",
    "<S> an open issue is the characterization and study of memory effects described by these irreversible dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> an important class of dynamical evolutions is given by quantum dynamical semigroups , which by construction ensure complete positivity ( cp ) and have a number of attracting physical and mathematical features . </S>",
    "<S> the semigroup property ensures the existence of a closed evolution equation , known as master equation , whose general expression has been determined in the 70 s just thanks to the requirement of cp @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the operators appearing in the the master equation can be easily linked to the microscopic events which characterize the dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> moreover the exact solution can be expressed in terms of a dyson expansion , which allows for a natural reading in terms of a piecewise dynamics consisting of a relaxing evolution interrupted by jumps .    in this letter </S>",
    "<S> we show how a similar construction can be exploited to obtain a large class of non - markovian completely positive trace preserving ( cpt ) maps , still admitting closed evolution equations . </S>",
    "<S> the building blocks of this construction are a collection of time dependent maps , together with a waiting time distribution describing the random occurrence in time of interaction events described by a quantum channel . </S>",
    "<S> the operational construction provides a direct physical reading of the different contributions to the dynamics . </S>",
    "<S> the resulting master equations exhibit an integral kernel which warrants cp of the solution , one of the crucial difficulties in looking for extensions of the lindblad result @xcite .    </S>",
