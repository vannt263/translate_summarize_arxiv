    "the optical spectra of sn  ia near maximum light are dominated by resonance lines of singly ionized , intermediate - mass elements , doppler - broadened due to the large expansion velocities in sn  ia envelopes ; see @xcite for an observational review .",
    "optically thick lines forming in such fast - expanding ejecta have a p - cygni profile shape , characterized essentially by absorption blueward of line center and peak emission at line center @xcite .",
    "although these two components always appear qualitatively similar in p - cygni profiles associated with optically thick outflows , there are significant differences , which may become relevant if one seeks an accurate association with , say , the velocity at the photosphere  defined here as the outflow location where the inward integrated _ continuum _ optical depth is 2/3  of the sn ejecta @xcite or , in another context , with the asymptotic velocity of a radiatively driven hot star wind @xcite .",
