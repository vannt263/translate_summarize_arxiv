    "<S> we predict the effect of the roton instability for a two - dimensional _ weakly _ interacting gas of tilted dipoles in a single homogeneous quantum layer . being typical for strongly correlated systems , </S>",
    "<S> the roton phenomena appear to occur in a weakly interacting gas . </S>",
    "<S> it is important that in contrast to a system of normal to wide layer dipoles , breaking of the rotational symmetry for a system of tilted dipoles leads to the convergence of the condensate depletion even up to the threshold of the roton instability , with mean - field approach being valid . </S>",
    "<S> predicted effects can be observed in a wide class of dipolar systems . </S>",
    "<S> we suggest observing predicted phenomena for systems of ultracold atoms and polar molecules in optical lattices , and estimate optimal experimental parameters .    </S>",
