    "we have carried out the measurement of the thermal conductivity of iron up to 120 gpa and 2000 k using single sided laser heated diamond anvil cell ( lhdac ) under a steady state condition .",
    "we have calculated thermal conductivity from the direct temperature measurement of the sample surface and estimating the heat absorbed by the metal foil using thermodynamical equations .",
