    "in this work , we found that the influence of deformations on drops is much more complicated than that on bubbles .",
    "when the drop density is smaller than the bulk fluid , the migration velocity will decrease with the increase of the deformation .",
    "when the drop density is larger than the bulk fluid , the migration velocity will increase with the increase of the deformation . with the assumption adopted in this paper , we believe that in the hot region the drop will always start to deform , and there is no way to have any constant migrating velocity .",
    "hence , keeping @xmath11 and @xmath0 small throughout simulations is the only possible way for the drop to keep spherical and reach the steady migrating state .",
