    "<S> in this paper we consider smooth affine elliptic plane curves having one place at infinity . we identify them with elliptic projective plane curves having only one cusp as their singular points and meeting with the line at infinity only at the cusp . </S>",
    "<S> we characterize such curves by the self - intersection number of the strict transform of them via the minimal embedded resolution of their cusp . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we prove that the self - intersection number of them is the maximum value among those of all the elliptic plane curves having only one cusp . </S>"
