    "<S> using symmetries of the current correlation function , we analyze the frequency dependence of the photon energy absorption rate of a striped hall gas . </S>",
    "<S> since the magnetic translational symmetry is spontaneously broken in the striped hall gas , a nambu - goldstone ( ng ) mode appears . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the ng mode causes a sharp absorption at the zero energy in the long wavelength limit by using the single mode approximation . </S>",
    "<S> the photon energy absorption rate at the ng mode frequency strongly depends on the direction of the wave number vector . whereas , the absorption rate at the cyclotron frequency does not depend on the direction of the wave number vector in the long wavelength limit . </S>",
    "<S> the cyclotron resonance is not affected in the striped hall gas . </S>",
    "<S> our result supplements the kohn s theorem in the system of the ng mode . </S>"
