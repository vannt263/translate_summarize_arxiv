    "since the discovery of superconductors without the inversion symmetry in cept@xmath3si , @xcite non - centrosymmetric superconductors has been studied intensively as unconventional superconductors . in particular , theoretical studies suggest interesting properties of non - centrosymmetric superconductors such as the magnetoelectric effect , @xcite anisotropic spin susceptibility , @xcite and the helical vortex state in magnetic fields .",

    "@xcite    in superconductors with the inversion symmetry , pairing symmetry is classified into even- and odd - parity , @xmath8 spin - singlet and -triplet . in the non - centrosymmetric superconductors , on the other hand , admixture of spin - singlet and -triplet pairings is realized .",
    "@xcite the admixture is induced by the antisymmetric spin - orbit coupling , which is generated by the lack of the inversion symmetry .",
    "for example , it has been proposed that admixture of spin - singlet @xmath9-wave and spin - triplet @xmath10-wave pairings is realized in a non - centrosymmetric heavy fermion superconductor cept@xmath3si from both theoretical @xcite and experimental @xcite studies .    in particular , non - centrosymmetric heavy fermion superconductors , @xmath4 cept@xmath3si , @xcite uir , @xcite cerhsi@xmath3 , @xcite ceirsi@xmath3 , @xcite and cecoge@xmath3 , @xcite are of interest because the superconductivity originates from the coulomb repulsion . however , there are few theoretical studies on these materials on the basis of the microscopic calculation .",
    "@xcite it is desired to microscopically understand the admixture of spin - singlet and -triplet pairings in non - centrosymmetric superconductors where coulomb repulsion mediates pairing in more detail .    in order to study the above admixture , it is valuable to employ the extended hubbard model because this model exhibits various pairing symmetries in the presence of the inversion symmetry .",
    "@xcite on the extended hubbard model , the off - site coulomb repulsion is considered in addition to the on - site one .",
    "it is well known that , while the on - site coulomb repulsion induces the spin fluctuation , the charge fluctuation coexists with the spin fluctuation by introducing the off - site coulomb repulsion .",
    "@xcite due to the coexistence of the charge fluctuation with the spin one , especially on a two - dimensional square lattice near half - filling , three types of pairing symmetries , @xmath8 spin - singlet @xmath11-wave , spin - triplet @xmath12-wave , and spin - singlet @xmath13-wave ones , compete against each others .",
