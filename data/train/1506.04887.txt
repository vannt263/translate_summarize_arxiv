    "[ def - classes ] the class @xmath277 of _ weak equivalences _ is the class of all morphisms @xmath252 such that @xmath251 is a simplicial homotopy equivalence . the class @xmath278 of _ cofibrations _ is the class of monomorphisms .",
    "the class of _ trivial cofibrations _ is the class of morphisms which are retracts of strong anodyne extensions .",
    "the class @xmath279 of _ fibrations _ is the class of morphisms with the right lifting property with respect to all horn inclusions .",
    "the class of _ trivial fibrations _ is the class of morphisms with the right lifting property with respect to all monomorphisms .",
    "[ lem - weq ] the weak equivalences have the 2-out - of-3 property and are closed under retracts .",
    "it is an easy exercise that homotopy equivalences have these properties .",
    "[ lem - factorization ] any map @xmath250 can be factorized as a cofibration followed by a fibration , and we may choose either map to be trivial .",
    "this is quillen s small object argument introduced in @xcite .",
    "note that in the ( trivial cofibration)fibration factorization we in fact get a strong anodyne extension for the first factor .",
    "[ lem - saturation ] the saturation axiom holds for these classes .",
    "that is , the class of trivial cofibrations is precisely the class of maps with the left - lifting property with respect to the fibrations , as is the class of cofibrations to the trivial fibrations ; and the class of trivial fibrations is precisely the class of maps with the right - lifting property with respect to the cofibrations , as is the class of fibrations to the trivial cofibrations .",
    "the third assertion is nothing more than definition [ def - classes ] , and the fourth follows easily from the definitions by standard colimit closure properties of left classes .",
    "the former two assertions are now easy consequences of [ lem - factorization ] and the fact that monomorphisms are stable under retracts .",
    "lemma [ lem - saturation ] is the only place in this argument where we essentially require the axiom of choice .",
