    "in the present work , we generalized the approach used in ref.@xcite for breaking @xmath0 symmetry in the neutrino mass matrix by considering a small but complex perturbation parameter .",
    "our starting point is a zeroth order mass matrix arranged to give three degenerate neutrino masses .",
    "this mass matrix can be diagonalized by e.g. the tbm mixing matrix .",
    "mass splittings are introduced by adding perturbations to the zeroth order neutrino mass matrix .",
    "this also leads to a relatively large reactor mixing angle and cp violation in the neutrino mass matrix .",
    "the perturbation matrices are chosen to be the @xmath0 group matrices in the three dimensional reducible representation belonging to the same class . replacing the real perturbation parameter used in the earlier work @xcite by a complex perturbation parameter",
    "has profound consequences for the phenomenology of neutrino masses and mixings .",
    "a real perturbation parameter produced very small reactor mixing angle and there was no cp violation in the model . to accommodate the recent t2k results indicating a relatively large reactor mixing angle it becomes necessary to consider a complex perturbation parameter which also generates cp violation .",
    "the neutrino masses are found to be quasidegenerate and a large effective neutrino mass is predicted which is testable in the ongoing and forthcoming experiments for neutrinoless double beta decay .",

    "<S> we study the effects of breaking @xmath0 symmetry in the neutrino mass matrix for the masses and mixing matrix of neutrinos . at zeroth order </S>",
    "<S> the model gives degenerate neutrino masses and accommodates tribimaximal mixing . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce perturbations in terms of a small and complex parameter . </S>",
    "<S> the perturbations are introduced in a manner such that the @xmath0 symmetry is broken by its elements in the same representation . </S>",
    "<S> successive perturbations introduce mass splitting , sizable non - zero reactor mixing angle and cp violation . </S>",
    "<S> this scheme of breaking @xmath0 symmetry can reproduce a relatively large reactor mixing angle as suggested by the recent t2k results . </S>",
    "<S> the effective neutrino mass is predicted to be large which is testable in the ongoing and forthcoming neutrinoless double beta decay experiments . </S>"
