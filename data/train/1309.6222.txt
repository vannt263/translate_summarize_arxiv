    "<S> according to the orbit method , the construction of a unitary irreducible representation of a nilpotent lie group requires a precise computation of some polarizing subalgebra subordinated to a linear functional in the linear dual of the corresponding lie algebra . </S>",
    "<S> this important step is generally challenging from a computational viewpoint . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we provide an algorithmic approach to the construction of the well - known vergne polarizing subalgebras @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the algorithms presented in this paper are specifically designed so that they can be implemented in computer algebra systems . </S>",
    "<S> we also show there are instances where vergne s construction could be refined for the sake of efficiency . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we adapt our refined procedure to free nilpotent finite - dimensional lie algebras of step - two to obtain simple and precise descriptions of vergne polarizing algebras corresponding to all linear functionals in a dense open subset of the linear dual of the corresponding lie algebra . </S>",
    "<S> also , a program written for mathematica is presented at the end of the paper . </S>"
