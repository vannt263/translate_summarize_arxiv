    "<S> in this article we study the transitivity of the group of automorphisms of real algebraic surfaces . </S>",
    "<S> we characterize real algebraic surfaces with very transitive automorphism groups . </S>",
    "<S> we give applications to the classification of real algebraic models of compact surfaces : these applications yield new insight into the geometry of the real locus , proving several surprising facts on this geometry . </S>",
    "<S> this geometry can be thought of as a half - way point between the biregular and birational geometries . </S>"
