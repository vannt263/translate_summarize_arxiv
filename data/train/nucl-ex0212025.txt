    "the study of nuclear reactions near the coulomb barrier involving loosely bound nuclei has received considerable attention in recent years .",
    "this is primarily driven by the advent of radioactive ion beams@xcite .",
    "it is frequently observed in stable beam experiments that the subbarrier fusion cross sections are enhanced over one - dimensional barrier penetration model predictions .",
    "the enhancement can be described by channel couplings where the interplay of the intrinsic degrees of freedom and reaction channels modify the single barrier to multiple barriers@xcite .",
    "the barriers appearing at lower energies are responsible for the fusion enhancement .",
    "breakup is a major reaction channel in the scattering of loosely bound nuclei ; this removal of the incident flux would lead to fusion suppression@xcite . on the other hand , the coupling to the breakup channel can change the barrier distribution which could result in fusion enhancement@xcite .",
    "experimental efforts have been put forward to study the influence of breakup on subbarrier fusion@xcite .",
    "the fusion excitation functions of the neutron skin nucleus @xmath2he on @xmath3bi@xcite and @xmath4u@xcite were measured and large subbarrier fusion enhancements were observed in both cases .",
    "the breakup cross sections of @xmath2he on @xmath3bi measured below the coulomb barrier are orders of magnitude greater than fusion@xcite .",
    "measurements with stable @xmath5be , which has a neutron binding energy of 1.665 mev , on @xmath1pb@xcite and @xmath3bi@xcite found that the fusion was not enhanced below the coulomb barrier and was suppressed by about 30% above the barrier . in the @xmath5be+@xmath1pb reaction ,",
    "the incomplete fusion reaction @xmath6+@xmath1pb following the breakup of @xmath5be into n+@xmath6+@xmath6 was observed .",
    "the suppression of fusion at energies above the barrier was attributed to the projectile breakup .",
    "the barrier distribution extracted from the fusion excitation functions is consistent with a single barrier for the @xmath5be+@xmath1pb and @xmath5be+@xmath3bi@xcite even though very large breakup yields were observed below the barrier@xcite .",
    "the fusion of @xmath5be+@xmath3bi was compared to that of @xmath7be ( a neutron halo nucleus with neutron binding energy of 0.504 mev)+@xmath3bi . at energies below the barrier",
    "the cross sections were similar to that of @xmath5be+@xmath3bi whereas at energies above the barrier the cross sections are significantly larger than the predictions from a coupled - channels calculation which takes into account the large rms radius of @xmath7be .",
    "however , the precision of the data was not very good and further measurements are required@xcite .",
    "on the proton rich side , fusion of a proton drip line nucleus , @xmath0f , with @xmath1pb was measured@xcite .",
    "the fusion excitation function is almost identical to that of @xmath8o+@xmath1pb and @xmath9f+@xmath1pb after correcting for the coulomb barrier arising from the charge and size differences .",
    "there was no enhancement or perhaps a small suppression of fusion below the barrier .",
    "it is noted that the loosely bound proton can be polarized in the large coulomb field of the target in such a way that the proton is shielded by the core and the breakup probability is reduced@xcite",
