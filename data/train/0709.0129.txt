    "<S> we analyze the spatially semidiscrete piecewise linear finite element method for a nonlocal parabolic equation resulting from thermistor problem . </S>",
    "<S> our approach is based on the properties of the elliptic projection defined by the bilinear form associated with the variational formulation of the finite element method . </S>",
    "<S> we assume minimal regularity of the exact solution that yields optimal order error estimate . </S>",
    "<S> the full discrete backward euler method and the crank - nicolson - galerkin scheme are also considered . </S>",
    "<S> finally , a simple algorithm for solving the fully discrete problem is proposed .    </S>",
    "<S> finite element method , nonlocal parabolic equation , elliptic projection , error estimates .    </S>",
    "<S> _ mathematics subject classification 2000 : _ 65m60 , 65n30 , 65n15 . </S>"
