    "<S> the van - der - waals version of the second virial coefficient is not far from being exact if the model parameters are appropriately chosen . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown how the van - der - waals resemblance originates from the interplay of thermal averaging and superposition of scattering phase shift contributions . </S>",
    "<S> the derivation of the two parameters from the quantum virial coefficient reveals a fermion - boson symmetry in non - ideal quantum gases . </S>",
    "<S> numerical details are worked out for the helium quantum gases .    </S>",
