    "<S> we consider different problems within the general theme of long - range percolation on oriented graphs . our aim is to settle the so - called truncation question , described as follows . </S>",
    "<S> we are given probabilities that certain long - range oriented bonds are open ; assuming that the sum of these probabilities is infinite , we ask if the probability of percolation is positive when we truncate the graph , disallowing bonds of range above a possibly large but finite threshold . </S>",
    "<S> we give some conditions in which the answer is affirmative . </S>",
    "<S> we also translate some of our results on oriented percolation to the context of a long - range contact process .    </S>",
    "<S> keywords : contact processes ; oriented percolation ; long - range percolation + msc numbers : 60k35 , 82b43 </S>"
