    "various algebraic structures are subject of research in algebra . for example , groups , rings , fields , modules , etc .",

    "this has shown to be a fruitful approach in the field of algebraic combinatorics and , in the recent years , has been a topic of interest among researchers . in particular , there are various graphs that have been associated with groups .",
