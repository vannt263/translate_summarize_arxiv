    "<S> we illustrate possible topological phases in honeycomb lattice with textures in electron hopping energy between _ nearest - neighboring _ sites and show that they are characterized by the mirror winding number intimately related to the chiral ( or sublattice ) symmetry . </S>",
    "<S> analytic wave functions of zero - energy edge modes in ribbon geometry are provided , which are classified into even and odd sectors with respect to the mirror operation with the mirror plane perpendicular to the edge , and evolve into the topological helical edge states at finite momenta . </S>",
    "<S> intriguingly our results demonstrate that in order to achieve the topological phase one can decorate the edge in a way adaptive to the bulk hopping texture . </S>",
    "<S> this paves a new way to tailoring graphene in the topological point of view .    _ </S>",
