    "<S> limit theorems for the time average of some observation functions in an infinite measure dynamical system are studied . </S>",
    "<S> it is known that intermittent phenomena , such as the rayleigh - benard convection and belousov - zhabotinsky reaction , are described by infinite measure dynamical systems . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the time average of the observation function which is not the @xmath0 function , whose average with respect to the invariant measure @xmath1 is finite , converges to the generalized arcsine distribution . </S>",
    "<S> this result leads to the novel view that the correlation function is intrinsically random and does not decay . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , it is also numerically shown that the time average of the observation function converges to the stable distribution when the observation function has the infinite mean .    </S>",
