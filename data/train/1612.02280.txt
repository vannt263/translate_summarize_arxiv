    ", we can transform the lfnrde into a local fractional ode .",
    "the non - differentiable solution of the lfnrde with constant coefficients is presented .",
    "an illustrative example with a cantor - like function is given .",
    "we claim that our method may be applied to improve the performance in image classification , such as tea classification @xcite , pathological brain detection @xcite , fruit classification @xcite , and alzheimer s disease detection @xcite .",
