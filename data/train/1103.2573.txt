    "<S> full - rate stbc ( space - time block codes ) with non - vanishing determinants achieve the optimal diversity - multiplexing tradeoff but incur high decoding complexity . to permit fast decoding , sezginer , sari and biglieri proposed an stbc structure with special qr decomposition characteristics . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we adopt a simplified form of this fast - decodable code structure and present a new way to optimize the code analytically . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the signal constellation topology ( such as qam , apsk , or psk ) has a critical impact on the existence of non - vanishing determinants of the full - rate stbc . in particular </S>",
    "<S> , we show for the first time that , in order for apsk - stbc to achieve non - vanishing determinant , an apsk constellation topology with constellation points lying on square grid and ring radius @xmath0 needs to be used . for signal constellations with vanishing determinants </S>",
    "<S> , we present a methodology to analytically optimize the full - rate stbc at specific constellation dimension . </S>"
