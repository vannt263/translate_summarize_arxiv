    "the two - mode model applied to double - well atomic bose - einstein condensates has been extensively studied in the last years @xcite assuming that the order parameter can be described as a superposition of localized on - site wave functions with time dependent coefficients , such a model predicts josephson and self - trapping regimes @xcite , which have been experimentally observed by albiez _",
    "the self - trapping ( st ) phenomenon , which is also present in extended optical lattices @xcite , is a non linear effect where an initially highly populated ( over a critical value ) site , remains with a larger number of particles than the remaining sites over all the evolution .",
    "there is nowadays an active research on the self - trapping effect , which involves different types of systems , including mixtures of atomic species @xcite .",
    "the dynamics of ring - shaped optical lattices with three @xcite and four wells @xcite , has been previously investigated through multiple - mode ( mm ) models which utilized _ ad - hoc _",
    "values for hopping and on - site energy parameters . in the present article instead",
    ", we will extract such parameters from a mean - field approach using localized on - site functions .",
    "we have shown in a previous work @xcite that in a ring - shaped optical lattice , localized on - site ( which we called ` wannier - like ' ( wl ) ) functions can be obtained in terms of stationary states of the gross - pitaevskii ( gp ) equation with different winding numbers . here",
    "we will show that the above parameters yield the same type of corrections to the mm model for large filling factors , as those obtained for the improved two - mode ( tm ) model for two - well systems @xcite .",
    "we will derive an approximate formula for the self - trapping period in terms of the on - site interaction energy parameter . using this formula and a single gp simulation results ,",
    "a renormalizing on - site energy parameter that substantially improves the mm model can be obtained , in what will be called the renormalized multiple - mode ( rmm ) model .",
    "taking into account the density deformation during the time evolution @xcite , it has been shown in a recent work that for a double - well system an effective interaction energy parameter should be considered in the tm model to properly describe the exact dynamics @xcite . here",
    "we will adapt the same approach to our multiple - well system , which will allow us to obtain such an effective parameter only in terms of the ground state density .",
    "finally , we will show that both approaches give similar results .",

    "<S> we study the population dynamics of a ring - shaped optical lattice with a high number of particles per site and a low , below ten , number of wells . using a localized on - site basis defined in terms of stationary states </S>",
    "<S> , we were able to construct a multiple - mode model depending on relevant hopping and on - site energy parameters . </S>",
    "<S> we show that in case of two wells , our model corresponds exactly to the latest improvement of the two - mode model . </S>",
    "<S> we derive a formula for the self - trapping period , which turns out to be chiefly ruled by the on - site interaction energy parameter . by comparing to time dependent gross - pitaevskii simulations , </S>",
    "<S> we show that the multimode model results can be enhanced in a remarkable way over all the regimes by only renormalizing such a parameter . finally , using a different approach which involves only the ground state density </S>",
    "<S> , we derive an effective interaction energy parameter that shows to be in accordance with the renormalized one . </S>"
