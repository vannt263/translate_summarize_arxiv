    "<S> the study of complex systems benefits from graph models and their analysis . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , the eigendecomposition of the graph laplacian lets emerge properties of global organization from local interactions ; e.g. , the fiedler vector has the smallest non - zero eigenvalue and plays a key role for graph clustering . </S>",
    "<S> graph signal processing focusses on the analysis of signals that are attributed to the graph nodes . </S>",
    "<S> the eigendecomposition of the graph laplacian allows to define the graph fourier transform and extend conventional signal - processing operations to graphs . here , </S>",
    "<S> we introduce the design of slepian graph signals , by maximizing energy concentration in a predefined subgraph for a graph spectral bandlimit . </S>",
    "<S> we establish a novel link with classical laplacian embedding and graph clustering , which provides a meaning to localized graph frequencies .    graph signal processing , graph laplacian , slepian functions , graph cut , laplacian embedding </S>"
