    "<S> we present a highly reliable and efficient mid - infrared colour - based selection technique for luminous active galactic nuclei ( agn ) using the wide - field infrared survey explorer ( wise ) survey . </S>",
    "<S> our technique is designed to identify objects with red mid - infrared power - law spectral energy distributions . </S>",
    "<S> we studied the dependency of our mid - infrared selection on the agn intrinsic luminosity and the effectiveness of our technique to uncover obscured agn missed in x - ray surveys . </S>",
    "<S> to do so we used two samples of luminous agn independently selected in hard x - ray and optical surveys . </S>",
