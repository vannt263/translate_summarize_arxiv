    "<S> in general there exists no relationship between the fixed point sets of the composition and of the average of a family of nonexpansive operators in hilbert spaces . in this paper </S>",

    "<S> when the operators are projectors onto closed convex sets , we prove a conjecture by de pierro which has so far been established only for projections onto affine subspaces .    </S>",
