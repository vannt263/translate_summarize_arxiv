    "nuclei far from the stability has attracted a considerable interest both experimentally and theoretically . exploring the multipole responses in unstable nuclei",
    "is in particular of great interest because they provide information on collective modes of excitation . in neutron - rich nuclei ,",
    "the surface structure is quite different to the stable ones due to the presence of the loosely bound neutrons .",
    "one of the unique structures is the neutron skin  @xcite .",
    "since the collective excitations are sensitive to the surface structure , we can expect new kinds of exotic excitation mode associated with the neutron skin to appear in neutron - rich nuclei .",
    "an example is the soft dipole excitation  @xcite , or the pygmy dipole resonance ( pdr ) , which is observed not only in light halo nuclei  @xcite , but also in heavier systems  @xcite , where an appreciable @xmath5 strength is observed above the neutron emission threshold , and it exhausts several percents of the energy - weighted sum rule ( ewsr ) value .    the structure of the pdr and its collectivity has been studied based on the mean - field calculations by many groups .",
    "( see ref .",

    "quite recently , by means of the deformed quasiparticle - random - phase approximation ( qrpa ) , the low - lying dipole excitation in neutron - rich ne isotopes  @xcite and in neutron - rich sn isotopes  @xcite have been investigated .",
    "presently , small excitation energies of the first @xmath6 state and striking enhancements of @xmath7 in @xmath8 mg  @xcite and @xmath9 mg  @xcite are under lively discussions in connection with onset of the quadrupole deformation , breaking of the @xmath10 spherical magic number , pairing correlation and continuum coupling effects  @xcite . in order to get clear understanding of the nature of quadrupole deformation and pairing correlations , it is strongly desirable to explore , both experimentally and theoretically , excitation modes in mg isotopes toward a drip line  @xcite .    in the present article",
    ", we investigate the microscopic structure of the low - lying dipole excitation in neutron - rich mg isotopes close to the drip line , paying special attention to the deformation effects on them . in a deformed system ,",
