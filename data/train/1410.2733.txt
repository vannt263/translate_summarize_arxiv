    "we have presented _ suzaku _ results for nearby _ fermi_-lat detected low - power radio galaxies , three of which are analyzed here for the first time . based on the fe - k and x - ray spectral slope , x - ray variability , and [ o iii ] line strength , we argued for the jet origin of the observed x - ray emission in pks0625@xmath1354  and 3c78 .",
