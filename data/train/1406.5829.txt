    "we have presented detailed measurements of the conductivity induced by superconducting fluctuations just above the superconducting transition of three high - quality optimally - doped bafe@xmath0ni@xmath1as@xmath2 single crystals .",
    "these measurements were performed with magnetic fields up to 14 t , which allowed to deeply penetrate into the finite - field ( or prange ) fluctuation regime .",
    "the magnetic field was applied with different orientations with respect to the crystals @xmath3 axis ( @xmath4 , 53@xmath5 , and 90@xmath5 ) , allowing to investigate the anisotropy of fluctuation effects .",
    "the analysis of the experimental data lead to solid evidence of the applicability to these compounds of a recently published gaussian gl approach for 3d anisotropic superconductors in presence of finite applied magnetic fields .",
    "our results contrast with the recent observation of a seemingly two - dimensional paraconductivity in clean lifeas single crystals , in spite that the coherence length amplitudes of this compound are similar to the ones in optimally doped bafe@xmath0ni@xmath1as@xmath2 .",
