    "coupled chaotic systems can synchronise their trajectories @xcite .",
    "the synchronisation of coupled chaotic systems has important applications in many fields , such as : biological systems @xcite , secure communications @xcite , chemical reactions @xcite , etc .",
    "recent works have shown that chaos synchronisation of a master - slave configuration is relevant to communication systems .",
    "the slave system is driven by a signal derived from the master @xcite . here",
    "we focus the study in two chaotic coupled colpitts oscillators @xcite .",
    "we choose this circuit due to the fact that it can be useful in applications to communication systems , as well as , it exhibits a rich dynamical behaviour for certain parameter values @xcite . in the colpitts oscillator",
    "the operation frequency can vary from few hertz up to the microwave frequency range , a characteristic that enables the use of this circuit to transmit information in channels with difference frequency bandwidths .",
    "there has been a great interest in the study of synchronisation and control of colpitts oscillators .",
    "control schemes were used to suppress chaos .",
    "li and collaborators used a controller to drive chaotic colpitts to a desired state @xcite , that is , the stabilisation of the chaotic motion to a steady state . in another work ,",
    "the circuit was controlled by using a non linear feedback @xcite .",
    "it has been found synchronisation between chaotic colpitts in identical and mismatched cases @xcite .",
    "furthermore , there are works about synchronisation of colpitts oscillators that operate in ultra high frequency range @xcite .    in this article",
    ", we study two coupled colpitts oscillators in a master - slave configuration and focus our attention to chaos synchronisation .",
    "our main objective is to verify the existence of super persistent chaotic transients @xcite , and the effect of noise on the synchronous behaviour @xcite .",
    "this article is organized as follows : in section 2 we present the coupled colpitts oscillators . in section 3",
    "we study the onset of synchronisation and show the existence of super persistent transients . in section 4",
    "we describe the effect of noise on the system .",
    "the last section contains our conclusions .",

    "in this paper , we studied some aspects of chaos synchronisation displayed by two coupled colpitts with master - slave configuration .",
    "we obtained a set of parameters which may lead the coupled circuits to a synchronised or non synchronised state .",
    "we verified the existence of super persistent transients .",
    "this transient is mainly situated in the border of the synchronised domain in the parameter space @xmath37 versus @xmath27 , where @xmath37 is the loop gain of the oscillator , and @xmath27 is the strength coupling .    the effect of noise on the coupled circuits were considered .",
    "noise acts on the system in a way that synchronisation can only be achieved for higher coupling strength .",
    "moreover , the transients become longer .",
    "our results enable us to predict a set of parameters of the coupled colpitts oscillators to observe super persistent transients that can be used in laboratory experiments .",
    "the described persistent transients are similar to those observed in dissipative systems , and should be related to the chaotic saddle of the coupled systems @xcite .    in future works , we plan to study this coupled system considering electronic simulations @xcite , and also to get experimental results through an electronic circuit .",

    "<S> master - slave systems have been intensively investigated to model the application of chaos to communications . </S>",
    "<S> we considered colpitts oscillators coupled according to a master - slave configuration to study chaos synchronisation . </S>",
    "<S> we revealed the existence of super persistent transients in this coupled system . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , we showed that an additive noise added to the slave system may suppress chaos synchronisation . </S>",
    "<S> when synchronisation is not suppressed , the noise induces longer transients . </S>"
