    "can reproduce the main cosmological data @xcite .",
    "a general property of extra - dimensional models is that although ordinary matter is supposed to be confined to a brane , gravity propagates in the whole space - time .",
    "this entails the effect that gravitons produced in reactions of particles on the brane can escape to the bulk .",
    "graviton production is strong in the early hot universe , and can alter the time evolution of matter on the brane and , in particular , the primordial nucleosynthesis .",
    "in this paper we calculate graviton production in a model of five - dimensional universe with one large extra dimension .",
