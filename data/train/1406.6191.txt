    "<S> we consider a gas of point particles moving in a one - dimensional channel with a hard - core inter - particle interaction that prevents particle crossings  this is called single - file motion . starting from equilibrium initial conditions we observe the motion of a tagged particle . </S>",
    "<S> it is well known that if the individual particle dynamics is diffusive , then the tagged particle motion is sub - diffusive , while for ballistic particle dynamics , the tagged particle motion is diffusive . here </S>",
    "<S> we compute exactly the large deviation function for the tagged particle displacement and show that this is universal , independent of the individual dynamics .    </S>",

    "<S> this concept was introduced by hodgkin and keynes  @xcite to describe ion transport in biological channels . </S>",
    "<S> the motion of a tagged particle in such a single - file system has been of great interest since the classic papers by jepsen  @xcite and harris  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these papers showed that , in a gas of hard rods evolving with hamiltonian dynamics , a tagged particle moves diffusively  @xcite with the mean square displacement ( msd ) growing linearly with time @xmath0 , whereas for a gas of brownian particles , the tagged particle shows sub - diffusion  @xcite with the msd growing as @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> there has been a revival of interest in tagged particle diffusion as several experiments are now able to observe this in single - file systems in both colloidal and atomic single - file systems  @xcite , and some of the theoretical predictions have been verified .    </S>",
    "<S> there have been a number of studies to understand tagged particle motion in systems with deterministic as well as stochastic dynamics  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> attempts have been made to obtain the full probability density function ( pdf ) for the tagged particle displacement . </S>",
