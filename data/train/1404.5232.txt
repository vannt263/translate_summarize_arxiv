    "we determined the probability distribution of the number of particles @xmath6 that have not been absorbed by time @xmath29 .",
    "apart from @xmath6 and @xmath0 , this distribution crucially depends on the parameter @xmath8 .",
    "we employed the macroscopic fluctuation theory ( mft ) to find the `` optimal path '' of the system , namely , the most probable density history conditioned on a given number of surviving particles at an arbitrary observation time @xmath7 .",
    "the optimal path gives fascinating insights into the nature of large fluctuations of the absorption process .",
    "a striking result arises in the situation where one demands that the particles , released far from the absorber , are absorbed in time @xmath29 that is short . here , to leading order , the spatial probability density moves like a material point particle with constant speed towards the origin , and",
    "is absorbed at time @xmath29 .",
    "the model of non - interacting diffusing particles is amenable to a complete analytical solution within the framework of the mft  @xcite . for diffusive lattice gases of interacting particles ,",
    "the mft problem becomes much harder to solve .",
    "nevertheless , some of the insights we gained here should be useful in studying particle or energy absorption and other dynamical processes in interacting lattice gases .",
