    "<S> we present a cohomological formulation of measurement - based quantum computation ( mbqc ) , employing the notion of a phase function . </S>",
    "<S> the phase function describes symmetries of the resource state of the mbqc , specifies the computational output , and acts as a contextuality witness . </S>",
