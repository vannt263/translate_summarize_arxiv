    "it consists of a surface array of cherenkov tanks , called icetop , and a large array of optical modules in the deep ice between 1.45 and 2.45 km below the ice sheet .",
    "data of the surface array allow reconstructing direction and energy of down - going primary cosmic rays in the energy range from about 100  tev to 1  eev .",
    "the main purpose of the deep detector array is to detect neutrinos , but it also permits the reconstruction of penetrating cosmic ray muons  @xcite . since may 2011 , icecube is taking data in its full configuration .",

    "a dom contains photomultipliers and readout electronics .",

    "<S> analysis of cosmic ray surface data collected with the icetop array of cherenkov detectors at the south pole provides an accurate measurement of the cosmic ray spectrum and its features in the `` knee '' region up to energies of about 1  eev . </S>",
    "<S> icetop is part of the icecube observatory that includes a deep - ice cubic kilometer detector that registers signals of penetrating muons and other particles . </S>",
    "<S> surface and in - ice signals detected in coincidence provide clear insights into the nuclear composition of cosmic rays . </S>",
    "<S> icecube already measured an increase of the average primary mass as a function of energy . </S>",
    "<S> we present preliminary results on both icetop - only and coincident event analyses . </S>",
    "<S> furthermore , we review the recent measurement of the cosmic ray anisotropy with icecube .    cosmic rays , energy spectrum , nuclear composition , anisotropy </S>"
