    "_ in conclusion _",
    ", we have shown an important influence of the semiconductor surface on the electron energy structure in bare spherical nanocrystals .",
    "the effect of the surface has been modeled through the choice of the boundary condition parameter that describes the nonzero value of the envelope function at the nanocrystal surface .",
    "the additional nonparabolicity of the quantum size energy levels , the spin  orbit splitting of the electron quantum size levels , and the additional magnetic moment of the electrons have been shown to be induced by the surface .",
    "the effects are significant in small nanocrystals and their considerations require a multiband effective mass approach because interband coupling is important there .",
    "the analysis of the experimental data allows us to determine the appropriate parameter of the boundary conditions that characterize the surface in studied bare cdse semiconductor nanocrystals .",
