    "we have shown that the ratios of the three main lines ( forbidden , intercombination and resonance ) of he - like ions provide very powerful diagnostics for totally or partially photo - ionized media .",

    "+ these diagnostics have strong advantages .",
    "the lines are emitted by the same ionization stage of one element , thus eliminating any uncertainties due to elemental abundances .",
    "in addition , since the line energies are relatively close together , this minimizes wavelength dependent instrumental calibration uncertainties , thus ensuring that observed photon count rates can be used almost directly .",
    "these diagnostics should be used not as stand - alone one but should be used combined to other plasma diagnostics such as those based on radiative recombination continuum ( rrc ; liedahl & paerels @xcite ) , fe - l shell lines ( see review of liedahl et al .",
