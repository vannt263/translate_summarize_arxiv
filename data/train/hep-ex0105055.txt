    "deep inelastic lepton nucleon scattering is particularly well suited for the study of the spatial structure of quarks and leptons .",
    "the sensitivity to substructure depends on the virtuality @xmath2 of the exchanged current and , therefore , on the energy of the scattering partners .",
    "the highest energies are provided by hera where by now a spatial resolution of @xmath3 cm or one thousandth of the proton radius has been achieved .",
