    "pestov s identity links the generator of the geodesic flow on the tangent bundle of a manifold with the riemannian curvature tensor and other geometrically motivated differential operators .",
    "this identity has many application in dynamics and in the solution of inverse problems such as the x - ray transform and the boundary rigidity problem .",
    "pestov and sharafutdinov @xcite introduced this identity in order to derive some useful estimates on symmetric tensor fields , and they give an answer to a question related to tomography , mathematical transport theory and other disciplines , i.e. , how uniquely a symmetric tensor field @xmath4 on a negatively curved manifold @xmath0 is determined by its integrals over all the geodesics in @xmath0 . since then , pestov s identity has been a key tool in giving answers to this kind of questions . for its main applications in this direction",
    "we refer the reader to @xcite , @xcite , @xcite , the survey @xcite and the references therein .",
    "another application of pestov s identity is in the topic of spectral rigidity .",
    "croke and sharafutdinov @xcite used it to prove that a compact manifold of negative curvature is spectrally rigid , i.e. , the laplace - beltrami spectra of a family of deformed metrics on @xmath0 are different up to trivial deformation .",
    "this also generalizes work of guillemin and kazhdan @xcite and min - oo @xcite .",
    "pestov s identity has also been adapted to magnetic and anosov flows ( see for example @xcite , @xcite ) in relation to magnetic tomography and the boundary rigidity problem .    a coordinate - free proof of the identity is given by knieper in his survey on hyperbolic dynamics and riemannian geometry @xcite .",
