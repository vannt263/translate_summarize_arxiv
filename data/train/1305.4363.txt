    "<S> we develop an analogy between right - angled artin groups and mapping class groups through the geometry of their actions on the extension graph and the curve graph respectively . the central result in this paper </S>",
    "<S> is the fact that each right - angled artin group acts acylindrically on its extension graph . from this result </S>",
    "<S> we are able to develop a nielsen  thurston classification for elements in the right - angled artin group . </S>",
    "<S> our analogy spans both the algebra regarding subgroups of right - angled artin groups and mapping class groups , as well as the geometry of the extension graph and the curve graph . on the geometric side </S>",
    "<S> , we establish an analogue of masur and minsky s bounded geodesic image theorem and their distance formula . </S>"
