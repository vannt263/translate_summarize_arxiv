    "<S> we present new possibilities for the charge response in the quantum critical regime in 2 + 1d using holography , and compare them with field theory and recent quantum monte carlo results . </S>",
    "<S> we show that a family of ( infinitely many ) higher derivative terms in the gravitational bulk leads to behavior far richer than what was previously obtained . </S>",
    "<S> for example , we prove that the conductivity becomes unbounded , undermining previously obtained constraints . </S>",
    "<S> we further find a non - trivial and infinite set of theories that have a self - dual conductivity . </S>",
    "<S> particle - vortex or s duality plays a key role ; notably , it maps theories with a finite number of bulk terms to ones with an infinite number . </S>",
    "<S> many properties such as sum rules and stability conditions are proved . </S>"
