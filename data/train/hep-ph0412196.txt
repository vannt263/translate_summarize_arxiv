    "the observed baryon asymmetry of the universe is one of the most interesting problems of particle physics and cosmology .",
    "as was suggested by sakharov , the cosmological baryon excess can be generated through the baryon number violating decays of heavy particles @xcite . any microscopic theory able to reproduce the observed asymmetry",

    "the condition of baryon number violation makes a strong appeal to physics beyond the standard model ( sm ) .",
    "it has been proven to be difficult to generate an excess of baryons through direct violation of the baryon number",
    ". a very attractive scenario of generating lepton number asymmetry through lepton number violating decays of heavy right  handed neutrinos , which is later converted into the baryon number by sphalerons @xcite , has been suggested by fukugita and yanagida @xcite .",
