    "<S> we extend the duality symmetry between the electric and the magnetic fields to the case in which an additional axion - like term is present , and we derive the set of maxwell s equations that preserves this symmetry . </S>",
    "<S> this new set of equations allows for a gauge symmetry extending the ordinary symmetry in the classical electrodynamics . </S>",
    "<S> we obtain explicit solutions for the new set of equations in the absence of external sources , and we discuss the implications of a new internal symmetry between the axion field and the electromagnetic gauge potential . </S>"
