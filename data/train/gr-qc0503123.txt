    "<S> we study spherically symmetric regular and black hole solutions in the einstein - skyrme theory with a negative cosmological constant . </S>",
    "<S> the skyrme field configuration depends on the value of the cosmological constant in a similar manner to effectively varying the gravitational constant . </S>",
    "<S> we find the maximum value of the cosmological constant above which there exists no solution . </S>",
    "<S> the properties of the solutions are discussed in comparison with the asymptotically flat solutions . </S>",
    "<S> the stability is investigated in detail by solving the linearly perturbed equation numerically . </S>",
    "<S> we show that there exists a critical value of the cosmological constant above which the solution in the branch representing unstable configuration in the asymptotically flat spacetime turns to be linearly stable . </S>"
