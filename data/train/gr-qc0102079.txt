    "<S> we discuss the class of solutions to the einstein equations for disks with constant angular velocity and constant relative density which was constructed in the first part . </S>",
    "<S> the metric for these spacetimes is given in terms of theta functions on a riemann surface of genus 2 . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the metric functions at the axis of symmetry and the disk . </S>",
    "<S> interesting limiting cases are the newtonian limit , the static limit , and the ultra - relativistic limit of the solution in which the central redshift diverges .    </S>",
