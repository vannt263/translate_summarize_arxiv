    "<S> the induced chern - simons term for a paired electron state is calculated in the quantum hall system by using a field theory on the von neumann lattice . </S>",
    "<S> the coefficient of the chern - simons term , which is the hall conductance , has not only the usual term proportional to a filling factor due to p ( parity ) @xmath0 t ( time reversal ) symmetry breaking but also correction terms due to p @xmath0 t @xmath0 u(1 ) symmetry breaking . </S>",
    "<S> the correction term essentially comes from the nambu - goldstone mode and depends on an infrared limit . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the correction term is related to a topological number of a gap function in the momentum space .    </S>",
