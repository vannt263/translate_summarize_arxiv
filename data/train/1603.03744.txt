    "<S> transverse mode coupling instability of a bunch with space charge and wake field is considered within the frameworks of the boxcar model . </S>",
    "<S> eigenfunctions of the bunch without wake are used as a basis for solution of the equations with the wake field included . </S>",
    "<S> dispersion equation for the bunch eigentunes is presented in the form of an infinite continued fraction and also as the recursive relation with arbitrary number of the basis functions . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that the influence of space charge on the instability essentially depends on the wake sign . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , threshold of the negative wake increases in absolute value until the space charge tune shift is rather small , and goes down at the higher space charge . the explanation is developed by analysis of the bunch spectrum . </S>"
