    "the random interchange model is a stochastic process where transpositions are selected at random .",
    "the product of these transpositions gives a random permutation and the main question deals with its cycle structure .",
    "we consider variants where possible transpositions are restricted to nearest - neighbours of a regular cubic lattice .",
    "we also consider related loop models where `` crosses '' are replaced by `` double bars '' . we provide evidence that a phase transition takes place where macroscopic loops occur .",
    "we give good estimates of the values of the parameters at the transition point , which should help to better comprehend the model .",
    "finally , we compute moments of the lengths of the loops ; it turns out that they are identical to those of the poisson - dirichlet distribution .",
    "the random interchange model was invented by harris @xcite ; tth used it as a representation of the spin @xmath0 quantum heisenberg ferromagnet @xcite .",
    "angel @xcite and hammond @xcite obtained results for the model on trees .",
    "schramm considered the variant on the complete graph and he proved that the joint distribution of the large cycle lengths is poisson - dirichlet of parameter 1 , following a conjecture of aldous @xcite ; see also @xcite for some simplifications and extensions .",
    "alon and kozma have obtained remarkable identities that give the probability of cyclic permutations in terms of eigenvalues of the graph laplacian , for arbitrary graphs @xcite .",
    "these have allowed berestycki and kozma to obtain further results on the complete graph @xcite .",
    "a similar model with `` double bars '' instead of `` crosses '' was introduced by aizenman and nachtergaele in order to describe the spin @xmath0 quantum heisenberg model and the spin 1 model with biquadratic interactions @xcite .",
    "it should be noticed that the representation of quantum systems involves the extra factor @xmath1 with @xmath2 ... in the survey @xcite , the authors conjectured that the joint distribution of the lengths of long loops , in dimensions three and higher , is given by the poisson - dirichlet distribution of parameter @xmath3 .",
    "the two representations of tth and aizenman - nachtergaele were recently combined so as to describe quantum models that interpolate between the two heisenberg models , such as the spin @xmath0 quantum xy model and further spin 1 models with su(2)-invariant interactions @xcite . for these models ,",
    "the joint distribution of long loops should be poisson - dirichlet with parameter @xmath4 .",
    "the consequences of this structure have yet to be worked out .",
    "one such consequence is to identify the nature of symmetry breaking in the spin 1 model @xcite .",
    "the poisson - dirichlet distribution is conjectured to be a common feature of `` loop soups '' in dimensions three and higher .",
    "this was confirmed numerically in lattice permutations @xcite and in o(n ) loop models @xcite .",
    "this was also confirmed , with a mathematically rigorous proof , in an annealed model of spatial permutations @xcite .",
    "a numerical study of the spin 1 model @xcite also provides indirect evidence , as explained in @xcite .",
    "however , this conjecture is far from being accepted nowadays .",
    "it thus seems necessary to verify it also in those loop models that are related to quantum spin systems .",
