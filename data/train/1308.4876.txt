    "<S> we use variational methods to study a spin impurity in a 1d bose lattice gas . </S>",
    "<S> both in the strongly interacting superfluid regime and the mott regime we find that the impurity binds with a hole , forming a polaron . </S>",
    "<S> our calculations for the dispersion of the polaron are consistent with recent experiments by fukuhara _ et . al . </S>",
    "<S> _ [ http://www.nature.com/nphys/journal/v9/n4/full/nphys2561.html[nature phys . </S>",
    "<S> * 9 * , 235 ( 2013 ) ] ] and give a better understanding of their numerical simulations . </S>",
    "<S> we find that for sufficiently weak interactions there are ranges of momentum for which the polaron is unstable . </S>",
    "<S> we propose experimentally studying the stability of the polaron by measuring the correlation between the impurity and holes . </S>",
    "<S> we also study two interacting impurities , finding stable bipolarons for sufficiently strong interactions . </S>"
