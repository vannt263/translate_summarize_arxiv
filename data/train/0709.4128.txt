    "<S> we study full counting statistics of coherent electron transport through multi - terminal interacting quantum - dots under a finite magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> microscopic reversibility leads to the symmetry of the cumulant generating function , which generalizes the fluctuation theorem in the context of quantum transport . using this symmetry </S>",
    "<S> , we derive the onsager - casimir relation in the linear transport regime and universal relations among nonlinear transport coefficients .    </S>",
    "<S> _ full counting statistics _ ( fcs ) has become an active topic in the mesoscopic physics  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> fcs addresses the probability distributions of charges transmitted during a measurement time , @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> it can characterize the statistical properties of quantum transport in the far - from - equilibrium regime . since the first paper by levitov and lesovik  @xcite , </S>",
    "<S> many theories have clarified various aspects of the distributions  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recently , experiments have been conducted to measure third current cumulants  @xcite and the distributions  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , fcs has never been applied to exploration of general aspects , such as nonequilibrium thermodynamic structures in coherent electron transport . in this paper , we discuss these general aspects by studying symmetries in fcs , which is valid beyond a linear response regime </S>",
    "<S> .    our argument is based on the microscopic reversibility , and is related to the steady state _ fluctuation theorem _ ( ft ) in nonequilibrium statistical mechanics @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> ft is an important theory that holds even in the far - from - equilibrium regime . </S>",
    "<S> it is written as @xmath1/\\tau \\ ! = \\ ! </S>",

    "<S> recently ft was studied with regard to classical mesoscopic electron transport , i.e. , in the sequential tunneling regime in the quantum - dots , with markovian approximations  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> these works highlighted the relation between ft and fcs . in this paper , we study the general relation between ft and fcs with respect to _ coherent electron transports _ in generic situations . </S>",
    "<S> we consider a multi - terminal interacting electron cavity under a finite magnetic field . </S>",
