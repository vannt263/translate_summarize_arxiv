    "<S> a geometrically invariant concept of fast - slow vector fields perturbed by transport terms ( describing molecular diffusion ) is proposed in this paper . </S>",
    "<S> it is an extension of our concept of singularly perturbed vector fields to reaction - diffusion systems . </S>",
    "<S> this paper is motivated by an algorithm of reaction - diffusion manifolds ( redim ) . </S>",
    "<S> it can be considered as its theoretical justification extending it from a practical algorithm to a robust computational method . </S>",
    "<S> fast - slow vector fields can be represented locally as `` singularly perturbed systems of pde '' . </S>",
    "<S> the paper focuses on development of the decomposition to a fast and slow subsystems . </S>",
    "<S> it is demonstrated that transport terms can be neglected ( under reasonable physical assumptions ) for the fast subsystem . </S>",
    "<S> a simple practical application example of the proposed algorithm for numerical treatment of reaction - diffusion systems is demonstrated . </S>"
