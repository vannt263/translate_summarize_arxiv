    "<S> the standard model includes neutrinos as massless particles , but neutrino oscillations showed that neutrinos are not massless . </S>",
    "<S> a simple extension of adding gauge singlet fermions to the particle spectrum allows normal yukawa mass terms for neutrinos . </S>",
    "<S> the smallness of the neutrino masses can be well understood within the seesaw mechanism . </S>",
    "<S> we analyse two cases of the minimal extension of the standard model when one or two right - handed fields are added to the three left - handed fields . </S>",
    "<S> a second higgs doublet is included in our model . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the one - loop radiative corrections to the mass parameters which produce mass terms for the neutral leptons . in both cases </S>",
    "<S> we numerically analyse light neutrino masses as functions of the heavy neutrinos masses . </S>",
    "<S> parameters of the model are varied to find light neutrino masses that are compatible with experimental data of solar @xmath0 and atmospheric @xmath1 neutrino oscillations for normal and inverted hierarchy . </S>"
