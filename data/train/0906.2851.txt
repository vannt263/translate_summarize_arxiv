    "<S> we discuss both massive and massless charged scalars and spinors in two , three , and four dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> both strong field and weak field limits are calculable . </S>",
    "<S> the latter limit results in an asymptotic expansion whose first term reproduces the euler  heisenberg effective lagrangian . </S>",
    "<S> we use the zeta function renormalization prescription , and indicate its relationship to schwinger s renormalized effective action </S>",
