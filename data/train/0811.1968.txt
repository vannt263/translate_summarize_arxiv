    "<S> in three dimensions there is a logarithmically divergent contribution to the entanglement entropy which is due to the vertices located at the boundary of the region considered . in this work </S>",
    "<S> we find the corresponding universal coefficient for a free dirac field , and extend a previous work in which the scalar case was treated . </S>",
    "<S> the problem is equivalent to find the conformal anomaly in three dimensional space where multiplicative boundary conditions for the field are imposed on a plane angular sector . as an intermediate step of the calculation </S>",
