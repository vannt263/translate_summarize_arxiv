    "locally supersymmetric extensions of the standard model predict the existence of the gravitino , the gauge fermion of supergravity @xcite .",
    "for some patterns of supersymmetry breaking , the gravitino is the lightest superparticle ( lsp ) , and therefore a natural dark matter candidate @xcite .",
    "heavy unstable gravitinos may cause the ` gravitino problem ' @xcite for large reheating temperatures in the early universe .",
    "this is the case for thermal leptogenesis @xcite , where gravitino dark matter has become an attractive alternative @xcite to the standard wimp scenario @xcite .",
    "recently , it has been shown that models with small r - parity and lepton number breaking naturally yield a consistent cosmology incorporating primordial nucleosynthesis , leptogenesis and gravitino dark matter @xcite .",
    "the gravitino is no longer stable , but its decays into standard model ( sm ) particles are doubly suppressed by the planck mass and the small r - parity breaking parameter .",
    "hence , its lifetime can exceed the age of the universe by many orders of magnitude , and the gravitino remains a viable dark matter candidate @xcite .",
    "gravitino decays lead to characteristic signatures in high - energy cosmic rays , in particular to a diffuse gamma - ray flux @xcite .",
    "the recent search of the fermi - lat collaboration for monochromatic photon lines @xcite and the measurement of the diffuse gamma - ray flux up to photon energies of @xmath7 @xcite severely constrain possible signals from decaying dark matter . in this paper",
    "we study the implications of this data for the decays of the next - to - lightest superparticle ( nlsp ) at the lhc , extending the estimates in @xcite .",
    "we shall restrict our analysis to the simplest class of supergravity models with universal boundary conditions at the grand unification ( gut ) scale , which lead to neutralino or @xmath2-nlsp .",
    "electroweak precision tests , thermal leptogenesis and gravitino dark matter together allow gravitino and nlsp masses in the range @xmath8 and @xmath9 @xcite .",
    "following @xcite , the breaking of r - parity is tied to the breaking of lepton number , which leads to a model with bilinear r - parity breaking @xcite .",
