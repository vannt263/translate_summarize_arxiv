    "the result is in good agreement with the measurements of other experiments based on the observation of the e.m . and muon components at ground level .",
    "the muon energies detected in the present experiment are however about three orders of magnitude larger than in previous n@xmath120-n@xmath123 experiments , and therefore the parent pions are produced in a different kinematic region ( at the edges of the fragmentation region , rather than in the central one ) and in the first stages of the cascade development .",
