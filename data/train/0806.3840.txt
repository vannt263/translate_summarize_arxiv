    "vortices in becs of atoms with spin degrees of freedom ( spinor becs ) have been attracting increasing interest since the berkeley group  @xcite recently observed spontaneous creation of polar - core vortices in the magnetization of a spin-1 @xmath3 bec using a nondestructive spin - sensitive imaging technique  @xcite . in this experiment ,",
    "spin vortices are created through the dynamical instability arising from ferromagnetic interaction  @xcite .",
    "the mit group has generated mermin - ho and anderson - toulouse vortices in a spin-1 @xmath4 bec using the berry phase - imprinting method  @xcite .",
    "these spin - vortex states are predicted to be stable in a rotating potential  @xcite .",

    "we have proposed a method to create a spin - dependent optical potential using near - resonant circularly polarized laser beams . we have shown that spin vortices can be nucleated in a spinor bec using the spin - dependent rotating potential .",
    "we considered a situation in which only the @xmath106 component of the antiferromagnetic ground state of a spin-1 @xmath4 bec is stirred by the spin - dependent rotating potential , and found that half - quantum vortices enter the condensate .",
    "to our knowledge this is the first proposal for nucleating fractional vortices by a rotating stirrer .",
    "the spin - vortex nucleation occurs at a low rotation frequency ( @xmath177 ) , compared with vortex nucleation in a scalar bec ( @xmath178 ) .",
    "moreover , the spin vortices easily enter the condensate : the nucleation time is @xmath133 ms without dissipation . the spin vortices exit from the condensate , and the entry - exit cycles are repeated .",
    "we have also shown that the half - quantum vortices can be observed in a nondestructive manner using the method of the berkeley group .",
