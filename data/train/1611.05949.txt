    "<S> in this paper , based on a novel definition of the condition number , the expression of the relative condition number of equality constrained indefinite least squares problem is first presented when the data space is measured by a general product norm . </S>",
    "<S> then , by setting the specific norms and parameters , we obtain the explicit expressions of the normwise , mixed and componentwise condition numbers , and we also present two equivalent but simpler expressions of the normwise condition number . </S>",
    "<S> moreover , when the problem is structured , the corresponding structured relative condition numbers are also taken into consideration , and their expressions are given . to estimate these condition numbers effectively and reliably , </S>",
    "<S> the probabilistic spectral norm estimator and the small - sample statistical condition estimation method are applied and three related algorithms are devised . </S>",
    "<S> finally , the obtained results are illustrated by numerical experiments .    </S>",
