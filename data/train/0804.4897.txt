    "<S> we propose a new pattern of the neutrino mixing matrix which can be parametrized as the product of an arbitrary hermitian matrix and the well - known tri - bimaximal mixing matrix . in this scenario , </S>",
    "<S> nontrivial values of the smallest neutrino mixing angle @xmath0 and the cp - violating phases entirely arise from the non - unitary corrections . </S>",
    "<S> we present a complete set of series expansion formulas for neutrino oscillation probabilities both in vacuum and in matter of constant density . </S>",
    "<S> we do a numerical analysis to show the non - unitary effects on neutrino oscillations . </S>",
    "<S> the possibility of determining small non - unitary perturbations and cp - violating phases is discussed by measuring neutrino oscillation probabilities and constructing  deformed unitarity triangles \" . </S>",
    "<S> some brief comments on the non - unitary neutrino mixing matrix in the type - ii seesaw models are also given . </S>"
