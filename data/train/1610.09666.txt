    "<S> we define a new class of generating function transformations related to polylogarithm functions , dirichlet series , and euler sums . </S>",
    "<S> these transformations are given by an infinite sum over the @xmath0 derivatives of a sequence generating function and sets of generalized coefficients satisfying a non  triangular recurrence relation in two variables . </S>",
    "<S> the generalized transformation coefficients share a number of analogous properties with the stirling numbers of the second kind and the known harmonic number expansions of the unsigned stirling numbers of the first kind .    </S>",
    "<S> we prove a number of properties of the generalized coefficients which lead to new recurrence relations and summation identities for the @xmath1order harmonic number sequences . </S>",
    "<S> other applications of the generating function transformations we define in the article include new series expansions for the polylogarithm function , the alternating zeta function , and the fourier series for the periodic bernoulli polynomials . </S>",
    "<S> we conclude the article with a discussion of several specific new `` almost '' linear recurrence relations between the integer  </S>",
    "<S> order harmonic numbers and the generalized transformation coefficients , which provide new applications to studying the limiting behavior of the zeta function constants , @xmath2 , at integers @xmath3 . </S>"
