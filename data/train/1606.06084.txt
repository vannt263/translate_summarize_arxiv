    "<S> constructing a set of universal quantum gates is a fundamental task for quantum computation . </S>",
    "<S> the existence of noises , disturbances and fluctuations is unavoidable during the process of implementing quantum gates for most practical quantum systems . </S>",
    "<S> this paper employs a sampling - based learning method to find robust control pulses for generating a set of universal quantum gates . </S>",
    "<S> numerical results show that the learned robust control fields are insensitive to disturbances , uncertainties and fluctuations during the process of realizing universal quantum gates .    </S>",
    "<S> quantum information technology has witnessed rapid development in the last twenty years @xcite . an important task to implement quantum computation </S>",
    "<S> is the realization of quantum gates . </S>",
    "<S> it is well known that a suitable set of single - qubit and two - qubit quantum gates can accomplish universal quantum computation . </S>",
    "<S> a universal gate set may consist of a quantum phase gate ( s gate ) , a hadamard gate ( h gate ) , a @xmath0 gate ( t@xmath1 gate ) , and a cnot gate @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> realizing such a universal gate set is a fundamental objective in quantum computation .    in practical applications , </S>",
    "<S> it is inevitable that there exist different uncertainties , inaccuracies and disturbances in external fields , or system hamiltonians @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> many cases of unknown information and errors , such as imprecise hamiltonian modeling and inaccurate control pulses , can also be treated as uncertainties . </S>",
    "<S> hence , the requirement of a certain degree of robustness against possible uncertainties and noises has been recognized as one of the key properties for a reliable quantum information processor . </S>",
    "<S> several methods have been developed to enhance robustness and reliability in quantum information processing @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> feedback control theory @xcite , including measurement - based feedback and coherent feedback @xcite , has been developed to achieve improved performance of robustness in quantum manipulation problems . from the perspective of experimental implementation , open - loop control is usually more feasible and practical . </S>",
    "<S> dynamical decoupling @xcite and noise filtering @xcite have been developed for enhancing robustness performance in manipulating quantum states or quantum gates . </S>",
    "<S> optimal control methods such as sequential convex programming @xcite and gradient - based optimal algorithms ( e.g. , grape @xcite ) can also be used to design robust control fields for manipulating quantum systems .    in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we apply a learning - based open - loop control method @xcite to guide the design of robust control fields for construction of universal quantum gates . in particular , we aim to generate the set of universal quantum gates \\{s , h , t@xmath1 , cnot}.the results show that the learning control method can efficiently find optimal control fields and the designed control fields are insensitive to different fluctuations and uncertainties in the process of generating quantum gates . </S>",
    "<S> the quantum gates with the designed control fields can have improved robustness and reliability . </S>"
