    "in recent years , monolayer and few - layer graphene have attracted great attention due to the unique properties observed experimentally .",
    "@xcite many intriguing transport phenomena , such as ballistic transport at room temperature,@xcite the anomalous quantum hall effect , @xcite and novel many - body couplings @xcite have been reported .",
    "in addition to being a physical system exhibiting novel properties , graphene and graphene layers have been proposed as promising candidates for future nanoelectronics .",
    "the epitaxial graphene grown on sic is of particular interest due to the compatibility with current silicon technology .",
    "@xcite    besides their unusual electronic structure , @xcite vibrational properties and phonon spectra are also of fundamental interest from which many physical properties ( such as thermal conductivity and heat capacity ) can be derived .",
    "furthermore , phonons are crucial for studying the quasiparticle dynamics @xcite and electrical transport properties .",
    "electrons excited by optical methods can be scattered into another states by electron - phonon coupling ( epc ) .",
    "it has been suggested that the scattering between electrons and the optical phonon modes greatly affects the high - field ballistic transport properties in carbon nanotubes .",
    "@xcite in graphene and metallic single - wall nanotubes , the epc strongly affects the phonon frequencies , giving rise to kohn anomalies @xcite and possible soft modes or peierls distortions .",
    "@xcite    many experimental methods have been used to measure the phonon dispersions of graphite , such as inelastic neutron scattering ( ins ) , @xcite electron - energy loss spectroscopy ( eels),@xcite high - resolution electron energy - loss spectroscopy ( hreels),@xcite and inelastic x - ray scattering ( ixs ) .",
    "@xcite these measurements require large enough samples of crystalline quality and are limited to specific directions or phonon modes . more recently , mohr et al .",
    "@xcite have presented complete measurements of both the optical and the acoustic phonon modes along the directions @xmath1-k - m-@xmath1 of graphite using ixs .",
    "the results in these measurements are very close .",
    "in contrast to bulk graphite , raman scattering has been widely used for probing the g - band in graphene layers that corresponds to the @xmath1 phonons .",
    "@xcite recently reported raman spectra for graphene layers show that the intensity and position of the first - order g - band as well as the second order d - band ( historically named the g band ) are modified with an increasing number of layers .",

    "this effect is important for understanding the epc in multilayer graphene as well as the interpretation of the raman spectra .",

    "it has been shown that in few - layer graphene , the electronic dispersions near the fermi level exhibit various features depending on the stacking order .",
    "@xcite in this work , the vibrational properties of one- and few - layer graphenes are calculated using density - functional perturbation theory ( dfpt ) .",
    "@xcite the monolayer , bilayer ( ab stacking ) , and trilayer ( aba and abc stackings ) are considered in order to illustrate the effects of stacking order and interlayer coupling .",
