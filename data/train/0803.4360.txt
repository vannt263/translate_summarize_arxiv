    "fermion pairings are mechanisms for superconductivity and superfluidity . in the case of weak attractive interaction between fermions the pairings",
    "are well described in bardeen - cooper - schrieffer ( bcs ) theory @xcite , where fermion pairs are typically of a size much larger than the mean interparticle distance . in some sense",
    "the pairing can be regarded as taking place in momentum space . as the attractive interaction gets strong enough fermion pairs",
    "become real bosonic bound states . below a critical temperature",
    "macroscopically large number of these molecular bosons occupy the ground state and form a bose - einstein condensate ( bec ) .",
    "the idea of covering bec regime in an extended bcs theory was proposed by eagles @xcite and leggett @xcite .",
    "a quantitative description of the crossover at finite temperatures from weak to strong coupling regime was given by nozieres and schmitt - rink @xcite , who wrote down the universal pair wave function which can be reduced to the correct ground states at bcs and bec limit .",
    "the bcs - bec crossover have been extensively investigated in cold atom systems since such a crossover can be observed in experiments by tuning the magnetic field around feschbach resonance to achieve situations with different scattering length among fermionic atoms @xcite .",
    "recently there is a growing interest in extending the theory of the bcs - bec crossover to relativistic systems .",
    "the crossover from pion condensation to cooper pairing of quarks and antiquarks at large isospin densities provides such an example @xcite .",
    "another example is color superconductivity where quarks form cooper pairs on the fermi surface due to an attractive interaction mediated by gluon exchange @xcite ( for reviews , see , for instance , @xcite ) . because of asymptotic freedom ,",
    "color superconductivity at very large densities can be studied in a weak - coupling approach @xcite using perturbative qcd ( see , for example , @xcite ) . for moderate densities more phenomenological models such as the nambu - jona - lasinio ( njl ) model @xcite or self - consistent dyson - schwinger equations",
    "@xcite are needed .",
    "all these approaches usually assume bcs - like picture .",
    "when the coupling strength turns even stronger , the bec - like diquark pairing may set in .",
    "a lot of authors used the njl model to study the bcs - bec crossover in relativistic superconductors or superfluids @xcite . in our previous work we set up a theory for the crossover in relativistic superfluids which includes both bosonic and fermionic degrees of freedom @xcite .",
    "it is inspired by the boson - fermion model of superconductivity @xcite .",
    "we only addressed the mean - field approximation of the model .",
    "the crossover is realized by tuning the difference between the boson mass and boson chemical potential .    in this paper",
    "we extend our previous work @xcite by systematic incorporating the fluctuation effect in the crossover from propagating modes of the di - fermion field within cornwall - jackiw - tomboulis formalism ( cjt ) @xcite .",
    "our starting point is the boson - fermion model with a global u(1 ) symmetry .",
    "similar efforts have been made in the njl model @xcite .",
    "the cjt formalism has been employed to analyze fluctuation effects in color superconductors @xcite .",
    "the inclusion of fluctuation contributions leads to the change of the second order phase transition of normal to superfluid to a first order one .",

    "<S> we investigate the fluctuation effect of the di - fermion field in the crossover from bardeen - cooper - schrieffer ( bcs ) pairing to a bose - einstein condensate ( bec ) in a relativistic superfluid . </S>",
    "<S> we work within the boson - fermion model obeying a global u(1 ) symmetry . </S>",
    "<S> to go beyond the mean field approximation we use cornwall - jackiw - tomboulis ( cjt ) formalism to include higher order contributions . </S>",
    "<S> the quantum fluctuations of the pairing condensate is provided by bosons in non - zero modes , whose interaction with fermions gives the two - particle - irreducible ( 2pi ) effective potential . </S>",
    "<S> it changes the crossover property in the bec regime . with the fluctuations the superfluid phase transition becomes the first order in grand canonical ensemble . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the condensate , the critical temperature @xmath0 and particle abundances as functions of crossover parameter the boson mass . </S>"
