    "<S> we study a number of possible extensions of the ramanujan master theorem , which is formulated here by using methods of umbral nature . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the implications of the procedure for the theory of special functions , like the derivation of formulae concerning the integrals of products of families of bessel functions and the successive derivatives of bessel type functions . </S>",
    "<S> we stress also that the procedure we propose allows a unified treatment of many problems appearing in applications , which can formally be reduced to the evaluation of exponential- or gaussian - like integrals . </S>"
