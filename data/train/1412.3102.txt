    "<S> wireless network applications , such as , searching , routing , self stabilization and query processing can be modeled as random walks on graphs . </S>",
    "<S> stateless opportunistic routing technique is a robust distributed routing technique based on random walk approach , where nodes transfer the packets to one of their direct neighbors uniformly , until the packets reach their destinations . </S>",
    "<S> simplicity in execution , fault tolerance , low overhead and robustness to topology changes made it more suitable to wireless sensor networks scenarios . </S>",
    "<S> but the main disadvantage of stateless opportunistic routing is estimating and studying the effect of network parameters on the packet latency . in this work , we derived the analytical expressions for mean latency or average packet travel time for @xmath0-nearest neighbor cycle , @xmath0-nearest neighbor torus networks . </S>",
    "<S> further , we derived the generalized expression for mean latency for @xmath1-dimensional @xmath0-nearest neighbor torus networks and studied the effect of number of nodes , nearest neighbors and network dimension on average packet travel time .    </S>",
