    "<S> , the ground - state wavefunction can be systematically improved in a controllable way . </S>",

    "<S> the magnetization and the entanglement entropy are exactly calculated . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that the magnetic critical exponent @xmath0 is converged to @xmath1 in the whole sub - ohmic bath regime @xmath2 , compared with that by the exactly solvable generalized silbey - harris ansatz . </S>",
    "<S> it is strongly suggested that the system with sub - ohmic bath is always above its upper critical dimension , in sharp contrast with the previous findings . </S>",

    "<S> the spin - boson model  @xcite describes a qubit ( two - level system ) coupled with a dissipative environment represented by a continuous bath of bosonic modes . </S>",
