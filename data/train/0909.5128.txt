    "<S> we present predictions for the pion - photon transition form factor , derived with the help of light - cone sum rules and including the main part of the nnlo radiative corrections . </S>",
    "<S> we show that , when the bakulev - mikhailov - stefanis ( bms ) pion distribution amplitude is used , the obtained predictions agree well with the cello and the cleo data . </S>",
    "<S> we found that no model distribution amplitude can reproduce the observed @xmath0 growth of the new babar data , though the bms model complies with several babar data points . </S>"
