    "<S> we compute the two - loop qcd helicity amplitudes for the process @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> the amplitudes are extracted in a scheme - independent manner from the coefficients appearing in the general tensorial structure for this process . </S>",
    "<S> the tensor coefficients are derived from the feynman graph amplitudes by means of projectors , within the conventional dimensional regularization scheme . </S>",
    "<S> the actual calculation of the loop integrals is then performed by reducing all of them to a small set of known master integrals . </S>",
    "<S> the infrared pole structure of the renormalized helicity amplitudes agrees with the prediction made by catani using an infrared factorization formula . </S>",
    "<S> we use this formula to structure our results for the finite part into terms arising from the expansion of the pole coefficients and a genuine finite remainder , which is independent of the scheme used to define the helicity amplitudes . </S>",
    "<S> the analytic result for the finite parts of the amplitudes is expressed in terms of one- and two - dimensional harmonic polylogarithms .    </S>",
