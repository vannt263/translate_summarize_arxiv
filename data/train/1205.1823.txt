    "in network coding one is looking at the transmission of information through a directed graph with possibly several senders and several receivers @xcite .",
    "one can increase the throughput by linearly combining the information vectors at intermediate nodes of the network .",
    "if the underlying topology of the network is unknown we speak about _ random linear network coding_. since linear spaces are invariant under linear combinations , they are what is needed as codewords @xcite .",
