    "in the last few years , coupled systems have attracted considerable attention in research communities .",
    "stabilization of the cascade of pde systems was dealt with in @xcite .",
    "controller design for pde - ode cascade systems has been extensively studied for many types of coupling such as ode - reaction diffusion equation ( see e.g. @xcite ) , ode - wave equation ( see e.g. @xcite ) , and ode - schrdinger equation ( see e.g. @xcite ) . in order to stabilize the cascaded pde - ode systems ,",
    "the backstepping method has been applied in @xcite .",
    "the idea is to use a volterra integral transformation to transform the original system to a target system @xcite .",
    "stabilization for systems described by pdes subject to time delay has received much attention in recent years . an effective linear matrix inequality ( lmi )",
    "approach is proposed to analysis and design for time delay pde systems in @xcite . in @xcite ,",
    "based on the backstepping method , a control strategy for reaction - diffusion equations with state delay is proposed .    for practical application of backstepping controllers , in many cases the constraints on the control input should be taken into account .",
    "there have been some important results about pdes subject to distributed control constraints ( see e.g. @xcite ) .",
    "however , boundary control of pdes in the presence of actuator saturation has not been studied yet in the literature . in the present paper",
    "we introduce stabilizing backstepping - based boundary controllers for coupled heat - ode systems with state delays in the presence of actuator saturation .",
    "we first extend the backstepping method to the latter class of delayed system . by using lyapunov method for the target system , we find a bound on the domain of attraction of this system , and further on the domain of attraction of the original system .",
    "the structure of the paper is as follows . in the next section ,",
    "the problem statement is presented and the backstepping transformation is introduced .",
    "based on the backstepping method , a state feedback controller is constructed and the boundary controller to the original system is designed .",
    "section 3 is devoted to the existence and uniqueness of the solution for the closed - loop system with state delay . in section 4 ,",
    "delay - independent lmi conditions are presented for the stability analysis of the target system . in section 5",
    ", we design a controller under actuator saturation via lmis .",
    "we find an estimate on the set of initial conditions ( as large as we can get ) from which the state trajectories of the system are exponentially convergent to zero .",
    "an example with numerical simulation is presented in section 6 for illustration of the effectiveness of the method .",
    "some concluding remarks are represented in section 7 .",

    "<S> numerical examples illustrate the efficiency of the method .    </S>",
