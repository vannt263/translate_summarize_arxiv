    "<S> direct evaluation of the rate - distortion function has rarely been achieved when it is strictly greater than its shannon lower bound . in this paper , we consider the rate - distortion function for the distortion measure defined by an @xmath0-insensitive loss function . </S>",
    "<S> we first present the shannon lower bound applicable to any source distribution with finite differential entropy . </S>",
    "<S> then , focusing on the laplacian and gaussian sources , we prove that the rate - distortion functions of these sources are strictly greater than their shannon lower bounds and obtain analytically evaluable upper bounds for the rate - distortion functions . small distortion limit and numerical evaluation of the bounds suggest that the shannon lower bound provides a good approximation to the rate - distortion function for the @xmath0-insensitive distortion measure . </S>"
