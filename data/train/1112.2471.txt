    "multi - dimensional shift is an important and a highly active area of ongoing research in dynamical system .",

    "<S> this investigation studies topological mixing and strong specification of two - dimensional shifts of finite type . connecting operators </S>",
    "<S> are introduced to reduce the order of high - order transition matrices to yield lower - order transition matrices that are useful in establishing finitely checkable conditions for the primitivity of all transition matrices . </S>",
    "<S> two kinds of sufficient condition for the primitivity of transition matrices are provided ; ( i ) invariant diagonal cycles and ( ii ) primitive commutative cycles . </S>",
    "<S> after primitivity is established , the corner - extendability and crisscross - extendability are introduced to demonstrate topological mixing . </S>",
    "<S> in addition to these sufficient conditions for topological mixing , the hole - filling condition implies the strong specification . </S>",
    "<S> all mentioned conditions are finitely checkable .    </S>",
