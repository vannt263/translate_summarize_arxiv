    "dirac fermions emerge always in a pair : they emerge at the @xmath5 and @xmath6 points just as in graphene .",

    "namely , the gapless dirac fermion has @xmath111 berry phase while the gapped dirac fermion has @xmath112 chern number .",
    "they appear in a pair so that the total topological number must be zero .",
    "one the other hand , this is not the case for triplet fermions .",
