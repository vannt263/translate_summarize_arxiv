    "<S> in this paper , we exhibit two matrix representations of the rational roots of generalized fibonacci polynomials ( gfps ) under convolution product , in terms of determinants and permanents , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> the underlying root formulas for gfps and for weighted isobaric polynomials ( wips ) , which appeared in an earlier paper by machenry and tudose , make use of two types of operators . </S>",
    "<S> these operators are derived from the generating functions for stirling numbers of the first kind and second kind . </S>",
    "<S> hence we call them stirling operators . to construct matrix representations of the roots of gfps </S>",
    "<S> , we use the stirling operators of the first kind . </S>",
    "<S> we give explicit examples to show how the stirling operators of the second kind appear in the low degree cases for the wip - roots . as a consequence of the matrix construction , we have matrix representations of multiplicative arithmetic functions under the dirichlet product into its divisible closure .    weighted isobaric polynomials , generalized fibonacci polynomials , matrix representation , stirling operators , multiplicative arithmetic functions .    </S>",
