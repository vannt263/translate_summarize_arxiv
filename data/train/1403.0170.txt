    "<S> we extend the study of _ melonic _ quartic tensor models to models with arbitrary quartic interactions . </S>",
    "<S> this extension requires a new version of the loop vertex expansion using several species of intermediate fields and iterated cauchy - schwarz inequalities . </S>",
    "<S> borel summability is proven , uniformly as the tensor size @xmath0 becomes large . </S>",
    "<S> every cumulant is written as a sum of explicitly calculated terms plus a remainder , suppressed in @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> together with the existence of the large @xmath0 limit of the second cumulant , this proves that the corresponding sequence of probability measures is uniformly bounded and obeys the tensorial universality theorem . </S>"
