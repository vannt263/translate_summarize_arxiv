    "<S> this implies , in particular , that for each of its points the envelope of holomorphy contains an embedded ( non - singular ) riemann surface ( and also an immersed analytic disc ) passing through this point with boundary contained in the natural embedding of the original domain into its envelope of holomorphy . </S>",
