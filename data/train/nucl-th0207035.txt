    "<S> a computer program is presented which calculates the elastic and inelastic scattering in intermediate and high energy nuclear collisions . </S>",
    "<S> a coupled - channels method is used for coulomb and nuclear excitations of e1 , e2 , e3 , m1 , and m2 multipolarities , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> the program applies to an arbitrary nucleus , specified by the spins and energies of the levels and by reduced matrix elements . for given bombarding conditions , </S>",
    "<S> the angular distribution of elastic and inelastic scattered particles and angular distributions of gamma - rays from the excited nucleus are computed . </S>"
