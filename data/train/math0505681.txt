    "recently , real large - scale network data have been analyzed in various fields , and the corresponding random graphs have been studied .",

    "the underlying assumption of these growing networks is that a vertex with a certain fixed number of edges is added to the graph one by one at each discrete time step . to obtain a power law , the growth mechanism",
    "is usually supplied by so - called preferential attachment , which stipulates that each newly introduced edge is more likely to be connected to a vertex ( which already exists in the graph ) that has a larger degree . for a review of the studies on such models",
