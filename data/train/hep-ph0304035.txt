    "the search for the higgs boson will be a top priority of the cern large hadron collider ( lhc ) .",
    "the lhc s discovery potential for the standard model higgs boson fully covers the mass range from the experimental lower bound established by the lep experiments ( @xmath2gev ) up to @xmath3tev , beyond which the concept of the higgs boson as an elementary particle becomes questionable .",
    "in addition , the fermilab tevatron could find evidence for or even discover the higgs boson if @xmath4gev and if sufficient luminosity  @xcite can be collected .",
    "the theoretical description of the signal processes for standard model higgs boson production is under good control . for a review ,",
    "see  ref.@xcite .",
    "the dominant production mode is gluon fusion , for which the next - to - next - to - leading order ( nnlo ) corrections are now available @xcite and have recently been reconfirmed by ref.@xcite .",
    "the radiative corrections for the weak boson fusion channel  @xcite and associated production with a weak gauge boson  @xcite have been known for several years , rendering the theoretical uncertainty in these processes very small .",
    "recently , next - to - leading order ( nlo ) corrections have also been evaluated for higgs boson production in association with top quarks  @xcite , resulting in a drastic reduction of the scale uncertainty .",
    "these results can be used for supersymmetric higgs boson production as well . however , because of the enriched particle spectrum in supersymmetric extensions of the standard model , they provide only a part of the full production rate in general .",
    "additional contributions arise through intermediate supersymmetric partners  @xcite and modified couplings of the standard model particles . in order to avoid unnecessary generalizations",
    ", we will focus on the minimal supersymmetric standard model ( mssm ) for the rest of this paper ( see , e.g. , ref.@xcite for an outline of the mssm ) .",
    "the extent to which our results can be transferred to other models should be clear from this discussion .",
