    "let us briefly summarize the main points of our work .",
    "we have derived the planar domain wall solution with the time - dependent thickness in the polynomial approximation .",
    "the planar domain wall oscillates with small amplitude .",
    "we have then proposed an approach enabling us to calculate the correction to the pure polynomial solution .",
    "we have split this correction into two parts .",
    "the time - dependent part contains the component which is interpreted as the radiation from the oscillating domain wall .",
    "there are several possible extensions and generalizations of our approach .",
    "it can be applied without much trouble to the other field - theoretical models . in our paper",
    "we discuss only the case of small oscillations around the static solution .",
