    "<S> in this paper , we continue the program initiated by i. beck s now classical paper concerning zero - divisor graphs of commutative rings . after the success of much research regarding zero - divisor graphs , </S>",
    "<S> many authors have turned their attention to studying divisor graphs of non - zero elements in the ring , the so called irreducible divisor graph . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we construct several different associated irreducible divisor graphs of a commutative ring with unity using various choices for the definition of irreducible and atomic in the literature . </S>",
    "<S> we continue pursuing the program of exploiting the interaction between algebraic structures and associated graphs to further our understanding of both objects . </S>",
    "<S> factorization in rings with zero - divisors is considerably more complicated than integral domains ; however , we find that many of the same techniques can be extended to rings with zero - divisors . </S>",
    "<S> this allows us to not only find graph theoretic characterizations of many of the finite factorization properties that commutative rings may possess , but also understand graph theoretic properties of graphs associated with certain commutative rings satisfying nice factorization properties . </S>",
