    "considerable research has been stimulated by the unique physics of topological insulators ( tis ) @xcite , which have conducting states on their surfaces ( 3d ) or edges ( 2d ) when an insulating gap exists in the bulk .",
    "strong spin - orbit coupling enables 3d ti surfaces to support 2d spin - polarized massless dirac fermions with a linear dispersion , exhibiting exotic phenomena with no counterpart in conventional conductors , such as a quantized anomalous hall effect @xcite .",
    "ti surfaces proximity - coupled to a superconductor may support majorana fermions @xcite enabling topological quantum computing @xcite .",
    "coupled spin - charge transport in ti is researched with a view to spintronics applications @xcite , while tis are also seen as a platform for studying the interplay of interactions and spin - orbit coupling @xcite .    at present ,",
    "ti surface transport is obscured by bulk doping .",
    "one pathway towards reducing bulk transport has been to make tis thinner . in a ti thin film ( titf ) ,",
    "a layer pseudospin indexes the top and bottom surfaces . in films",
    "thinner than 6 quintuple layers @xcite tunneling is enabled between the surfaces , referred to as interlayer tunneling .",
    "this gives a mass in the dirac dispersion and , with time reversal symmetry preserved , the conduction and valence bands are each twofold degenerate .",
    "layer pseudospin dynamics can be tuned by modulating the tunneling parameter , for example by altering the film thickness , by strain and external electromagnetic fields .",
    "nontrivial surface states make titf physics different from metal and semiconductor films .",
    "furthermore , unlike bilayer graphene , titfs are gapped , scattering through @xmath9 is allowed , and the hamiltonian describes the real spin in a single - valley system . with the study of titf taking off @xcite , understanding the interplay of spin - orbit interaction , layer pseudospin and electron - electron interactions in these structures is critical for their future functionality .",
    "exciting predictions exist , such as topological exciton condensation @xcite and quantum hall superfluidity @xcite .",
    "the dielectric function is the first step in understanding interaction physics such as fermi - velocity renormalizations @xcite , fermi liquid properties , plasmons , the kohn anomaly @xcite , as well as spontaneous symmetry breaking such as ferromagnetism , which would enable seamless integration of spin and semiconducting technology .",
    "it also determines the screened impurity potentials responsible for electrical resistance at low temperatures . in this work , we calculate the titf dielectric function in the random phase approximation ( rpa ) , obtaining an analytic result .",
    "tunneling has a drastic effect on screening , which takes on a distinct form from that in any other known conductor .",

    "the cusp alters also the form of friedel oscillations @xcite , which can be studied using scanning tunneling microscopy @xcite .",
