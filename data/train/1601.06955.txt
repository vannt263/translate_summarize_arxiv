    "over the years people have been interested in rare event experiments like neutrinoless double beta decay@xcite and dark matter@xcite searches .",
    "liquid argon and xenon are considered to be good media for low rate tpc detectors such as icarus@xcite and lanndd@xcite .",

    "it is challenging to reduce the distance between two wires to a few hundreds microns or tens of microns for a large scale liquid argon or xenon detector@xcite .    on the other hand ,",
    "a direct charge cmos sensor with large pixel array is a good choice of readout for such low rate and large scale detectors , since cmos sensors offer small resolution and high granularity .",
