    "<S> we study the numerical approximation of integrals with respect to the standard gaussian measure for integrands which lie in certain hermite spaces of functions . </S>",
    "<S> the decay rate of the associated sequence is specified by a single parameter and determines the smoothness classes and for certain integer values of the parameter , the inner product can be expressed via @xmath0 norms of the derivatives of the function .    </S>",
    "<S> we map higher order digital nets from the unit cube to a suitable subcube of @xmath1 via a linear transformation and show that such rules achieve , apart from powers of @xmath2 , the optimal rate of convergence of the integration error . </S>",
    "<S> numerical examples illustrate the performance of these quadrature rules and show their power compared to other quadrature rules .    </S>",
