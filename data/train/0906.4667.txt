    "the most beautiful framework for physics beyond the standard model is the grand unified theory ( gut ) where the three gauge groups of the model are unified into a larger gauge group at a very high energy scale@xcite .",
    "the most important prediction of the grand unified theory is the unification of the gauge coupling constants at a very high energy scale .",
    "remarkably , the extrapolations of the three gauge coupling constants of the standard model to higher energies roughly suggest the unification of the gauge coupling constants .",
    "despite such a suggestion of the coupling unification , however , the precise measurements of the gauge coupling constants have revealed that three couplings do not coincide on one scale@xcite .",
    "another important prediction of the grand unified theory is the finite lifetime of the proton which decays via the interactions mediated by the heavy gauge bosons of the grand unified gauge theory .",

    "<S> we consider a small extension of the standard model by adding two majorana fermions ; those are adjoint representations of the @xmath0 and @xmath1 gauge groups of the standard model . in this extension , </S>",
    "<S> the gauge coupling unification at an energy scale higher than @xmath2gev is realized when the masses of the triplet and the octet fermions are smaller than @xmath3gev and @xmath4gev , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> we also show that an appropriate symmetry ensures a long lifetime of the neutral component of the triplet fermion whose thermal relic density naturally explains the observed dark matter density . </S>",
    "<S> the electron / positron excesses observed in recent cosmic - ray experiments can be also explained by the decay of the triplet fermion . </S>"
