    "<S> it s a common knowledge that the quasielastic neutrino - neutron and antineutrino - proton cross sections tend to the same constant as ( anti)neutrino energy becomes high . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the exact expression of the limit in terms of the parameters describing quasielastic scattering . </S>",
    "<S> we check that even at very high energies only small absolute values of the four - momentum transfer contribute to the cross section , hence the fermi theory can be applied . </S>",
    "<S> the dipole approximation of the form factors allows to perform analytic calculations . </S>",
    "<S> obtained results are neutrino - flavour independent . </S>"
