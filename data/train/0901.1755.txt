    "similar phenomena take place also in unconventional superconductors .",
    "the existence of the surface bound states has been observed in a variety of unconventional superconductors by tunneling spectroscopy experiments and in superfluid @xmath0he by transverse acoustic impedance measurements.@xcite    some anomalous superconducting properties due to the surface bound states have been predicted .",
    "the quasiparticle current carried by the surface bound states is so large that the meissner current can be paramagnetic.@xcite tanaka and kashiwaya@xcite have studied the effect on the charge transport in dirty normal metal / spin - triplet superconductor ( dn / ts ) junctions and found that the surface bound states cause an unusually small electric resistance of the junction .",
    "the meissner effect in such a dirty metal layer is also unusual.@xcite    the dn / ts junctions have recently attracted much attention from the aspect of a proximity - induced odd - frequency pair.@xcite in bulk superconductors , the pair function ( anomalous green s function ) is usually even in the matsubara frequency . in general , however , it can be an odd function ; superconducting and superfluid states with this property are referred to as odd - frequency pairing states . because of the fermi statistics , the spin and orbital symmetries of the odd - frequency pair are classified into spin - singlet odd - parity and spin - triplet even - parity , in contrast to the even - frequency pair",
    ". possibilities of the odd - frequency pair in bulk superconductors have been discussed in the context of the high-@xmath3 superconductors@xcite and more recently for ce - based compounds.@xcite in the dn layer of the dn / ts junction , there is a possibility that an odd - frequency spin - triplet superconductivity is generated by the proximity effect.@xcite    superfluid @xmath0he is the first material identified as a spin - triplet pairing state .",

    "this system is an ideal model system for studying the proximity effect in dirty normal fermi liquid / spin - triplet superfluid junctions when the impurity pair - breaking effect by the aerogel is strong enough to destroy the superfluidity of liquid @xmath0he .",
    "such a situation occurs at low pressures below the critical pressure@xcite or at temperatures higher than the reduced critical temperature of liquid @xmath0he in the aerogel .",

    "we used this method to calculate self - consistently the spatial variations of the @xmath1-wave order parameter and the impurity self - energy . to clarify the microscopic structure of the proximity - induced superfluidity",

    "although the order parameter in the above system is purely @xmath1-wave , any partial wave components of the pair function can coexist owing to broken translational symmetry .",

    "only the @xmath2-wave pair can penetrate deep into the aerogel layer .",
    "this is because non-@xmath2-wave pairs are destroyed by impurity scattering in the interior of the aerogel layer .",
    "as is expected qualitatively from the usadel equation and was confirmed by our fully self - consistent numerical calculations , the @xmath2-wave pair function extends over the whole aerogel layer when the frequency is of order or less than the thouless energy @xmath143 .",
    "the @xmath2-wave pair in the present system has odd - frequency symmetry and therefore the proximity - induced superfluidity in a thick aerogel layer is an example of the odd - frequency pairing states .",
    "we have found that the local density of states in the aerogel layer has a zero - energy peak of width of order @xmath143 , in agreement with previous theoretical works@xcite using the usadel equation .",
