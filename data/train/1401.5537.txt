    "<S> an overview is given of the construction of a differential polynomial ring of functions on the moduli space of calabi - yau threefolds . </S>",
    "<S> these rings coincide with the rings of quasi modular forms for geometries with duality groups for which these are known . </S>",
    "<S> they provide a generalization thereof otherwise . </S>",
    "<S> higher genus topological string amplitudes can be expressed in terms of the generators of this ring giving them a global description in the moduli space . </S>",
    "<S> an action of a duality exchanging large volume and conifold loci in moduli space is discussed . </S>",
    "<S> the connection to quasi modular forms is illustrated by the local @xmath0 geometry and its mirror , the generalization is extended to several compact geometries with one - dimensional moduli spaces . </S>"
