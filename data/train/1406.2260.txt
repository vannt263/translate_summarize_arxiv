    "<S> more precisely the solutions are approximated by their projections on finite dimensional subspaces spanned by the eigenvectors of @xmath1 .    </S>",
    "<S> this approximation is uniform in time and in the control @xmath3 , if this control has bounded variation with a priori bounded total variation . </S>",
