    "<S> high fidelity entanglement of an on - chip array of spin qubits poses many challenges . </S>",
    "<S> spin - orbit coupling ( soc ) can ease some of these challenges by enabling long - ranged entanglement via electric dipole - dipole interactions , microwave photons , or phonons . </S>",
    "<S> however , soc exposes conventional spin qubits to decoherence from electrical noise . </S>",
    "<S> here we propose an acceptor - based spin - orbit qubit in silicon offering long - range entanglement at a sweet spot where the qubit is protected from electrical noise . </S>",
    "<S> the qubit relies on quadrupolar soc with the interface and gate potentials . </S>",
