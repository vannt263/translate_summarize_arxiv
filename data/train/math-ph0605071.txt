    "<S> the newton algorithm is the most useful tool for solving non - linear equations . </S>",
    "<S> it consists of solving a series of linear system of equations ( jacobian system ) . in this article </S>",
    "<S> , we adaptively define the tolerance of the jacobian systems . </S>",
    "<S> numerical experiment shows that compared to the traditional method our approach can save a substantial amount of computational work . </S>",
    "<S> the presented algorithm can be easily incorporated in existing simulators . </S>"
