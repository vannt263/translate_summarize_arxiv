    "quantum computation and quantum information processing @xcite promise to outperform classical implementations for efficient algorithm , secure communication @xcite , and genuine teleportation of quantum states @xcite .",
    "this superiority of quantumness even envisions a quantum network or quantum internet @xcite which links various quantum systems to process tasks that are intractable in classical regimes .",
    "these quantum systems include , to name a few , photonic qubits , trapped ions , atomic ensembles , and solid state systems @xcite , in which unfortunately both strengths and weaknesses @xcite coexist .",
    "the fact that there is no prefect quantum system ( high efficiency , long coherence time , strong coupling , and scalability , for example ) demands a integrability of the well - controlled interfaces between these quantum systems .",
    "a good quantum interface @xcite involves an efficient generation , distribution , and storage of quantum information .",
    "one manifestation of these functionalities is long - distance quantum communication @xcite using cold atomic ensembles for a quantum repeater @xcite .",
    "the building blocks for this quantum repeater protocol therefore rely on a generation of light - matter entanglement @xcite and storage of it @xcite using @xmath0-type atomic configurations , making the atomic ensemble ( mostly alkali metals ) a good candidate for quantum network .",
    "however one drawback for the fiber - based quantum information transmission is the attenuation loss for @xmath1 line transitions in alkali metals .  to reach a minimal - loss optical fiber transmission , telecommunication ( telecom ) bandwidth from diamond - type atomic configurations @xcite serves the purpose and allows for an optimal operation in long - distance quantum communication .",
    "in addition to the advantage of the telecom bandwidth in fiber transmission , continuous frequency entanglement of cascade emissions from diamond - type atomic transitions can be generated @xcite and spectrally shaped @xcite to create a highly entangled biphoton source .",
    "this high communication capacity in continuous variables @xcite is also present in various degrees of freedom , for example of the transverse momentum @xcite , space @xcite , time @xcite , and orbital angular momenta of light @xcite .",
    "here we propose to generate a multiphoton source of cascaded cascade emissions from cold atomic ensembles .",
    "we take a two - photon source from quantum correlated infrared and telecom wavelength photons emitted from a diamond - type atomic configuration , as an initial seed for multiphoton generation in cascaded atomic ensembles .",

    "large - scale implementation of such cascaded scheme is feasible when preparing the atoms either in free - space , multiplexed setting @xcite , or optical lattices @xcite , but will suffer from the low generation rate .",
    "however this can be overcome by raising the repetition rates in excitations .",
    "finite spectral windows for collapsing the multiphoton spectral functions are not considered here .",
    "but we expect of no significant modifications in their spectral properties qualitatively , where essentially the finite spectral window of projection averages out the spectral distribution .",
    "a gate time of several hundreds of nanoseconds in the photon counting device would be enough to neglect the effect from finite detection windows .",
    "the other merit of the multiphoton source in the cascaded scheme is flexibility to entangle the photons either in signal or idler frequencies , making a repertoire of many possible entangled multiphoton sources .",

    "<S> we theoretically investigate the spectral entanglement of a multiphoton source generated by cascaded cascade emissions from cold atomic ensembles .  </S>",
    "<S> this highly directional photon source is guaranteed under the four - wave mixing condition and is also highly frequency - correlated due to finite driving pulse durations and enhanced superradiant decay constants .  </S>",
    "<S> we can manipulate its entropy of entanglement by controlling the driving parameters and superradiant decay rates , where schmidt decomposition is utilized to study the projected or collapsed multiphoton spectral functions .  </S>",
    "<S> this cascaded scheme enables a multiphoton source useful in quantum information processing , and potentially allows for applications in multimode quantum communication and for spectral shaping of high - dimensional continuous frequency entanglement . </S>"
