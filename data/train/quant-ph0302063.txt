    "<S> a framework to describe a broad class of physical operations ( including unitary transformations , dissipation , noise , and measurement ) in a quantum optics experiment is given . </S>",
    "<S> this framework provides a powerful tool for assessing the capabilities and limitations of performing quantum information processing tasks using current experimental techniques . </S>",
    "<S> the gottesman - knill theorem is generalized to the infinite - dimensional representations of the group stabilizer formalism and further generalized to include non - invertable semigroup transformations , providing a theorem for the efficient classical simulation of operations within this framework . as a result , </S>",
    "<S> we place powerful constraints on obtaining computational speedups using current techniques in quantum optics . </S>"
