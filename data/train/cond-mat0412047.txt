    "<S> theory of weak antilocalization is developed for high - mobility two - dimensional systems . </S>",
    "<S> spin - orbit interaction of rashba and dresselhaus types is taken into account . </S>",

    "<S> obtained expressions are valid for both ballistic and diffusive regimes of weak localization . proposed theory includes both backscattering and nonbackscattering contributions to the conductivity . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that magnetic field dependence of conductivity in high - mobility structures is not described by earlier theories . </S>"
