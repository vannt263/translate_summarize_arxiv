    "the role of qcd jets in high - energy nuclear collisions is currently strongly debated . in a high - energy physics ( hep )",
    "context dijet production has been long accepted as an important mechanism for hadron formation by parton fragmentation  @xcite .",
    "projectile - nucleon fragmentation ( dissociation ) is the other principal hadron production mechanism in elementary  collisions  @xcite .",
    "but in a heavy - ion context `` freezeout '' from a flowing bulk medium is assumed to be the nearly - exclusive hadron formation mechanism  @xcite .",
    "recent experimental results are interpreted to suggest that bulk - medium collectivity may even play a role in small , @xmath0-a and @xmath1-a systems  @xcite . in effect , previously - accepted contributions from minimum - bias ( mb ) dijets to high - energy nuclear collisions are displaced by the claimed presence of strong collective motion ( flows ) in a thermalized bulk medium or quark - gluon plasma to explain spectrum and correlation structure , in small as well as large collision systems .",
    "claims of a flowing bulk medium , quark - gluon plasma ( qgp ) or `` perfect liquid ''  @xcite are based in part on the _ a priori _ assumption that all hadrons with transverse momentum @xmath2 gev / c emerge from a thermalized bulk medium  @xcite , an interval that includes almost all final - state hadrons and more than 90% of minimum - bias ( mb ) jet fragments , consistent with direct jet measurements and qcd predictions  @xcite .",
    "differential analysis of spectra and correlations in the same systems appears to confirm a dominant role for dijet production  @xcite .",
    "resolution of the apparent contradiction requires establishment of a more comprehensive description of dijet manifestations in elementary  collisions that applies to all jet phenomenology ( yields , spectra and correlations ) over the complete hadron fragment momentum space down to the kinematic energy and momentum limits _ where most jet fragments should appear_.    in previous studies a jet - related _ hard component _ was isolated from the  spectrum of 200 gev  collisions by means of its charge multiplicity  dependence , leading to a two - component ( soft+hard ) spectrum model ( tcm )  @xcite .",
    "a similar differential analysis of  spectra revealed that the spectrum hard component persists for all centralities , although the form changes quantitatively in more - central collisions  @xcite .",
    "the spectrum hard components for  and  have been described quantitatively by a pqcd calculation  @xcite based on a mb parton spectrum  @xcite and measured parton fragmentation functions ( ffs )  @xcite .",
    "those results strongly suggest that ( a ) the spectrum and angular - correlation hard components in  collisions and  collisions for all centralities are jet related  @xcite , ( b ) one third of final - state hadrons in 200 gev central  collisions are included within intact mb jets ( minijets )  @xcite and ( c ) dijet production in  collisions increases as @xmath3 , a trend inconsistent with the eikonal approximation  @xcite .",
    "the present study extends that program by introducing a method to predict two - dimensional ( 2d ) trigger - associated ( ta ) hadron correlations arising from dijets produced in high - energy  collisions based on measured ffs and a large - angle - scattered parton spectrum .",
    "comparisons with measured ta correlations may establish the kinematic lower limits on dijet energy and fragment momentum .",
    "identification of unique dijet contributions to ta correlations in  collisions may then test claims of novel nonjet physics ( bulk collectivity ) in , @xmath1-a and heavy ion (  ) collisions at the relativistic heavy - ion collider ( rhic ) and the large hadron collider ( lhc ) .",
    "the 1d  spectrum tcm describes a marginal projection of 2d ta correlations formed by pairing the highest - momentum hadron in each event ( trigger particle ) with any other hadron ( associated particle )  @xcite .",
    "some fraction of those pairs ( the ta hard component , possibly jet related ) can be isolated by subtracting a ta tcm soft - component model  @xcite .",
    "the purpose of the present study is prediction of the 2d ta hard component obtained from  data using measured ffs and a mb jet spectrum to confirm a dijet mechanism for the hard component .",
    "the method consists in relating the qcd parton - fragment system to the hadronic trigger - associated system via the probability chain rule and bayes theorem .",
