    "<S> time dependent entropy of harmonic oscillator with time dependent mass and frequency are investigated . </S>",
    "<S> the joint entropy so called leipnik s entropy is calculated by using time dependent wave function obtained by the feynman path integral method . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that , leipnik s entropy fluctuates with time . </S>",
    "<S> however in constant mass and time dependent frequency case , entropy increases monotonically with time .    </S>",
    "<S> keywords : path integral , joint entropy , harmonic oscillator with time dependent mass and frequency . </S>"
