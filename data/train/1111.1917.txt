    "<S> focusing on the weakly coupled free fermionic string ( wcffhs ) formalism , we present systematic extensions of a variation on the nahe ( nanopoulos , antoniadis , hagelin , ellis ) set of basis vectors . </S>",
    "<S> this variation is more conducive to the production of  mirrored \" models , in which the observable and hidden sector gauge groups ( and possibly matter content ) are identical . </S>",
    "<S> this study is parallel to the extensions of the nahe set itself @xcite , and presents statistics related to similar model properties . </S>",
    "<S> statistical coupling between specific gauge groups and spacetime supersymmetry ( st susy ) is also examined . </S>",
    "<S> finally , a model with completely mirrored gauge groups is discussed . </S>"
