    "this phenomenon corresponds to the scattering process of an incoming electron from the normal side being reflected as a hole , thereby creating a cooper pair in the condensate . in a tunnel junction of a normal metal and a ( 110 ) oriented @xmath0-wave sc",

    ", we have studied the quantum - interference effect caused by the crossed andreev reflection in a hybrid nanostructure made up of a @xmath0-wave sc and two quantum wires .",
    "when the ( 110 ) oriented surface of sc is in contact with the wires via tunnel barriers , the andreev bound states are formed at low energies due to the crossed andreev reflection , and two sharp resonance peaks appear in the conductance well below the superconducting gap structure .",
    "the lower and higher conductance peaks correspond to the bonding and the antibonding andreev bound states of the hole wave functions .",
    "the result predicted here is possibly observable in a @xmath0-wave sc with conducting molecular wires , self - organized nanowires , or a stm tip with two atomic point contacts ."
