    "<S> we present a causal trajectory interpretation for the massive vector field , based on the flows of rest energy and a conserved density defined using the time - like eigenvectors and eigenvalues of the stress - energy - momentum tensor . </S>",
    "<S> this work extends our previous work which used a similar procedure for the scalar field . </S>",
    "<S> the massive , spin - one , complex vector field is discussed in detail and solutions are classified using the pauli - lubanski spin vector . </S>",
    "<S> the flows of energy - momentum are illustrated in a simple example of standing waves in a plane . </S>"
