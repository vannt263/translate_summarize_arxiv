    "<S> we consider fractional order optimal control problems in which the dynamic control system involves integer and fractional order derivatives and the terminal time is free . </S>",
    "<S> necessary conditions for a state / control / terminal - time triplet to be optimal are obtained . </S>",
    "<S> situations with constraints present at the end time are also considered . under appropriate assumptions </S>",
    "<S> , it is shown that the obtained necessary optimality conditions become sufficient . </S>",
    "<S> numerical methods to solve the problems are presented , and some computational simulations are discussed in detail .    </S>",
