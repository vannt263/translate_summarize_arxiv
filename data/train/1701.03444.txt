    "<S> this paper contains an error analysis of two randomized explicit runge - kutta schemes for ordinary differential equations ( odes ) with time - irregular coefficient functions . in particular , the methods are applicable to odes of carathodory type , whose coefficient functions are only integrable with respect to the time variable but are not assumed to be continuous . </S>",
    "<S> a further field of application are odes with coefficient functions that contain weak singularities with respect to the time variable .    </S>",
    "<S> the main result consists of precise bounds for the discretization error with respect to the @xmath0-norm . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , convergence rates are also derived in the almost sure sense . an important ingredient in the analysis </S>",
    "<S> are corresponding error bounds for the randomized riemann sum quadrature rule . </S>",
    "<S> the theoretical results are illustrated through a few numerical experiments . </S>"
