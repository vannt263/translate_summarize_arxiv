    "<S> the functional renormalization group equation is expanded to a two - loop form . </S>",
    "<S> this two - loop form equation involves one - loop effective action . </S>",
    "<S> an intermediate effective action perspective is adopted toward the one - loop effective action . </S>",
    "<S> that is to say , the intermediate effective action could not be of the same form of the bare action and one can make an ansatz to it . </S>",
    "<S> thus by focusing on different high dimensional operators , effects of the chosen operators can be investigated . </S>",
    "<S> qcd through intermediate fermion-4 interactions is investigated . </S>",
    "<S> of the 6 kinds of fermion-4 interactions generated by one - loop qcd , 4 kinds generate fermion mass while the other 2 kinds degenerate it . </S>",
    "<S> the net effect is fermion mass degeneration when dimensionless mass is large . </S>",
    "<S> flow patterns on the @xmath0 plane are drawn . </S>"
