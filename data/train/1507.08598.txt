    "<S> we utilize undetermined coefficient method and an iterative method to construct the series solutions of the 3d cauchy problem for a class of incompressible navier - stokes and euler equations . </S>",
    "<S> then we can turn the navier - stokes equations ( euler equations ) into the cauchy problem for finitely ( infinitely ) many ordinary differential equations . </S>",
    "<S> we get the finite series solution of the navier - stokes equations . by using some combinatorial identities techniques , we prove that the sum of the solutions to these ordinary differential equations is an infinite series solution of the euler equations in some cases . </S>"
