    "<S> the dynamics of short intense electromagnetic pulses propagating in a relativistic pair plasma with small temperature asymmetry is of current interest . </S>",
    "<S> such pulses were shown to be governed by a nonlinear schrodinger equation with a new type of focusing - defocusing nonlinearity . </S>",
    "<S> we provide an existence theory and numerical analysis for a special class of solutions , known as `` ring - profiled '' optical vortex solitons . </S>",
    "<S> our methods include both a direct and constrained minimization . </S>",
    "<S> we give a necessary condition for the existence of nontrivial solutions and a series of interesting estimates in terms of the relevant parameters describing the electromagnetic pulse . </S>",
    "<S> additionally , we use the constrained minimization approach and a finite element formalism to numerically study the behavior of the profiles of soliton solutions . </S>"
