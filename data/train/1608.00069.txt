    "the study of medium modifications of properties of hadrons is a topic of research which has attracted a lot of attention in recent years in strong interaction physics , in particular because of its relevance to the heavy ion collision experiments .",

    "in the present paper , we have investigated the partial decay widths of @xmath4 within a field theoretical model of composite hadrons .",
    "the medium modifications of these decay widths arise from the medium changes of the masses of the bottomonium and open bottom mesons .",
    "the masses of the @xmath1 and @xmath2 mesons , calculated in an effective chiral model , arise due to their interactions with the baryons and the scalar mesons . on the other hand ,",
    "the mass modifications of the bottomonium states in the hadronic medium are due to the medium modification of a scalar dilaton field , which is introduced in the effective hadronic model to simulate the gluon condensates of qcd .",
    "the effects of isospin asymmetry , strangeness on the bottomonium decay widths for decays @xmath189 , @xmath97 , have also been studied in the present work .",
    "the density effects are seen to be the dominant medium effects which should have observable consequences from the dense hadronic matter created in heavy ion collision experiments .",
    "the decay width of @xmath16 is observed to show nodes at specific densities , similar to what was observed for the partial decay widths of the excited charmonium states to @xmath8 pair within a @xmath10 model , as well as , within a field theoretical model of composite hadrons with quark ( antiquark ) constitutents .",
    "the isospin asymmetry effects are observed to be quite pronounced at high densities , leading to quite different values for the decay widths of @xmath186 and @xmath183 .",
    "these isospin asymmetry effects at high densities on these partial decay widths should show in the asymmetric heavy ion collision experiments in cbm planned at fair .",
