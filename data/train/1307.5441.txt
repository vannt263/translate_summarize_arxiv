    "<S> we present a class of confining potentials which allow one to reduce the one - dimensional schrdinger equation to a named equation of mathematical physics , namely either bessel s or whittaker s differential equation . in all cases , </S>",
    "<S> we provide closed form expressions for both the symmetric and antisymmetric wavefunction solutions , each along with an associated transcendental equation for allowed eigenvalues . </S>",
    "<S> the class of potentials considered contains an example of both cusp - like single wells and a double - well . </S>"
