    "<S> using 3d - mhd eulerian - grid numerical simulations , we study the formation and evolution of rising magnetic towers propagating into an ambient medium . the towers are generated from a localized injection of pure magnetic energy . </S>",
    "<S> no rotation is imposed on the plasma . </S>",
    "<S> we compare the evolution of a radiatively cooling tower with an adiabatic one , and find that both bend due to pinch instabilities . </S>",
    "<S> collimation is stronger in the radiative cooling case ; the adiabatic tower tends to expand radially . </S>",
    "<S> structural similarities are found between these towers and the millimeter scale magnetic towers produced in laboratory experiments . </S>"
