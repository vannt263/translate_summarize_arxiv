    "<S> the relation is implemented through the modified commutation relation @xmath0=i\\hbar[(1+\\beta\\hat{p}^2)\\delta_{ij } + \\beta'\\hat{p}_i\\hat{p}_j]$ ] . </S>",
    "<S> we point out that this relation , which is an example of an uv / ir relation , implies the finiteness of the cosmological constant . </S>",
    "<S> while our result does not solve the cosmological constant problem , it does shed new light on the relation between this outstanding problem and uv / ir correspondence . </S>",
    "<S> we also point out that the blackbody radiation spectrum will be modified at higher frequencies , but the effect is too small to be observed in the cosmic microwave background spectrum . </S>"
