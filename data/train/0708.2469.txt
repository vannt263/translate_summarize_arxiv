    "<S> in addition to the boosted static solution there are two other classes of stationary string - like solutions of the vacuum einstein equation in ( 4 + 1)-dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> each class is characterized by three parameters of mass , tension , and momentum flow along the fifth coordinate . </S>",
    "<S> we analyze the metric properties of one of the two classes , which was previously assumed to be naked singular , and show that the solution spectrum contains black string and wormhole in addition to the known naked singularity as the momentum flow to mass ratio increases . </S>",
    "<S> interestingly , there does not exist new zero momentum solution in these cases . </S>"
