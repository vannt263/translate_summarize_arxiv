    "<S> two - photon states entangled in continuous variables such as wavevector or frequency represent a powerful resource for quantum information protocols in higher - dimensional hilbert spaces . at the same time </S>",
    "<S> , there is a problem of addressing separately the corresponding schmidt modes . </S>",
    "<S> we propose a method of engineering two - photon spectral amplitude in such a way that it contains several non - overlapping schmidt modes , each of which can be filtered losslessly . </S>",
    "<S> the method is based on spontaneous parametric down - conversion ( spdc ) pumped by radiation with a comb - like spectrum . </S>",
    "<S> there are many ways of producing such a spectrum ; here we consider the simplest one , namely passing the pump beam through a fabry - perot interferometer . </S>",
    "<S> for the two - photon spectral amplitude ( tpsa ) to consist of non - overlapping schmidt modes , the crystal dispersion dependence , the length of the crystal , the fabry - perot free spectral range and its finesse should satisfy certain conditions . </S>",
    "<S> we experimentally demonstrate the control of tpsa through these parameters . </S>",
    "<S> we also discuss a possibility to realize a similar situation using cavity - based spdc .    </S>",
    "<S> _ introduction . </S>",
    "<S> _ entanglement of two - photon states ( biphotons ) in continuous variables such as frequency or wavevector suggests the use of biphotons as a quantum - information resource in higher - dimensional hilbert spaces  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the dimensionality of the hilbert space is determined in this case by the schmidt number , the effective number of schmidt modes , which can reach several hundred  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> but in order to realize any protocols with multimode states , it is necessary to address single schmidt modes separately . for wavevector variables , </S>",
    "<S> any single schmidt mode can be filtered out using a single - mode fibre and a spatial light modulator  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> this filtering , in principle , can be lossless , which is crucial for experiments with twin - beam squeezing  @xcite . for frequency variables </S>",
    "<S> , it is far more difficult to losslessly select a single schmidt mode . </S>",
    "<S> attempts are being made in homodyne detection  @xcite ; in direct detection experiments the procedure is more difficult . </S>",
    "<S> for instance , methods based on nonlinear frequency conversion are proposed , technically complicated to realize with high efficiency  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> here we propose and demonstrate a method of engineering a two - photon state in such a way that it contains _ non - overlapping _ </S>",
