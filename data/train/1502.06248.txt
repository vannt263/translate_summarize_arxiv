    "<S> the paper is devoted to mellin convolution operators with meromorphic kernels in bessel potential spaces . </S>",
    "<S> we encounter such operators while investigating boundary value problems for elliptic equations in planar 2d domains with angular points on the boundary .    </S>",
    "<S> our study is based upon two results . </S>",
    "<S> the first concerns commutants of mellin convolution and bessel potential operators : bessel potentials alter essentially after commutation with mellin convolutions depending on the poles of the kernel ( in contrast to commutants with fourier convolution operatiors . ) </S>",
    "<S> the second basic ingredient is the results on the banach algebra @xmath0 generated by mellin convolution and fourier convolution operators in weighted @xmath1-spaces obtained by the author in 1970 s and 1980 s . </S>",
    "<S> these results are modified by adding hankel operators . </S>",
    "<S> examples of mellin convolution operators are considered .    the first version of the paper was published in _ memoirs on differential equations and mathematical physics _ * 60 * , 135 - 177 , 2013 . </S>",
    "<S> the formulations and proofs there contain fatal errors , which are improved in the present preprint . </S>",
