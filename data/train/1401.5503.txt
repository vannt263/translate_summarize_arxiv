    "<S> we discuss the dynamics of the two - photon resonant ionization of helium involving two autoionizing states in the presence of two - color laser fields . </S>",
    "<S> the first source is tuned around the transition from the ground state to the 2s2p @xmath0 autoionizing state , and the second couples the state 2s2p @xmath0 to the @xmath1  @xmath2 autoionizing state . </S>",
    "<S> the laser coupling between the doubly excited states is shown to lead to modifications of the beutler - fano profile and the appearance of an autler - townes doublet . </S>",
    "<S> this double resonance effect between autoionizing states can be observed at moderate laser intensities easily attainable by currently operated sources . </S>"
