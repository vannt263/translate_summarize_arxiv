    "<S> the mathematical properties of a family of generalized beta distribution , including beta - normal , skewed - t , log - f , beta - exponential , beta - weibull distributions have recently been studied in several publications . </S>",
    "<S> this paper applies these distributions to the modeling of the size distribution of income and computes the maximum likelihood estimation estimates of parameters . </S>",
    "<S> their performances are compared to the widely used generalized beta distributions of the first and second types in terms of measures of goodness of fit . </S>"
