    "<S> we study the motion of charged brownian particles in an external magnetic field . </S>",
    "<S> it is found that a correlation appears between the components of particle velocity in the case of anisotropic friction , approaching asymptotically zero in the stationary limit . </S>",
    "<S> if magnetic field is smaller compared to the critical value , determined by frictional anisotropy , the relaxation of the correlation is non - oscillating in time . </S>",
    "<S> however , in a larger magnetic field this relaxation becomes oscillating . </S>",
