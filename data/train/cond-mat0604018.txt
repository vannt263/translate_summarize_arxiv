    "<S> shot noise has been used to measure quasiparticle charge in strongly correlated systems , including the fractional quantum hall regime@xcite and normal - superconductor interfaces@xcite , and to investigate regimes where coulomb interactions are strong , including coupled localized states in mesoscopic tunnel junctions  @xcite and quantum dots in the sequential tunneling  @xcite and cotunneling  @xcite regimes . </S>",

    "<S> most noise measurements have investigated either noise auto - correlation@xcite or cross - correlation of noise in a common current@xcite , with only a few experiments@xcite investigating cross - correlation between two distinct currents . </S>",
