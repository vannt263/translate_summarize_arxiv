    "<S> helton and vinnikov showed that every rigidly convex curve in the real plane bounds a spectrahedron . </S>",
    "<S> this leads to the computational problem of explicitly producing a symmetric ( positive definite ) linear determinantal representation for a given curve . </S>",
    "<S> we study three approaches to this problem : an algebraic approach via solving polynomial equations , a geometric approach via contact curves , and an analytic approach via theta functions . </S>",
    "<S> these are explained , compared , and tested experimentally for low degree instances . </S>"
