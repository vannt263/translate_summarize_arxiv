    "<S> we study ground state properties of the double - exchange model on triangle chain in the classical limit on @xmath0 spins . </S>",
    "<S> the ground state is determined by a competition among the kinetic energy of the @xmath1 electron , the antiferromagnetic exchange energy between the @xmath0 spins , and frustration due to a geometric structure of the lattice . </S>",
    "<S> the phase diagrams are obtained numerically for two kinds of the models which differ only in the transfer integral being real or complex . </S>",
    "<S> the properties of the states are understood from the viewpoint of the spin - induced peierls instability . </S>",
    "<S> the results suggest the existence of a chiral glass phase which is characterized by a local spin chirality and a continuous degeneracy . </S>"
