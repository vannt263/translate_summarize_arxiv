    "<S> experimental limits on the violation of four - dimensional lorentz invariance imply that noncommutativity among ordinary spacetime dimensions must be small . in this talk </S>",
    "<S> , i review the most stringent bounds on noncommutative field theories and suggest a possible means of evading them : noncommutativity may be restricted to extra , compactified spatial dimensions . </S>",
    "<S> such theories have a number of interesting features , including abelian gauge fields whose kaluza - klein excitations have self couplings . </S>",
    "<S> we consider six - dimensional qed in a noncommutative bulk , and discuss the collider signatures of the model . </S>"
