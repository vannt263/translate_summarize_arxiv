    "<S> we reexamine the one - dimensional spin-1 @xmath0 model with on - site uniaxial single - ion anisotropy as to the appearance and characterization of the symmetry - protected topological haldane phase . by means of large - scale density - matrix renormalization group ( dmrg ) calculations </S>",
    "<S> the central charge can be determined numerically via the von neumann entropy , from which the ground - sate phase diagram of the model can be derived with high precision . </S>",
    "<S> the nontrivial gapped haldane phase shows up in between the trivial gapped even haldane and nel phases , appearing at large single - ion and spin  exchange interaction anisotropies , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> we furthermore carve out a characteristic degeneracy of the lowest entanglement level in the topological haldane phase , which is determined using a conventional finite - system dmrg technique with both periodic and open boundary conditions . defining the spin and neutral gaps in analogy to the single - particle and neutral gaps in the </S>",
    "<S> intimately connected extended bose - hubbard model , we show that the excitation gaps in the spin model qualitatively behave just as for the bosonic system . </S>",
    "<S> we finally compute the dynamical spin structure factor in the three different gapped phases and find significant differences in the intensity maximum which might be used to distinguish these phases experimentally . </S>"
