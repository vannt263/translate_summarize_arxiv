    "<S> in this note we clarify the relationship between the local and global definitions of dual pairs in poisson geometry . </S>",
    "<S> it turns out that these are not equivalent . for the passage from local to global </S>",
    "<S> one needs a connected fiber hypothesis ( this is well known ) , while the converse requires a dimension condition ( which appears not to be known ) . </S>",
    "<S> we also provide examples illustrating the necessity of the extra conditions . </S>"
