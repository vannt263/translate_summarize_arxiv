    "in recent years , a number of papers have considered the feedback control of systems whose dynamics are governed by the laws of quantum mechanics instead of classical mechanics ; see e.g. , @xcite .",
    "also , an important class of quantum system models are linear quantum stochastic systems which describe quantum optical devices such as optical cavities , linear quantum amplifiers , and finite bandwidth squeezers ; e. g. see @xcite . for such linear quantum system models an important class of quantum control problems are referred to as coherent control problems ; e.g. , see @xcite .",

    "the controller itself is required to be a quantum system .",
