    "<S> we present a new set of kinematic equations for front motion in bistable media . </S>",
    "<S> the equations extend earlier kinematic approaches by coupling the front curvature with the order parameter associated with a parity breaking front bifurcation . </S>",
    "<S> in addition to naturally describing the core region of rotating spiral waves the equations can be be used to study the nucleation of spiral - wave pairs along uniformly propagating fronts . </S>",
    "<S> the analysis of spiral - wave nucleation reduces to the simpler problem of droplet , or domain , nucleation in one space dimension . </S>"
