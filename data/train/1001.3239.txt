    "graphene is a single sheet of carbon atoms tightly packed into a two - dimensional ( 2d ) honeycomb lattice .",
    "this material has recently become available for experimental study  @xcite , and its exotic electronic properties are attracting a lot of interest  @xcite .",
    "in particular , the conical shape of conduction and valence bands , together with the absence of a gap , near the @xmath1 and @xmath2 points in the brillouin zone renders graphene an intriguing type of quasi - relativistic condensed - matter system  @xcite .",
    "recent experiments have verified that the band dispersion of charge carriers in graphene is indeed linear as expected for massless dirac fermions  @xcite .",
    "a multitude of interesting physical effects arising in single - layer and bilayer graphene samples have been discussed  @xcite , both theoretically and experimentally .    in this work ,",
    "we focus on the ac electric transport properties of graphene , which have been the subject of numerous theoretical ( mostly numerical ) studies  @xcite and several recent experiments  @xcite",
    ". measurements of quantities related to the optical conductivity are expected to give deeper insight into the electronic properties of graphene samples , making it possible to infer details of their morphology  @xcite and suitability for applications .",
    "it is thus important to obtain a clear understanding of the features exhibited in the frequency - dependent conductivity , in particular , their relation to microscopic parameters and behavior at finite temperature @xmath3 .",
    "furthermore , it is advantageous to have mathematical expressions available that can be straightforwardly used for comparison with data .",
    "this is the motivation for our study .",
    "we have developed a formalism that lends itself for generalization to many situations , in particular , the treatment of inelastic scattering .",
    "extrapolation to zero frequency will enable us to discuss the dc conductivity , shedding new light on the phenomenon of minimal conductivity in graphene .",
    "the full range of our results will be presented elsewhere ; here we focus on discussing the method and present selected results .",

    "<S> we are focusing especially on the interband contribution to the optical conductivity @xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> analytical results are obtained for a variety of situations , which allow clear identification of features in @xmath0 that are associated with relevant electronic energy scales . </S>",
    "<S> our work extends previous numerical studies and elucidates ways to infer electronic properties of graphene samples from optical - conductivity measurements .    </S>",
