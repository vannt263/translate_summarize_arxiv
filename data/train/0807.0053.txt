    "<S> a new computation method of frequentist @xmath0-values and bayesian posterior probabilities based on the bootstrap probability is discussed for the multivariate normal model with unknown expectation parameter vector . </S>",
    "<S> the null hypothesis is represented as an arbitrary - shaped region . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce new parametric models for the scaling - law of bootstrap probability so that the multiscale bootstrap method , which was designed for one - sided test , can also computes confidence measures of two - sided test , extending applicability to a wider class of hypotheses . </S>",
    "<S> parameter estimation is improved by the two - step multiscale bootstrap and also by including higher - order terms . </S>",
    "<S> model selection is important not only as a motivating application of our method , but also as an essential ingredient in the method . </S>",
    "<S> a compromise between frequentist and bayesian is attempted by showing that the bayesian posterior probability with an noninformative prior is interpreted as a frequentist @xmath0-value of `` zero - sided '' test . </S>"
