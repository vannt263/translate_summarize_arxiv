    "<S> we show this condition is weaker than the collet - eckmann condition , and than the summability condition with exponent  @xmath0 .    </S>",
    "<S> our main result is a connecting lemma for backward contracting rational maps , roughly saying that we can perturb a rational map to connect each critical orbit in the julia set with an orbit that does not accumulate on critical points . </S>",
    "<S> the proof of this result is based on thurston s algorithm and some rigidity properties of quasi - conformal maps . </S>",
    "<S> we also prove that the lebesgue measure of the julia set of a backward contracting rational map is zero , when it is not the whole riemann sphere . </S>",
