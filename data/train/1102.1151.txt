    "<S> we study poisson - noise induced switching between coexisting vibrational states in driven nonlinear micromechanical resonators . </S>",
    "<S> in contrast to gaussian noise induced switching , the measured logarithm of the switching rate is proportional not to the reciprocal noise intensity , but to its logarithm , for fixed pulse area . </S>",
    "<S> we also find that the switching rate logarithm varies as a square root of the distance to the bifurcation point , instead of the conventional scaling with exponent 3/2 . </S>"
