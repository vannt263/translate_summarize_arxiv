    "<S> unlike cuprate or iron - based superconductors , mgb@xmath0 is indisputably a phonon - mediated high temperature superconductor . </S>",
    "<S> the emergence of high temperature superconductivity in this material results from the strong coupling between the boron @xmath1-bonding electrons around the fermi level and the bond - stretching optical phonon modes . </S>",

    "<S> a @xmath1-bond in graphite or other materials is a strong covalent bond of a spin singlet pair formed by two electrons with opposite spins . in </S>",
