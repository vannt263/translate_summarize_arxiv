    "this is because the underlying physics is non - trivial and the models are manageable technically despite their essentially nonperturbative nature .",

    "they are both boundary integrable quantum field theories with a quantum - group spin at the boundary which takes values in standard or cyclic representations of the quantum group @xmath0.@xcite    the akm is related to the spin - boson ( sb ) model .",
    "@xcite the bsg model is equivalent to the schmid model , which describes a damped particle in a tilted cosine potential  @xcite and has a great variety of applications to charge and particle transport including josephson junction dynamics .",
