    "the search for quantum spin liquid states in frustrated magnets has long been of great interest for condensed matter physicists .",
    "these exotic states exhibit fractionalized quantum numbers and may play important roles in understanding properties of novel complex materials such as high-@xmath2 cuprates . in the spin liquid states , there exist deconfined spinons with spin quantum number @xmath3 , as opposed to the more familiar magnon excitations in the nel state , or the spin triplet @xmath4 excitations in the paramagnetic spin - peierls states.@xcite previous studies of frustrated heisenberg models on two dimensional lattices have revealed possible quantum spin liquid phases corresponding to the deconfined phases of @xmath5 gauge theories.@xcite .",
    "these phases are characterized by gapped spinons and gapped @xmath5-vortices that carry the @xmath5 flux.@xcite due to the gapped spectra of both spinons and @xmath5-vortices , it is difficult to observe an effect on measurable `` local '' quantities that distinguish the @xmath5 spin liquid from a regular paramagnetic state .",

    "deconfined phase of u(1 ) gauge theory can be stabilized in three dimensions ( 3d)@xcite and the resulting u(1 ) spin liquid states support gapped spinons , gapped monopoles , and gapless `` photons''.@xcite experimentally , these phases could be more easily observed since the gapless `` photons '' would show up in the low - energy physical properties of the system such as thermodynamics and energy / entropy transport . for example , an additional @xmath6 contribution to the specific heat from `` photons '' could be observed .",

    "in this paper , we studied the quantum ground states of the sp(@xmath0 ) antiferromagnetic heisenberg model on the cubic lattice with nn ( @xmath7 ) , and nnn ( @xmath8 ) exchange interactions .",
    "this is the simplest 3d model with frustration .",
    "we have shown that the sole addition of the nnn interaction on the cubic lattice gave rise to three neighboring u(1 ) spin liquids with magnetic short - range correlations at @xmath204 .",
    "moreover , there are indications that @xmath27 u(1 ) spin liquid is more likely to `` survive '' at finite @xmath0 values .",
    "this model has an advantage over other models because of its direct connection to realistic microscopic models .",
    "thus , verifying the results of our model in real systems would be more amenable .",
    "we first obtained the mean - field phase diagram of this model in the @xmath205 limit as a function of @xmath51 , which controls the frustration , and @xmath52 , which controls quantum fluctuations . for large values of @xmath22 , we find various magnetically ordered phases ; @xmath26 , @xmath27 , @xmath25 collinear - ordered and @xmath206 , @xmath207 helically - ordered states . upon decreasing @xmath22 ,",
    "we find three quantum - disordered paramagnetic phases ( sro ) with enhanced spin correlations at @xmath204 . since these are quantum - disordered states of collinear - ordered magnets , we see already at the mean - field level that these phases are possible u(1 ) spin liquids .",
    "methods have been developed by haldane,@xcite read , and sachdev@xcite to study ( 2 + 1)d quantum antiferromagnets . in this work ,",
    "we have extended these methods to ( 3 + 1)d and analyzed the effects of singular fluctuations about the mean - field states .",
    "these monopole events , or the topological defects in the u(1 ) gauge theory , represent tunneling between different topologically distinct sectors .",
    "we find that , when @xmath205 ( small monopole fugacity ) , the three paramagnetic phases are indeed u(1 ) spin liquids . at finite @xmath0 values ( large monopole fugacity ) , the system may become confined due to monopole condensation , and we discover various possible vbs ordered phases for sro @xmath26 and @xmath25 states .",
    "however , it appears that , when @xmath208 , the corresponding u(1 ) spin liquid with @xmath27 sro seems to be more stable in the simplest consideration of monopole condensation than the two other spin liquid states .",
    "we would need a more elaborate analysis to determine the ultimate fate of this state .",

    "<S> the phase diagram is obtained in the large-@xmath0 limit and fluctuation effects are considered via appropriate gauge theories . in particular , we find three u(1 ) spin liquid phases with different short - range magnetic correlations . </S>",
    "<S> these phases are characterized by deconfined gapped spinons , gapped monopoles , and gapless `` photons '' . as @xmath0 becomes smaller , a confinement transition from these phases to valence bond solids ( vbs ) may occur . </S>",

    "<S> we determine the resulting vbs phases emerging from two of the three spin liquid states . on the other hand , </S>",
