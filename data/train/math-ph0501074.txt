    "<S> we establish universality of the limits of the eigenvalue correlation kernel at such a critical point in a double scaling limit . </S>",
    "<S> the limiting kernels are constructed out of functions associated with the second painlev equation . </S>",
    "<S> this extends a result of bleher and its for the special case of a critical quartic potential .    </S>",
    "<S> the two main tools we use are equilibrium measures and riemann - hilbert problems . in our treatment of equilibrium measures </S>",
    "<S> we allow a negative density near the critical point , which enables us to treat all cases simultaneously . </S>",
    "<S> the asymptotic analysis of the riemann - hilbert problem is done with the deift / zhou steepest descent analysis . for the construction of a local parametrix at the critical point </S>",
    "<S> we introduce a modification of the approach of baik , deift , and johansson so that we are able to satisfy the required jump properties exactly . </S>"
