    "<S> for smooth embeddings of an integral homology @xmath0-sphere in the @xmath1-sphere , we define an integer invariant in terms of their seifert surfaces . </S>",
    "<S> our invariant gives a bijection between the set of smooth isotopy classes of such embeddings and the integers . </S>",
    "<S> it also gives rise to a complete invariant for homology bordism classes of all embeddings of homology @xmath0-spheres in the @xmath1-sphere . as a consequence , </S>",
    "<S> we show that two embeddings of an oriented integral homology @xmath0-sphere in the @xmath1-sphere are isotopic if and only if they are homology bordant . </S>",
