    "<S> we investigate precursors of critical behavior in the quasienergy spectrum due to the dynamical instability in the kicked top . using a semiclassical approach </S>",
    "<S> , we analytically obtain a logarithmic divergence in the density of states , which is analogous to a continuous excited state quantum phase transition in undriven systems . </S>",
    "<S> we propose a protocol to observe the cusp behavior of the magnetization close to the critical quasienergy .    </S>",
    "<S> the emerging field of excited state quantum phase transitions ( esqpts ) describes the nonanalytical behavior of excited states upon changes of parameters in the hamiltonian  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> this is in direct correspondence to quantum phase transitions ( qpts )  @xcite , but takes place at critical energies above the ground state energy  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> they entail dramatic dynamic consequences , e.g. , environments with esqpts lead to enhanced decoherence , which could be a major drawback for building a quantum computer  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> they appear in models of nuclear physics , such as the interacting boson model  @xcite and the lipkin - meshkov - glick model ( lmg )  @xcite . in molecular physics , singularities of the density of states ( dos ) </S>",
    "<S> arise in the vibron model  @xcite , which are closely related to the monodromy in molecular bending degrees of freedom  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> esqpts have been predicted to occur in prominent models of quantum optics such as the dicke and jaynes - cummings models  @xcite , too . despite the striking observation of the qpt in the dicke and the lmg model  @xcite , </S>",
    "<S> esqpts have so far not been found experimentally for systems different to molecular ones , as the energies at which they occur are difficult to reach with standard techniques . </S>",
    "<S> recently , however , the observation of low - energy singularities of the dos in twisted graphene layers  @xcite , and monodromy in diverse molecules  @xcite , has opened an increasing interest in the experimental investigation of spectral singularities .    </S>",
    "<S> quantum critical behavior is usually defined with respect to system energies  @xcite . under the effect of a nonadiabatic external control </S>",
    "<S> the energy is not conserved and it is not possible to uniquely define a ground state and the corresponding excited states . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we make use of floquet theory to introduce the concept of critical quasienergy states ( cqs ) , which are a direct generalization of esqpts to driven quantum systems . </S>",
    "<S> our model of choice is a paradigmatic model in the quantum chaos community : the kicked top . </S>",
    "<S> quantum kicked systems play a prominent role in the investigation of quantum signatures of chaos and have intriguing relations to condensed matter systems  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> examples of these relations are the metal - supermetal  @xcite and metal - topological - insulator  @xcite qpts in the kicked rotator , which can be thought of as a limiting case of the kicked top . </S>",
    "<S> such a limit is established when the top is restricted to evolve along a small equatorial band , which is topologically equivalent to a cylinder  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we are motivated by a recent experimental realization of the kicked top with driven ultra - cold cesium - atoms  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the large body of previous work on the kicked top is mostly focused on aspects of chaotic dynamics  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> contrary to this , our approach is strictly limited to the regular regime , taking advantage of the well - known level clustering  @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> we show that in this regime , the kicked top can be described by means of a time - independent effective hamiltonian , which allows one to study the dynamical instabilities leading to cqs . </S>",
    "<S> signatures of cqs then appear in the density of quasienergy states ( doqs ) , and the transverse magnetization .    </S>",
