    "<S> we present new results of 3-d amr mhd simulations focusing on two distinct aspects of ppn evolution . </S>",
    "<S> we first report new simulations of collimated outflows driven entirely by magnetic fields . </S>",
    "<S> these poynting flux dominated `` magnetic towers '' hold promise for explaining key properties of ppn flows . </S>",
    "<S> our simulations address magnetic tower evolution and stability . </S>",
    "<S> we also present results of a campaign of simulations to explore the development of accretion disks formed via wind capture . </S>",
    "<S> our result focus on the limits of disk formation and the range of disk properties . </S>"
