    "in this paper we investigate the quantum correlations arising within the ground state of noninteracting fermi gases trapped by an external space - dependent harmonic potential .",
    "quantum correlations can be characterized by the expectation values of the products of local operators , such as the particle density and one - particle operators , or by their integral over a space region @xmath6 , such as the particle - number fluctuations within @xmath6 .",
    "quantum correlations are also characterized by the fundamental phenomenon of entanglement , which gives rise to nontrivial connections between different parts of extended quantum systems  @xcite .",
    "a measure of entanglement is achieved by computing von neumann ( vn ) or rnyi entanglement entropies of the reduced density matrix of a subsystem .",
    "one - particle correlations and bipartite entanglement entropies provide important and complemetary information of the quantum behavior of many - body systems , because they probe different features of the quantum dynamics .",
    "we consider fermi gases of @xmath0 particles confined by harmonic traps of arbitrary dimension , and study the large-@xmath0 scaling behavior of the above - mentioned observables to characterize the quantum correlations of the ground state .",
