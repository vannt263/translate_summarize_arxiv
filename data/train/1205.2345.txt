    "in the last decade the field of visual recognition had an outstanding evolution from classifying instances of objects towards recognizing the classes of objects and scenes in natural images .",
    "much of this progress has been sparked by the creation of realistic image datasets as well as by the new and robust methods for image description and classification .",
    "we take inspiration from this progress and aim to transfer previous experience to the domain of video recognition and the recognition of human actions in particular during hajj and umrah seasons  @xcite .",
    "action recognition from video shares common problems with object recognition in static images .",
    "both tasks have to deal with significant intra - class variations , background clutter and occlusions . in the context of object recognition in static images ,",
    "these problems are surprisingly well handled by a bag - of - features representation @xcite combined with state - of - the - art machine learning techniques like support vector machines .",
    "it remains , however , an open question whether and how these results generalize to the recognition of realistic human actions , e.g. , in feature films or personal videos .    the hajj and umrah event recognition datasets are capable of recognizing a wide range of human actions during the hajj and umrah rituals ( tawaf , say , arafat . etc ) under different conditions .",
    "the main goal is to develop the hajj and umrah event recognition datasets to solve all the following problems :    * detecting injured , dead , and sleeping pilgrims inside masjid el - harram and around kabaa . * detecting abnormal pilgrims during safa / marwa and recognizing overcrowded areas .",
    "* detecting abnormal human events in makkah and in particular outside el - harram . * detecting people setting in sidewalks , allies , sub - roads , and stairs . * detecting missing and found people or objects inside el - harram and outside el - harram . * detecting and recognizing empty spaces inside and outside el - harram .",

    "<S> in this note , new hajj and umrah event recognition datasets ( huer ) are presented . </S>",
    "<S> the demonstrated datasets are based on videos and images taken during 2011 - 2012 hajj and umrah seasons . </S>",
    "<S> huer is the first collection of datasets covering the six types of hajj and umrah ritual events ( rotating in tawaf around kabaa , performing say between safa and marwa , standing on the mount of arafat , staying overnight in muzdalifah , staying two or three days in mina , and throwing jamarat ) . </S>",
    "<S> the huer datasets also contain video and image databases for nine types of human actions during hajj and umrah ( walking , drinking from zamzam water , sleeping , smiling , eating , praying , sitting , shaving hairs and ablutions , reading the holy quran and making duaa ) . </S>",
    "<S> the spatial resolutions are @xmath0 pixels for images and @xmath1 pixels for videos and have lengths of @xmath2 seconds in average with @xmath3 frame per second rates . </S>"
