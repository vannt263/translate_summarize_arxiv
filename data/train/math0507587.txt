    "<S> the functions we consider are the complex valued ray  </S>",

    "<S> they are associated essentially to a closed smooth manifold equipped with a ( co)euler structure and a riemannian metric in the first case , a smooth triangulation in the second case , and a smooth flow of type described in section  [ s:2 ] in the third case . in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we define these functions , describe some of their properties and calculate them in some case . </S>",
    "<S> we conjecture that they are essentially equal and have analytic continuation to rational functions on the variety of representations . </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the case of one dimensional representations and other relevant situations when the conjecture is true . </S>",
    "<S> as particular cases of our torsions , we recognize familiar rational functions in topology such as the lefschetz zeta function of a diffeomorphism , the dynamical zeta function of closed trajectories , and the alexander polynomial of a knot . a numerical invariant derived from ray  </S>",
