    "we have studied the stability of five - dimensional myers - perry black holes with equal angular momenta . utilizing the symmetry of these solutions , @xmath240 , we have identified the master variables with which the perturbed equations can be reduced to master equations . by analyzing the master equations ,",
    "we have shown the stability of myers - perry black holes with equal angular momenta .",
    "strictly speaking , we have not shown the stability of myers - perry black holes completely , because we analyzed restricted modes .",
    "empirically , however , the instability appears in the lower eigenvalue modes .",
    "for example , the gregory - laflamme instability appears in the _ s_-wave .",
    "therefore , our results for @xmath241 modes give strong evidence for the stability of myers - perry black holes .    in this paper , we considered the myers - perry black holes with equal angular momenta .",
    "there are several qualitative arguments for the stability of general myers - perry black holes . in the case of @xmath242 ,",
    "the argument goes as follows .",
    "the myers - perry black hole with one rotating axis reduces to a black brane solution in the limit of large angular momentum .",
    "since gregory - laflamme instability occurs in the black brane system  @xcite , we can speculate that the myers - perry black hole with one large angular momentum is unstable in the ( @xmath242)-dimension  @xcite . in the case of @xmath243",
    ", we can not use the above argument .",
    "instead , we can use the fact that the black ring solution is thermodynamically more preferable than the myers - perry black hole with one large angular momentum in order to infer the instability of the five - dimensional myers - perry black hole with one large angular momentum  @xcite .",
    "although these arguments seem to be reasonable , the direct dynamical analysis must be performed to achieve the final conclusion .",
