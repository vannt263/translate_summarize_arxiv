    "<S> this study investigates a multiplicative integer system by using a method that was developed for studying pattern generation problems . </S>",
    "<S> the entropy and the minkowski dimensions of general multiplicative systems can thus be computed . </S>",
    "<S> a multi - dimensional decoupled system is investigated in three main steps . </S>",
