    "<S> the experimental results for zrzn@xmath0 , urhge , and in some pressure ranges also for uge@xmath0 , have shown that the ferromagnetic superconductors are weak itinerant ferromagnets . guided by these results </S>",
    "<S> we describe the phenomenon of coexistence between equal spin triplet pairing superconductivity ( sc ) and ferromagnetism ( f ) using the extended stoner model , which includes in hamiltonian the on - site coulomb interaction , @xmath1 , and occupation dependent hopping integral . </S>",
    "<S> we use hartree - fock ( h - f ) approximation and the green functions technique . in the h - f approximation the on - site coulomb interaction plays the role of the on - site exchange ( hund s ) field . </S>",
    "<S> all inter - site interactions will have included the inter - site kinetic correlation , @xmath2 , within the h - f approximation . </S>",
    "<S> we introduce the pressure - dependence to the hopping integral . </S>",
    "<S> numerical results are compared with experimental data for zrzn@xmath0 .  </S>",
    "<S> the kinetic correlation creates the superconductivity without help of negative values of coulomb interactions . </S>",
    "<S> the model can explain stimulation of triplet sc by the weak itinerant ferromagnetism . </S>",
    "<S> this effect was observed experimentally in zrzn@xmath0 . </S>",
    "<S> numerical analysis also confirms the experimental effect of decrease in critical temperatures ( curie and superconducting ) with increasing external pressure .    </S>",
