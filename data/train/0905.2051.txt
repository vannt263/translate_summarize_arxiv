    "<S> we investigate the bounds on the mass of the lightest neutralino from rare meson decays within the mssm with and without minimal flavor violation . </S>",
    "<S> we present explicit formulae for the two - body decays of mesons into light neutralinos and perform the first complete calculation of the loop - induced decays of kaons to pions and light neutralinos and @xmath0 mesons to kaons and light neutralinos . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the supersymmetric branching ratios are strongly suppressed within the mssm with minimal flavor violation , and that no bounds on the neutralino mass can be inferred from experimental data , _ </S>",
    "<S> i.e. _  a massless neutralino is allowed . </S>",
    "<S> the branching ratios for kaon and @xmath0 meson decays into light neutralinos may , however , be enhanced when one allows for non - minimal flavor violation . </S>",
    "<S> we find new constraints on the mssm parameter space for such scenarios and discuss prospects for future kaon and @xmath0 meson experiments . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we comment on the search for light neutralinos in monojet signatures at the tevatron and at the lhc . </S>"
