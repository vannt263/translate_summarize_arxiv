    "<S> the soft - core boson system is one of the simplest models of supersolids , which have both off - diagonal long - range order ( bose  einstein condensation ) and diagonal long - range order ( crystalline order ) . </S>",
    "<S> although this model has been studied from various points of view , studies of the stability of current - flowing states are lacking . solving the gross  pitaevskii and bogoliubov equations , we obtain excitation spectra in superfluid , supersolid , and stripe phases . on the basis of the results of the excitation spectra , we present a stability phase diagram that shows the region of the metastable superflow states for each phase .    _ introduction . _ </S>",
    "<S> a supersolid is a quantum phase that has both superfluidity and solidity . </S>",
    "<S> after it was demonstrated in some seminal works@xcite that this intriguing state can be realized in quantum solids , much research has been done . </S>",
    "<S> recently , kim and chan@xcite presented the possibility of a supersolid in solid @xmath0he . </S>",
    "<S> they reported experimental results of non - classical rotational inertia ( ncri ) in solid @xmath0he . while there have been many experimental and theoretical studies made@xcite , there still remains controversy over the origin of ncri in solid @xmath0he . </S>",
    "<S> ultra - cold atomic gases have also become a field for the study of supersolids . </S>",
    "<S> many theoretical works support the existence of a supersolid phase in systems with strong long - range interactions such as dipole - dipole@xcite or van der waals interactions@xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recently , bose  einstein condensates ( bec ) of @xmath1cr@xcite , @xmath2dy@xcite , and @xmath3er@xcite , which have large magnetic moments , have been realized experimentally . </S>",
    "<S> owing to the high controllability of experimental conditions , cold atomic gases may provide insights into the nature of supersolids .    in the many previous studies , </S>",
    "<S> the properties of equilibrium states of supersolids have been investigated . </S>",
    "<S> however , dynamical stability of a finite superflow state can not be obtained by calculating equilibrium states in the absence of a current@xcite.we need to investigate the stability of a current - carrying state that has both off - diagonal long - range order ( odlro ) and diagonal long - range order ( dlro ) .    </S>",
    "<S> the stability of condensates can be investigated by calculating the excitation spectra . in many cases , </S>",
    "<S> the low - energy excitations determine the critical velocity of superfluids . </S>",
    "<S> for example , the landau critical velocity@xcite is determined by the excitation energy , and the critical velocity of a condensate in a moving optical lattice can be calculated from the excitation spectrum@xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the excitation spectrum can be not only theoretically calculated but also experimentally observed in neutron scattering experiments for @xmath0he@xcite or bragg spectroscopy of cold atoms@xcite . </S>",
    "<S> one of the most striking properties of superfluids is the existence of a critical velocity , and determining the critical velocity of a supersolid is an important problem . to determine the supersolid critical velocity </S>",
    "<S> , we use a simple continuum model of supersolids that was proposed by pomeau and rica@xcite . using the gross  pitaevskii ( gp ) equation with a finite - range interaction ( soft - core interaction ) </S>",
    "<S> , they showed that the ground state exhibits odlro and dlro at a sufficiently high density of the condensate . </S>",
    "<S> although this model uses a simplified interaction potential for the purposes of manageability , it is suitable for investigating supersolidity . </S>",
    "<S> in fact , the properties of bosons with finite - range interactions have been studied in various contexts@xcite .    in this paper , we present a phase diagram of metastable superflow states for each phase(we call this the `` stability phase diagram '' in the following ) of two - dimensional soft - core bosons at zero temperature by solving the gp and the bogoliubov equations@xcite . although a similar analysis has been done for a lattice system@xcite , a continuum system has not yet been studied . </S>",
    "<S> we find that three phases are stable against the superflow : a superfluid phase , a supersolid phase , and a stripe phase .    _ model . </S>",
