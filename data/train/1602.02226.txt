    "<S> we study scaling limits of a laplacian pinning model in @xmath0 dimension and derive sample path large deviations for the profile height function . </S>",
    "<S> the model is given by a gaussian integrated random walk ( or a gaussian integrated random walk bridge ) perturbed by an attractive force towards the zero - level . </S>",
    "<S> we study in detail the behaviour of the rate function and show that it can admit up to five minimisers depending on the choices of pinning strength and boundary conditions . </S>",
    "<S> this study complements corresponding large deviation results for gaussian gradient systems with pinning in @xmath1-dimension ( @xcite ) in @xmath2-dimension ( @xcite ) , and recently in higher dimensions in @xcite .    </S>",
