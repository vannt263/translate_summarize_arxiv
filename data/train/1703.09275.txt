    "<S> it is established the optimal harvesting policy . </S>",
    "<S> the boundedness , feasibility of interior equilibria , bionomic equilibrium have been determined . </S>",
    "<S> the main observation is that coefficient of refuge plays an important role in regulating the dynamics of the present system . </S>",
    "<S> moreover the variation of the coefficient of refuge change the system from stable to unstable and vice - versa . some numerical illustration </S>",
