    "<S> recently , hodgson and kerckhoff found a small bound on dehn surgered @xmath0-manifolds from hyperbolic knots not admitting hyperbolic structures using deformations of hyperbolic cone - manifolds . </S>",
    "<S> they asked whether the area normalized meridian length squared of maximal tubular neighborhoods of the singular locus of the cone - manifold is decreasing and that summed with the cone angle squared is increasing as we deform the cone - angles . </S>",
    "<S> we confirm this near @xmath1 cone - angles for an infinite family of hyperbolic cone - manifolds obtained by dehn surgeries along the whitehead link complements . </S>",
    "<S> the basic method is based on explicit holonomy computations using the @xmath2-polynomials and finding the maximal tubes . </S>",
    "<S> one of the key tool is the taylor expression of a geometric component of the zero set of the @xmath2-polynomial in terms of the cone - angles . </S>",
    "<S> we also show a sequence of taylor expressions for dehn surgered manifolds converges to one for the limit hyperbolic manifold . </S>"
