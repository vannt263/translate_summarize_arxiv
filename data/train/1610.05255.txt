    "the study of quantum correlations has been object of numerous investigations in the last years , since it is a remarkable resource in quantum information science . in this way , quantum information quantifiers @xcite are a useful tool to verify the presence of quantum correlations in a quantum system . in spite of that , the detection of quantum correlations is a hard task theoretically and experimentally speaking @xcite .",
    "nowadays , it has become understood that quantum correlations can be quantified through the measurement of some macroscopic properties of magnetic systems @xcite .",
    "the study of the magnetic properties of molecular materials are typically done approximating magnetic parameters of a hamiltonian model by the fit of some thermodynamic properties , such as magnetic susceptibility , internal energy and specific heat @xcite . in this context ,",
    "correlations functions has a great importance to describe these properties ; in addition , it can be directly measurable , e.g. , in neutron scattering experiments via _ structure factors_. structure factors can be defined as a two - point correlations @xcite and it is widely used to describe the crystal structure of molecular systems ruled by hamiltonians @xcite , e.g. , heisenberg models @xcite .",
    "quantum information quantifiers are expressed in terms of statistical correlation functions @xcite , due to the fact that these functions are presents on the elements of the density matrix of the quantum system , linking their macroscopic properties with the quantum ones .",
    "therefore , it is possible quantify the presence of quantum correlations in a system via structure factors @xcite , since these factors are directly associated to the correlation functions ; allowing the measurement of quantum information quantifiers by neutron scattering experiments .    in this scenario ,",
    "the present work shows analytical expressions for the entanglement witness , entanglement of formation , bell s inequality violation and geometric quantum discord , based on schatten 1-norm as a function of quantities typically obtained in neutron scattering via scalar structure factor .",
    "our results provide one path toward to identify the presence of quantum correlations ( and quantum nonlocality ) in a molecular magnet such as a heisenberg spin-@xmath0 dimer , by diffractive properties .",
    "this is an alternative way to describe the quantum properties of a sample material via neutron scattering experiments , without make any assumption about their macroscopic quantities , leading to promising applications in quantum information science .",
