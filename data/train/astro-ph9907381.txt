    "the calibration generates models with cores of larger temperature , density , helium content and sound speed with the concomitant increase of the neutrino fluxes , except for @xmath165 and @xmath81[8]{b}}$ ] ; for this last one , the decrease is due to the smaller rate of the reaction @xmath4[7]{be}(p,\\gamma)\\element[][8]{b^*}$ ] .",
