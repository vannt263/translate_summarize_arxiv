    "a magnetic system is called frustrated if local pairwise interactions between spins can not be satisfied simultaneously .",
    "frustration can arise from competing interactions and/or from a specific lattice geometry , as illustrated , _",
    "e.g. _ , by a triangular lattice .",
    "magnetic frustration gives rise to an extremely rich phenomenology in both quantum and classical systems .",
    "quantum frustrated magnets are the main candidates for a variety of unconventional phases and phase transitions such as spin liquids and critical points with deconfined fractional excitations @xcite .",
    "frustration also plays an important role in ( semi)classical spin systems , for which the order - by - disorder mechanism @xcite is capable of producing new unexpected types of long - range magnetic order . for this phenomenon",
    "certain low - temperature configurations are favored not by the energy , but by their entropy .",
    "if the entropy selection fails , fluctuation between degenerate ground states may result in a cooperative paramagnet or a classical spin liquid .",
