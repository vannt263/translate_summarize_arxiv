    "<S> _ a treatise on probability _ was published by john maynard keynes in 1921 . </S>",
    "<S> the _ treatise _ contains a critical assessment of the philosophical foundations of probability and of the statistical methodology at the time . </S>",
    "<S> we review the aspects of the book that are most related with statistics , avoiding uninteresting neophyte s forrays into philosophical issues . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we examine the arguments provided by keynes again the bayesian approach , as well as the sketchy alternative of a return to lexis theory of analogies he proposes . </S>",
    "<S> our conclusion is that the _ treatise _ is a scholarly piece of work looking at past advances rather than producing directions for the future .     </S>",
