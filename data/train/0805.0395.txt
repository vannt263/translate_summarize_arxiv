    "<S> these atomic data are used to provide semiclassical electron- , proton- and ionized helium- impact line widths and shifts for 15 si vi muliplet . </S>",
    "<S> calculated results have been used to consider the influence of stark broadening for do white dwarf atmospheric conditions .    </S>",
