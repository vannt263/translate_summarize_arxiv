    "<S> for smooth manifolds equipped with various geometric structures , we construct complexes that replace the de  rham complex in providing an alternative fine resolution of the sheaf of locally constant functions . in case </S>",
    "<S> that the geometric structure is that of a parabolic geometry , our complexes coincide with the bernstein - gelfand - gelfand complex associated with the trivial representation . </S>",
    "<S> however , at least in the cases we discuss , our constructions are relatively simple and avoid most of the machinery of parabolic geometry . moreover </S>",
    "<S> , our method extends to contact and symplectic geometries ( beyond the parabolic realm ) . </S>"
