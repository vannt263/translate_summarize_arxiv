    "<S> we investigate theoretically spin and orbital pseudospin magnetic properties of a molecular orbital in parabolic and elliptic double quantum dots ( dqds ) . in our many body calculation </S>",
    "<S> we include intra- and inter - dot electron - electron interactions , in addition to the intradot exchange interaction of ` p ' orbitals . </S>",
    "<S> we find for parabolic dqds that , except for the half or completely filled molecular orbital , spins in different dots are ferromagnetically coupled while orbital pseudospins are antiferromagnetically coupled . for elliptic dqds spins and pseudospins are either ferromagnetically or antiferromagnetically coupled , depending on the number of electrons in the molecular orbital . </S>",
    "<S> we have determined orbital pseudospin quantum numbers for the groundstates of elliptic dqds . </S>",
    "<S> an experiment is suggested to test the interplay between orbital pseudospin and spin magnetism . </S>"
