    "<S> we study generic constrained differential equations ( cdes ) with three parameters , thereby extending takens s classification of singularities of such equations . in this approach , </S>",
    "<S> the singularities analyzed are the swallowtail , the hyperbolic , and the elliptic umbilics . </S>",
    "<S> we provide polynomial local normal forms of cdes under topological equivalence . </S>",
    "<S> generic cdes are important in the study of slow - fast ( sf ) systems . </S>",
    "<S> many properties and the characteristic behavior of the solutions of sf systems can be inferred from the corresponding cde . </S>",
    "<S> therefore , the results of this paper show a first approximation of the flow of generic sf systems with three slow variables .    constrained differential equations , slow - fast systems , normal forms , catastrophe theory </S>"
