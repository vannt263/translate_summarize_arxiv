    "<S> we prove here that , if two minimal rays have a common asymptotic direction , which is not a fixed point of the group of deck transformations , then the two rays can intersect at most in a common initial point . </S>",
    "<S> this has strong consequences for the structure of the set of minimal geodesics , as well as for the set of busemann functions associated to the finsler metric . </S>"
