    "<S> product distribution matching ( pdm ) is proposed to generate target distributions over large alphabets by combining the output of several parallel distribution matchers ( dms ) with smaller output alphabets . </S>",
    "<S> the parallel architecture of pdm enables low - complexity and high - throughput implementation . </S>",
    "<S> pdm is used as a shaping device for probabilistic amplitude shaping ( pas ) . for 64-ask and a spectral efficiency of bits per channel use ( bpcu ) , </S>",
    "<S> pdm is as power efficient as a single full - fledged dm . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown how pdm enables pas for parallel channels present in multi - carrier systems like digital subscriber line ( dsl ) and orthogonal frequency - division multiplexing ( ofdm ) . </S>",
    "<S> the key feature is that pdm shares the dms for lower bit - levels among different sub - carriers , which improves the power efficiency significantly . </S>",
    "<S> a representative parallel channel example shows that pas with pdm is more power efficient than conventional uniform signaling and pdm is more power efficient than individual per channel dms .    </S>",
    "<S> probabilistic amplitude shaping , distribution matcher , rate adaptation , parallel channels , bit loading , dsl , ofdm , coded modulation </S>"
