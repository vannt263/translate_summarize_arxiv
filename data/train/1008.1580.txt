    "one of the main objectives of the experiments at the large hadron collider ( lhc ) is to search for new phenomena beyond the standard model of particle physics ( bsm ) at and above the tev energy scale .",
    "a well motivated scenario is the @xmath0-parity violating minimal supergravity model ( msugra ) with baryon triality ( b@xmath3 ) @xcite .",
    "contrary to the @xmath0-parity conserving msugra model ( also called the constrained mssm ) @xcite , lepton number is violated .",
    "this leads to naturally light neutrino masses without introducing either a new see  saw energy scale at @xmath4 or new gauge  singlet superfields @xcite .",
    "however , within this model the lightest supersymmetric particle ( lsp ) is not stable .",
    "thus the lightest neutralino is no longer a viable dark matter particle - parity violating couplings need in this case to be smaller than @xmath5 @xcite .",

    "any other supersymmetric particle is possible @xcite .",
    "this can lead to dramatically different signatures at colliders @xcite . within the b@xmath3 msugra model , certain non  neutralino lsp candidates",
