    "<S> linear eigenmodes of a spherically symmetrical ultra - relativistic blast wave ( the blandford - mckee , bmk , solution ) are calculated . </S>",
    "<S> the bmk solution is shown to be stable and strongly non - universal . </S>",
    "<S> it is stable because all the eigenmodes decay . </S>",
    "<S> non - universality of the bmk solution ( bmk is not an intermediate asymptotic ) is a consequence of causality . in terms of eigenmodes  </S>",
    "<S> some eigenmodes decay too slowly . for each degree </S>",
    "<S> @xmath0 , there exists an eigenmode which decays at the smallest rate . </S>",
    "<S> the amplitude of this eigenmode , defined as relative perturbation of energy behind the shock front , is constant at early time . </S>",
    "<S> later , when the blast wave slows down to @xmath1 , an oscillatory decay commences , and the amplitude drops to less than 10% of the initial value at @xmath2 . </S>",
    "<S> the non - universality is surprisingly strong . near the end of the ultra - relativistic stage , </S>",
    "<S> perturbations with @xmath3 , that is more than 10,000 different harmonics , are suppressed by less than a factor of 10 . </S>",
