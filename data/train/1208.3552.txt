    "<S> a simulation - assisted hypothesis testing procedure is proposed for testing significance and parameter constancy . </S>",
    "<S> we further propose an information criterion that can consistently the true set of significant predictors . </S>",
    "<S> our method is applied to autoregressive models with time - varying coefficients . </S>",
