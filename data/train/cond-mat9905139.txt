    "<S> the macroscopic quantum interference effects in ballistic josephson microstructures are investigated . </S>",
    "<S> the studied system consists of four bulk superconductors ( terminals ) which are weakly coupled through the mesoscopic rectangular normal metal ( two dimensional electron gas ) . </S>",
    "<S> we show that nonlocal electrodynamics of ballistic systems leads to specific current - phase relations for the mesoscopic multiterminal josephson junction . </S>",
    "<S> the nonlocal coupling of supercurrents produces the `` dragging '' effect for phases of the superconducting order parameter in different terminals . </S>",
    "<S> the composite josephson junction , based on this effect , exhibits the two -level system behaviour controlled by the external magnetic flux . </S>",
    "<S> the effect of magnetic flux transfer in a system of nonlocally coupled superconducting rings is studied .    _ keywords _ : multiterminal josephson junction ; nonlocal coupling ; drag effect ; magnetic flux transfer . </S>"
