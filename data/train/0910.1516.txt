    "<S> in this article we study the numerical approximation of incompressible miscible displacement problems with a linearised crank - nicolson time discretisation , combined with a mixed finite element and discontinuous galerkin method . at the heart of the analysis </S>",
    "<S> is the proof of convergence under low regularity requirements . </S>",
    "<S> numerical experiments demonstrate that the proposed method exhibits second - order convergence for smooth and robustness for rough problems . </S>"
