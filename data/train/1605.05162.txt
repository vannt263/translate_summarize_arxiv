    "<S> we study electrical and optical conductivities of hole gas in @xmath0-doped bulk iii - v semiconductors described by the luttinger hamiltonian . </S>",
    "<S> we provide exact analytical expressions of the drude conductivity , inverse relaxation time for various impurity potentials , drude weight and optical conductivity in terms of the luttinger parameters @xmath1 and @xmath2 . </S>",
    "<S> the back scattering is completely suppressed as a result of the helicity conservation of the heavy and light hole states . </S>",
    "<S> the energy dependence of the relaxation time for the hole states is different from the brooks - herring formula for electron gas in @xmath3-doped semiconductors . </S>",
    "<S> we find that the inverse relaxation time of heavy holes is much less than that of the light holes for coulomb - type and gaussian - type impurity potentials and vice - versa for short - range impurity potential . </S>",
    "<S> the drude conductivity increases non - linearly with the increase of the hole density . </S>",
    "<S> the exponent of the density dependence of the conductivity is obtained in the thomas - fermi limit . </S>",
    "<S> the drude weight varies linearly with the density even in presence of the spin - orbit coupling . </S>",
    "<S> the finite - frequency optical conductivity goes as @xmath4 and its amplitude strongly depends on the luttinger parameters . </S>",
    "<S> the luttinger parameters can be extracted from the optical conductivity measurement . </S>"
