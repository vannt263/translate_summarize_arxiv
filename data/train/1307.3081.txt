    "<S> however , even though the hf ground state of jellium has been subject of research all over the years@xcite , the ground state phase diagram as a function of the density has still not fully been established . at low density , potential energy largely dominates over the kinetic energy , and the electrons form the so - called wigner crystal ( wc ) , the ground state in the classical limit , whereas in the limit of vanishing @xmath5 the ideal fermi gas ( fg ) is approached . </S>",
