    "<S> when multiple planets stir the disk , our simulations yield the new result that large - scale migration ceases . </S>",
    "<S> thus , growing planets do not migrate through planetesimal disks . to extend these results to migration in gaseous disks , we compare physical interactions and rates . </S>",
