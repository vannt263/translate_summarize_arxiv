    "<S> recently , diverse phase transition ( pt ) types have been obtained in multiplex networks , such as discontinuous , continuous , and mixed - order pts . however , </S>",
    "<S> they emerge from individual systems , and there is no theoretical understanding of such pts in a single framework . here </S>",
    "<S> , we study a spin model called the ashkin - teller ( at ) model in a mono - layer scale - free network ; this can be regarded as a model of two species of ising spin placed on each layer of a double - layer network . </S>",
    "<S> the four - spin interaction in the at model represents the inter - layer interaction in the multiplex network . </S>",
    "<S> diverse pts emerge depending on the inter - layer coupling strength and network structure . </S>",
    "<S> especially , we find that mixed - order pts occur at the critical end points </S>",
    "<S> . the origin of such behavior is explained in the framework of landau - ginzburg theory .    </S>",
    "<S> recently , multiplex networks have become a platform for research in network science because in real - world systems , networks are intertangled and function together . </S>",
    "<S> examples include infrastructure in everyday life such as power stations , transportation systems , information networks , and water supply systems . </S>",
    "<S> multiplex networks can be fragile because failure in one network can cause failure in another , leading to cascading back - and - forth failures . </S>",
    "<S> then , the entire system can exhibit a discontinuous percolation transition  @xcite .    </S>",
    "<S> mixed - order phase transitions ( pts ) ( or hybrid pts ) are also observed in multiplex networks @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> the size of a giant viable cluster in a multiplex network shows such a pt as a function of the fraction of undamaged nodes . here </S>",
    "<S> , a mixed - order pt means that while the order parameter exhibits a discontinuous pt , the mean cluster size ( susceptibility ) diverges . </S>",
    "<S> consequently , features of both continuous and discontinuous pts appear at the same transition point . </S>",
    "<S> mixed - order pts have been found in several physical models , such as the bootstrap model @xcite , jamming percolation @xcite , the ising model with long - range interactions @xcite , and the synchronization model @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> however , the mechanism underpinning such mixed - order pts is not fully understood .    </S>",
    "<S> effort has been made to understand the diverse patterns that emerge from cooperative phenomena in complex networks using spin models in thermal equilibrium . </S>",
    "<S> for example , opinion formation and the spread of epidemics have been studied using the ising  @xcite and potts @xcite models , respectively . </S>",
    "<S> such studies of spin models give some insight into what might happen in complex systems . </S>",
    "<S> motivated by this idea , the ising model was studied on a double - layer network @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> interestingly , it exhibited a discontinuous pt , whereas it shows a continuous pt in a mono - layer network . </S>",
    "<S> these results led us to speculate that the type of pt can be changed systematically by controlling the inter - layer coupling strength ; in the above examples , zero coupling strength ( no connection ) results in a continuous pt , whereas finite coupling strength leads to a change in pt type .    </S>",
    "<S> to investigate the origin of such diverse types of pt in a single theoretical framework systematically , we studied a spin model called the ashkin  </S>",
