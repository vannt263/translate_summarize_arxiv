    "<S> interactions of phonons with charge carriers increase the electrical resistivity of the material . </S>",
    "<S> our approach captures the lattice vibrations as curvature changes in the space through which electrons move following hydrodynamic equations . in this picture , </S>",
    "<S> inertial corrections to the electronic flow arise naturally effectively producing electron - phonon interactions . </S>",
