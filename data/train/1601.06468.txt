    "<S> we construct error correcting codes for jointly transmitting a finite set of independent messages to an _ informed receiver _ which has prior knowledge of the values of some subset of the messages as side information . </S>",
    "<S> the transmitter is oblivious to the message subset already known to the receiver and performs encoding in such a way that any possible side information can be used efficiently at the decoder . </S>",
    "<S> we construct and identify several families of algebraic error correcting codes for this problem using cyclic and maximum distance separable ( mds ) codes . </S>",
    "<S> the proposed codes are of short block length , many of them provide optimum or near - optimum error correction capabilities and guarantee larger minimum distances than known codes of similar parameters for informed receivers . </S>",
    "<S> the constructed codes are also useful as error correcting codes for index coding when the transmitter does not know the side information available at the receivers </S>",
    "<S> .    cyclic codes , index coding , informed receivers , maximum distance separable codes , side information . </S>"
