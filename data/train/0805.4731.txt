    "even though , strictly speaking , singularities in complex theories of gravity exist , they occupy just one point on an 8 dimensional phase space , and consequently they are reached only by special geodesics / observers . indeed , we have argued that the space of initial conditions of observers which encounter singularities is of measure zero , when compared to the space of all initial conditions .",

    "<S> we formulate holomorphic theory of gravity and study how the holomorphy symmetry alters the two most important singular solutions of general relativity : black holes and cosmology . </S>",
    "<S> we show that typical observers ( freely ) falling into a holomorphic black hole do not encounter a curvature singularity . </S>",
    "<S> likewise , typical observers do not experience big bang singularity . </S>",
    "<S> unlike hermitian gravity  @xcite , holomorphic gravity does not respect the reciprocity symmetry and thus it is mainly a toy model for a gravity theory formulated on complex space - times . </S>",
    "<S> yet it is a model that deserves a closer investigation since in many aspects it resembles hermitian gravity and yet calculations are simpler . </S>",
    "<S> we have indications that holomorphic gravity reduces to the laws of general relativity correctly at large distance scales . </S>"
