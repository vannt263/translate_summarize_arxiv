    "the present paper is meant to move forward the art of computing and drawing complex amoebas of algebraic hypersurfaces . a  special attention paid to the geometrically most interesting and computationally most challenging case of optimal hypersurfaces ( see definition  [ def : optimalamoeba ] ) .",

    "the developed algorithms are used in higher dimensions for depicting sections of  amoebas of  polynomials in three variables .",
