    "<S> in this paper , we study a lotka - volterra model of two prey species and one predator species presented in @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> first , we give existence of an invariant set under a set of sufficient conditions of parameters in the model . </S>",
    "<S> these conditions also guarantee that the growth of all the species is permanent . </S>",
    "<S> second , we present a condition under which the predator species falls into decay . </S>",
    "<S> finally , by using lyapunov functions , we prove global asymptotic stability of solutions of the model . </S>"
