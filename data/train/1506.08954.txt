    "<S> hybrids of graphene and two dimensional transition metal dichalcogenides ( tmdc ) have the potential to bring graphene spintronics to the next level . </S>",
    "<S> as we show here by performing first - principles calculations of graphene on monolayer mos@xmath0 , there are several advantages of such hybrids over pristine graphene . </S>",
    "<S> first , dirac electrons in graphene exhibit a giant global proximity spin - orbit coupling , without compromising the semimetallic character of the whole system at zero field . remarkably , these spin - orbit effects can be very accurately described by a simple effective hamiltonian . </S>",
    "<S> second , the fermi level can be tuned by a transverse electric field to cross the mos@xmath0 conduction band , creating a system of coupled massive and massles electron gases . both charge and spin transport in such systems </S>",
    "<S> should be unique . </S>",
    "<S> finally , we propose to use graphene / tmdc structures as a platform for optospintronics , in particular for optical spin injection into graphene and for studying spin transfer between tmdc and graphene .    </S>",
    "<S> graphene spintronics @xcite has relied exclusively on electrical spin injection @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> combining graphene with semiconducting two - dimensional tdmc @xcite can open new venues for spintronics applications @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> indeed , tmdc are becoming increasingly popular in optoelectronics as sensitive photodetectors @xcite or , forming lateral heterostructures @xcite , as two - dimensional solar cells @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> important , tmdc have a sizeable spin - orbit coupling and lack space inversion symmetry . as a result </S>",
    "<S> , their band structure @xcite allows for a valley resolved optical spin excitation by circularly polarized light @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> tmdc can thus facilitate optical spin injection into graphene , in hybrid structures .    </S>",
    "<S> efficient growth of mos@xmath0 on graphene has already been demonstrated @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> it was reported that graphene on mos@xmath0 is ultraflat , having large mean free paths @xcite ; angle - resolved photoemission found an intact dirac point but a strong hybridization elsewhere in the @xmath1 system @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> technological potentials for these hybrid structures are already being discussed @xcite , mainly as a basis for nonvolatile memory @xcite , sensitive photodetection @xcite , and gate - tunable persistent photoconductivity @xcite . </S>",
    "<S> recently , the spin hall effect in graphene on few - layer ws@xmath0 was observed at room temperature @xcite .    in this paper </S>",
    "<S> we establish by first - principles calculations fundamental electronic properties and the spin - orbit fine structure of the graphene dirac bands for graphene on monolayer mos@xmath0 , and introduce an effective spin - orbit hamiltonian which explains the proximity induced spin splittings of the dirac states . </S>",
    "<S> we show that the induced spin - orbit coupling is giant , being 20 times more than in pristine graphene . </S>",
