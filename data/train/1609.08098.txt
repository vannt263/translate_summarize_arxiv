    "<S> the celebrated cwikel - lieb - rozenblum inequality gives an upper estimate for the number of negative eigenvalues of schrdinger operators in dimension three and higher . </S>",
    "<S> the situation is much more difficult in the two dimensional case . </S>",
    "<S> there has been significant progress in obtaining upper estimates for the number of negative eigenvalues of two dimensional schrdinger operators on the whole plane . in this thesis </S>",
    "<S> , we present upper estimates of the cwikel - lieb - rozenblum type for the number of eigenvalues ( counted with multiplicities ) of two dimensional schrdinger operators lying below the essential spectrum in terms of the norms of the potential . the problem is considered on the whole plane with different supports of the potential ( in particular , sets of dimension @xmath0 $ ] and on a strip with various boundary conditions . in both cases , </S>",
    "<S> the estimates involve weighted @xmath1 norms and orlicz norms of the potential . </S>"
