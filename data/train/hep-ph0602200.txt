    "gpds relate quite different physical quantities , such as feynman s parton distribution functions ( pdfs ) and hadron form factors , in the same framework . by generalizing pdfs one dimensional parton distribution pictures , gpds",
    "provide the three - dimensional pictures @xcite .",
    "furthermore , gpds also give information on highly desirable quantities such as the quark orbital angular momentum contribution to proton spin ji .",
    "useful constraints on the forms of the nucleon gpds have been obtained at desy @xcite and jefferson lab @xcite .",
    "typically the gpds contribute to experimental observables through convolutions ; they are not directly measurable through those experiments .",
    "thus inputs from theories are important .",
    "valuable insights are gained through the impact parameter distribution interpretation @xcite ( see also @xcite ) and model computations of gpds [ see e.g. @xcite for a review ] .",
    "recently , lattice techniques have first been applied to compute the moments of nucleon gpds  directly from qcd @xcite .",

    "in general , the quark mass and momentum transfer dependence have non - analytic structures which should be incorporated in the parametrization of the extrapolation formulas . fortunately",
    ", chiral perturbation theory ( @xmath2pt ) chpt , gl , hbchpt , chptulf , which is an  effective field theory of qcd , can be applied to extract those non - analytic structures in a systematic , model - independent way .",
