    "<S> this procedure is different from that normally found in quantum topology .  </S>",
    "<S> we then demonstrate the power of this method by using it to quantize braids .    </S>",
    "<S> this general method produces a blueprint of a quantum system which is physically implementable in the same sense that shor s quantum factoring algorithm is physically implementable .  </S>",
