    "dwarf spheroidal galaxies ( dsph ) belong to the most numerous galaxy population in the universe , but their low surface brightness hampers their detection . the local group offers the nearest laboratory to analyse the formation and evolution processes of the low - luminosity dsph galaxy population . the historical picture of dsphs containing relatively little interstellar material has changed during the past few years owing to the improved spatial resolution and sensitivity of infrared , ( sub-)millimeter and radio observing facilities ( e.g. , _ herschel _ , evla , bima , iram ) .",
    "the detection of gas ( e.g. , ) and dust ( e.g. , ) shows that a subpopulation of dsphs does contain a significant reservoir of interstellar material .",
    "the presence of a young stellar population , within galaxies otherwise dominated by old stars , also demonstrates that some dsphs have experienced a recent episode of star formation ( e.g. , ) .    in this paper",
    ", we focus on the study of the dust reservoir in the dsph companions of the andromeda galaxy ( m31 ) .",

    "overall , the dsph galaxies ngc147 and ngc185 appear very similar in terms of their galaxy mass @xcite , stellar kinematics @xcite and c star fraction @xcite . from a detailed analysis of their star formation history , @xcite , however , concluded that the bulk of stellar mass was formed around 12.5 gyr ago in ngc185 , while most of the stars in ngc147 only formed about 5 to 7 gyr ago .",
    "the latter result is interpreted as an earlier infall time for ngc185 compared to ngc147 into the m31 group .",
    "other than the age variations for the bulk of stars in the two dwarf spheroidals , ngc185 is characterised by a more recent star formation episode limited to its central regions , that started a few 100 myr ago .",
    "a similar central concentration of young stars is also found in the other local group dsph ngc205 , suggesting that the build - up of mass from evolved stars and planetary nebulae has initiated a recent cycle of star formation in their central regions .",
    "the ongoing star formation in ngc185 contrasts with the lack of any sign of recent star formation activity during the last 1 gyr in ngc147 @xcite and the absence of any detected ism material in ngc147 @xcite .",
