    "<S> exchange - correlation potential of dft was approximated in the frame of both local density approximation ( lda ) and generalized gradient approximation ( gga ) schemes . </S>",
    "<S> vibrational properties of this molecule are given by the assignments in the range for wavenumber @xmath0 @xmath1 . </S>",
    "<S> stable equilibrium structure of the molecule was also obtained by using lda and gga . </S>",
    "<S> obtained optimized geometrical structure was used to calculate vibrational properties and stm images . </S>",
    "<S> a remarkable agreement was obtained between theory and experiment , especially in the symmetric and asymmetric vibrations of nh groups . _ </S>",
