    "<S> here we introduce an optomechanical system combining strong nonlinear optomechanical coupling , low mass and large optical mode spacing . </S>",
    "<S> this nanoscale `` paddle nanocavity '' supports mechanical resonances with hundreds of fg mass which couple nonlinearly to optical modes with a quadratic optomechanical coupling coefficient @xmath0 mhz / nm@xmath1 , and a two phonon to single photon optomechanical coupling rate @xmath2 hz . </S>",
