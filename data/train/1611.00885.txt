    "<S> we investigate qualitative and quantitative behavior of a solution to the problem of pricing american style of perpetual put options . </S>",
    "<S> we assume the option price is a solution to a stationary generalized black - scholes equation in which the volatility may depend on the second derivative of the option price itself . </S>",
    "<S> we prove existence and uniqueness of a solution to the free boundary problem . </S>",
    "<S> we derive a single implicit equation for the free boundary position and the closed form formula for the option price . </S>",
    "<S> it is a generalization of the well - known explicit closed form solution derived by merton for the case of a constant volatility . </S>",
    "<S> we also present results of numerical computations of the free boundary position , option price and their dependence on model parameters .    _ </S>",
