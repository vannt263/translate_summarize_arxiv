    "matrix models are tractable toy models for studying non - perturbative aspects of string theory @xcite .",
    "not only their perturbation theory is controllable to all orders @xcite ( including correlators , free - energy and those of instanton corrections ) but also non - perturbative completions ( such as their associated stokes phenomena ) have revealed intriguing and solvable features in these models @xcite .",
    "such solvable aspects of matrix models have pushed forward our fundamental understanding of string theory beyond the perturbative perspectives . although its beautiful framework is already considered to be `` well - established ''",
    ", it is our belief that solvability of matrix models is now opening the next stage of development toward a non - perturbative definition of string theory .    in this paper",

    "<S> the resolvent operator plays a central role in matrix models . for instance , with utilizing the loop equation , all of the perturbative amplitudes including correlators , the free - energy and those of instanton corrections can be obtained from the spectral curve of the resolvent operator . </S>",
    "<S> however , at the level of non - perturbative completion , the resolvent operator is generally _ not _ sufficient to recover all the information from the loop equations . </S>",
    "<S> therefore it is necessary to find _ a sufficient set of operators _ which provide the missing non - perturbative information .    in this paper </S>",
    "<S> , we study _ generalized wronskians _ of the baker - akhiezer systems as a manifestation of these new degrees of freedom . </S>",
    "<S> in particular , we derive their isomonodromy systems and then extend several spectral dualities to these systems . </S>",
    "<S> in addition , we discuss how these wronskian operators are naturally aligned on the kac table . </S>",
    "<S> since they are consistent with the seiberg - shih relation , we propose that these new degrees of freedom can be identified as fzzt - cardy branes in liouville theory . </S>",
    "<S> this means that fzzt - cardy branes are the bound states of _ elemental fzzt branes _ </S>",
    "<S> ( i.e.  the twisted fermions ) rather than the bound states of principal fzzt - brane ( i.e.  the resolvent operator ) .    </S>",
