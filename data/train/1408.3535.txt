    "in the recent years , noncentral potentials have received a lot of attention in various fields of physics .",
    "this is due to the occurrence of `` accidental '' degeneracy and `` hidden '' symmetry in those noncentral potentials . in quantum chemistry and nuclear physics ,",
    "these noncentral potentials are use in the descriptions of ring - shaped molecules like benzene and the interactions between deformed pair of nuclei .",
    "this study started with the pioneering work of hartmann @xcite and makarov et al @xcite on ring - shaped potentials .",
    "because of its applications in quantum chemistry and nuclear physics , numerous studies have been reported in the recent years .",
    "quesne proposed a ring - shaped potential in which the coulomb part of the hartmann potential was replaced by a harmonic oscillator term .",
    "in addition , the dynamical invariance algebra and accidental degeneracy have been carried out for this new model potential @xcite .",
    "recently , chen and dong found a new ring - shaped ( noncentral ) potential and obtained the exact solution of the schrdinger for the coulomb potential plus this new ring shaped potential @xcite .",
    "shortly thereafter , ikhdair and sever @xcite , proposed a new non - central potential .",
    "they obtained the energy eigenvalues and eigenfunctions of the bound - states for the schrdinger equation in d - dimensions for the potential via the nikiforov - uvarov method .",
    "cheng and dai @xcite , proposed a new potential consisting of a modified kratzer s potential @xcite plus the new proposed ring - shaped potential in @xcite .",
    "furthermore , the schrdinger equation with noncentral electric dipole ring - shaped potential have been investigated by working in a complete square integrable basis that supports an infinite tridiagonal matrix representation of the wave operator @xcite .",
    "very recently , falaye obtained the solutions of three dimensional klein - gordon equation for the spherically and non - spherically harmonic oscillatory ring - shaped potentials within the framework of asymptotic iteration method@xcite .",
    "these contributions were achieved by solving the schrdinger equation through separating the variables in spherical , parabolic or other orthogonal curvilinear coordinate systems .",
    "few of the approaches which have been employed in solving this problems include the asymptotic iteration method ( aim ) @xcite-@xcite , feynman integral formalism @xcite , functional analysis approach @xcite , exact quantization rule method @xcite , proper quantization rule @xcite , nikiforov - uvarov ( nu ) method @xcite , supersymetric quantum mechanics @xcite-@xcite , etc",

    "it is therefore the priority purpose of the present work to find the bound state solution of mie - type ring shaped diatomic molecular potential and then to obtain the information - theoretic measures for this potential via the fisher s information entropy .",
    "shannon entropy ( s ) and the fisher s information measure ( f ) of the probability distributions are becoming increasingly important tools of scientific analysis in a variety of disciplines of scientific inquiry @xcite .",
    "for instance , the frieden extreme physical information principle uses the fisher s information measure to derive important laws of chemistry and physics , such as the equations of the nonrelativistic quantum mechanics or relevant results in density functional theory .",
    "jaynes maximum entropy principle , which utilizes s , provides a method for constructing the whole of statistical thermodynamics which has led to a large variety of applications centered around s. there has been several worthy attempt in applying the two probability distribution functions in physics and related areas @xcite . these implies that both s and f can be used as complementary tools to describe the information behavior , pattern , or complexity of physical systems and the electronic processes involving them @xcite .",
    "the f controls the localization of the density around its nodes @xcite . in this sense , it is also a measure of the oscillatory degree of the wavefunction .",
    "further , the f has been shown to be closely related to some density functionals describing physical and chemical observables @xcite .",
    "in particular , it describes the weiszcker energy functional of atomic and molecular systems @xcite and it has an intimate connection with the kinetic energy @xcite .",
    "recently , romera and dehesa @xcite used the f to analyze some correlation properties of many - electron systems , to characterize the so - called avoided crossings of atomic systems in external electric and magnetic fields , and to derive the uncertainty relation of cramer  rao type .    for rank-2 density matrices and operators with zero diagonal elements in the eigenbasis of the density matrix , toth and petz",
