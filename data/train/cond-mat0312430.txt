    "we have presented a theory for the statistics of energy exchange between coupled quantum systems . as an application we have calculated the statistics of energy dissipation into the leads connected to a mesoscopic conductor .",
    "general and exact expressions can be obtained for the energy flow from a quantum system into a linear environment .",
    "we have used these results to calculate the moments of the heat dissipated in a linear circuit around a mesoscopic conductor .",
    "they have been expressed in terms of moments of the current fluctuations produced by the conductor .",
    "as one expects , the variance of this dissipated heat depends on the fourth cumulative moment of current fluctuations produced by the conductor .",
    "heat fluctuations may therefore serve as a tool to detect the fourth cumulant , whose direct measurement is difficult .",
