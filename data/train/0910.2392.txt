    "one of the basic questions in finite temperature qcd is : what is the nature of the finite temperature phase transition , and at which temperature does it happen ? in spite of enormous computational efforts  @xcite ,",
    "the answer to this question has remained controversial .",
    "the wuppertal group  @xcite finds the deconfining transition , which we identify with the peak of the polyakov - loop susceptibility , and the chiral transition at widely separated temperatures .",
    "in contrast , the brookhaven / bielefeld collaboration claims both temperatures to coincide .",
