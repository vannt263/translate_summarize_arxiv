    "<S> gravitational redshift is generally calculated without considering the rotation of a body . neglecting the rotation , </S>",
    "<S> the geometry of space time can be described by using the spherically symmetric schwarzschild geometry . </S>",
    "<S> rotation has great effect on general relativity , which gives new challenges on gravitational redshift . when rotation is taken into consideration spherical symmetry is lost and off diagonal terms appear in the metric . </S>",
    "<S> the geometry of space time can be then described by using the solutions of kerr family . in the present paper </S>",
    "<S> we discuss the gravitational redshift for rotating body by using kerr metric . </S>",
    "<S> the numerical calculations has been done under newtonian approximation of angular momentum . </S>",
    "<S> it has been found that the value of gravitational redshift is influenced by the direction of spin of central body and also on the position ( latitude ) on the central body at which the photon is emitted . </S>",
    "<S> the variation of gravitational redshift from equatorial to non - equatorial region has been calculated and its implications are discussed in detail . </S>"
