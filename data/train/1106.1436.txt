    "<S> we discuss the classification of symmetries and the corresponding symmetry groups in the two - higgs - doublet model  ( thdm ) . </S>",
    "<S> we give an easily useable method how to determine the symmetry class and corresponding symmetry group of a given thdm higgs potential . </S>",
    "<S> one of the symmetry classes corresponds to a higgs potential with several simultaneous generalised cp symmetries . extending the cp symmetry of this class to the yukawa sector in a straightforward way </S>",
    "<S> , the so - called maximally - cp - symmetric model  ( mcpm ) is obtained . </S>",
    "<S> we study the evolution of the quartic higgs - potential parameters under a change of renormalisation point . </S>",
    "<S> finally we compute the so called oblique parameters @xmath0 , @xmath1 , and @xmath2 , in the mcpm and we identify large regions of viable parameter space with respect to electroweak precision measurements . </S>",
    "<S> we present the corresponding allowed regions for the masses of the physical higgs bosons . </S>",
    "<S> reasonable ranges for these masses , up to several hundred gev , are obtained which should make the ( extra ) higgs bosons detectable in lhc experiments . </S>"
