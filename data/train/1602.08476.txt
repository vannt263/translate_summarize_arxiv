    "<S> we disclose the effects of the logarithmic nonlinear electrodynamics on the holographic conductivity of lifshitz dilaton black holes / branes . </S>",
    "<S> we analyze thermodynamics of these solutions as a necessary requirement for applying gauge / gravity duality , by calculating conserved and thermodynamic quantities such as the temperature , entropy , electric potential and mass of the black holes / branes . </S>",
    "<S> we calculate the holographic conductivity for a @xmath0-dimensional brane boundary and study its behavior in terms of the frequency per temperature . </S>",
    "<S> interestingly enough , we find out that , in contrast to the lifshitz - maxwell - dilaton black branes which has conductivity for all @xmath1 , here in the presence of nonlinear gauge field , the holographic conductivity do exist provided @xmath2 and vanishes for @xmath3 . </S>",
    "<S> it is shown that independent of the nonlinear parameter @xmath4 , the real part of the conductivity is the same for a specific value of frequency per temperature in both ads and lifshitz cases . </S>",
    "<S> besides , the behavior of real part of conductivity for large frequencies has a positive slope with respect to large frequencies for a system with lifshitz symmetry whereas it tends to a constant for a system with ads symmetry </S>",
    "<S> . this behavior may be interpreted as existence of an additional charge carrier rather than the ads case , and is due to the presence of the scalar dilaton field in model . </S>",
