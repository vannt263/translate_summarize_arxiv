    "<S> an exactly solvable lattice model with infinite - range potential is applied to uniaxial ferroelectrics . </S>",
    "<S> asymptotically exact expression for free energy as a function of an order parameter at any temperatures is obtained . </S>",
    "<S> effect of thermal expansion of lattice unit cell is taken into account . </S>",
    "<S> the free energy expansion in powers of order parameter in the vicinity of critical point is presented . </S>",
    "<S> corrections to landau expansion are obtained . in particular </S>",
    "<S> , it is shown that summand with external field contains a contribution of higher powers over order parameter . </S>"
