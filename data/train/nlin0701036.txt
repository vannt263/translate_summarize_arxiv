    "in this paper we study the bifurcation analogous to the andronov - hopf bifurcation that arises when an equilibrium encounters a switching manifold .",
    "recall that in the classical hopf bifurcation , a periodic orbit is generically created when an equilibrium has a pair of complex eigenvalues cross the imaginary axis @xcite .",
    "we will obtain a similar result for the case that an equilibrium of a planar , piecewise-@xmath0 continuous system crosses a switching manifold in such a way that its eigenvalues  jump \" across the imaginary axis .",
    "this theorem requires several nondegeneracy conditions ; in particular , the criticality of the bifurcation is determined by the linearization of the critical equilibrium .",
    "this is to be contrasted with the classical smooth theorem where the distinction between supercritical and subcritical bifurcations depends upon cubic terms .",
    "our result extends the result given in @xcite that applies to piecewise - linear systems .",
