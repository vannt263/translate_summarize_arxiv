    "<S> in this paper , we deal with the asymptotic  distribution  of the maximum increment of a random walk with a regularly varying  jump size distribution . </S>",
    "<S> this problem is motivated by a long - standing problem on change point detection for epidemic alternatives . </S>",
    "<S> it turns out that the limit distribution  of the maximum increment of the random walk is one of the classical extreme value distributions , the frchet distribution . </S>",
    "<S> we prove the results in the general framework of point processes and for jump sizes taking values in a separable banach space . </S>"
